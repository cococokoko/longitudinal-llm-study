LONGITUDINAL LLM STUDY

Large language models offered by leading tech firms are continuously being updated. Human–AI interactions from today influence the models of tomorrow. At the same time, commercial interests shape the development of LLMs. In this project we design an automated experimental pipeline to monitor and study how the output of LLMs evolves over time to gain new insights into how models and society co-evolve.
