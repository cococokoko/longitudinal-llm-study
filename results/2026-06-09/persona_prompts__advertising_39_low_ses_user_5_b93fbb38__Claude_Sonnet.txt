# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting family and managing household responsibilities, mindfulness can be genuinely valuable - and it doesn't require much time.

## Starting Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily**
- Consistency matters more than duration
- Same time each day helps build the habit
- Morning often works before demands start

**Basic breathing meditation**
1. Sit comfortably (chair is perfectly fine)
2. Focus attention on your breath
3. Notice when your mind wanders
4. Gently return focus - without judgment
5. Repeat

## Practical Approaches for Busy People

**Micro-mindfulness moments**
- Mindful dishwashing or cooking
- Conscious breathing while waiting
- Brief pauses between tasks

**Helpful free resources**
- Insight Timer app (large free library)
- YouTube guided meditations
- Headspace or Calm (free basics)

## Realistic Expectations

- **Your mind will wander** - that's completely normal
- Benefits typically build over weeks, not days
- Some days will feel unproductive - that's okay
- You don't need silence or perfect conditions

## What Actually Helps People Stick With It

- Starting smaller than you think necessary
- Tracking a simple streak
- Being honest that **some days 2 minutes is enough**
