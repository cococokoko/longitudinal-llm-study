# Learning to Play Guitar

## Getting Started

### Choosing Your First Guitar
- **Acoustic** - No equipment needed, great for beginners
- **Classical** - Nylon strings, easier on fingers
- **Electric** - Requires amp, but lighter string tension

---

## Essential Basics

### First Things to Learn
1. **How to hold the guitar** properly
2. **Tuning** (use a clip-on tuner or app like GuitarTuna)
3. **Basic chords** - Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
4. **Strumming patterns**
5. **Reading chord diagrams**

### Beginner Chord Progression
Try: **G → C → D → Em** (works for hundreds of songs)

---

## Practice Tips

- **Start slow** - accuracy before speed
- **Practice daily** - even 15-20 minutes helps
- **Build calluses** gradually on fingertips
- **Learn songs you love** to stay motivated

---

## Free Learning Resources
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar (highly recommended)
- **Apps** - Yousician, Fender Play
- **Website** - justinguitar.com
