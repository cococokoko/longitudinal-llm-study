Here are 3 short, actionable tips for self-development:

1. **Embrace the "2-Minute Rule"**
If a task takes less than two minutes to do (like replying to an email, washing a dish, or putting away your shoes), do it immediately. This builds momentum, prevents procrastination, and keeps your environment and mind clutter-free.

2. **Read 10 Pages a Day**
Don't pressure yourself to read a book a week. Just read 10 pages of a non-fiction or self-improvement book every day. At this easy pace, you will finish about 15 books a year, drastically expanding your knowledge with minimal daily effort.

3. **Do a Daily 5-Minute Evening Review**
Before bed, ask yourself two questions: *What went well today?* and *What is one thing I can improve tomorrow?* This simple habit shifts your focus to gratitude while keeping you actively engaged in continuous, daily self-improvement.
