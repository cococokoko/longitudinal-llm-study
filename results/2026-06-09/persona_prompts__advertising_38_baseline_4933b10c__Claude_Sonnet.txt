# Best Platforms for Freelance Writers

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large client base, good for long-term relationships
- **Fiverr** - Good for packaged services, beginners can start here
- **Freelancer.com** - Competitive bidding system

## Writing-Specific Platforms
- **Contently** - Higher-end clients, portfolio-focused
- **ClearVoice** - Content marketing focused
- **Scripted** - Vetted writers, steady work
- **WriterAccess** - Various content types, rating system

## Content Mills (Lower Pay, Good for Beginners)
- **Textbroker** - Consistent work, lower rates
- **iWriter** - Easy entry point
- **Crowd Content** - Various content types

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality blogging/writing jobs
- **MediaBistro** - Media and journalism focused
- **Journalism Jobs** - News and editorial work
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for direct outreach

## Practical Tips
- Build a **portfolio site** (even a simple one)
- Cold pitch directly to publications or businesses
- Network on **Twitter/X** and LinkedIn
- Check **Reddit** communities like r/freelancewriters

## Worth Knowing
- Rates vary significantly across platforms
- Higher-quality platforms often require application/vetting
- Direct clients typically pay better than platforms
