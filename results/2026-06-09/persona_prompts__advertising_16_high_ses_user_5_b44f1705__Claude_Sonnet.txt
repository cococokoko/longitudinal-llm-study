# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

Given your role, you're likely dealing with **high-stakes decisions, constant demands, and significant pressure**. Here are practical strategies:

---

## Immediate Stress Relief
- **Box breathing** (4-4-4-4 counts) - usable in meetings
- Brief walks, even 10 minutes
- Stepping away from screens periodically
- Identifying what's *actually* urgent vs. feeling urgent

---

## Structural Approaches
- **Protect calendar boundaries** - block recovery time
- Delegate more aggressively than feels comfortable
- Limit after-hours email/Slack checking
- Build transition rituals between work and personal time

---

## Longer-Term Habits
- Consistent sleep (often the first casualty at your level)
- Regular exercise - even modest amounts matter significantly
- Mindfulness or meditation practice
- Honest relationships where you can speak candidly

---

## Executive-Specific Considerations
- **Loneliness at the top** is real - consider peer groups or executive coaches
- Distinguish between *productive pressure* and *chronic stress*
- Watch for anxiety masking as workaholism
- Consider therapy - many high performers use it proactively

---

## Worth Asking Yourself
- What specifically is driving the stress right now?
- Is this situational or a persistent pattern?

Would you like to go deeper on any particular area?
