── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I optimize my home office for productivity?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to optimize your home office for productivity:

## Physical Setup
- **Dedicated space** - Use a specific area only for work to mentally separate work from home life
- **Ergonomic furniture** - Invest in a good chair and desk at proper heights
- **Monitor positioning** - Eye level, arm's length away to reduce strain
- **Good lighting** - Natural light when possible, reduce screen glare

## Reducing Distractions
- **Minimize noise** - Use noise-canceling headphones or white noise
- **Set boundaries** - Communicate work hours to household members
- **Keep it tidy** - Clutter competes for mental attention
- **Phone management** - Keep it out of reach or use focus modes

## Digital Environment
- **Organize your desktop** and file system logically
- **Use focus apps** like Freedom or Cold Turkey to block distracting sites
- **Manage notifications** aggressively

## Routine & Habits
- **Start and end at consistent times**
- **Take regular breaks** (Pomodoro technique works well)
- **Get dressed** - signals your brain it's work time
- **Move regularly** - short walks improve focus

## Quick Wins
- Good ventilation and comfortable temperature
- Keep water nearby
- Have a task list ready before you start

**What matters most depends on your specific challenges** - are you dealing with distractions, discomfort, or motivation issues? I can give more targeted advice.
