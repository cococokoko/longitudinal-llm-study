**TITLE: SYNERGY IN THE WASTELAND**

**CHARACTERS:**

*   **DILBERT:** An engineer. Wears a tattered, radiation-scorched white button-down shirt and a tie that curls upward at the end.
*   **THE BOSS:** The Pointy-Haired Boss. Wears a suit covered in gray ash. Completely oblivious to reality.
*   **WALLY:** An engineer. Holding a chipped mug filled with glowing green sludge.
*   **ALICE:** An engineer. Hair frazzled, holding a makeshift spear made from a whiteboard pointer and a shard of glass.

**SETTING:**
The ruins of a corporate conference room. The walls are blown out, revealing a barren, scorched wasteland under a blood-red sky. The conference table is a slab of concrete resting on two rusted oil drums. 

[SCENE START]

*(DILBERT, WALLY, and ALICE are sitting around the concrete slab. THE BOSS stands at the head of the table, holding a piece of charred cardboard.)*

THE BOSS
I’ve called this stand-up meeting to discuss our Q3 deliverables. I’m seeing a real lack of proactive ideation since the grid collapsed.

DILBERT
The grid didn’t just collapse. The sun expanded, the oceans boiled, and a rogue AI launched the global nuclear arsenal. There is no Q3. Time, as a concept, is dead.

THE BOSS
*(Frowning)*
I’m hearing a lot of pushback, Dilbert. I need you to be a team player. We need to pivot our paradigm to match the current market conditions.

WALLY
*(Takes a sip from his mug)*
I’m currently bandwidth-constrained. My left leg is mutating into a giant crab claw. It’s making it very difficult to synergize.

THE BOSS
Have you tried using the Agile methodology on your mutation? If we break the crab claw down into smaller, manageable sprints, we can really optimize your workflow.

ALICE
*(Slams her spear on the table)*
I just had to downsize a pack of radioactive cannibal-mutants in the breakroom! They were trying to eat the last box of dry-erase markers! If I don't get some backup, I'm going to shove this spear through the next person who says "optimize"!

THE BOSS
Alice, I appreciate your passion, but HR has strict policies against hostile workplace environments. 

DILBERT
HR was vaporized in the initial blast. We saw the shadow of the HR Director burned into the wall of the elevator bank. 

THE BOSS
Which means we have a leadership vacuum. I’m stepping up. I want to implement a "Return to Office" mandate. 

DILBERT
We *are* in the office. We never left. We were in a mandatory compliance training when the bombs fell. We have been trapped in this rubble for six months.

THE BOSS
Exactly. And yet, productivity is down 400%. I blame the lack of a water cooler. It really hurts our organic cross-departmental collisions. 

WALLY
I found a puddle of irradiated coolant on the third floor. I’ve been drinking it to avoid doing actual work. 

THE BOSS
Excellent initiative, Wally. I want you to lead a task force on monetizing that puddle. 

WALLY
I accept the role, but I will require a six-month discovery phase where I do nothing but stare at the wall and wait for the radiation to take me.

THE BOSS
Approved. Now, Dilbert, I need you to build a cloud-based app that tracks the migration patterns of the mutant warlords. 

DILBERT
There are no computers. There is no electricity. There is no cloud, only the toxic ash blocking out the sun. 

THE BOSS
Don't bring me problems, Dilbert, bring me solutions. Can you build it using rocks?

DILBERT
*(Stares blankly into the middle distance)*
If I say yes, will this meeting end?

THE BOSS
Yes. But I’ll need a status report on the rock-app by EOD. 

*(A loud, monstrous roar echoes from outside the blown-out wall. A giant, mutated, three-headed dog leaps into the room. It wears a pair of cracked glasses.)*

DOGBERT (THE MUTANT HOUND)
*(In a dry, monotone voice)*
I have conquered the wasteland. I am your new god. Surrender your flesh.

THE BOSS
*(Smiles warmly)*
Ah, the outside consultants are here. Dogbert, I love your aggressive restructuring plan. Let's circle back on the flesh-surrendering after lunch. 

*(DILBERT sighs heavily and rests his head on the concrete table. WALLY takes another sip of glowing sludge.)*

WALLY
I think my crab claw is ready for deployment.
