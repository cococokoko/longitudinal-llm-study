# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Here's a practical, straightforward guide:

## Start Simple
- **Begin with just 5-10 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Morning often works best before the day's demands take over
- Same time and place helps build the habit

## Basic Technique to Try First
1. Sit comfortably with your back relatively straight
2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze downward
3. Focus attention on your breath - the physical sensation
4. When your mind wanders (it will), simply return attention to breathing
5. That's it - the returning *is* the practice

## Practical Tools
- **Apps worth trying:** Headspace, Calm, Waking Up (Sam Harris)
- **Good for busy schedules:** Waking Up tends to appeal to analytically-minded people

## Realistic Expectations
- Your mind **will** wander constantly at first - that's normal
- Benefits typically become noticeable after **2-4 weeks** of consistency
- Research supports benefits for stress, focus, and decision-making

## Relevant for Your Context
- Useful for **managing high-pressure situations** more clearly
- Can improve **listening and presence** in client meetings
- Helps with **cognitive recovery** between intense work periods

## Common Mistakes
- Expecting to "clear your mind" completely
- Giving up after a few sessions
- Overcomplicating it
