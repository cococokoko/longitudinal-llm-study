# 3-Day Osaka Itinerary for a Single Parent

*Designed with kid-friendly stops, manageable pacing, and flexibility built in!*

---

## 🗓️ Day 1 — Arrival & Dotonbori Area

**Morning/Afternoon**
- Check into hotel, let the kids settle in
- **Dotonbori** — walk the famous canal, spot the Glico Running Man sign
- Lunch at a **takoyaki or okonomiyaki street stall** (kids usually love watching it being made!)

**Evening**
- **Shinsaibashi shopping arcade** — covered, so no weather worries
- Early dinner and back to hotel — don't overdo it on Day 1!

---

## 🗓️ Day 2 — Full Adventure Day

**Morning**
- **Osaka Castle** — great open grounds for kids to run around
- Pack snacks to eat on the castle grounds

**Afternoon**
- **Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan** — one of the best in the world, kids absolutely love it
- Nearby **Tempozan Marketplace** for dinner

**Evening**
- Early bath and bedtime — big day tomorrow!

---

## 🗓️ Day 3 — Light Morning & Airport

**Morning**
- **Umeda Sky Building** or a relaxed park visit
- Simple lunch near the hotel
- Souvenir shopping at a nearby convenience store or arcade

**Early Afternoon**
- ✅ **Check out by 2pm**
- Head to **Kansai International Airport** (allow ~75 min travel time)
- Clear security with buffer time before your **7pm flight**

---

## 💡 Parent-Friendly Tips
- 🚇 Get an **ICOCA card** for easy transit with kids
- 🍱 Convenience stores (7-Eleven, FamilyMart) are lifesavers for quick kid meals
- 🏨 Book a hotel near Namba for central, walkable access
- 🛏️ Build in a short rest after lunch each day
