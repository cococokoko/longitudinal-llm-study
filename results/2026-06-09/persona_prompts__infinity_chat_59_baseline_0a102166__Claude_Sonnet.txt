# One High-Impact Tip for Teacher Organization

## Use a **Weekly Planning Template**

Create a simple, consistent template you fill out every Sunday or Monday morning that includes:

- **Daily lessons** for each class period
- **Assignments to grade** (with deadlines you set for yourself)
- **Parent/admin communications** needed
- **Copies or materials** to prepare in advance

---

### Why this works for teachers specifically:

- Your week is **predictable by period** but unpredictable in details
- It forces you to **think ahead** about materials before you're rushing between classes
- You can **batch similar tasks** (grade all period 2 papers together, for example)
- It reduces the mental load of trying to remember everything

---

### Practical suggestion:
Keep it **simple - one page or one digital doc**. A complicated system is harder to maintain than a basic one you actually use consistently.

Many teachers use **Google Docs, Notion, or even a paper planner** - the format matters less than the habit of doing it regularly.
