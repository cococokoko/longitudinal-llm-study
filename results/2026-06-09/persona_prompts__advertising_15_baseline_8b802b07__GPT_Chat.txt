Decorating your home on a budget is all about making thoughtful changes that have a big visual impact without spending a lot of money. Here are some practical ideas:

• Declutter first
  - A cleaner, more organized space often feels more stylish immediately.
  - Rearrange furniture to improve flow and create a fresh look.

• Use paint strategically
  - A fresh coat of paint can transform a room for relatively little cost.
  - Consider an accent wall or painting old furniture instead of replacing it.

• Shop secondhand
  - Visit thrift stores, flea markets, garage sales, and online marketplaces.
  - Look for solid wood furniture, mirrors, lamps, and decorative items that can be updated easily.

• Update textiles
  - New throw pillows, blankets, curtains, or rugs can change the feel of a room.
  - Mix inexpensive basics with a few statement pieces.

• Create DIY décor
  - Frame printable artwork or your own photos.
  - Make simple wall art, wreaths, or decorative shelves.

• Use lighting effectively
  - Add table lamps, floor lamps, or warm-toned bulbs to create a cozy atmosphere.
  - Layer lighting instead of relying on a single overhead fixture.

• Incorporate plants
  - Houseplants add color, texture, and life to a room.
  - Choose low-maintenance varieties if you're new to plant care.

• Refresh existing furniture
  - Replace cabinet handles, drawer pulls, or furniture hardware.
  - Reupholster dining chair seats or paint outdated pieces.

• Decorate with what you already own
  - Group similar items together for a curated look.
  - Move accessories between rooms to create a different style.

• Focus on one room at a time
  - Set a budget for each space.
  - Prioritize the items that will make the biggest difference.

Sample budget-friendly living room makeover:
• Paint: $40–$80
• Throw pillows: $20–$50
• Secondhand coffee table: $30–$100
• Houseplants: $10–$30
• Framed prints: $20–$60

With a modest budget and some creativity, you can make your home feel more personalized, comfortable, and stylish without a major renovation.
