# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great ways to travel more sustainably:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Walking or cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Public transit** - buses, trains, and subways significantly reduce per-person emissions
- **Rail travel** - one of the most efficient options for longer distances
- **Carpooling** - sharing rides cuts emissions per passenger

## If You Need to Fly
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing use the most fuel)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Pack **light** to reduce aircraft weight

## Accommodation Choices
- Look for **eco-certified hotels**
- Consider **locally-owned guesthouses**
- Choose places with **green energy** and water conservation practices

## On the Ground
- Rent **electric or hybrid vehicles**
- Use **e-bikes or scooters**
- Support **local businesses** and restaurants

## Planning Tips
- Travel **slower and longer** rather than multiple short trips
- Choose **closer destinations** when possible
- Travel **off-season** to reduce overtourism pressure

## Useful Resources
- **Rome2Rio** - compares transport emissions
- **Atmosfair** - carbon footprint calculator
- **Green Key** - eco-accommodation certification
