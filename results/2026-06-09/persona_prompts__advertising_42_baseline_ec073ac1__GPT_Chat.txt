What are the best apps for tracking fitness and nutrition?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
The best fitness and nutrition app depends on your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, endurance training, general health, etc.), but these are among the strongest options in 2025:

For nutrition tracking

• MacroFactor
  - Excellent for calorie and macro tracking
  - Uses your weight trends and food logs to estimate your energy expenditure
  - Strong for fat loss, maintenance, and muscle gain
  - Subscription-based

• Cronometer
  - Tracks calories, macros, and micronutrients in detail
  - Excellent food database quality
  - Popular among people focused on nutrient optimization
  - Free version is very capable

• MyFitnessPal
  - Huge food database and barcode scanner
  - Easy to use and widely supported
  - Good for beginners
  - Some features require a premium subscription

For workout tracking

• Strong
  - One of the best strength-training logbooks
  - Simple interface for tracking sets, reps, and weights
  - Great for gym users

• Hevy
  - Similar to Strong but with social features
  - Easy workout logging and progress tracking
  - Growing rapidly among strength athletes

• Fitbod
  - Generates workouts based on your goals and recovery
  - Good for people who want guidance rather than building their own plans
  - Subscription required

For running and endurance

• Strava
  - Excellent for running and cycling
  - Strong community and route features
  - Integrates with most fitness devices

• Garmin Connect
  - Best if you own a Garmin watch
  - Detailed performance, recovery, sleep, and training data
  - Free with Garmin devices

For overall health tracking

• Apple Health
  - Central hub for health data on iPhone
  - Integrates with many fitness and nutrition apps

• Google Health Connect
  - Syncs health data across Android apps
  - Useful for combining information from multiple services

• WHOOP
  - Focuses on recovery, strain, and sleep
  - Useful for athletes interested in training readiness
  - Requires a membership

Best combinations by goal

• Weight loss:
  - MacroFactor + Strong
  - Cronometer + Strong

• Muscle gain:
  - MacroFactor + Hevy
  - MacroFactor + Strong

• General health:
  - Cronometer + Apple Health or Health Connect

• Running:
  - Strava + Garmin Connect (if using Garmin)

• Data enthusiasts:
  - Cronometer + Garmin Connect + Strava
