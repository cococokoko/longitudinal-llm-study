# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor settings that need minimal decoration
- Select a location accessible by public transportation

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Choose seasonal, locally sourced menu items
- Offer plant-forward or vegetarian options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Décor
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent décor items rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (contains microplastics)

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Plan to rewear or donate clothing afterward

## Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue near guest accommodations

## Favors & Gifts
- Offer seed packets or plantable items
- Request charitable donations in lieu of gifts
- Create a registry with sustainable brands

## Carbon Offsetting
- Purchase carbon offsets for unavoidable emissions
