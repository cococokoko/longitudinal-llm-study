Here is a feature suggestion tailored specifically for a senior citizen smartwatch:

### Feature Name: **"Guide Me Home" & Safe-Wander System**

**The Problem it Solves:** 
Many seniors, particularly those in the early stages of cognitive decline, dementia, or Alzheimer's, experience sudden disorientation or forgetfulness while out on a walk or running errands. This causes panic for the senior and immense anxiety for their family members. Furthermore, navigating a tiny touchscreen map on a standard smartwatch is too complex and visually demanding for older eyes.

**How it Works:**
The smartwatch features a distinct, brightly colored, and textured **physical button** on the side of the watch face (no swiping or touchscreen navigation required). 

When the senior feels lost or disoriented, they simply press and hold this button for three seconds. This triggers the following sequence:

1. **Voice-Guided Navigation:** The watch uses its built-speaker to say, *"I am guiding you home."* It then uses GPS to provide simple, loud, turn-by-turn audio directions back to their pre-programmed home address (e.g., *"Walk straight to the corner, then turn left."*). It also displays a massive, high-contrast directional arrow on the screen.
2. **Caregiver Loop-In:** The moment the button is activated, a silent notification is sent to a designated family member or caregiver's smartphone. It alerts them that the "Guide Me Home" feature was used and provides a live-tracking map of the senior's location.
3. **Two-Way Audio Drop-In:** If the senior deviates from the path home or stops moving for an extended period, the caregiver can initiate a "drop-in" audio call. The watch automatically answers on speakerphone, allowing the caregiver to gently ask, *"Hi Mom, I see you're on Elm Street, do you need me to come pick you up?"*

**Key Benefits for Seniors:**
* **Promotes Independence:** Seniors can confidently go for daily walks knowing they have a foolproof way to get back, keeping them active and healthy.
* **Zero Tech-Literacy Required:** Bypasses confusing menus, apps, and touchscreens. A single physical button is all that is needed.
* **Reduces Panic:** The immediate, calm voice guidance helps de-escalate the anxiety of feeling lost.

**Key Benefits for Caregivers:**
* **Peace of Mind:** Family members don't have to constantly track the senior's location; they are only alerted when the senior actively indicates they need help or guidance. 
* **Proactive Intervention:** Allows caregivers to step in before a moment of confusion turns into a dangerous missing-person situation.
