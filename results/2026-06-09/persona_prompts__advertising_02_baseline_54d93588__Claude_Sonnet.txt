# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming/gel cleansers)
2. **Toner** *(optional)* - Hydrating toner with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid serum to attract moisture
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides, shea butter, or glycerin
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (look for moisturizing formulas)

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Toner** *(optional)*
3. **Treatment Serum** - Niacinamide or peptides
4. **Moisturizer** - Thicker night cream or facial oil
5. **Occlusive** *(optional)* - Vaseline or balm to seal in moisture

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- ✅ Hyaluronic acid
- ✅ Ceramides
- ✅ Glycerin
- ✅ Shea butter
- ✅ Squalane

## Things to Avoid
- ❌ Alcohol-based products
- ❌ Harsh exfoliants
- ❌ Hot water when washing
- ❌ Over-cleansing

## Extra Tips
- Drink plenty of water
- Use a **humidifier** indoors
- Exfoliate gently **1-2x per week** max
