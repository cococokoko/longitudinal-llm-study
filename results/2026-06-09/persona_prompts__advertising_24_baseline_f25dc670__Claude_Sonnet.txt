What are the best ways to save for retirement?

# Best Ways to Save for Retirement

## Tax-Advantaged Accounts (Start Here)

**Employer-Sponsored Plans**
- **401(k) or 403(b)** - contribute enough to get full employer match (free money)
- 2024 contribution limit: $23,000 ($30,500 if 50+)

**Individual Retirement Accounts**
- **Traditional IRA** - tax deduction now, pay taxes in retirement
- **Roth IRA** - pay taxes now, tax-free withdrawals later
- 2024 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+)

## Key Strategies

- **Start early** - compound interest rewards time significantly
- **Automate contributions** so you don't skip them
- **Increase contributions** when you get raises
- **Diversify investments** across stocks, bonds, and other assets
- **Minimize fees** - even small differences matter over decades

## General Priority Order
1. Get full employer 401(k) match
2. Max out HSA (if eligible) - triple tax advantage
3. Max out Roth/Traditional IRA
4. Return to max out 401(k)
5. Taxable brokerage account

## Things to Avoid
- Cashing out early (penalties + taxes)
- Being too conservative too young
- Ignoring inflation

## Worth Considering
- Talking to a **fee-only financial advisor**
- Social Security timing strategy
