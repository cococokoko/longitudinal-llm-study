# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are practical, evidence-based approaches:

## Bodyweight Training
- **Push-up variations** (standard, wide, diamond, pike)
- **Squats and lunges** for lower body strength
- **Planks and core work**
- **Pull-ups** using a doorframe bar or sturdy branch

## Cardiovascular Options
- Running or brisk walking
- Cycling
- Jump rope (highly efficient)
- Swimming if accessible

## Structured Programs Worth Considering
- **HIIT** (High-Intensity Interval Training) - time efficient
- **Yoga** - flexibility, strength, and stress management
- **Calisthenics progressions** - scalable difficulty

## Practical Tips
- **Consistency matters more than intensity** - 30 minutes daily beats occasional hard sessions
- Use stairs deliberately
- Walk or cycle for errands
- Set a recurring schedule to protect the time

## Minimal Equipment (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands (~$15-20)
- Kettlebell (versatile)
- Foam roller for recovery

## Tracking Progress
- Apps like **Nike Training Club** or **Freeletics** offer free structured workouts
- Keep a simple log to maintain motivation
