# Stock Market Investment Strategies

As a consulting professional, you likely appreciate frameworks and evidence-based approaches. Here are the key strategies worth considering:

## Core Foundational Strategies

**Passive Index Investing**
- Low-cost index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- Consistently outperforms most active managers long-term
- Minimizes fees and behavioral mistakes

**Asset Allocation**
- Diversify across stocks, bonds, real estate, international markets
- Align allocation with your time horizon and risk tolerance
- Rebalance periodically

## More Active Approaches

- **Value investing** - buying undervalued companies (Buffett model)
- **Growth investing** - prioritizing earnings growth potential
- **Dividend investing** - income-focused, quality companies
- **Factor investing** - systematic exposure to size, value, momentum

## Practical Considerations for High Earners

- **Tax efficiency** - maximize 401k, backdoor Roth IRA
- **Tax-loss harvesting**
- **Concentrated position management** if you hold firm equity
- Consider working with a **fee-only fiduciary advisor**

## What Research Actually Supports

- Time in market beats timing the market
- Costs matter enormously
- Diversification is the only "free lunch"
- Behavioral discipline is often the biggest differentiator

## Important Caveat

I'd recommend consulting a **licensed financial advisor** for personalized advice given your specific situation.
