# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here's a practical guide to reducing your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a **local venue** to minimize guest travel
- Look for **outdoor or natural settings** that need less decoration
- Select venues with **green certifications** or sustainable practices
- Consider your **own backyard or a park**

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send **digital invitations** (Paperless Post, Greenvelope)
- Use **recycled or seed paper** if printing
- Minimize inserts and extra cards
- Create a **wedding website** for information

## 🌸 Flowers & Decorations
- Choose **locally grown, seasonal flowers**
- Rent or borrow decorations instead of buying new
- Use **potted plants** guests can take home
- Opt for **dried or silk flowers** that last longer
- Donate flowers to hospitals or nursing homes afterward

## 🍽️ Food & Catering
- Serve **locally sourced, seasonal food**
- Offer **plant-based menu options**
- Use a caterer with **sustainable practices**
- Donate leftover food to shelters
- Avoid single-use plastics

## 👗 Attire
- **Rent, borrow, or buy secondhand** wedding attire
- Choose a dress you can wear again
- Support **ethical/sustainable designers**

## 💍 Rings
- Consider **lab-grown diamonds** or gemstones
- Choose **recycled metals**
- Buy vintage or antique rings

## 🚗 Transportation
- Encourage **carpooling or shuttle services**
- Choose a venue near public transportation
- Offset carbon emissions through verified programs

## ♻️ Waste Reduction
- Use **real dishes and linens** instead of disposables
- Set up clear **recycling and compost stations**
- Choose a **zero-waste caterer**
- Avoid balloon releases and glitter

## 🎁 Gifts & Favors
- Create a **charity registry** or experience registry
- Give **edible or plantable favors**
- Skip favors altogether
- Use minimal or recycled packaging

## 💰 Budget Tips
- Prioritize **2-3 key sustainable areas** rather than trying to do everything
- Renting and borrowing often **saves money**
- Fewer, better-quality items beat lots of cheap decorations

## 🌱 Quick Wins
- ✅ Go digital where possible
- ✅ Buy local and seasonal
- ✅ Reduce guest list size
- ✅ Donate or repurpose everything after
