---

### **Minimum Specs to Look For**
- **RAM:** 8GB (4GB can work but feels slow)
- **Storage:** 256GB SSD (avoid HDD if possible)
- **CPU:** Intel i5 or equivalent
- **OS:** Windows 10/11, Linux, or macOS

---

### **Tips**
- **ThinkPads are often the top recommendation** for beginners - very reliable
- Check **eBay, Facebook Marketplace, or local shops**
- Business laptops tend to be more durable than consumer models
- Linux runs Python very smoothly if you're comfortable with it

---

### **Avoid**
- Chromebooks (limited unless you enable Linux mode)
- Very old 32-bit processors
- Laptops with only HDDs (slow)
