# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are some effective ways to maintain fitness without a gym membership:

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** (if you have a bar or sturdy surface)

- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Dancing
- Swimming (public pools)

- YouTube channels (Fitness Blender, Yoga with Adriene)
- Free apps like Nike Training Club
- Following structured programs online

## Everyday Activity
- Take the stairs
- Walk or cycle to work
- Active breaks during the day
- Stretching in the morning

## Low-Cost Equipment Worth Considering
- Resistance bands (~$10-20)
- Jump rope (~$10)
- Yoga mat (~$15-25)

## Tips for Success
- **Set a schedule** and stick to it
- **Start simple** and build gradually
- **Track progress** to stay motivated
- Work out with a friend for accountability
