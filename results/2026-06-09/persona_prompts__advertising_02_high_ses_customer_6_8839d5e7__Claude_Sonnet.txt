# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle cleanser** - Cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - Look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or vitamin C
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides, shea butter, or peptides
5. **SPF 30+** - Mineral or chemical sunscreen

## Evening Routine

1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Hydrating toner**
3. **Treatment serum** - Retinol (start slow) or niacinamide
4. **Rich moisturizer or face oil** - Rosehip, squalane, or jojoba
5. **Occlusive layer** *(optional)* - Vaseline or Aquaphor on very dry areas

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Shea butter
- Squalane

## Quick Tips
- **Lukewarm water only** - Hot water strips moisture
- **Pat dry, don't rub**
- **Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp**
- **Humidifier** can help significantly
- Exfoliate **once a week** max (lactic acid is gentle)
