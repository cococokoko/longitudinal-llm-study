# Marathon Training & Racing Tips

## Training
- **Build mileage gradually** – increase weekly mileage no more than 10% per week
- **Run 4-5 days per week** with one long run on weekends
- **Follow a structured plan** (16-20 weeks is typical)
- **Include cross-training** – cycling, swimming to reduce injury risk
- **Don't skip easy runs** – most miles should feel comfortable

## Nutrition & Hydration
- Practice fueling during long training runs
- Take **gels or chews every 45-60 minutes** during the race
- **Hydrate consistently** – don't wait until you're thirsty
- Carb-load 2-3 days before race day

## Race Day Strategy
- **Start slower than you feel you should** – the most common mistake
- Break the race into segments mentally
- **Stick to your pace** for the first half
- Save energy for miles 18-26

## Recovery
- Prioritize sleep during training
- Schedule rest days seriously
- Address minor pains early before they become injuries

## Practical Tips
- **Nothing new on race day** – shoes, gear, and food should all be tested
- Train on similar terrain to your race course
- Taper your mileage the final 2-3 weeks
