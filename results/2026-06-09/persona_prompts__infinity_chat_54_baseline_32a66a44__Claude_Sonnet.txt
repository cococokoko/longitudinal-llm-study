# The Legacy of Rome

The Roman Empire stands as one of history's most influential civilizations. Its contributions shaped the modern world profoundly. Roman law formed the foundation of legal systems across Europe and beyond. Latin evolved into today's Romance languages, including French, Spanish, and Italian.
