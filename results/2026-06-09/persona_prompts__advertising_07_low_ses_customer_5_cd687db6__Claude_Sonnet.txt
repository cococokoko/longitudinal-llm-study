# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

Here are practical strategies that can make a real difference, especially for a busy household managing multiple people's needs:

## Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- **Adjust thermostat settings** - even 2-3 degrees makes a meaningful difference
- **Unplug devices** when not in use (TVs, chargers, gaming consoles draw standby power)
- **Run full loads** in dishwashers and washing machines
- **Use cold water** for laundry when possible
- **Turn off lights** when leaving rooms

## Behavioral Changes
- Shift energy-heavy tasks (laundry, dishwasher) to **off-peak hours**
- **Air dry dishes and clothes** when practical
- Keep refrigerator and freezer **well-organized and full** for efficiency
- Shorten shower times across the household

## Moderate Investments
- **LED bulbs** throughout the home
- **Smart power strips** to eliminate phantom loads
- **Programmable thermostat** to match your household's schedule
- Weatherstripping and door seals

## Bigger Upgrades Worth Considering
- Energy-efficient appliances when replacing old ones
- Improved insulation
- Smart home energy monitoring

## Managing a Larger Household
- **Assign energy responsibilities** to family members
- Track monthly usage to spot patterns
- Consider **time-of-use billing** if your utility offers it
