For international students, TOEFL and IELTS are the two most widely accepted English-language proficiency tests used for university admissions, visa applications, and professional registration. The TOEFL iBT is administered primarily in a computer-based format and emphasizes academic English through integrated tasks that combine reading, listening, speaking, and writing skills.
