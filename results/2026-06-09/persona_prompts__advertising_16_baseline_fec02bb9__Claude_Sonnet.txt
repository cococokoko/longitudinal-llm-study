# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures (deadlines, conflicts, demands)
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause

Both are normal, but both can become overwhelming.

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based
- **Identify triggers** – notice what sets it off
- **Challenge anxious thoughts** – ask "Is this realistic?"
- **Limit news/social media** if it increases worry
- **Journaling** – writing can clarify and release tension

### 🫁 Body-Based
- **Deep breathing** – slow exhale activates calm (try 4-7-8 breathing)
- **Regular exercise** – even walking helps significantly
- **Sleep** – poor sleep amplifies anxiety considerably
- **Reduce caffeine/alcohol** – both worsen anxiety

### 🔗 Lifestyle
- **Routine** – structure reduces uncertainty
- **Social connection** – isolation makes anxiety worse
- **Set boundaries** – learn to say no
- **Break tasks down** – overwhelm fuels stress

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, panic attacks)
- It's been persistent for weeks

**Therapy (especially CBT) is highly effective.**
