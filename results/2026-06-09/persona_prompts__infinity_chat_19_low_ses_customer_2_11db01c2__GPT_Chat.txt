- People with access to powerful AI tools may gain advantages in education, networking, and career opportunities.
- Ensuring broad access to beneficial AI technologies may become an important policy goal.

7. Human interaction may become more intentional
- As routine communication becomes automated, genuinely human experiences could become more valued.
- Face-to-face conversations, shared physical activities, and community gatherings may gain importance precisely because they are less mediated by technology.
