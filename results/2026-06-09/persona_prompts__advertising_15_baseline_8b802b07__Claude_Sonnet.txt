# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Create a mood board** using free apps or Pinterest

---

## Low-Cost, High-Impact Ideas

### Paint
- One of the **cheapest ways to transform a room**
- Try an accent wall instead of painting everything
- Neutral colors make spaces feel larger and cleaner

### Rearrange What You Have
- Move furniture around for a fresh feel
- **Costs nothing** and can completely change a room

### Thrift & Secondhand Shopping
- Thrift stores, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist
- Estate sales and garage sales
- **Upcycle old furniture** with paint or new hardware

---

## Budget-Friendly Upgrades
- **Swap cabinet hardware** for an instant kitchen refresh
- **Add mirrors** to make rooms feel bigger and brighter
- **Use plants** for natural, affordable decor
- **DIY artwork** using frames and free printables
- **Update throw pillows and blankets** for seasonal changes

---

## Smart Shopping Tips
- Shop **end-of-season sales**
- Check **discount stores** like IKEA, HomeGoods, or Amazon
- **Buy secondhand frames** and add new art
- Compare prices before buying

---

- YouTube DIY tutorials
- Pinterest for inspiration
- Library books on interior design
