# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

## Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- **Adjust thermostat** – Lower heating by 1-2°C, raise cooling similarly
- **Unplug devices** when not in use to eliminate standby power
- **Use cold water** for laundry when possible
- **Run full loads** in dishwashers and washing machines
- **Turn off lights** when leaving rooms

## Heating & Cooling
- Seal drafts around doors and windows
- Use curtains strategically (close at night in winter, block sun in summer)
- Service HVAC systems regularly
- Use ceiling fans to circulate air
- Add insulation to attic/walls if lacking

## Appliances & Electronics
- Replace old appliances with **Energy Star rated** models
- Switch to **LED lighting** throughout the home
- Use smart power strips
- Set computers to sleep mode automatically

## Bigger Investments
- Install a **programmable/smart thermostat**
- Upgrade insulation and windows
- Consider solar panels
- Install a heat pump water heater

## Habit Changes
- Take shorter showers
- Air-dry dishes and clothes when possible
- Cook with lids on pots
- Use microwave/toaster oven instead of full oven for small meals

## Typical Impact
| Action | Estimated Savings |
|--------|------------------|
| LED lighting | 75% less lighting energy |
| Smart thermostat | 10-15% on heating/cooling |
| Sealing drafts | Up to 20% on heating |
