Honey is one of the only foods on Earth that **never goes bad**. Archaeologists have excavated pots of honey from ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old—and it is still perfectly edible! Honey has natural antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties. For centuries, it was used as a salve for cuts, scrapes, and burns. Medical-grade honey (like Manuka honey) is still used in hospitals today to help heal stubborn wounds and soothe irritated skin.
