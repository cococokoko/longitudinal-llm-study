# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- Compare prices on multiple sites (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Be **flexible with dates** — midweek flights are often cheaper
- Set price alerts for flights you're watching
- Use **incognito mode** when searching to avoid price increases

## Transportation
- Consider budget airlines
- Use points/miles from credit cards or loyalty programs
- Take trains or buses instead of flying for short distances
- Book **connecting flights** instead of direct
- Use public transit instead of taxis/rideshares

## Accommodation
- Try **Airbnb, Hostel World, or Vrbo**
- Use hotel reward programs
- Stay slightly outside city centers
- Consider house swapping or Couchsurfing
- Travel with others to split costs

## Food & Activities
- Cook some meals instead of eating out every time
- Look for **free attractions** (museums, parks, walking tours)
- Get a city tourist card for bundled discounts
- Eat where locals eat — avoid tourist traps

## General Tips
- Travel during **shoulder season** (just before/after peak)
- Get a **no foreign transaction fee** credit card
- Pack light to avoid baggage fees
- Travel to **lower cost-of-living destinations**
