# Managing Stress and Anxiety with an Unpredictable Fast Food Schedule

That combination is genuinely tough - fast food work is physically demanding AND the schedule uncertainty makes it hard to plan your life or rest properly.

## Why Your Situation Is Particularly Stressful
- Can't plan ahead reliably
- Physical exhaustion on top of mental stress
- Often dealing with difficult customers
- May have inconsistent income
- Hard to maintain routines when shifts change

## Practical Strategies That Work With Your Reality

**For schedule unpredictability specifically:**
- Keep a small "buffer" routine that works regardless of shift time
- Prepare meals/clothes in advance when you *do* know your schedule
- Identify 1-2 things you can control each day

**For quick stress relief (even on breaks):**
- Box breathing - 4 counts in, hold 4, out 4 (takes 2 minutes)
- Step outside briefly if possible
- Cold water on your wrists

**For recovery after shifts:**
- Give yourself a wind-down transition before sleeping
- Protect sleep even if timing varies

## Worth Thinking About
- Is the anxiety mostly work-related or affecting other areas too?
- Do you have any support system outside work?
