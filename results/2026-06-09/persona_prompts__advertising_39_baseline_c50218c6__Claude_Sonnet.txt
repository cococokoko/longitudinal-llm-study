# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration. Many people quit because they try too long too soon.

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting, lying down, or even walking
2. **Focus on your breath** - notice the inhale and exhale
3. **When your mind wanders** (it will) - gently return attention to breathing
4. **That's it** - the returning is actually the practice

## Practical Tips for Beginners

- **Same time daily** builds habit (morning works well for many people)
- **Use an app** like Headspace, Calm, or Insight Timer for guidance
- **Start guided** before trying silent meditation
- **Don't judge your sessions** - there's no "bad" meditation

## Common Misconceptions

- ❌ You don't need to "clear your mind"
- ❌ It doesn't require special equipment or positions
- ❌ Thoughts during meditation aren't failures

## Everyday Mindfulness

- Pay full attention while eating, walking, or washing dishes
- Take a few conscious breaths before checking your phone
- Notice your surroundings with curiosity

## Realistic Expectations

Benefits like **reduced stress and better focus** typically develop over **weeks to months** of regular practice.
