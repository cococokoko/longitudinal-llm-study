  - Understand deep learning, large language models, retrieval systems, multimodal AI, and model evaluation.

  - Learn how data is collected, cleaned, stored, and governed.

  - Learn FHIR, HL7, DICOM, electronic health record integration, and clinical decision support systems.

• Statistics and causal inference
  - Learn study design, Bayesian reasoning, causal inference, and experimental evaluation.
  - Many future healthcare AI failures will come from poor evaluation rather than poor coding.

• Cybersecurity and privacy
  - Understand authentication, encryption, security principles, and healthcare compliance requirements.
  - As medical AI expands, security expertise becomes increasingly important.

• Cloud and distributed systems
  - Learn the basics of AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  - Understand how modern applications are deployed and scaled.

• Human-computer interaction
  - Learn how clinicians actually use software.
  - Many successful healthcare products succeed because they fit workflows rather than because of superior algorithms.
