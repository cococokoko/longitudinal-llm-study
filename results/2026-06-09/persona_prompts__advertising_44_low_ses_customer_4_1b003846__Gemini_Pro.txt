**1. Personal Vehicle (The Safest Option)**
*   **Drive alone:** Traveling in your own car is the safest method since you control the environment.

**3. Rideshares or Carpooling**
*   **Ventilation is key:** If you must take an Uber, Lyft, or carpool with a coworker, roll the windows down at least a few inches to create continuous airflow. 
*   **Sit strategically:** Sit in the back seat on the passenger side to maximize the distance between you and the driver.
