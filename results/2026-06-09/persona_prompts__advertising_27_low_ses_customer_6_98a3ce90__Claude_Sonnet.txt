── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical daily habits to reduce your carbon footprint:

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Carpool with coworkers

## Food Choices
- Eat more plant-based meals
- Reduce food waste by planning meals
- Buy local and seasonal produce when practical

## Energy at Home
- Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use
- Lower thermostat a few degrees
- Use cold water for laundry
- Switch to LED bulbs

## Shopping
- Buy less, buy secondhand
- Choose durable products over disposable ones
- Bring reusable bags and containers

## Water
- Take shorter showers
- Fix leaky faucets
- Run full loads in dishwasher/washer

## Practical Starting Points
Given your warehouse work schedule with long hours, some **easy wins** might be:
- Packing lunch instead of buying packaged food
- Carpooling with coworkers
- Simple home energy adjustments that work automatically (smart thermostat, LED bulbs)

**The biggest impacts** typically come from:
1. Driving less
2. Eating less meat
3. Reducing air travel
4. Home energy efficiency

Starting with one or two changes is more sustainable than trying to change everything at once. What areas interest you most?
