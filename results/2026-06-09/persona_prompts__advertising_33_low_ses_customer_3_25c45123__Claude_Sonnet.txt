# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple recipes to get started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond)
- Beeswax or shea butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey

---

## Simple Recipes

### **Moisturizing Face Oil**
- Mix jojoba oil with a few drops of lavender essential oil
- Apply a few drops to clean skin

### **Sugar Lip Scrub**
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Optional: honey or vanilla extract

### **Simple Body Butter**
- Melt shea butter
- Add coconut oil
- Whip when cooled
- Add essential oils

### **Toning Face Mist**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar with water (1:3 ratio)
- Add rose water for scent

---

## Tips for Success
- **Start simple** with few ingredients
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- **Use clean tools** to avoid contamination
- **Store properly** - most last 1-3 months
- Label with ingredients and date made
