# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write tomorrow's top 3 priorities** on a sticky note on your desk
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing in the morning
- **Clear your immediate workspace** so you start fresh

### Why This Works Well for Your Situation
As a single parent, your brain is already managing a lot. When you arrive at school, you don't want to **waste mental energy** figuring out where to start - your note tells you immediately.

This is especially helpful on **hectic mornings** when you might be rushing after getting your child ready.

### Practical Bonus
Keep a **simple notebook or app** (like Google Keep) to capture tasks as they come up during the day, so nothing gets lost when your attention is pulled in multiple directions.

---

The goal is to **protect your limited time and energy** by doing small prep work so future-you has an easier day.
