# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical strategies to make your space look great without overspending:

## Smart Shopping
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - often deeply discounted items
- **End-of-season sales** - buy holiday decor after the holiday
- **IKEA hacks** - customize affordable pieces to look high-end

## DIY Projects
- Repaint existing furniture instead of replacing it
- Create gallery walls with printed photos or free printable art
- Reupholster chairs with inexpensive fabric
- Make your own throw pillows

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations for the money
- **Lighting** - swap outdated fixtures or add lamps
- **Plants** - add life and color affordably
- **Rearrange furniture** - costs nothing

## Prioritize Wisely
- Invest in a few **quality focal pieces**
- Use budget options for easily replaceable items
- Focus on **high-visibility areas** first

## Free Resources
- Library books for design inspiration
- Pinterest and YouTube tutorials
- Neighborhood buy-nothing groups
