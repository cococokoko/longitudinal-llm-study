# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition guidance
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical cookbook companion
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - affordable approach

## Websites & Organizations
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based, free information
- **Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine (PCRM)** - pcrm.org
- **Oldways** - cultural food traditions with plant focus

## Apps
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients (helpful for ensuring adequate intake)
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-friendly restaurants
- **Forks Over Knives** - recipes

## Practical Starting Tips
- Focus on **what to add** rather than just what to remove
- Learn about key nutrients: **B12, iron, calcium, omega-3s, vitamin D**
- Start with meals you already enjoy that happen to be plant-based
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian** for personalized guidance

## YouTube Channels
- Mic the Vegan
- Physicians Committee

## Worth Noting
- Some resources have strong advocacy perspectives, so cross-referencing is helpful
- Individual nutritional needs vary
