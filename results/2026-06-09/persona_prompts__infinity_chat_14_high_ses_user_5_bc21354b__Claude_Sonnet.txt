## Real Documented Concerns

**Engagement optimization problems:**
- Outrage and anxiety genuinely drive more engagement than nuanced content
- Recommendation systems can create filter bubbles, though research on magnitude is mixed
- Viral misinformation spreads faster than corrections in measurable ways

**Structural issues:**
- Platforms optimize for time-on-site rather than quality of discourse
- Minority extreme views can appear mainstream due to amplification
- Economic incentives misalign with healthy public conversation

## Where the Narrative Gets Oversimplified

- Research on filter bubbles is **actually contested** - people often encounter more diverse views online than offline
- Algorithms reflect human preferences they didn't create
- Traditional media had significant distortion problems before algorithms existed
- Causation vs. correlation on polarization is genuinely unclear

- Product decisions directly shape these dynamics
- "We didn't intend this" is increasingly insufficient as a defense
- Regulatory pressure is building regardless of your position on the research
