- **Use visuals** alongside explanations
- **Provide written summaries** of verbal content
- **Check for understanding** frequently

- Good lighting and clear audio matter significantly
- Have a backup plan for technical failures
- Keep interfaces simple for learners

## Accessibility
- Provide captions when possible
- Offer multiple ways to demonstrate understanding
- Be flexible with different learning paces
