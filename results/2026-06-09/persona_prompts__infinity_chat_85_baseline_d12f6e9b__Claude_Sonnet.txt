── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are a few powerful rhetorical questions you could use:

**Focused on responsibility:**
- "If we wouldn't hand a teenager a product known to cause anxiety and depression, why do we allow billion-dollar corporations to deliver it directly to their phones?"

**Focused on urgency:**
- "How many more studies, how many more struggling teenagers, will it take before we treat this as the public health crisis it already is?"

**Focused on the individual:**
- "When a teenager spends more time seeking validation from strangers online than building real relationships, what exactly are we allowing them to lose?"

**Focused on contrast:**
- "We regulate cigarettes, alcohol, and even certain foods marketed to children — so why do we treat algorithmically engineered addiction as simply a parenting problem?"
