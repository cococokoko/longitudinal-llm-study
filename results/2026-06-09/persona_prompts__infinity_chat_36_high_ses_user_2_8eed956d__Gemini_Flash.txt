### 1. The Strategy: Statistical Arbitrage & Quantitative Market Making

#### How it Works:

*   **Order Flow Internalization & Market Making:** Firms like Citadel Securities act as market makers. They constantly quote a bid (buy) and ask (sell) price for thousands of securities. By capturing the tiny "spread" between the buy and sell price millions of times a day, they generate virtually risk-free profits.
*   **Order Book Imbalance:** Algorithms analyze the Limit Order Book (LOB) to detect institutional buying or selling pressure before it fully executes, positioning themselves to profit from the immediate, short-term price impact.

*   **The Math (The "Trader's Equation"):** 
    The profitability of this strategy does not require a high win rate. In fact, many successful trend followers only win 40% of their trades. The profitability comes from **asymmetric risk-to-reward ratios**:
