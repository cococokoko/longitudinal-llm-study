# Best Fitness & Nutrition Tracking Apps

## Fitness Tracking

**Comprehensive Tracking**
- **Strava** - Great for running, cycling, and outdoor activities
- **Garmin Connect** - Best if you use Garmin devices
- **Apple Fitness+** - Good for iPhone/Apple Watch users

**Workout Focused**
- **Strong** - Simple weightlifting tracker
- **JEFIT** - Detailed strength training logs
- **Nike Training Club** - Guided workouts, free content

## Nutrition Tracking

- **MyFitnessPal** - Largest food database, very popular
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - User-friendly calorie counting
- **Noom** - Combines tracking with coaching

## All-in-One Options

- **MyFitnessPal** - Handles both reasonably well
- **Fitbit app** - Works well with Fitbit devices
- **Whoop** - Recovery and strain focused

## Things to Consider When Choosing

- **Free vs. paid** - Many have limited free versions
- **Device compatibility** - Check if it syncs with your wearable
- **Your main goal** - Weight loss, performance, or general health
- **Ease of use** - You'll actually stick with simpler apps

**Most popular starting point:** MyFitnessPal for nutrition + Strava for cardio
