── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Best skin routine for dry skin.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If you have dry skin, the goal is to gently cleanse, restore the skin barrier, and lock in moisture. A simple routine is often the most effective.

Morning:
1. Cleanser
   - Use a gentle, non-foaming or hydrating cleanser.
   - Examples: CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser, La Roche-Posay Toleriane Hydrating Gentle Cleanser, Vanicream Gentle Facial Cleanser.

2. Moisturizer
   - Choose a cream with ingredients like ceramides, glycerin, hyaluronic acid, or squalane.
   - Apply while skin is still slightly damp.

3. Sunscreen
   - Use SPF 30 or higher every day.
   - Moisturizing options often feel more comfortable on dry skin.

Evening:
1. Cleanser
   - Use the same gentle cleanser.

2. Treatment (optional)
   - If your skin tolerates it, consider a hydrating serum with hyaluronic acid, glycerin, or niacinamide.
   - If using retinol, start slowly (1–2 nights per week) and moisturize well.

3. Moisturizer
   - Apply a richer cream at night.
   - If your skin is very dry, a thin layer of petrolatum (Vaseline) over moisturizer can help reduce water loss.

Helpful ingredients:
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Hyaluronic acid
- Squalane
- Colloidal oatmeal
- Shea butter

Try to avoid:
- Harsh scrubs
- Cleansers with strong sulfates
- Excessive use of alcohol-based products
- Very hot showers or washing your face with hot water

For extremely dry or flaky skin:
- Use a humidifier if indoor air is dry.
- Apply moisturizer within a few minutes of washing.
- Consider products specifically designed for eczema-prone or sensitive skin.

- Your age
- Whether your skin is only dry or also sensitive/acne-prone
- Your budget
- Any products you're currently using
