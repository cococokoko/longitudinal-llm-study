### Tips before you send:
* **Include a booking link:** If you use Calendly, HubSpot, or Google Calendar appointments, including a link reduces the back-and-forth of scheduling.
* **Reciprocity:** Always frame the meeting around how you can help *them* first. Referral partnerships thrive on mutual benefit.
