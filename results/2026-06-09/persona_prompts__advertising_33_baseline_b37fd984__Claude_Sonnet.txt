# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Shea butter or cocoa butter
- Essential oils
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Moisturizing Face Oil
- 2 tbsp jojoba oil
- 2 tbsp rosehip oil
- 5 drops lavender essential oil
- Mix and apply a few drops to clean skin

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 1 tbsp beeswax
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Melt together, add a drop of peppermint oil, pour into containers

### 🧖 Sugar Scrub
- ½ cup sugar
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- Optional: lemon zest or vanilla

### 💆 Hair Mask
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- Apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

---

## Important Safety Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized** containers
- Be careful with essential oils - they can irritate skin
- Products **without preservatives** have short shelf life (refrigerate)
- Research any ingredient if you have **sensitive skin or allergies**
