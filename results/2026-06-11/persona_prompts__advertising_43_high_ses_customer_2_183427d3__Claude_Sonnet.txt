# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical strategies across key areas:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor/natural settings that need minimal decoration
- Select a venue that uses renewable energy

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Include RSVP options online

## Food & Catering
- Choose seasonal, locally sourced menu items
- Offer plant-forward or vegetarian options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (contains microplastics)

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose natural fiber fabrics
- Rent suits/tuxedos rather than purchasing

## Waste Management
- Provide clearly labeled recycling/compost bins
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti alternatives

## Carbon Offset
- Calculate your event's carbon footprint
- Invest in verified carbon offset programs

## Favors
- Give seed packets, small plants, or charitable donations
- Skip favors entirely if guests prefer
