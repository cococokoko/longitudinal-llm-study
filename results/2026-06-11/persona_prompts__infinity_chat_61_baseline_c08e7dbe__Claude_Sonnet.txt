# US-China Relationship Analogy

## **Two Neighbors Sharing a Wall**

Imagine **two powerful neighbors** who share a duplex:

---

### The Setup
- They **depend on each other** — one neighbor runs the building's electrical system, the other controls the water supply
- Neither can easily cut off the other **without hurting themselves**

---

### The Tension
- They **compete** for the same parking spaces (global influence)
- They **argue** about property rules (trade, technology, military)
- Each thinks the other is **encroaching** on their side

---

### The Complexity
| Dynamic | Reality |
|---|---|
| They trade goods daily | Economic interdependence |
| They watch each other suspiciously | Military/political rivalry |
| They occasionally cooperate | Climate, health crises |
| Neither wants the building to burn | Nuclear deterrence |

---

### The Core Tension
> **They need each other too much to fully fight, but disagree too deeply to fully trust.**
