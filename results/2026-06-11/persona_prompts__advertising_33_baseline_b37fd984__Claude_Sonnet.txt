# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some simple, popular DIY natural beauty recipes:

## Skin Care

### Basic Face Moisturizer
- 2 tbsp coconut oil or shea butter
- 1 tbsp jojoba oil
- Few drops of lavender essential oil
- Whip together and store in a clean jar

### Simple Face Scrub
- 1 tbsp honey
- 2 tbsp sugar or oatmeal
- Few drops lemon juice
- Mix and gently massage onto skin

### Clay Face Mask
- 1 tbsp bentonite clay
- Enough water or apple cider vinegar to make a paste
- Apply for 10-15 minutes, rinse well

---

## Hair Care

### Deep Conditioning Mask
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- Blend, apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes

### Scalp Scrub
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- Massage into scalp, rinse

---

## Body Care

### Body Scrub
- 1 cup sugar or coffee grounds
- ½ cup coconut oil
- Essential oils of your choice

### Lip Balm
- 1 tbsp beeswax
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

---

## Important Tips

- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean containers** and tools
- Products **without preservatives** have short shelf life (1-2 weeks refrigerated)
- Be careful with **essential oils** - always dilute them
- Some ingredients can cause **allergic reactions**
- Citrus ingredients can make skin **sun sensitive**
