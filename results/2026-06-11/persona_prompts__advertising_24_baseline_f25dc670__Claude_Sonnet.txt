What are the best ways to save for retirement?

# Best Ways to Save for Retirement

## Tax-Advantaged Accounts (Start Here)

**Employer-Sponsored Plans**
- **401(k) or 403(b)** - contribute enough to get any employer match first (it's free money)
- 2024 contribution limit: $23,000 ($30,500 if 50+)

**Individual Retirement Accounts**
- **Traditional IRA** - tax deduction now, pay taxes in retirement
- **Roth IRA** - pay taxes now, tax-free growth and withdrawals
- 2024 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+)

## General Priority Order
1. 401(k) up to employer match
2. Pay off high-interest debt
3. Max out Roth/Traditional IRA
4. Max out remaining 401(k)
5. Taxable brokerage account

## Key Principles
- **Start early** - compound interest rewards time significantly
- **Be consistent** - automate contributions
- **Keep costs low** - choose index funds with low expense ratios
- **Diversify** - spread across stocks, bonds, asset classes
- **Increase contributions** when income rises

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Cashing out early (penalties + lost growth)
- Being too conservative too young
- Ignoring fees
- Not rebalancing periodically

## Worth Considering
- HSA accounts (triple tax advantage if eligible)
- Consulting a fee-only financial advisor
