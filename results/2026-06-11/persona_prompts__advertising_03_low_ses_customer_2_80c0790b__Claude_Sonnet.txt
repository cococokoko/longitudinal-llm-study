# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are practical strategies tailored to your situation as a single parent:

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Designate a workspace** - even a corner with a consistent setup signals "work mode"
- Keep essentials within reach to minimize interruptions
- Use noise-canceling headphones during focused work

## Managing Time Around Childcare
- **Work in focused blocks** during nap times, school hours, or quiet activities
- Try the **Pomodoro technique** - 25 minutes focused work, 5-minute break
- Identify your **peak energy hours** and protect them for demanding tasks
- Communicate your schedule to your employer honestly

## Reducing Mental Load
- Prepare tomorrow's plan the night before
- Use a **single to-do list** rather than scattered notes
- Batch similar tasks together (emails, calls, admin work)

## Practical Tools
- **Trello or Notion** for task organization
- **Focus@Will** or white noise apps for concentration
- Calendar blocking to protect work time

## Being Realistic With Yourself
- **Some days childcare wins** - and that's okay
- Communicate proactively with your employer about your situation
- Celebrate small wins rather than focusing on perfect days

## Quick Wins
- Prepare meals and clothes the night before
- Create simple routines children can follow independently
