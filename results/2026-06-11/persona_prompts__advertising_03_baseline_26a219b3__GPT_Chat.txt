• Create a dedicated workspace

  - Consider using website blockers during focused work sessions.

  - Work in dedicated blocks of time, such as 25–50 minutes.
  - Take short breaks between sessions.

  - Prepare agendas to keep discussions focused.
  - Group meetings together to preserve uninterrupted work time.
