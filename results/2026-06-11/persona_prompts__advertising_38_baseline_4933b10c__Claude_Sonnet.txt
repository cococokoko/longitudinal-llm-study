# Best Platforms for Freelance Writers

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large client base, good for long-term contracts
- **Fiverr** - Good for packaged services, beginners can start here
- **Freelancer.com** - Competitive bidding system

## Writing-Specific Platforms
- **Contently** - Higher-paying, portfolio-focused
- **ClearVoice** - Content marketing focused
- **Scripted** - Vetted writers, steady work
- **Textbroker** - Easy entry, volume-based work

## Content Mills (Lower Pay, Good for Beginners)
- **iWriter**
- **WriterAccess**
- **Constant Content**

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality listings
- **MediaBistro** - Media industry focused
- **Journalism Jobs** - News/editorial work
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for direct clients

## Higher-Paying Options
- **Toptal** - Rigorous vetting, premium rates
- **Skyword** - Enterprise content clients

## Practical Tips
- Build a **portfolio site** (even a simple one)
- Cold pitching directly to companies often pays better
- Niche specialization typically commands higher rates
- Magazine/publication **direct submissions** can be lucrative
