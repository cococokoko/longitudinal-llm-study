# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

Here are practical strategies that can make a real difference, especially for a busy household managing multiple people's needs:

## Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- **Adjust thermostat settings** - even 2-3 degrees makes a meaningful difference
- **Unplug devices** when not in use (TVs, chargers, gaming consoles draw standby power)
- **Run full loads** in dishwashers and washing machines
- **Wash clothes in cold water** - works well for most laundry
- **Turn off lights** when leaving rooms

## Managing a Larger Household
- **Coordinate appliance use** during off-peak hours when possible
- **Set clear habits** with family members about lights, screens, and heating/cooling
- **Use power strips** with switches to cut phantom loads from entertainment areas
- **Batch cooking** reduces oven use significantly

## Moderate Investments
- **LED bulbs** throughout the home
- **Smart power strips or plugs**
- **Programmable or smart thermostat**
- **Weatherstripping** on doors and windows

## Bigger Impact Changes
- **Insulation improvements**
- **Energy-efficient appliances** when replacing old ones
- **Water heater temperature adjustment** (120°F is typically sufficient)

## Tracking Progress
- Review monthly utility bills to spot patterns
- Many utilities offer **free energy audits**
