# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some popular DIY options that are straightforward to make:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, cool, and store in a clean jar

**Facial Scrub**
- Sugar or oatmeal + honey + coconut oil
- Mix and use immediately or store briefly

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (steep rose petals in distilled water)

## Hair Care

**Hair Mask**
- Avocado + olive oil + egg yolk
- Apply, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Scrub**
- Brown sugar + coconut oil + tea tree oil

## Lip Care

**Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Practical Tips

- **Sanitize** all equipment and containers thoroughly
- **Patch test** new products before full use
- **Label** with ingredients and date made
- **Refrigerate** products without preservatives
- **Short shelf life** - make small batches
- Use **distilled water** to reduce contamination risk
