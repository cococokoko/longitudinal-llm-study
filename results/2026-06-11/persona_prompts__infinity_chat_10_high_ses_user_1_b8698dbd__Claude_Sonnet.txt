- **Compassion as motivation** vs **compassion as behavior** - intelligence may help translate one to the other
- Context matters enormously

## Honest Bottom Line
- The correlation, if it exists, is **weak and inconsistent**
- Clinical observation probably gives you useful perspective here
- Intelligence seems more likely to **amplify existing motivations** than create compassion
