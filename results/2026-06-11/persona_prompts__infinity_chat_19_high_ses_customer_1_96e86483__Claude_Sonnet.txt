This is worth thinking through carefully rather than just optimistically.

## Likely Significant Changes

**Communication mediation**
- AI increasingly filtering, translating, and summarizing interactions
- Risk of people losing tolerance for unmediated, inefficient human communication
- Potential atrophy of conflict resolution and negotiation skills

**Relationship formation**
- AI companions potentially competing with human relationships for emotional investment
- Matching algorithms becoming increasingly sophisticated, possibly reducing serendipitous connection
- Real question about whether this expands or narrows who people connect with

## Genuine Tensions Worth Acknowledging

**The dependency problem** - if AI handles social friction, humans may become less capable of handling it independently, similar to how GPS affects spatial navigation

**Authenticity questions** - when AI drafts your messages, mediates your conflicts, coaches your conversations, whose relationship is it actually?

**Stratification risks** - access to sophisticated social AI could create new social class divisions

## What's Honestly Uncertain

- Whether human connection needs will fundamentally change or remain stable
- Whether AI companionship satisfies or displaces human bonding needs
- How institutions like medicine, education, and family adapt
