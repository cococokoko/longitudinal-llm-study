# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, effective DIY beauty products you can make at home:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Coconut oil or shea butter as a base
- Add a few drops of essential oils (lavender, tea tree)
- Mix and store in a clean jar

**Honey Face Mask**
- Raw honey applied directly to skin
- Leave on 15-20 minutes
- Rinse with warm water

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coconut oil
- Optional: lemon juice or vanilla extract

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado
- Coconut oil
- Honey
- Apply, leave 30 minutes, rinse well

**Scalp Treatment**
- Castor oil massaged into scalp
- Leave overnight
- Wash out in morning

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax
- Coconut oil
- Honey
- Melt together and pour into small containers

## General Tips

- **Start simple** with few ingredients
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- **Use clean containers** to prevent bacteria
- **Store properly** - refrigerate products without preservatives
- **Label everything** with date made
