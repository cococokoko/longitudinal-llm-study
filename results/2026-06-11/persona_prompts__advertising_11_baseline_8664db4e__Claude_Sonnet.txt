# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks** – core strength
- **Burpees** – full body + cardio
- **Dips** – using a chair or bench

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Jump rope
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Dancing or aerobics videos

## Home Equipment (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells or kettlebells
- Pull-up bar (doorframe)
- Yoga mat

## Everyday Activity
- Take the stairs
- Walk or bike instead of driving
- Active chores (gardening, cleaning)
- Stand instead of sitting when possible

## Useful Approaches
- **HIIT workouts** – short, intense, effective
- **Yoga or Pilates** – flexibility and strength
- **YouTube/apps** – free guided workouts

## Tips for Success
- Set a consistent schedule
- Track your progress
- Start simple and build gradually
- Mix cardio and strength training
- Stay hydrated and eat well

**The key is consistency** – even 20–30 minutes a day makes a real difference over time.
