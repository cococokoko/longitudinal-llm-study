Given your full-time fast food job with an unpredictable schedule, here's how to make guitar learning work for you:

## Starting Out
- **Get a decent beginner guitar** - acoustic or electric, whichever style interests you
- **Consider a used guitar** to save money while starting out

## Learning Approaches That Fit Your Schedule

- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar channel is highly recommended and free

## Realistic Practice Goals
- Even **10-15 minutes daily** builds real progress
- Practice during breaks or before/after shifts
- **Consistency beats long sessions** - short and regular works well

## Beginner Priorities
1. Learn basic **chord shapes** (G, C, D, Em, Am)
2. Practice **smooth chord transitions**
3. Learn simple **strumming patterns**
4. Pick **songs you actually like**

## Keeping Motivation Up
- Set small, achievable goals
- Record yourself to hear progress
- Join online communities like **r/guitarlessons**
