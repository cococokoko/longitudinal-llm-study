# Getting Started with Coding from Scratch

Great question! Here's a practical roadmap:

## Choose Your First Language
- **Python** - Most recommended for beginners (readable, versatile)
- **JavaScript** - Good if you're interested in web development
- **Start with one** - don't jump around early on

## Free Learning Resources
- **freeCodeCamp.org** - Structured, project-based
- **The Odin Project** - Comprehensive web dev path
- **CS50** (Harvard, free on edX) - Solid fundamentals
- **Codecademy** - Interactive, beginner-friendly

## Practical Tips
- **Code daily**, even just 15-30 minutes
- **Build small projects** as soon as possible
- **Don't just watch tutorials** - type the code yourself
- **Google errors freely** - everyone does this

## Realistic Expectations
- Expect frustration early - that's completely normal
- Focus on **problem-solving thinking**, not memorizing syntax
- Progress feels slow at first, then accelerates

## Given Your Schedule
Since you work long warehouse hours, consider:
- Short sessions on breaks or after work
- Mobile apps like **Grasshopper** or **Mimo** for learning on the go
- Weekends for longer practice sessions

## What to Build First
Simple projects like a calculator, to-do list, or something related to your interests keep motivation high.
