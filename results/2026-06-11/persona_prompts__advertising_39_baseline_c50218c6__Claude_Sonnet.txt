# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple - Don't Overcomplicate It

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** rather than ambitious sessions you won't maintain.

---

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting, lying down, or even walking
2. **Focus on your breath** - notice the inhale and exhale
3. **When your mind wanders** (it will) - gently return attention to breathing
4. **That's it** - the returning is actually the practice

---

## Practical Tips for Beginners

- **Same time daily** builds the habit (morning often works well)
- **Use an app** like Headspace, Calm, or Insight Timer for guidance
- **Start with guided meditations** if silence feels uncomfortable
- **Don't judge your sessions** - there's no "bad" meditation

---

## Simple Mindfulness in Daily Life

- Eat one meal without your phone
- Take a few conscious breaths before checking email
- Notice sensations during routine activities like showering or walking

---

## Realistic Expectations

- Benefits often take **weeks of consistency** to notice
- Common benefits include reduced stress, better focus, improved sleep
- **Consistency matters more than duration**

---

## Good Free Resources

- YouTube: search "guided meditation for beginners"
- Apps: Insight Timer (free version is solid)
- Books: *Wherever You Go, There You Are* by Jon Kabat-Zinn
