# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

Given your role, you're likely dealing with **high-stakes decisions, constant demands, and significant pressure**. Here are practical strategies:

---

## Immediate Stress Relief
- **Box breathing** - 4 counts in, hold, out, hold
- **Brief walks** - even 10 minutes significantly reduces cortisol
- **Single-tasking** - close unnecessary tabs/notifications temporarily

---

## Structural Approaches
- **Protect recovery time** - schedule it like a meeting
- **Define "off hours"** - boundaries actually improve decision quality
- **Delegate deliberately** - anxiety often signals over-ownership
- **Regular exercise** - non-negotiable for executive performance

---

## Mindset Shifts Worth Considering
- Distinguish between **productive worry** (actionable) and **rumination** (not)
- Recognize that **uncertainty tolerance** is a learnable skill
- Separate **your identity from company performance**

---

## When to Seek More Support
- Persistent sleep disruption
- Difficulty concentrating on decisions
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, headaches)
- Consider an **executive coach** or therapist familiar with leadership roles

---

## A Honest Note
Many executives normalize unhealthy stress levels. **High performance doesn't require chronic anxiety.**
