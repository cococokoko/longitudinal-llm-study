### 2. The 5-Minute Meal Blueprints
These meals require zero culinary skill, cost under $5 per serving, and are packed with brain-boosting nutrients (Omega-3s, complex carbs, clean protein).

#### **Breakfast: The "Deep Work" Fuel (Cost: ~$1.50)**
*   **The Meal:** Overnight Oats.
*   **How-To:** In a jar, mix 1/2 cup oats, 1 tbsp chia seeds, a handful of frozen berries, a scoop of protein powder, and almond milk. Leave in the fridge.
*   **Why it works:** Zero morning prep. Slow-burning carbs keep your blood sugar stable through back-to-back morning meetings.

#### **Lunch: The "No-Crash" Power Bowl (Cost: ~$3.50)**
*   **The Meal:** Salmon & Avocado Grain Bowl.
*   **How-To:** Microwave a bag of frozen quinoa. Top with canned wild-caught salmon (drained), 1/2 an avocado, a handful of spinach, and a drizzle of olive oil/lemon juice.
*   **Why it works:** Omega-3s from salmon and monounsaturated fats from avocado boost focus and prevent the 2:00 PM post-lunch slump.

#### **Dinner: The "One-Pan" Recovery (Cost: ~$4.00)**
*   **The Meal:** Sheet Pan Tofu/Chicken & Roasted Veggies.
*   **How-To:** Toss cubed tofu or pre-cooked rotisserie chicken breast with frozen broccoli florets and cubed sweet potatoes in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 25 minutes.
*   **Why it works:** One pan to wash. High in magnesium (from sweet potatoes and broccoli) to help you wind down and improve sleep quality.

### 3. Time & Cost Arbitrage (How to Optimize)

To scale this efficiently, apply basic business principles to your kitchen:

*   **Outsource the Prep (Micro-Delegation):** If you use a grocery delivery service (Instacart, Amazon Fresh), buy pre-chopped vegetables. While they cost 10-15% more than whole veggies, the **opportunity cost** of your time spent chopping makes this a massive net positive.
*   **The "Single-Source" Rule:** Limit your fresh produce to 3 versatile items per week (e.g., spinach, sweet potatoes, avocados). This reduces food waste (sunk cost) to near zero.
*   **Leverage Frozen Assets:** Frozen vegetables and fruits do not spoil. You pay only for what you eat, eliminating the "forgotten produce in the crisper" tax.

### Summary of the Weekly System
1.  **Sunday (15 mins):** Order groceries online. Set up 5 jars of overnight oats.
2.  **Wednesday (10 mins):** Mid-week inventory check.

This system keeps your physical engine running on premium fuel, keeps your food budget highly optimized, and protects your most valuable asset: your time.
