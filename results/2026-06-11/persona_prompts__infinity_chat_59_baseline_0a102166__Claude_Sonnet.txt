# One High-Impact Tip for Teacher Organization

## Use a **Weekly Planning Template**

Create a simple, consistent template you fill out every Sunday or Monday morning that includes:

- **Daily lessons** for each class period
- **Assignments to grade** (with deadlines you set for yourself)
- **Parent/admin communications** needed
- **Copies or materials** to prepare in advance

---

### Why this works for teachers specifically:

- Your week is **unpredictable** (assemblies, absences, behavior issues), so having a plan helps you **recover quickly** when things go sideways
- It prevents the **"what am I doing today?"** panic
- You can **batch similar tasks** like grading or copying to save mental energy

---

### Practical suggestion:
Keep it **simple** - even a basic Google Doc or a paper notebook works. The format matters less than the **habit of doing it consistently**.

---

This one habit tends to reduce stress significantly because you're making decisions **ahead of time** rather than reacting all day.
