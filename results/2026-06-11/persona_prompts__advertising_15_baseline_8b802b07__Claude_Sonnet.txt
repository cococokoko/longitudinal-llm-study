# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines

---

## Smart Shopping Strategies
- **Thrift stores & secondhand shops** - furniture, frames, décor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - great for larger pieces
- **Discount stores** - HomeGoods, TJ Maxx, IKEA
- **Seasonal sales** - end of season clearance
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly useful for small accents

---

## DIY Ideas
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations for little money
- **Reupholster** old chairs or cushions
- **Refinish furniture** with chalk paint or stain
- **Make your own art** - abstract painting, framed fabric, photos
- **Swap hardware** on cabinets and drawers

---

## Free or Nearly Free Ideas
- **Rearrange existing furniture**
- **Declutter** - less can look more intentional
- **Use nature** - branches, stones, plants
- **Borrow or swap** with friends and family

---

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Updates
- Throw pillows and blankets
- Mirrors to make spaces feel larger
- Plants and greenery
- New lighting or lampshades
- Curtains hung high to make ceilings look taller
