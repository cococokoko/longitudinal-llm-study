# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition information
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - companion to the documentary
- **"The Plant-Based Diet for Beginners" by Gabriel Miller** - practical starter guide

## Websites & Organizations
- **NutritionFacts.org** - Dr. Greger's research-based site
- **PCRM.org** (Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine)
- **Oldwayspt.org** - Mediterranean and plant-based diet resources
- **Forks Over Knives website** - recipes and meal plans

## Practical Tools
- **Cronometer or MyFitnessPal** - tracking nutrients
- **Meal planning apps** like PlantNicks or Mealime

## Key Things to Learn Early
- Getting enough **B12, iron, calcium, omega-3s, and protein**
- Simple meal prep strategies
- Reading food labels

## Honest Considerations
- Consulting a **registered dietitian** is genuinely helpful
- Transitioning gradually often works better than sudden changes
- Individual nutritional needs vary
