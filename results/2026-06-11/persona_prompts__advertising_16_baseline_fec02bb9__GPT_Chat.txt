- Practice mindfulness, meditation, or relaxation exercises.

- Notice anxious thoughts without immediately treating them as facts.
- Ask yourself what evidence supports or challenges a worry.
- Focus on what you can control right now rather than trying to solve every possible future problem.
- Gradually face situations that cause anxiety when safe to do so, rather than avoiding them completely.

Connecting with others can also help:
