# How to Improve Home WiFi

## Quick Fixes
- **Restart your router** regularly (weekly helps)
- **Update router firmware** through the admin settings
- **Change WiFi channel** to avoid interference from neighbors

## Router Placement
- Place it **centrally** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not floor)
- Avoid **walls, microwaves, and cordless phones** nearby
- Keep it **out in the open**, not in a cabinet

## Boost Your Signal
- **Upgrade your router** if it's more than 3-5 years old
- Add a **WiFi extender/repeater** for dead zones
- Consider a **mesh network system** (like Eero, Google Nest, or Orbi) for larger homes
- Use a **powerline adapter** for wired-like speeds through electrical outlets

## Settings to Adjust
- Switch to **5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby
- Use **2.4GHz band** for better range/penetration
- Set a **strong password** to prevent unauthorized users
- Use **WPA3 or WPA2** security

## Other Tips
- **Reduce connected devices** you don't need
- Use an **ethernet cable** for gaming/streaming when possible
- Check if your **internet plan** is fast enough for your needs
- Contact your **ISP** if problems persist
