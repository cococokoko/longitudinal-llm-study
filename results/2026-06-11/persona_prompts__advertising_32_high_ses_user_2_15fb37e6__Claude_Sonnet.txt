I am a senior attorney at a well-established law firm.

What are the best ways to improve indoor air quality?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve indoor air quality:

## Ventilation
- Open windows when outdoor air quality permits
- Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms
- Ensure HVAC systems are properly maintained

## Filtration
- Use high-quality air filters (MERV 13 or HEPA filters)
- Replace filters regularly per manufacturer recommendations
- Consider standalone air purifiers for specific rooms

## Reducing Pollutant Sources
- Choose low-VOC paints, furniture, and cleaning products
- Avoid smoking indoors
- Use natural cleaning products when possible
- Allow new furniture/carpets to off-gas before bringing indoors

## Humidity Control
- Maintain humidity between **30-50%**
- Use dehumidifiers in damp areas to prevent mold
- Fix leaks and water damage promptly

## Plants
- Some houseplants can help filter certain pollutants
- Though their impact is modest compared to other measures

## Regular Maintenance
- Vacuum with HEPA-filter vacuums regularly
- Clean air ducts periodically
- Test for radon, especially in lower levels
- Have HVAC systems professionally serviced annually

## Testing
- Consider professional air quality testing if you have concerns about specific pollutants like mold, asbestos, or carbon monoxide
