# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are some effective ways to maintain fitness without a gym membership:

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** (if you have a bar or sturdy surface)

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Swimming
- Brisk walking

## Home Workout Tools (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells
- Yoga mat
- Kettlebell

## Free Resources
- YouTube workout channels (Fitness Blender, Yoga with Adriene)
- Fitness apps (Nike Training Club, MyFitnessPal)
- Couch to 5K program

## Practical Tips
- **Be consistent** - short daily workouts beat occasional long ones
- **Use your environment** - stairs, parks, benches
- **Set a schedule** that fits your routine
- **Track progress** to stay motivated

## Quick Routine Ideas
- 20-minute morning bodyweight circuit
- Lunchtime walk
- Evening yoga or stretching

Since you're in a new job and still settling in, keeping workouts **flexible and low-commitment** at first can help you stay consistent without added pressure. 💪
