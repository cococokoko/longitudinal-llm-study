# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Pick a location accessible by public transportation

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Choose soy-based inks for printing

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations instead of buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral centerpieces

## Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Choose dresses that can be worn again
- Rent suits/tuxedos

## Waste Reduction
- Use real dishes instead of disposables
- Set up clear recycling and composting stations
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## Carbon Offset
- Plant trees in honor of your wedding
- Purchase carbon offsets for travel

## Favors
- Give seed packets or small plants
- Choose locally made edible gifts
