- Leading lines

- Golden hour
- Backlighting
- Indoor lighting
- Basic flash concepts

4. Learn photo editing
Popular tools:
- Adobe Lightroom
- Capture One
- Darktable (free)
- Snapseed (mobile)

Focus on:
- Exposure adjustments
- Contrast
- Color correction
- Cropping
- Noise reduction
