# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great ways to travel more sustainably:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Walking or cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Public transit** - buses, trains, and subways reduce per-person emissions significantly
- **Electric vehicles** - lower emissions, especially with renewable energy charging
- **Carpooling** - sharing rides cuts emissions per passenger

## For Longer Distances
- **Train travel** - generally much lower carbon footprint than flying
- **Coach/bus services** - efficient for regional travel
- **Ferry options** - can be lower impact for some routes

## If Flying is Necessary
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing use most fuel)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Pack **lighter luggage**

## Accommodation Choices
- Look for **eco-certified hotels**
- Consider **locally-owned guesthouses**
- Choose places with **green energy** and water conservation

## General Tips
- **Travel slower** - stay longer in fewer places
- Support **local businesses** and restaurants
- Bring **reusable items** (water bottle, bags, utensils)
- Research destinations' **environmental policies**

## Helpful Resources
- Google Maps (shows transit options)
- Rome2Rio (compares transport emissions)
- Atmosfair (carbon footprint calculator)
