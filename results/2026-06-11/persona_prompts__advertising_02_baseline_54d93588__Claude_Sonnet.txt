# Best Skincare Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming/gel cleansers)
2. **Toner** *(optional)* - Hydrating toner with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or vitamin C serum
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides, shea butter, or glycerin
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (look for moisturizing formulas)

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Toner** *(optional)*
3. **Treatment Serum** - Retinol (start slow) or niacinamide
4. **Eye Cream** *(optional)*
5. **Moisturizer** - Heavier night cream or facial oil

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- ✅ Hyaluronic acid
- ✅ Ceramides
- ✅ Glycerin
- ✅ Shea butter
- ✅ Squalane
- ✅ Niacinamide

## Things to Avoid
- ❌ Alcohol-based products
- ❌ Harsh exfoliants
- ❌ Hot water when washing
- ❌ Over-cleansing

## Extra Tips
- **Drink plenty of water**
- Use a **humidifier** indoors
- Exfoliate **1-2x per week** with a gentle chemical exfoliant (lactic acid)
- Apply moisturizer while skin is **slightly damp**
