### **Trend Following / Momentum**
- Trade in direction of established trends
- Uses moving averages, breakouts

### **Opening Range Breakout (ORB)**
- Trade breakouts from the first 15-30 min range
- Popular and well-documented

### **Mean Reversion**
- Fade extreme moves back to average
- Works well in ranging markets

### **VWAP Trading**
- Institutional benchmark
- High reliability in liquid markets
