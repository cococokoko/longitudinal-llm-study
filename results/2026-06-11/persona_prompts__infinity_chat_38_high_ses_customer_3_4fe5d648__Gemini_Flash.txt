### 1. Biblical and Near Eastern Origins
In the Hebrew Bible (primarily in Leviticus and Kings), *Molech* is depicted as a deity of the Ammonites associated with child sacrifice by fire.

> *"First Moloch, horrid King besmear’d with blood / Of human sacrifice, and parents tears..."*
