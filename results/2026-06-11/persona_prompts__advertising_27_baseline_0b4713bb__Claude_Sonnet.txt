── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint:

## Food & Diet
- Eat less meat, especially beef (try even 1-2 meatless days/week)
- Buy local and seasonal produce when possible
- Reduce food waste by planning meals
- Compost food scraps

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Consider carpooling
- Maintain your vehicle properly (tire pressure, tune-ups)

## Home Energy
- Adjust thermostat a few degrees (lower in winter, higher in summer)
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Air dry laundry when possible

## Shopping & Consumption
- Buy less overall
- Choose secondhand items
- Repair things instead of replacing them
- Bring reusable bags and containers

## Biggest Impact Areas
Research suggests the **highest impact actions** are:
- Flying less
- Driving less or switching to electric
- Eating less animal products
- Having smaller families (longer-term consideration)

## Realistic Approach
- Start with changes that fit your lifestyle
- Small consistent habits add up
- Don't aim for perfection
