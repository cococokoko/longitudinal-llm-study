# CUBICLE OF THE DAMNED
### A Play in Three Acts
*In the Style of Scott Adams' Dilbert, Set During the Apocalypse*

---

## CAST OF CHARACTERS

**DILBERT** - An engineer trying to optimize survival in a post-apocalyptic wasteland while still somehow attending status meetings

**DOGBERT** - A small dog with delusions of world domination, now technically correct that the world needs dominating

**THE POINTY-HAIRED BOSS (PHB)** - Somehow survived. Nobody is surprised. Cockroaches and managers always do.

**ALICE** - Engineer. Fist of Death now legally classified as a weapon of mass destruction by the remaining government (population: 4)

**WALLY** - Has been "working from home" since the collapse. Has not noticed the difference.

**ASOK** - Intern. Still an intern. The apocalypse did not come with a promotion budget.

**CATBERT** - Former Evil HR Director. Now just Evil Director of Everything.

---

## ACT ONE: THE MORNING STANDUP

*SCENE: A collapsed office building. Three cubicle walls remain standing amid rubble and ash. A motivational poster reading "HANG IN THERE" dangles from a bent rebar. DILBERT sits at a desk made from a car hood, staring at a laptop powered by a hand crank. His tie is still perfectly knotted. Outside, distant explosions. He does not look up.*

**DILBERT:** *(typing)* My performance review is in two weeks. I need to document my accomplishments. *(pause)* I helped the tribe not get eaten by the Raider Clan of Sector Seven. *(pause)* PHB will say that's not aligned with Q3 objectives.

*PHB enters, wearing his usual suit, now tattered, with a shoulder pad made from a stop sign.*

**PHB:** Good morning, team! Big day! Big day!

**DILBERT:** The water supply is contaminated and we're down to four cans of beans.

**PHB:** Right, right. So I'm going to need you to put together a PowerPoint on our bean strategy. Something visual. Maybe a pie chart.

**DILBERT:** A pie chart. About beans.

**PHB:** People respond to visuals, Dilbert. I read that in a book before books became fuel.

**ALICE:** *(emerging from rubble, covered in soot, holding a crossbow)* I just fought off fourteen raiders, rerouted the last clean water pipe, AND fixed the generator. All before 9 AM.

**PHB:** Great! Can you also take notes at the standup?

*ALICE's fist begins to glow.*

**ALICE:** I will take notes. I will take VERY THOROUGH NOTES.

**PHB:** *(backing away)* Maybe Asok can do it.

*ASOK crawls out from under a collapsed filing cabinet. He is still wearing his intern badge.*

**ASOK:** In my village in India, we had a saying: "The intern who survives the apocalypse shall finally receive full-time employment."

**PHB:** We're doing a hiring freeze.

**ASOK:** *(staring into the middle distance)* The apocalypse was more merciful than I expected.

*WALLY rolls in on an office chair with wheels made from hubcaps. He is holding a coffee mug. The mug is somehow still steaming.*

**WALLY:** Morning.

**DILBERT:** Wally, where did you get hot coffee? The power grid collapsed eighteen months ago.

**WALLY:** I've been managing expectations about my coffee situation since 2003. I simply never told the coffee it was supposed to be cold.

**DILBERT:** That's not how thermodynamics works.

**WALLY:** I also don't do thermodynamics.
