── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Learning to cook healthy meals quickly is very achievable. Here are some practical approaches:

## Start with the Basics
- Learn a few **fundamental techniques** (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Understand how to season food well - this makes healthy food taste good
- Get comfortable with knife skills to speed up prep

## Build a Simple System
- **Meal prep on weekends** - cook grains, proteins, and vegetables in batches
- Keep a rotation of 5-10 reliable recipes you know well
- Plan meals before shopping so you have what you need

## Stock Smart
- Keep pantry staples ready (olive oil, canned beans, whole grains, spices)
- Have frozen vegetables on hand for quick additions
- Buy proteins you can cook multiple ways

## Practical Learning Resources
- YouTube channels focused on quick healthy cooking
- Apps like Yummly or Budget Bytes
- Books like *Salt Fat Acid Heat* for understanding cooking principles
- Start with recipes labeled "30 minutes or less"

## Quick Meal Frameworks
- **Sheet pan meals** - protein + vegetables, roast together
- **Grain bowls** - base + protein + vegetables + sauce
- **Stir fries** - fast and flexible

## Key Mindset Tips
- Accept that it gets faster with practice
- Simple meals can be very nutritious
- Mistakes are part of learning
