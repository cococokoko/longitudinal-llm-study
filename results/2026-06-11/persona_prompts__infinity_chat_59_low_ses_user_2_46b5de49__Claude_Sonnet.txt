── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write tomorrow's top 3 priorities** on a sticky note or notepad
- **Clear your desk** of anything you don't need overnight
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing in the morning

### Why this works well for your situation:
As a single parent, your brain is already managing a lot. This habit means you **don't have to remember where you left off** when you arrive the next morning, possibly rushed or tired.

You walk in with a clear starting point instead of spending mental energy figuring out what to do first.

---

**Bonus thought:** Keep your top 3 priorities realistic. On harder parenting days, even completing **one meaningful task** is a win worth acknowledging.
