## The Core Method (Pan Sear)

**Before cooking:**
- Bring steak to **room temperature** (30-45 min)
- Pat **completely dry**
- Season generously with salt and pepper

**Cooking:**
1. Cast iron skillet on **high heat** until smoking
2. Add high smoke-point oil
3. Sear **2-3 minutes per side** without moving
4. Add butter, garlic, thyme - **baste continuously**

**Critical step:**
- **Rest 5-10 minutes** before cutting

---

## Temperature Guide
- Medium-rare: **130-135°F** *(most recommended)*
- Medium: **140-145°F**

## Quick Tips for Busy Days
