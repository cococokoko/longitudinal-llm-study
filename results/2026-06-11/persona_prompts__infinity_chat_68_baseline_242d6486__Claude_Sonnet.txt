# Team Management Tip: **Have Regular One-on-One Meetings**

Schedule brief, consistent check-ins with each team member individually. This helps you:

- **Understand their challenges** before they become bigger problems
- **Build trust** so people feel comfortable being honest with you
- **Recognize individual needs** since different people are motivated differently
- **Give personalized feedback** rather than only group feedback

Even 15-30 minutes every week or two can make a significant difference in team morale and performance.

**A simple approach:**
- Ask what's going well
- Ask what obstacles they're facing
- Ask how you can support them

This shows you value each person as an individual, not just as a worker, which tends to increase engagement and loyalty.
