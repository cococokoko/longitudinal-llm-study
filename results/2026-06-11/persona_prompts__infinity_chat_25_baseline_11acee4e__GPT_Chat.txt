── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line expressing thanks for someone's time, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for Your Time Today
- Thank You for Your Time and Assistance
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Many Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You for the Opportunity
- Thank You for Speaking with Me
- Thank You for Your Time and Insight

If the email follows an interview, these are common choices:

- Thank You for the Interview
- Thank You for Meeting with Me Regarding [Position Name]
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration – [Your Name]

For maximum professionalism and clarity, "Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is often a strong choice.
