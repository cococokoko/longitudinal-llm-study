Here is a powerful rhetorical question tailored to be sharp, engaging, and easy to write about, even when you are short on time and energy after a long shift:

> **"If a stranger followed our children everywhere they went, whispering in their ears what they should look like, how they should act, and why they aren't good enough, we would call the police—so why do we let them carry that exact stranger in their pockets every single day?"**

### Why this works for your essay:

* **It is easy to transition from:** You can easily follow this question with your thesis statement. For example: *"Social media is no longer just a tool for connection; it has become a constant, unregulated source of anxiety, depression, and isolation for today's youth."*
