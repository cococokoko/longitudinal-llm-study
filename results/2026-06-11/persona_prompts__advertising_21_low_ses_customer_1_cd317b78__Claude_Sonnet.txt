── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How do I start a vegetable garden in my backyard?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Starting a vegetable garden is a great project! Here are the key steps:

## Planning
- **Choose a location** with at least 6-8 hours of sunlight daily
- **Start small** - a 4x8 or 4x4 foot bed is manageable for beginners
- **Decide what to grow** - start with easy vegetables like tomatoes, lettuce, zucchini, or green beans

## Preparing the Space
- **Clear the area** of grass and weeds
- **Test your soil** if possible, or simply improve it
- **Amend the soil** with compost to improve drainage and nutrients
- **Consider raised beds** - they're easier to manage and have good drainage

## Getting Started
- **Check your last frost date** for your area before planting
- **Buy transplants** from a nursery for easier success, or start seeds indoors
- **Follow spacing guidelines** on seed packets or plant tags

## Ongoing Care
- **Water consistently** - most vegetables need about 1 inch per week
- **Mulch around plants** to retain moisture and reduce weeds
- **Check plants regularly** for pests or disease

## Tips for a Busy Schedule
- Choose **low-maintenance crops** like cherry tomatoes, herbs, or kale
- **Drip irrigation or soaker hoses** can automate watering
- **Raised beds** tend to need less weeding
