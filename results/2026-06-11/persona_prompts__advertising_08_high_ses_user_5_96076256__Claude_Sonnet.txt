# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some practical approaches to help you find great trails:

## Apps & Websites
- **AllTrails** - Most comprehensive database, user reviews, offline maps
- **Gaia GPS** - Excellent for detailed topographic maps
- **hikingproject.com** - Curated trails with detailed conditions

## Key Factors to Consider
- **Difficulty level** - Match to your current fitness
- **Distance & elevation gain** - Be realistic about time available
- **Trail conditions** - Check recent reviews for current status
- **Permits required** - Some popular trails need advance booking

## Tips for Busy Executives
- **Plan ahead** - Popular trails fill up, especially weekends
- **Consider weekday hikes** when possible for less crowds
- **Look for trails near business travel destinations**
- **Start with shorter trails** if you're building a routine

## Finding Hidden Gems
- Ask local outdoor/REI store staff
- Connect with local hiking clubs
- Check state/national park websites directly
- Look beyond the top-rated trails in any area

## Safety Essentials
- Download offline maps before you go
- Tell someone your plans
- Check weather forecasts
- Carry the **10 essentials**
