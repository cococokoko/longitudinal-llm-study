What are the best apps for tracking fitness and nutrition?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some popular and well-regarded options:

## Fitness Tracking
- **Strava** - great for running/cycling, strong community
- **Nike Run Club** - running focused, free coaching plans
- **Garmin Connect** - pairs well with Garmin devices
- **Apple Fitness+** - integrated with Apple Watch
- **Peloton** - workouts beyond just the bike

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - large food database, very popular
- **Cronometer** - detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - user-friendly calorie counting
- **Noom** - includes behavioral coaching

## All-in-One Options
- **MyFitnessPal** - covers both reasonably well
- **Fitbit app** - pairs with device, tracks both
- **Samsung Health** - good if you use Samsung devices

## Things to Consider When Choosing
- **Free vs. paid** - many have limited free tiers
- **Device compatibility** - works with your phone/watch?
- **Your specific goal** - weight loss, performance, general health
- **How detailed you want to be** - some are more complex than others

## Honest Considerations
- Tracking can be helpful but also stressful for some people
- Accuracy varies, especially for calorie estimates
- Consistency matters more than which app you pick
