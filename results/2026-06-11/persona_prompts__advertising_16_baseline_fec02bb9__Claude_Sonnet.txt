# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures (deadlines, conflicts, demands)
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause

Both are normal, but both can become overwhelming.

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based
- **Identify triggers** — notice what sets it off
- **Challenge anxious thoughts** — ask "Is this realistic? What's the evidence?"
- **Limit news/social media** consumption
- **Journaling** — writing can clarify and release worry

### 🫁 Body-Based
- **Deep breathing** — slow exhale activates calm (try 4-7-8 breathing)
- **Regular exercise** — even a 20-minute walk helps significantly
- **Sleep consistency** — poor sleep amplifies anxiety
- **Reduce caffeine and alcohol**

### 🔗 Connection-Based
- Talk to someone you trust
- Don't isolate — social support is protective

### ⏱️ Lifestyle Structure
- Break overwhelming tasks into small steps
- Build in rest and recovery time
- Set realistic expectations

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, panic attacks)
- Feeling hopeless or unable to cope

**Therapy (especially CBT) and/or medication can be very effective.**
