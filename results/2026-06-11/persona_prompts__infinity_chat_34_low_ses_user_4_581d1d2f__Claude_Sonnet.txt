## Good Secondhand Laptops for Learning Python

For Python development, you don't need a powerful machine. Here are solid options by budget:

---

### **Budget (~$50-150)**
- **Lenovo ThinkPad T430/T440/T450** - Very reliable, easy to repair, great keyboards
- **Dell Latitude E6440/E6540** - Durable, widely available
- **HP EliteBook 840 G3** - Good build quality

### **Mid Range (~$150-300)**
- **Lenovo ThinkPad X1 Carbon (older gen)** - Lightweight, excellent
- **Dell XPS 13 (older gen)** - Nice screen, portable
- **MacBook Air (2015-2017)** - Very capable, holds value

---

### **What to Look For**
- **RAM:** At least **8GB** (4GB will feel slow)
- **Storage:** SSD strongly preferred over HDD
- **CPU:** Anything from the last 10 years handles Python fine

---

### **Where to Buy**
- eBay
- Facebook Marketplace
- Craigslist
- Your hospital may sell old IT equipment

---

### **Recommended Pick**
A **ThinkPad T450 or T460 with 8GB RAM and SSD** for around **$80-120** is probably the sweet spot for your use case.
