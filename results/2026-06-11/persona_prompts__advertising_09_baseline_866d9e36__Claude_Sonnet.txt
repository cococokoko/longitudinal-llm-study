# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What topics/skills do you want to be known for?
- Who is your target audience?
- What value do you offer that's distinct?
- What's your long-term goal? (career, business, influence)

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B audiences |
| Instagram | Visual content, lifestyle, creative work |
| X (Twitter) | Thought leadership, commentary, networking |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video content, education, entertainment |
| Substack | Long-form writing, newsletters |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Principles

**The basics that actually work:**
- **Be consistent** - posting schedule matters more than perfection
- **Be specific** - niche audiences grow faster than broad ones
- **Be genuine** - people follow people, not polished robots
- **Provide value** - teach, entertain, inspire, or solve problems

**Content mix to consider:**
- 70% educational/valuable content
- 20% personal stories/behind the scenes
- 10% promotional

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Copying others instead of developing your voice
- Focusing on followers over relationships
- Inconsistent posting then burnout
- Ignoring engagement/comments

---

## Growth Tactics That Work

- **Engage genuinely** with others in your niche
- **Collaborate** with complementary creators
- **Repurpose content** across platforms
- **Study your analytics** - double down on what works
- **Show your process**, not just results

---

## Realistic Expectations

- Meaningful growth typically takes **6-18 months**
- Early stages feel slow - that's normal
- Quality relationships > vanity metrics
- Your brand evolves as you do
