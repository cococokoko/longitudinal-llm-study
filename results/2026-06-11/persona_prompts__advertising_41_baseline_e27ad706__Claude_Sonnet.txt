How can I improve my credit score quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve your credit score:

## Highest Impact Actions
- **Pay down credit card balances** - lowering your credit utilization ratio (below 30%, ideally below 10%) can raise scores quickly
- **Pay all bills on time** - payment history is the biggest factor (~35% of score)
- **Become an authorized user** on someone else's account with good history

## Fix Errors
- Check your credit reports at **AnnualCreditReport.com** (free)
- Dispute any inaccurate negative items
- This can sometimes produce fast results

## Avoid These Mistakes
- Don't close old accounts (hurts credit history length)
- Don't apply for multiple new cards at once (hard inquiries lower scores)
- Don't max out cards

## Realistic Timeline
| Action | Potential Timeframe |
|--------|-------------------|
| Paying down balances | 1-2 billing cycles |
| Disputing errors | 30-45 days |
| Building payment history | Several months |

## Honest Expectations
- Significant improvement typically takes **3-6+ months**
- Be cautious of services promising instant dramatic results
- Consistent habits matter more than quick fixes
