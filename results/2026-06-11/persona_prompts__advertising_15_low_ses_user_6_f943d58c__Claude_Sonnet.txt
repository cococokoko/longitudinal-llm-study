# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Low-Cost Decorating Ideas

### Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & garage sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - find free or cheap items locally
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly good for frames, candles, and small accents

### DIY Projects
- Repaint old furniture instead of buying new
- Create wall art with canvas and basic paint
- Make throw pillow covers from inexpensive fabric

### Rearrange What You Have
- Moving furniture around can completely change a room's feel
- **Costs nothing** and can make a big difference

### Focus on Key Impact Areas
- **Lighting** - swap bulbs or add lamps for a warm atmosphere
- **Plants** - affordable and bring life to any room
- **Throw blankets and pillows** - easy way to update a space

### Free Resources
- Pinterest for inspiration boards
- YouTube tutorials for DIY projects
- Freecycle groups in your community

## General Tips
- Set a **clear budget** before shopping
- Focus on **one room at a time**
- Buy items **gradually** rather than all at once
