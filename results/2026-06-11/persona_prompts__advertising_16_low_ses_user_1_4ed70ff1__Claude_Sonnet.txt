# Managing Stress and Anxiety with a Fast Food Job

Fast food work is genuinely tough - unpredictable schedules make planning your life really difficult, and that uncertainty itself creates stress. Here are practical approaches:

## Dealing with Schedule Unpredictability Specifically
- **Check your schedule as soon as it's posted** so you can plan around it quickly
- **Keep a simple weekly template** for sleep, meals, and downtime that you adjust each week
- **Build a small financial buffer** if possible, so schedule changes feel less threatening

## During Stressful Shifts
- **Box breathing** - inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4 (can do this in a bathroom break)
- **Name what's happening** - "this is a rush, it will end in about 20 minutes"
- **Focus only on the next task**, not the whole pile

## Recovery After Work
- Give yourself a **transition ritual** before home responsibilities hit
- Protect sleep even when shifts vary - it's your biggest stress regulator
- Eat something real, not just leftover work food

## Bigger Picture
- **Talk to coworkers** - shared experience genuinely helps
- Know your **legal rights** around scheduling in your area
- Consider whether anxiety feels manageable or is getting worse over time
