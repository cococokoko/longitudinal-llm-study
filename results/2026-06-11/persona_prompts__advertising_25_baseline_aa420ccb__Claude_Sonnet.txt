# Decorating Your Apartment on a Minimalist Budget

Here are practical approaches that work well together:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for better flow
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items in new ways

## Low-Cost Sources
- **Thrift stores** - furniture, frames, vases
- **Facebook Marketplace / Craigslist** - often free or very cheap
- **Dollar stores** - basic organizational items
- **Your own photos** printed cheaply online

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** - one accent wall transforms a room
- **New throw pillows** - change the whole feel
- **Plants** - affordable and add life (pothos, snake plants are cheap/easy)
- **Better lighting** - swap bulbs for warmer tones, add a lamp

## Minimalist Principles That Save Money
- Choose fewer, more intentional pieces
- Stick to a simple color palette (2-3 colors)
- Empty space is actually a design element
- Quality over quantity

## Free Options
- Natural light (keep windows clean and unblocked)
- Organize and display books
- Borrow or swap items with friends

## Things to Avoid
- Buying lots of small decorative items
- Trendy pieces that date quickly
