# Smartwatch Feature for Senior Citizens: **Family Caregiver Dashboard**

## Core Concept: "Family Circle" Connectivity

Since many seniors support extended family and manage significant household responsibilities, this feature addresses both their safety needs and their active role in family life.

---

## Key Components

### 🏠 Household Task Manager
- Simple voice-activated reminders for medications, appointments, and household tasks
- Shared family calendar visible to multiple family members
- Grocery and errand list syncing with family members

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Extended Family Hub
- **One-touch calling** to multiple family members with large, labeled buttons
- Quick status updates ("I'm okay" / "Need help" / "Call me")
- Location sharing with trusted family contacts

### 🚨 Smart Safety Features
- Fall detection with automatic alerts to designated family members
- **Wellness check-ins** that notify family if routines seem disrupted
- Emergency SOS with GPS location sent to entire family circle

### 💡 Why This Works
- Respects the senior's **active, responsible role** in the family
- Reduces caregiver anxiety without making seniors feel monitored
- Keeps them **connected rather than isolated**

---

## Design Priority
Large display, simple interface, long battery life, and water resistance for daily household activities.
