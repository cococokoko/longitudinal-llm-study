How to improve home WiFi?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve your home WiFi:

## Router Placement
- Place it in a **central location** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not the floor)
- Avoid walls, appliances, and metal objects nearby

## Basic Settings
- Use the **5GHz band** for faster speeds (shorter range)
- Use **2.4GHz** for better range through walls
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**
- Keep router **firmware updated**

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Restart your router** regularly
- Consider a **newer router** if yours is old (5+ years)
- Add a **WiFi extender or mesh system** for larger homes
- Use a **wired Ethernet connection** for devices that need reliability

## Reduce Interference
- Keep away from microwaves and cordless phones
- Distance from neighboring networks
- Limit devices using bandwidth simultaneously

## Security
- Use **WPA3 or WPA2** encryption
- Change default passwords
- Regularly check connected devices

## Quick Wins
- Restart your router and modem
- Move closer to the router
- Disconnect unused devices
