Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Planning First
- **Define your style** before buying anything

## Low-Cost Strategies

### Shopping Smart
- Thrift stores, estate sales, and Facebook Marketplace
- IKEA and similar affordable retailers
- End-of-season sales for textiles and decor
- Dollar stores for small accents

### DIY Projects
- Repaint furniture instead of replacing it
- Create gallery walls with personal photos
- Make your own throw pillow covers
- Refinish old wood pieces

### High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations for little money
- Swap out hardware on cabinets and doors
- Add mirrors to make spaces feel larger
- Rearrange existing furniture

### Free Options
- Swap items between rooms
- Borrow or trade with friends/family
- Nature-inspired decor (branches, stones, pinecones)

## General Tips
- Buy secondhand first, new last
- Focus on **lighting** - it changes everything
- Less clutter often looks more intentional
- Neutral bases with colorful accents are versatile
