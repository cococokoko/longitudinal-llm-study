# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting family and managing household responsibilities, mindfulness can be genuinely helpful - and it doesn't require much time.

## Starting Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily**
- Consistency matters more than duration
- Same time each day builds the habit (morning often works well before demands start)

**Basic breathing meditation**
- Sit comfortably
- Focus on your breath going in and out
- When your mind wanders, gently return attention to breathing
- That's genuinely it

## Practical Approaches for Busy People

- **Micro-moments** - mindful dishwashing, mindful coffee, mindful commute
- **Transition moments** - pause briefly between tasks
- **Body scan** before sleep - notice tension you're carrying

## Free Resources Worth Trying

- **Apps:** Insight Timer (free), Headspace, Calm
- **YouTube:** search "guided meditation for beginners"
- **Podcasts:** many free guided sessions available

## Realistic Expectations

- Your mind *will* wander - that's normal, not failure
- Benefits often appear after **2-4 weeks** of regular practice
- Some days will feel pointless - keep going anyway

## Especially Relevant for Caregivers

Mindfulness can help you **notice stress building** before it overwhelms you, which protects both you and the people you support.
