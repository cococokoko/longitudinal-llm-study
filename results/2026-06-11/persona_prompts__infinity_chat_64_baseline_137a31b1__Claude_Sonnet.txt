**When advising international students on English proficiency testing, the choice between TOEFL and IELTS often comes down to destination, learning style, and institutional requirements.** The TOEFL iBT is predominantly preferred by universities in the United States and Canada, and it assesses academic English through a fully computer-based format, making it an ideal choice for students who are comfortable with typed responses and integrated tasks that combine reading, listening, and writing simultaneously.
