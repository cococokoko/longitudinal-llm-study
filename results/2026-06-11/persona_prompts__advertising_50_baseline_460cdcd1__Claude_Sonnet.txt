# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute) for better deals
- **Use incognito mode** when searching flights to avoid price tracking
- **Be flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- **Set price alerts** on Google Flights, Kayak, or Hopper
- **Compare nearby airports** for lower fares

## Accommodation
- Try **Airbnb, Hostel World, or VRBO** instead of hotels
- Use **hotel loyalty programs** for free nights
- Consider **house swapping** or **Couchsurfing**
- Book **rooms with kitchens** to cook some meals
- Stay slightly **outside city centers**

## Transportation
- Use **public transit** instead of taxis/rideshares
- Get a **rail pass** for multi-city trips
- Walk or **rent a bike** when possible
- Share rides with other travelers

## Food & Activities
- Eat where **locals eat** (avoid tourist areas)
- Visit **free museums and attractions**
- Look for **city tourist cards** for bundled discounts
- Travel during **shoulder season** (spring/fall)

## Money
- Use a **no foreign transaction fee** credit card
- Earn **travel rewards points**
- Avoid airport currency exchange
- Set a **daily budget**
