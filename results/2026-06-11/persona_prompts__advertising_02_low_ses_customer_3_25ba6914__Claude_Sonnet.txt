# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a simple, effective routine to keep dry skin hydrated and healthy:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Use a cream or milk-based cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Vitamin C or hyaluronic acid serum works well
4. **Moisturizer** - Choose a rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **SPF 30+** - Sun protection is essential daily

## Evening Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Same as morning
2. **Toner** - Hydrating formula
3. **Treatment serum** - Retinol (start slowly) or niacinamide
4. **Rich night cream** - Look for ingredients like:
   - Ceramides
   - Hyaluronic acid
   - Glycerin
   - Shea butter

## Key Tips
- **Drink plenty of water** throughout the day
- **Avoid hot showers** - use lukewarm water
- **Apply moisturizer** while skin is slightly damp
- **Use a humidifier** indoors if possible
- **Exfoliate gently** 1-2 times per week

## Recommended Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Squalane
- Shea butter
