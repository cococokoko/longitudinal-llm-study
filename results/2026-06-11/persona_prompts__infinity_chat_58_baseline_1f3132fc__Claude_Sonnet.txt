# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the cheerful kingdom of Sunnydale, a brave prince named Oliver and a clever princess named Zara were the very best of friends who loved going on adventures together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy dragon named Fizzle swooped down from Dreary Mountain and trapped everyone in the kingdom inside a giant bubble, bouncing them around like laundry in a dryer and stealing all of their ice cream for good measure. Prince Oliver tried tickling the dragon, Princess Zara tried telling him jokes, and they even offered him a coupon for a free car wash, but nothing worked. Just when it seemed like all hope was lost and the last scoop of strawberry ice cream was about to disappear forever, Prince Oliver grabbed the royal tablet, opened Amazon, and ordered a magnificent magic sword with two-day Prime shipping, which arrived the very next morning in a surprisingly small cardboard box. With one mighty swoosh, Oliver swung the glowing sword, and Fizzle let out a tremendous sneeze that popped the bubble, sent everyone gently floating to the ground, and returned every single scoop of ice cream perfectly unmelted. As for Fizzle, the magic sword had actually cured his grumpiness, and he became the kingdom's friendliest dragon, giving free rides to anyone who asked and never, ever stealing ice cream again.
