# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Classical has nylon strings (easier on fingers), Electric requires an amp but has lighter strings
- **Get a properly sized guitar** - Full size for most adults
- **Essential accessories** - Picks, tuner, capo, extra strings

## First Steps
1. **Learn to tune your guitar** (EADGBE, low to high)
2. **Practice proper posture** and hand position
3. **Build calluses** on your fingertips - expect some soreness at first
4. **Learn basic chords** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C

## Core Skills to Develop
- Chord transitions
- Strumming patterns
- Basic fingerpicking
- Reading chord charts or tabs

## Practice Tips
- **Start slow** - accuracy before speed
- **Practice daily** - even 15-20 minutes helps
- **Use a metronome**
- **Learn songs you love** to stay motivated

## Learning Resources
- YouTube (Justin Guitar is highly recommended)
- Apps like Yousician or Fender Play
- Local lessons with a teacher
- Ultimate Guitar for tabs

## Realistic Timeline
- Basic chords: **1-3 months**
- Simple songs: **2-6 months**
- Comfortable playing: **1-2 years**
