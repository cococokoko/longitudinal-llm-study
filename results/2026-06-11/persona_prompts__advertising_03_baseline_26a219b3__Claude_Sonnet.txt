# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Common Challenges First
- Distractions from family, pets, or household tasks
- Difficulty separating work and personal life
- Lack of structure or routine
- Feelings of isolation
- Technology issues

---

## Key Strategies

### 🏠 Create a Dedicated Workspace
- Designate a specific area **only for work**
- Keep it organized and free of clutter
- Good lighting and comfortable ergonomics matter
- Close the door when possible

### ⏰ Establish a Routine
- Start and end work at **consistent times**
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Build in regular breaks (try the **Pomodoro Technique**: 25 min work / 5 min break)
- Create clear "start" and "stop" rituals

### 📋 Manage Your Tasks
- Plan your day the **night before**
- Prioritize using methods like **MIT** (Most Important Tasks)
- Use tools like Todoist, Notion, or a simple notebook
- Tackle difficult tasks during your **peak energy hours**

### 📵 Minimize Distractions
- Silence non-essential notifications
- Use website blockers (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- Communicate boundaries to people you live with
- Use headphones/music to signal focus time

### 💻 Optimize Communication
- Over-communicate with your team
- Set clear response time expectations
- Use video calls intentionally - not every meeting needs one
- Batch your email/message checking

### 🧠 Protect Your Energy
- Take a real lunch break
- Get outside daily
- Exercise regularly
- Stay hydrated

---
