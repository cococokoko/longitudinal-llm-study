# Creating a Successful YouTube Channel

## Strategic Foundation

**Define your niche clearly**
- Specific beats broad (e.g., "budget travel for solo women over 40" vs. "travel")

## Content Strategy

**The core pillars**
- Consistency matters more than frequency

- Clear structure keeps viewers watching
