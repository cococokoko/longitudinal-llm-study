# Managing Stress and Anxiety

Here are evidence-based strategies organized by approach:

## Immediate Relief Techniques
- **Deep breathing** - 4-7-8 breathing or box breathing activates the parasympathetic nervous system
- **Progressive muscle relaxation** - systematically tensing and releasing muscle groups
- **Grounding exercises** - 5-4-3-2-1 sensory technique to anchor to the present moment

## Lifestyle Foundations
- **Regular exercise** - consistently one of the most effective interventions
- **Sleep hygiene** - prioritizing 7-9 hours significantly reduces baseline anxiety
- **Limiting caffeine and alcohol** - both can worsen anxiety symptoms
- **Nutrition** - stable blood sugar helps regulate mood

## Cognitive Approaches
- **Cognitive restructuring** - identifying and challenging unhelpful thought patterns
- **Mindfulness meditation** - building awareness without judgment
- **Journaling** - externalizing worries can reduce their intensity

## Longer-Term Strategies
- **Therapy** - CBT has strong evidence for anxiety disorders
- **Social connection** - relationships buffer stress significantly
- **Setting boundaries** - reducing unnecessary stressors at the source

## When to Seek Help
Consider professional support if anxiety:
- Interferes with daily functioning
- Persists despite self-management
- Involves panic attacks or avoidance behaviors
