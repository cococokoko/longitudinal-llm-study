# Fall Detection with Automatic Emergency Response

## Feature Overview
An enhanced fall detection system that goes beyond basic accelerometer detection to provide comprehensive emergency response.

## Key Components

### Detection
- **Multi-sensor analysis** combining accelerometer, gyroscope, and heart rate data to distinguish genuine falls from normal movements
- **Reduced false alarms** compared to standard fall detection

### Response Protocol
1. **Immediate alert** with vibration and audio prompt
2. **30-second confirmation window** allowing the user to cancel if uninjured
3. **Automatic emergency contact notification** if no response
4. **GPS location sharing** with family members and emergency services
5. **Two-way audio** enabling direct communication through the watch

### Medical Context Feature
- Transmits a **brief medical summary** (blood type, medications, conditions) to responders
- Particularly valuable for users with **cognitive decline** who may struggle to communicate

## Why This Matters
- Falls are the **leading cause of injury** among adults over 65
- Response time is critical, especially for seniors living alone
- Reduces anxiety for both seniors and their families

## Design Considerations
- Large, simple interface for confirmation/cancel
- Long battery life to ensure 24/7 coverage
- Comfortable enough for **overnight wear**
