# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a practical overview of sustainable travel choices:

## Transportation
- **Train/Rail** - Often the lowest carbon footprint for medium distances
- **Bus/Coach** - Efficient per-passenger emissions
- **Electric vehicles** - Especially effective where renewable energy is available
- **Cycling/Walking** - Zero emissions for short distances
- **Carpooling** - Reduces per-person emissions significantly

## If Flying is Necessary
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Choose airlines with **newer, fuel-efficient fleets**

## Accommodation
- **Certified eco-hotels** or green-certified properties
- **Local/independent lodging** over large chains
- Minimize towel/linen changes
- Choose places with **solar power or renewable energy**

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation** at your destination
- Rent **e-bikes or electric scooters**
- Support **local businesses and restaurants**
- Minimize single-use plastics

## Planning Tips
- **Travel slower** - fewer trips, longer stays
- **Choose closer destinations** when possible
- **Off-season travel** reduces overtourism pressure
