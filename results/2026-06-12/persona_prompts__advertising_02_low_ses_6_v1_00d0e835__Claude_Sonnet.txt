# Simple Skin Routine for Dry Skin (Warehouse Worker)

Working long hours in a warehouse can be tough on skin due to **dust, temperature changes, and physical activity**. Here's a practical routine:

## Morning (Quick & Simple)
- **Gentle cleanser** - avoid harsh soaps that strip moisture
- **Moisturizer with SPF** - even indoors, light protection helps
- **Lip balm** - warehouses can be very drying

## Evening (Most Important)
- **Cleanse** - wash off dust and grime from the day
- **Thicker moisturizer** - look for ingredients like:
  - Hyaluronic acid
  - Ceramides
  - Shea butter
  - Glycerin

## Key Tips for Your Situation
- **Drink water consistently** throughout your shift
- **Hand cream** is essential - frequent hand washing dries skin fast
- Apply moisturizer **right after washing** while skin is slightly damp
- Consider a **humidifier** at home if warehouse air is very dry

## Budget-Friendly Product Options
- CeraVe Moisturizing Cream
- Neutrogena Hydro Boost
- Vaseline (great overnight treatment)

## What to Avoid
- Hot showers after work (use warm water)
- Fragranced soaps
- Skipping moisturizer when tired
