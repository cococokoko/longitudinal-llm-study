Here are some practical tips for minimalist apartment decorating on a budget:

## Start with What You Have
- Declutter first - removing excess items often makes spaces look better immediately
- Rearrange existing furniture for better flow
- Clean and repair items before replacing them

## Low-Cost Decorating Ideas
- **Thrift stores and secondhand shops** for unique pieces at low prices
- **Plants** - even small succulents add life and warmth inexpensively
- **Library books** arranged on shelves add color and personality
- **Framed prints** from free art sites like Unsplash or public domain collections

## Minimalist Principles That Save Money
- Focus on **fewer, better items** rather than lots of cheap things
- Choose **neutral colors** that work together easily
- Let **negative space** be part of the design
- **Multi-functional furniture** does more with less

## Free or Nearly Free Options
- Rearrange furniture seasonally for a fresh feel
- Use items you already own in new ways
- Natural light is free - maximize it with clean windows and light curtains
- Collect interesting rocks, branches, or pinecones as natural decor

## Smart Spending
- Prioritize items you see and use daily
- Wait for sales on anything you genuinely need
- Check Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist
