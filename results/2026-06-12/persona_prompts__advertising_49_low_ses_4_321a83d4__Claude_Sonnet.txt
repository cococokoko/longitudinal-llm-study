# How to Improve Home WiFi

Here are some effective ways to boost your WiFi performance:

## Router Placement
- Place router in a **central location** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not the floor)
- Avoid placing near **microwaves, cordless phones, or thick walls**
- Keep it **out in the open**, not in a cabinet

## Router Settings
- **Update firmware** regularly
- Switch to **5GHz band** for faster speeds (shorter range)
- Use **2.4GHz band** for better range/coverage
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Restart your router** regularly
- Consider a **WiFi extender or repeater**
- Upgrade to a **mesh network system** (like Eero or Google Nest)
- Replace an **older router** (5+ years old)

## Reduce Interference
- Limit devices using bandwidth simultaneously
- Disconnect devices you're not using
- Use **ethernet cables** for stationary devices (TVs, gaming consoles)

## Security
- Use a **strong password** to prevent unauthorized users
- Use **WPA3 or WPA2** encryption

## Check Your Internet Plan
- Make sure your **plan speed** meets your household needs
- Contact your **ISP** if speeds are consistently low
