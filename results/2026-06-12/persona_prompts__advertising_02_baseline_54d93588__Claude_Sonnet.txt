# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** *(optional)* - Choose alcohol-free, hydrating formulas
3. **Serum** - Look for **hyaluronic acid** or **glycerin**
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (choose a moisturizing formula)

---

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Serum** - Consider **niacinamide** or **peptides**
3. **Moisturizer** - Heavier night cream or facial oil
4. **Facial Oil** *(optional)* - Rosehip, jojoba, or squalane oil

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- ✅ Hyaluronic acid
- ✅ Ceramides
- ✅ Glycerin
- ✅ Shea butter
- ✅ Squalane

## Things to Avoid
- ❌ Alcohol-based products
- ❌ Harsh exfoliants
- ❌ Hot water when washing

---

## Extra Tips
- **Exfoliate** 1-2x per week with a gentle chemical exfoliant (lactic acid)
- Drink plenty of **water**
- Use a **humidifier** indoors
- Apply moisturizer on **slightly damp skin**
