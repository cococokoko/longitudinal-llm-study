- Backpacks filled with books can add resistance.
- Water jugs, grocery bags, or resistance bands can supplement bodyweight exercises.

- Tuesday: Brisk walk or jog
- Wednesday: Mobility and core work
- Thursday: Full-body strength workout
- Friday: Cardio session
- Saturday: Recreational activity (hike, bike ride, sports)
- Sunday: Light movement and stretching
