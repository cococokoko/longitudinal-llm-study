Honey has some genuinely impressive properties, though I'd describe them as **scientifically interesting** rather than magical:

**Real notable properties:**
- Low water content and acidic pH inhibit bacterial growth

- Has been used medicinally for wound care with some evidence behind it

**Worth being straightforward about:**
- Some claims about honey are overstated or lack strong evidence
