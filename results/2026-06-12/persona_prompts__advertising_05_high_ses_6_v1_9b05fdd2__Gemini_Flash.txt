### The Strategy: "High-ROI" Meal Planning
We are optimizing for **Time-to-Table, Nutritional Density, and Cost-Efficiency**. 

*   **The "Semi-Homemade" Rule:** We do not make sauces from scratch or chop raw butternut squash. We buy pre-prepped components and assemble them.
*   **The 3x3 Matrix:** Pick 3 proteins, 3 complex carbs, and 3 veggies for the week. Mix and match.
*   **The "Double-Down" Dinner:** Cook once, eat twice. Dinner is always tomorrow’s lunch.

### The "Zero-Decision" Weekly Menu

#### **Breakfast: The 3-Minute Brain Fuel**
*   **Option A: The Executive Parfait**
    *   1 cup Greek Yogurt + handful of walnuts + drizzle of honey + berries.
    *   *Cost:* ~$2.00 | *Time:* 90 seconds.
*   **Option B: The "Out-the-Door" Scramble**
    *   3 eggs scrambled with a handful of pre-washed spinach, topped with salsa and 1/2 avocado.
    *   *Cost:* ~$2.50 | *Time:* 4 minutes.

#### **Lunch: The "Desk-Side" Assembly (No Cooking)**
*   **Option A: The Mediterranean Power Bowl**
    *   Shredded rotisserie chicken + 1/2 pouch microwave quinoa + cucumber/tomato (pre-chopped) + dollop of hummus.
    *   *Cost:* ~$4.00 | *Time:* 2 minutes.
*   **Option B: The Salmon/Tuna Salad**
    *   Canned wild salmon mixed with 1 tbsp Greek yogurt (instead of mayo) and Dijon mustard, served over a bed of arugula with olive oil.
    *   *Cost:* ~$3.50 | *Time:* 3 minutes.

#### **Dinner: The 10-Minute Hot Meal**
*   **Option A: The "Sheet Pan" Cheat**
    *   Buy pre-cut salmon fillets or chicken breasts. Toss on a baking sheet with frozen broccoli florets, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake at 400°F for 20 mins while you check emails.
    *   *Cost:* ~$6.00 | *Active Time:* 2 minutes.
*   **Option B: The 10-Minute Chili**
    *   Brown 1 lb lean ground turkey with the pre-chopped mirepoix. Add a can of black beans, a jar of marinara sauce, and chili powder. Simmer for 10 mins. (Makes 4 servings).
    *   *Cost:* ~$3.00/serving | *Active Time:* 10 minutes.

### Partner-Level Efficiency Hacks

1.  **Outsource the Grocery Run (Instacart/Whole Foods Delivery):**
    *   Your billable rate or personal downtime is worth more than the $10 delivery fee. Set up a "recurring cart" of your staples.
2.  **The "Sous Chef" Tax:**
    *   Pay the extra $1.50 for pre-chopped onions, pre-washed kale, or riced cauliflower. The time saved on prep and cleanup yields a massive ROI.
3.  **The Travel Contingency (For Road Weeks):**
    *   If you are traveling to client sites, do not rely on airport food. Pack **"emergency protein"** in your Tumi: RxBars, individual almond packs, and high-quality jerky. 
    *   When ordering room service or dining with clients, use the **"Rule of 2"**: Order a lean protein (steak/salmon) and double vegetables. Skip the starch and bread basket to avoid the 3 PM energy crash.
