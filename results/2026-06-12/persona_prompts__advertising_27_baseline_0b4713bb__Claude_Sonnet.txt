── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint:

## Food Choices
- Eat less meat, especially beef (plant-based meals have significantly lower emissions)
- Reduce food waste by planning meals and using leftovers
- Buy local and seasonal produce when practical

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Consider carpooling
- If buying a car, consider electric or hybrid

## Home Energy
- Lower your thermostat a few degrees in winter
- Use LED bulbs
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Air dry laundry when possible

## Shopping & Consumption
- Buy secondhand when possible
- Choose quality items that last longer
- Repair rather than replace
- Reduce single-use plastics

## High-Impact Actions Worth Knowing
- Flying less has a significant effect
- Home heating/cooling choices matter a lot
- Having one fewer child is often cited as the highest individual impact (though this is a personal decision)

## Realistic Perspective
- Individual actions matter but **systemic changes** (policy, corporate practices) have larger scale impact
- Focus on your highest-impact areas rather than trying to be perfect everywhere
- Small consistent habits add up
