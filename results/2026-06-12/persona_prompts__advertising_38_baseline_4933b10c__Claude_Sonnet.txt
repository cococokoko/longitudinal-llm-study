# Best Platforms for Freelance Writers

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large platform, good for building long-term client relationships
- **Fiverr** - Good for packaged services, competitive but high volume
- **Freelancer.com** - Bid-based system with varied projects

## Writing-Specific Platforms
- **Contently** - Portfolio-focused, connects with larger brands
- **ClearVoice** - Content marketing focused
- **Scripted** - Vetted writers, steady content work
- **WriterAccess** - Various content types, tiered pay system

## Content Mills (Lower Pay, Good for Beginners)
- **Textbroker** - Easy entry, consistent work
- **iWriter** - Simple to start
- **Constant Content** - Sell pre-written articles

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality listings
- **MediaBistro** - Media and journalism focus
- **Journalism Jobs** - News and editorial work
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for direct outreach

## Practical Tips
- **Build a portfolio** before applying to competitive platforms
- **Specialize** in a niche to command higher rates
- **Direct outreach** to businesses often pays better than platforms
- Start with 2-3 platforms rather than spreading thin
