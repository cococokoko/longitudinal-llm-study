# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Here's a practical framework, structured the way you likely think about client problems:

---

## Strategic Principles

- **Plan before you shop** — reduces waste and impulse spending
- **Cook in batches** — maximizes time efficiency
- **Protein diversification** — shift from expensive to cost-effective sources

---

## Core Tactics

### 🛒 Shopping
- Build meals around **weekly sales**
- Buy **staples in bulk** — rice, oats, lentils, beans, frozen vegetables
- Choose **store brands** over name brands (often identical quality)
- Shop **seasonally** for produce

### 🍳 Meal Construction
| Protein | Cost | Nutrition |
|---|---|---|
| Eggs | Very Low | Excellent |
| Canned tuna/salmon | Low | High |
| Lentils/beans | Very Low | High |
| Chicken thighs | Low | Good |

### 📅 Weekly Planning
- **2-3 batch cook sessions** covers most meals
- Repurpose ingredients across multiple meals
- Freeze portions to prevent waste

---

## Realistic Budget Targets
- **$50-75/week** per person is very achievable with planning
- **$35-50/week** possible with discipline
