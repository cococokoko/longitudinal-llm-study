── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - Annual checkups and vaccinations
- **Proper nutrition** - Age and species-appropriate food
- **Fresh water** - Always available
- **Exercise** - Daily activity suited to their needs
- **Parasite prevention** - Flea, tick, and heartworm protection

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - Toys, puzzles, and activities that stimulate their mind
- **Social interaction** - Time with you and appropriate companions
- **Consistent routine** - Pets generally feel secure with predictability
- **Safe space** - A comfortable place they can retreat to

## Preventive Care
- **Dental hygiene** - Regular teeth cleaning
- **Grooming** - Brushing, nail trims, ear cleaning
- **Spay/neuter** - Can prevent health issues and behavioral problems
- **Watch for changes** - Early detection of illness matters

## Environment
- **Safe home** - Remove hazards and toxic plants/foods
- **Appropriate temperature** - Comfortable living conditions
- **Clean living space** - Regular cleaning of bedding and areas

## General Tips
- Learn the **specific needs** of your pet's species and breed
- Build a relationship with a **trusted veterinarian**
- Give them **attention and affection** regularly
