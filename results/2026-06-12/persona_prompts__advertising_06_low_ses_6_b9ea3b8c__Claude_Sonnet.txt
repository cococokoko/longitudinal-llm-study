# Mastering Digital Photography

Here's a comprehensive guide to help you develop your skills:

---

## 📷 Camera Fundamentals

### The Exposure Triangle
- **Aperture (f-stop)** – Controls depth of field and light intake
- **Shutter Speed** – Freezes or blurs motion
- **ISO** – Sensor sensitivity to light
- *Balancing these three is the foundation of good exposure*

### Shooting Modes
- **Manual (M)** – Full creative control
- **Aperture Priority (Av)** – You set aperture, camera sets shutter
- **Shutter Priority (Tv)** – You set shutter, camera sets aperture

---

## 🎯 Composition Techniques
- **Rule of thirds** – Place subjects off-center
- **Leading lines** – Draw the eye through the frame
- **Framing** – Use natural elements to frame subjects
- **Negative space** – Let subjects breathe

---

## 💡 Lighting Essentials
- **Golden hour** – Soft warm light after sunrise/before sunset
- **Diffused light** – Overcast days reduce harsh shadows
- **Direction matters** – Side lighting adds depth

---

## 🖥️ Post-Processing
- Learn **Lightroom** or **Capture One**
- Shoot in **RAW format** for maximum editing flexibility
- Master exposure, color grading, and sharpening

---

## 📈 Practice Tips
- Shoot **daily**
- Study photographers you admire
- Review and **critique your own work**
