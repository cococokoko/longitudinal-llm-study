# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

Here are practical strategies organized by effort level:

## Quick & Easy Changes
- **Switch to LED bulbs** - use up to 75% less energy than incandescent
- **Unplug devices** when not in use (chargers, TVs, appliances draw standby power)
- **Use power strips** to easily cut power to multiple devices
- **Adjust thermostat** a few degrees - even 1-2° makes a noticeable difference
- **Run full loads** in dishwasher and washing machine

## Moderate Effort
- **Wash clothes in cold water** - most of the energy goes to heating water
- **Air dry dishes and clothes** when possible
- **Seal drafts** around doors and windows with weatherstripping
- **Use ceiling fans** to reduce heating/cooling needs
- **Cook efficiently** - use lids on pots, match burner size to pan

## Bigger Investments
- **Smart/programmable thermostat** - can save significantly on heating/cooling
- **Upgrade to Energy Star appliances** when replacing old ones
- **Add insulation** to attic or walls
- **Check water heater temperature** (120°F is usually sufficient)

## Habit Changes
- Turn off lights when leaving rooms
- Take shorter showers
- Keep refrigerator/freezer well-stocked (they run more efficiently)
