# Effective Online Teaching Techniques

Here are the most evidence-based and practical approaches:

## Engagement & Interaction
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minutes max for video lectures)

- Build in **regular check-ins** rather than long passive sessions
- Create **discussion forums** with clear prompts and expectations

## Course Design
- Establish **clear structure and navigation** so students aren't confused
- State **learning objectives explicitly** for each module
- Provide **multiple content formats** (video, text, audio) for different learners

## Communication
- Set **clear response time expectations** upfront
- Use **video announcements** for important updates (more personal than text)
- Create a **FAQ document** that evolves throughout the course
- **Personalize feedback** rather than generic comments

## Building Community
- Use **small group breakout rooms** for collaboration
- Create **low-stakes early assignments** to build confidence
- Acknowledge students **by name** regularly

## Technical Considerations
- **Test everything** before going live
- Have a **backup plan** for technical failures
- Keep **accessibility** in mind (captions, readable fonts)
