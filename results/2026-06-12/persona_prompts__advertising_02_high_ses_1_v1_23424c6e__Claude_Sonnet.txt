# Skin Care Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a straightforward, effective routine:

## Morning
- **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming/sulfate-based)
- **Hydrating toner** - hyaluronic acid or glycerin-based
- **Moisturizer** - look for ceramides, shea butter, or squalane
- **SPF 30+** - mineral or chemical sunscreen

## Evening
- **Gentle cleanser**
- **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
- **Richer moisturizer** or facial oil (rosehip, jojoba)

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Squalane
- Shea butter

## Helpful Tips
- **Lukewarm water** only - hot water strips moisture
- Apply moisturizer **while skin is slightly damp**
- Consider a **humidifier** especially in winter
- Drink adequate water
- Avoid harsh exfoliants

## Recommended Products (widely available)
- CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser
- La Roche-Posay Toleriane moisturizer
- Neutrogena Hydro Boost
