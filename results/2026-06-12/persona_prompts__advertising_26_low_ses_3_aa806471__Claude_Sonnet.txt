# Data Security Best Practices for Small Businesses

## Foundation Steps

- **Inventory your data** - know what sensitive information you hold and where it's stored
- **Assess your risks** - identify what threats are most relevant to your business
- **Create a written security policy** - even a simple one establishes accountability

## Access Control

- Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
- Implement **multi-factor authentication (MFA)** wherever possible
- Give employees access only to data they need (least privilege principle)
- Remove access immediately when employees leave

## Technical Protections

- Keep software and operating systems updated
- Use reputable antivirus/anti-malware software
- Encrypt sensitive data, especially on laptops and mobile devices
- Secure your Wi-Fi network with WPA3 encryption
- Use a firewall

## Data Backup

- Follow the **3-2-1 rule**: 3 copies, 2 different media types, 1 offsite
- Test backups regularly to confirm they work
- Consider cloud backup solutions

## Employee Training

- Train staff to recognize phishing emails
- Establish clear procedures for handling sensitive data
- Create an incident response plan

## Compliance Considerations

- Know which regulations apply (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
- Document your security measures

## Quick Wins

- Audit who has admin privileges
- Enable automatic updates
- Use a password manager
