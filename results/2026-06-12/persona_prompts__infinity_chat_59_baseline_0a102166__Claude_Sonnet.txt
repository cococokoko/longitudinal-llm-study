# Organization Tip for Teachers: The "Sunday Reset" Routine

Try spending **20-30 minutes every Sunday** to set up your week before it starts.

## What to do during your reset:

- **Review your lesson plans** for the upcoming week
- **Prepare any materials** you'll need (copies, supplies, handouts)
- **Check your calendar** for meetings, deadlines, or events
- **Write your top 3 priorities** for the week
- **Clear and organize your desk** mentally or physically

## Why it works for teachers:

- Monday mornings feel **less chaotic**
- You avoid the **last-minute scramble** for materials
- You feel **more in control** when students have unexpected needs
- It reduces the mental load during **busy school days**

## Simple bonus tip:
Keep a **dedicated notebook or app** (like Google Keep or Notion) just for school tasks, so everything is in one place instead of scattered on sticky notes.

---

The goal isn't perfection - even a **quick 15-minute reset** can make a noticeable difference in how your week flows.
