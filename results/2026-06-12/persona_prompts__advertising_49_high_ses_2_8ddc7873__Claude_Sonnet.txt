# How to Improve Home WiFi

Here are some practical steps to boost your home WiFi performance:

## Router Placement
- **Position centrally** in your home for even coverage
- Place it **elevated** (shelf or table, not the floor)
- Keep it **away from walls, microwaves, and cordless phones**
- Avoid placing it in closets or cabinets

## Router Settings
- **Update firmware** regularly
- Switch to **5GHz band** for faster speeds (shorter range)
- Use **2.4GHz band** for better range through walls
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Replace an old router** (technology improves significantly every few years)
- Add a **WiFi extender or mesh network system** for larger homes
- Consider a **mesh system** (like Eero, Google Nest, or Orbi) for whole-home coverage

## Reduce Interference
- Minimize competing devices on the same frequency
- Keep distance from **baby monitors and Bluetooth devices**

## Other Tips
- **Restart your router** periodically
- Use **ethernet cables** for stationary devices (gaming consoles, smart TVs)
- Check if your **internet plan speed** meets your household needs
- Secure your network to prevent **unauthorized users**
