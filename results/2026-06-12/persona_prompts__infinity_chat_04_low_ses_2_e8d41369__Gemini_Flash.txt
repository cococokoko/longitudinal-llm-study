However, Marxist-feminists (like Silvia Federici or Nancy Fraser) have sometimes critiqued this as romanticizing unpaid domestic labor, which historically fell on women.
* **The Thesis Idea:** Reconstruct a **"Gorzian Social Reproduction Theory."** How can we use Gorz to defend the "non-market sphere" of parenting and care without trapping women (or single parents) in unpaid, isolated domestic labor?

*The Angle: Gorz, Platform Capitalism, and the Colonization of Autonomy.*

You can use Gorz to critique this "productivist eco-socialism."
