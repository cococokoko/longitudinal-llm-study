# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options that are simple and effective:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- **Carrier oils**: coconut, jojoba, argan, sweet almond
- **Butters**: shea, cocoa
- **Essential oils**: lavender, tea tree, rosehip
- **Other basics**: beeswax, aloe vera gel, apple cider vinegar, honey

---

## Simple Recipes to Start With

### 🧴 Moisturizing Body Lotion
- 1/2 cup shea butter
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil
- Whip together and store in a jar

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 2 tbsp beeswax
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Melt together, pour into small containers

### 🧖 Simple Face Mask
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp turmeric
- Mix and apply for 10-15 minutes

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- Essential oils to scent

---

## Important Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- Add **vitamin E** as a natural preservative
- Label with **date made** - most last 1-3 months
- Research essential oil **safety and dilution ratios**
