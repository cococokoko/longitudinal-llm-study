How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some good options for a formal "thank you for your time" subject line:

## Direct Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Thank You for Your Time and Consideration"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You for Today's Meeting"**
- **"Thank You for the Interview Opportunity"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time on [Date]"**

## Slightly More Formal
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time"**
- **"With Gratitude – [Your Name or Topic]"**
- **"Thank You for Your Time Regarding [Subject]"**

## Tips to Keep in Mind
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the meeting topic or date)
- **Keep it short** and clear
- **Capitalize** main words for a professional look
- Avoid **exclamation points** in very formal emails

The most commonly used and widely accepted option is simply:

> **"Thank You for Your Time"**
