After a long, quiet night shift at the hospital, especially when it's raining outside, there is one instrumental track that perfectly captures that exact mood:

**"Gymnopédie No. 1"** by **Erik Satie** (performed on solo piano).

### Why it matches a rainy night:
* **The Pace:** It has a slow, drifting tempo that mimics the steady, rhythmic patter of raindrops against a window.
