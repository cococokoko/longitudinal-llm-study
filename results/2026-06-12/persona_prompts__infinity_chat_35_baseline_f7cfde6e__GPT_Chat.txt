Give me a 3-day plan for Osaka with a flight leaving on the 3rd day at 7pm.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here's a balanced 3-day Osaka itinerary that assumes you're staying in central Osaka and departing from Kansai International Airport (KIX) on Day 3 at 7:00 PM. For an international flight, it's wise to arrive at the airport around 4:00 PM, so plan to leave central Osaka by about 3:00 PM.

Day 1: Osaka Highlights & Food

Morning
- Osaka Castle and Castle Park
  - Explore the castle grounds and museum.
  - Allow 2–3 hours.
- Walk through Nishinomaru Garden if it's open and the weather is good.

Lunch
- Try okonomiyaki (Osaka-style savory pancake) near Osaka Castle or in Umeda.

Afternoon
- Umeda district
  - Visit Umeda Sky Building's Floating Garden Observatory.
  - Explore Grand Front Osaka and nearby shopping areas.

Evening
- Dotonbori and Namba
  - Walk along the canal.
  - See the Glico sign and lively nightlife district.
- Dinner
  - Try takoyaki, kushikatsu, or yakiniku.
- Optional
  - Visit Hozenji Yokocho, a charming lantern-lit alley.

Day 2: Culture, Views & Local Neighborhoods

Morning
- Shitennoji Temple
  - One of Japan's oldest Buddhist temples.
- Walk through nearby traditional streets.

Lunch
- Shinsekai area
  - Famous for kushikatsu and retro Osaka atmosphere.

Afternoon
Option A (Best for first-time visitors)
- Abeno Harukas 300 Observatory
  - Panoramic city views.
- Explore Tennoji Park and surrounding area.

Option B (If you enjoy markets)
- Kuromon Ichiba Market
  - Sample seafood, fruit, and street food.

Evening
- Nakanoshima riverside area or return to Namba.
- Enjoy a more relaxed dinner at an izakaya.

Day 3: Flexible Final Day Before Departure

Morning
- Sumiyoshi Taisha Shrine
  - Beautiful historic shrine with distinctive architecture.
- Or visit Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan if that's more appealing.

Lunch
- Eat in the Tempozan area (if visiting the aquarium) or near your hotel.

Early Afternoon
Choose one:
- Last-minute shopping in Shinsaibashi.
- Explore Nakazakicho's cafés and small boutiques.
- Visit the National Museum of Art, Osaka.

2:30–3:00 PM
- Return to hotel, collect luggage.

3:00 PM
- Depart for Kansai International Airport (KIX).
  - Nankai Airport Express/Rapi:t from Namba: about 35–45 minutes.
  - JR Haruka from Osaka/Umeda area: about 50–60 minutes.

4:00 PM
- Arrive at KIX and check in.

7:00 PM
- Flight departure.

If this is your first visit to Japan and you'd like to include a day trip, Day 2 can easily be swapped for Kyoto or Nara.
