# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Dedicated workspace** - separate from relaxation areas when possible
- **Good lighting** - natural light when available
- **Minimize clutter** - keeps focus sharper
- **Reliable tech setup** - stable internet, proper equipment

## Managing Your Time
- **Set consistent work hours** - start and end at the same time
- **Morning routine** - treat it like going to an office
- **Time blocking** - schedule specific tasks in specific slots
- **Use the Pomodoro technique** - 25 min work / 5 min break cycles

## Reducing Distractions
- **Silence non-essential notifications**
- **Communicate boundaries** to household members
- **Website blockers** (Freedom, Cold Turkey) during focus time
- **Headphones/music** to signal focus mode

## Staying Motivated
- **Daily to-do lists** with realistic goals
- **Prioritize your hardest task first** (eat the frog)
- **Track accomplishments** - not just tasks remaining
- **Regular check-ins** with colleagues

## Protecting Your Wellbeing
- **Take real breaks** - step away from screens
- **Move your body** throughout the day
- **Hard stop time** - avoid overworking
- **Social connection** - combat isolation intentionally

## Key Principle
> **Structure creates freedom** - the more intentional your system, the less mental energy you waste.
