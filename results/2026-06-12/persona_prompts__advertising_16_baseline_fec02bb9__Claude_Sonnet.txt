# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures (deadlines, conflicts, demands)
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause

Both are normal, but both can become overwhelming.

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based Approaches
- **Identify triggers** — notice what consistently causes distress
- **Challenge anxious thoughts** — ask "Is this realistic? What's the evidence?"
- **Limit news/social media** if it amplifies worry
- **Journaling** — writing can clarify and release tension

### 🫁 Body-Based Approaches
- **Deep breathing** — slow exhale activates the calm response
- **Regular exercise** — one of the most effective stress relievers
- **Consistent sleep** — poor sleep worsens anxiety significantly
- **Reduce caffeine/alcohol** — both can increase anxiety

### 🔄 Lifestyle Habits
- Build **routine and structure**
- **Connect with others** — isolation worsens stress
- Set **realistic boundaries** with work and people
- Schedule **genuine rest**, not just distraction

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily functioning
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, insomnia)
- Feeling hopeless or overwhelmed consistently

**Therapy (especially CBT) and/or medication can be very effective.**
