# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** - Alcohol-free, hydrating toner
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid serum to attract moisture
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (look for moisturizing formulas)

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Toner** - Hydrating toner
3. **Treatment** - Retinol (start slow) or nourishing face oil
4. **Night Cream** - Heavier moisturizer or sleeping mask

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid**
- **Ceramides**
- **Glycerin**
- **Shea butter**
- **Squalane**

## Helpful Tips
- Drink plenty of water
- Use a **humidifier** especially in dry environments
- Avoid **hot showers** on your face
- Pat skin **gently dry** instead of rubbing
- Exfoliate **once a week** with a gentle scrub
