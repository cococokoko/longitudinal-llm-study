# Improving Productivity While Working From Home as a Professor

Here are practical strategies organized by category:

## Managing Your Time
- **Establish clear work hours** - especially important given the flexibility academic roles allow
- **Time-block your calendar** for deep work (writing, research) vs. administrative tasks
- **Protect morning hours** for your highest-cognitive work if possible
- **Set hard stop times** to prevent work from bleeding into all hours

## Managing Your Space
- Dedicate a specific workspace if possible
- Minimize interruptions during focused work periods
- Separate your "teaching presence" space from your writing space

## Managing Academic-Specific Challenges
- **Batch student communications** rather than responding continuously
- **Schedule virtual office hours** with clear boundaries
- **Protect research time** - it's easiest to let it get crowded out
- Use tools like Zotero, Notion, or Obsidian for research organization

## Managing Energy
- Build in transition rituals between work modes
- Account for the **hidden energy cost** of video meetings
- Take genuine breaks - academic work is cognitively demanding

## Honest Considerations
- Tenure can **reduce external accountability** - consider building in your own
- Identify whether your challenge is **focus, motivation, or structure** - solutions differ
- Collaboration and campus visits may matter more than you think
