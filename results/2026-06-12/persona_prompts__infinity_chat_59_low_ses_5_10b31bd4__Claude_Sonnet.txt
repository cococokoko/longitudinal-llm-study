# Organization Tip for Teachers: Use a **Weekly Planning Block**

Set aside **30-45 minutes every Sunday** (or Friday afternoon) to:

## What to do during that time:
- **Review the upcoming week** - check what lessons need prep
- **Prioritize your top 3 tasks** for each day
- **Prepare materials in advance** so you're not rushing before class
- **Check deadlines** for grading, meetings, or reports

## Why it works for teachers:
- Your week is already structured around class periods, so planning *around that schedule* reduces stress
- You walk in Monday feeling **in control** instead of reactive
- It prevents the "I forgot to copy those worksheets!" moments

## Simple tool to try:
A **one-page weekly template** with:
- Class periods blocked out
- A small to-do list per day
- A "this week's priority" box at the top

---

**The key idea:** Teachers often get pulled in many directions, so having a clear plan *before* the week starts helps you stay focused instead of just responding to whatever comes up.
