Here are some options for a formal "thank you for your time" subject line:

## Direct Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Thank You for Your Time and Consideration"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You – [Meeting/Interview/Call] on [Date]"**
- **"Following Up: Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time – [Topic/Context]"**

## Professional Variations
- **"With Gratitude for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for the Opportunity to Connect"**
- **"Many Thanks for Your Time and Insights"**

## Tips
- **Be specific** when possible (e.g., mention the meeting topic or date)
- **Keep it concise** — subject lines are most effective under 50 characters
- **Match the tone** to your relationship and context

The most commonly used and universally appropriate option is simply:
> **"Thank You for Your Time"**
