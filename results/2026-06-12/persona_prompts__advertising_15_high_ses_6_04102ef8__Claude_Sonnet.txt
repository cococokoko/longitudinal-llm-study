# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical strategies to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Start With What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** - less stuff often looks more intentional
- Repurpose items from other rooms

## Low-Cost Updates With Big Impact
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest ways to transform a room
- **New throw pillows or blankets** - change colors seasonally
- **Swap out hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **Update light fixtures** or just change bulb warmth

## Smart Shopping
- Thrift stores and estate sales
- Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist
- End-of-season sales
- Dollar stores for small decorative items
- IKEA for affordable basics

## DIY Ideas
- Frame fabric or wrapping paper as wall art
- Paint or refinish old furniture
- Create gallery walls with personal photos
- Make your own curtains

## Free Options
- Nature-inspired decor (branches, stones, plants)
- Printable art from free websites
- Borrow or swap items with friends

## Key Principles
- **Focus on a few statement pieces** rather than lots of small items
- **Stick to a color palette** for a cohesive look
- **Good lighting** makes everything look better
