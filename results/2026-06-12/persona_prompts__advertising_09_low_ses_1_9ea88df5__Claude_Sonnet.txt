# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Starting With Your Foundation

**Define your niche clearly**
- What specific value do you offer?
- Who is your target audience?
- What makes your perspective unique?

**Craft a simple brand statement**
- "I help [audience] achieve [result] through [your approach]"

---

## Practical Strategy for a Busy Schedule

Since your time is limited and unpredictable, focus on **efficiency**:

### Content Batching
- Use days off to create **multiple posts at once**
- Schedule them using free tools like **Buffer or Later**
- Even 1-2 hours can produce a week of content

### Content Ideas That Work
- Short behind-the-scenes moments
- Quick tips in your niche
- Personal lessons and experiences
- Repurposing one idea across multiple formats
