# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the cheerful kingdom of Sunnydale, Prince Leo and Princess Zara were the very best of friends who loved going on adventures together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy dragon named Fizzle swooped down from Dreary Mountain and trapped the whole kingdom under a giant smoky cloud, turning everyone's ice cream into broccoli and their laughter into sneezes. Prince Leo tried throwing his royal pillow at the dragon, but that didn't work. Princess Zara tried reasoning with Fizzle, offering him cookies and a warm blanket, but the dragon just sneezed fire and turned the cookies to ash. The royal knights tripped over their own armor, the court wizard accidentally turned himself into a frog, and even the royal dog just hid under the bed. All hope seemed completely, utterly, totally lost — until Prince Leo remembered he had a Prime membership! He grabbed his royal tablet, ordered the Legendary Sword of Sparkle with two-day delivery, and sure enough, a smiling cardboard box arrived right on time. Leo drew the glowing sword, gave Fizzle one mighty boop on the nose, and the dragon sneezed so hard he flew all the way back to Dreary Mountain, never to bother anyone again. The ice cream returned, the kingdom cheered, and Princess Zara gave the sword a five-star review.
