# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition focus
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical cookbook companion
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - affordable approach

## Websites & Apps
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based information
- **Forks Over Knives** (forksoverknives.com) - recipes and guides
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients to ensure you're meeting needs
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-based restaurants

## Practical Starting Tips
- Start gradually rather than all at once
- Learn about key nutrients: **B12, iron, calcium, omega-3s, vitamin D**
- Focus on whole foods rather than just processed substitutes
- Meal prep to make it sustainable

## Things Worth Knowing
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian** for personalized guidance
- Some resources have strong advocacy angles, so cross-referencing is helpful
- Individual nutritional needs vary

## YouTube Channels
- Mic the Vegan (research-focused)
- Pick Up Limes (practical recipes)
