── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What are the best apps for tracking fitness and nutrition?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some popular and well-regarded options:

## Fitness Tracking
- **Strava** - great for running and cycling
- **Nike Run Club** - running focused with coaching
- **Garmin Connect** - pairs well with Garmin devices
- **Apple Fitness+** - integrated with Apple Watch
- **Peloton** - workouts and classes

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - large food database, very popular
- **Cronometer** - detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - user-friendly calorie counting
- **Noom** - combines tracking with coaching

## All-in-One Options
- **MyFitnessPal** - covers both fitness and food
- **Fitbit app** - pairs with Fitbit devices
- **Samsung Health** - good for Android users

## Things to Consider When Choosing
- **Cost** - many have free and premium tiers
- **Device compatibility** - works with your phone/watch
- **Database size** - important for food tracking
- **Community features** - if motivation matters to you
- **Specific goals** - weight loss vs. performance vs. general health

## Honest Considerations
- Consistency matters more than which app you pick
- Some people find tracking obsessive or stressful
- Free versions are often sufficient for basic needs
