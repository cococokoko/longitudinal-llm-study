# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces like parks or gardens
- Pick a location accessible by public transport

## Invitations & Stationery
- Use digital invitations or e-RSVPs
- Choose recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Use soy-based or vegetable inks
- Minimize unnecessary paper inserts

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations instead of buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid single-use plastic decorations

## Attire
- Consider renting or buying second-hand
- Choose natural, sustainable fabrics
- Support ethical clothing brands

## Waste Management
- Set up recycling and composting stations
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Encourage carpooling
- Choose a venue near accommodation

## Carbon Offsetting
- Plant trees in honor of your wedding
- Purchase carbon offsets for unavoidable emissions
