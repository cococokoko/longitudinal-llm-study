Where can I find organic and sustainable clothing?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some good options for finding organic and sustainable clothing:

## Online Retailers
- **Patagonia** - well-known for environmental commitment
- **Eileen Fisher** - organic fabrics and take-back program
- **Pact** - affordable organic cotton basics
- **Tentree** - plants trees with purchases
- **Thought Clothing** - natural and recycled fabrics

## Marketplaces
- **ThredUp** - online secondhand/consignment
- **Poshmark** - peer-to-peer resale
- **Depop** - popular for vintage and secondhand
- **eBay** - wide selection of used clothing

## In-Person Options
- Local thrift stores (Goodwill, Salvation Army)
- Consignment shops
- Farmers markets sometimes feature local makers
- Co-ops and natural food stores may carry clothing

## Things to Look For
- **Certifications** like GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), Fair Trade, or B Corp
- **Recycled materials** labels
- **Transparent supply chain** information

## Tips
- Buying secondhand is often the most sustainable option
- Check brand websites for sustainability reports
- Apps like **Good On You** rate brands on ethics and sustainability
