# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a practical guide to get you started:

## Getting Started

### Choosing Your First Guitar
- **Acoustic** - No equipment needed, builds finger strength, great for beginners
- **Classical** - Nylon strings, easier on fingers, good for fingerpicking
- **Electric** - Easier to press strings, requires amp, good for rock/blues

### Essential Gear
- Guitar tuner (or tuning app like GuitarTuna)
- Extra strings
- Pick (medium thickness recommended)
- Capo (useful later)

---

## Building Foundations

### First Skills to Learn
1. **Proper posture** - Sitting and holding the guitar correctly
2. **Tuning** - EADGBE (standard tuning)
3. **Basic chords** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
4. **Strumming patterns** - Simple down strokes first

### Practice Tips
- **Start slow** - 15-20 minutes daily beats occasional long sessions
- **Use a metronome** - Builds timing from the beginning
- **Expect finger soreness** - Calluses develop in 2-4 weeks

---

## Learning Resources
- **Apps:** Yousician, Fender Play, Justin Guitar
- **YouTube:** JustinGuitar (free, highly recommended)
- **Consider lessons** for faster progress and avoiding bad habits
