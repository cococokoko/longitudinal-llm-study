# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some simple, effective recipes you can make during nap time or quiet moments!

## Quick & Easy Basics

### **Simple Face Moisturizer**
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Few drops lavender essential oil
- Melt together, cool, and store in a jar

### **Sugar Body Scrub**
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Mix and store in a sealed container

### **Honey Face Mask**
- 1 tbsp raw honey
- Apply directly, leave 10-15 minutes, rinse

---

## Helpful Tips for Busy Parents

- **Batch make** on weekends to save time
- Use **small mason jars** for storage
- Most recipes take **under 10 minutes**
- Many ingredients **double as cooking ingredients**

## Good Starter Ingredients to Keep on Hand

- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree)
