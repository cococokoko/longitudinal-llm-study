## Building a Personal Brand with a Fast Food Schedule

This is genuinely challenging but very doable. Here's how to think about it realistically:

---

### Your Real Constraints
- Shifts change week to week
- You're physically tired after work
- Limited predictable "content creation windows"
- Probably on your feet all day

---

### What Actually Works Here

**Batch content when you have energy**
- Use days off or pre-shift time to create multiple posts at once
- Schedule them out using free tools like Buffer or Later

**Keep it simple and consistent**
- 3 posts per week beats daily posting you can't sustain
- Short-form content (TikTok, Reels, Twitter/X) requires less production time

**Your job is actually content**
- Behind-the-scenes of fast food work resonates with a huge audience
- Customer service stories, work life reality, hustle culture commentary

---

### Honest Considerations

**What do you actually want the brand to be about?** This matters more than the schedule problem because it determines:
- Which platform fits best
- How much production effort is realistic
- Whether your job is an asset or just background

---

### What would help me give better advice:
- What topic or niche are you thinking?
- What's your goal - income, community, career pivot?
- Which platforms interest you?
