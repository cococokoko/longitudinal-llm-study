- Europa (a moon of Jupiter) and Enceladus (a moon of Saturn), which have subsurface oceans.
- Exoplanet atmospheres, where telescopes such as the James Webb Space Telescope search for gases that might indicate biological activity.

Scientists often distinguish between:
- Simple life (such as microbes), which may be relatively common.
- Intelligent, technological civilizations, whose prevalence is much more uncertain.
