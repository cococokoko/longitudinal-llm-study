- Stir-fry: frozen vegetables + protein (chicken, tofu, shrimp, eggs) + bottled sauce over rice.

- Pasta with vegetables and lean protein.

Stock your kitchen with:
- Frozen vegetables and fruit
- Eggs
- Canned beans
- Rice or microwave rice packets
- Oats
- Greek yogurt
- Whole-grain bread or tortillas
- Frozen chicken, fish, or other proteins
- Basic seasonings and sauces

To build skills quickly:
- Pick one cooking method at a time:
  - Week 1: sautéing
  - Week 2: roasting
  - Week 3: cooking rice and grains
  - Week 4: simple sauces and seasoning
- Watch short videos focused on one recipe and cook along.
- Repeat the same recipes several times before adding new ones.

- Keep "emergency meals" available:
  - Frozen vegetables + microwave rice + rotisserie chicken

- 1 carbohydrate: rice, potatoes, oats, bread, pasta
