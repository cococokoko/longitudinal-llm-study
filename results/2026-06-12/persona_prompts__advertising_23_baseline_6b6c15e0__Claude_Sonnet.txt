── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Learning to cook healthy meals quickly is very achievable. Here are some practical approaches:

## Start with the Basics
- Learn a few **fundamental techniques** (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Understand simple flavor combinations
- Get comfortable with knife skills to speed up prep

## Build a Strategy
- **Meal prep** on weekends or one day a week
- Cook grains and proteins in batches
- Keep a well-stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, olive oil, spices)

## Focus on Simple Recipes First
- Stir-fries (15-20 minutes)
- Sheet pan meals (minimal prep, oven does the work)
- Grain bowls with whatever vegetables you have
- Egg-based dishes (quick and nutritious)

## Good Learning Resources
- YouTube channels like Joshua Weissman or Ethan Chlebowski
- Apps like Yummly or Mealime
- Books like *Salt, Fat, Acid, Heat* by Samin Nosrat
- Budget Bytes website (healthy, simple recipes)

## Practical Tips
- Read the whole recipe before starting
- Prep ingredients before cooking (mise en place)
- Start with 3-4 recipes you rotate regularly
- Gradually add new dishes as you get comfortable

## Key Mindset
- **Consistency beats perfection** - simple meals made regularly beat elaborate ones you never attempt
