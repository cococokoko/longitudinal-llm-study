- Stay in touch with friends and family
- Talk to someone you trust about how you're feeling
- Consider joining a community group

- Take real breaks during work
- Practice deep breathing or mindfulness

- Identify what specifically causes you stress

- Stretch and care for your body
- Recognize when physical exhaustion affects your mood

## Mental Practices
- Focus on things within your control
- Practice gratitude
- Limit negative news/social media
- Do activities you genuinely enjoy

## When to Seek Help
- Talk to a doctor or therapist if you're struggling consistently
- Many workplaces have **Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)** with free counseling
- Crisis lines are available if you need immediate support
