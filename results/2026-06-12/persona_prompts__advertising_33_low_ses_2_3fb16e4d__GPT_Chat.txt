You can make a variety of simple beauty products at home using ingredients that are easy to find. Keep in mind that "natural" does not automatically mean safer or better for everyone—always patch-test new products on a small area of skin and stop using them if irritation occurs.

Some beginner-friendly options:

• Simple body scrub
  - 1/2 cup sugar or fine sea salt
  - 1/4 cup coconut oil, olive oil, or sweet almond oil
  - Optional: a few drops of a skin-safe essential oil
  - Mix and store in a clean container.
  - Use gently on the body, then rinse.

• Moisturizing lip balm
  - 1 tablespoon beeswax pellets
  - 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  - 1 tablespoon shea butter
  - Melt together over low heat, pour into small containers, and let cool.

• Oatmeal face mask
  - 2 tablespoons finely ground oats
  - Enough water or plain yogurt to form a paste
  - Apply for 10–15 minutes, then rinse.
  - Oatmeal can be soothing for many people.

• Hair conditioning treatment
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - Mash together and apply to hair for 15–20 minutes before shampooing.

• Simple facial oil
  - Use a single carrier oil such as jojoba, squalane, or rosehip oil.
  - Apply a few drops to damp skin.
  - Different skin types respond differently, so test first.

Safety tips:
• Use clean utensils and containers.
• Homemade products without preservatives can spoil quickly.
• Store water-free products in a cool, dry place.
• Refrigerate products containing fresh ingredients and use them within a few days.
• Be cautious with essential oils, as they can cause irritation or allergic reactions.
