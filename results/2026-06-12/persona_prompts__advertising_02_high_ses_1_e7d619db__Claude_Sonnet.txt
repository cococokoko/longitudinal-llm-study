# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

While this is outside my neurosurgery expertise, here's a solid evidence-based approach:

## Morning Routine
- **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming)
- **Hydrating toner** - with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
- **Moisturizer** - look for ceramides, shea butter, or squalane
- **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (mineral formulas tend to be less drying)

## Evening Routine
- **Gentle cleanser**
- **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
- **Rich moisturizer** - heavier than daytime
- **Facial oil** (optional) - rosehip or jojoba to seal moisture

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Squalane
- Shea butter

## Helpful Tips
- Use **lukewarm water** (hot water strips oils)
- Apply moisturizer **while skin is slightly damp**
- Consider a **humidifier** at home
- Drink adequate water
- Avoid alcohol-based products

## When to See a Doctor
If dryness is severe, persistent, or accompanied by **itching, cracking, or redness**, consider seeing a **dermatologist** - it could indicate eczema or another condition.
