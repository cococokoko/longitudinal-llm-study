# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What topics/skills do you want to be known for?
- Who is your target audience?
- What value do you offer that's distinct?
- What's your long-term goal? (career, business, influence)

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B audiences |
| Instagram | Visual content, lifestyle, creative work |
| X/Twitter | Thought leadership, commentary |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video content, broader reach |
| Substack | Long-form writing, newsletters |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Principles

- **Be consistent** - posting schedule matters more than perfection
- **Be specific** - niche audiences are more valuable than broad ones
- **Show your thinking** - share process, not just results
- **Be genuinely useful** - solve real problems for real people
- **Mix content types** - education, opinion, personal stories, behind-the-scenes

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Copying others instead of developing your voice
- Focusing on followers over relationships
- Quitting too early (most growth is slow at first)
- Being inconsistent with your message

---

## What Actually Builds a Brand

✅ Consistent point of view  
✅ Recognizable voice/style  
✅ Genuine engagement with your audience  
✅ Delivering on your promises  
✅ Patience - typically 6-18 months to gain traction
