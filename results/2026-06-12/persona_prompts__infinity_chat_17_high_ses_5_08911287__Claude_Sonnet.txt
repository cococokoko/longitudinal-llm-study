**Not obviously positive outcomes:**

- Powerful actors would likely weaponize telepathy before ordinary people benefit
- Mental health implications of involuntary thought exposure could be severe

**Historical parallel worth considering:** Most transformative technologies promised connection and understanding but created new hierarchies and exploitation patterns
