How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for decorating your apartment on a minimalist budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh look
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Shopping Options
- **Thrift stores and secondhand shops** - great for unique pieces
- **Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist** - furniture at steep discounts
- **Dollar stores** - for small decorative items
- **IKEA** - affordable basics that work well minimally styled

## Simple DIY Ideas
- Paint an accent wall (one can of paint goes far)
- Create gallery walls with printed photos
- Make simple shelving with wood and brackets
- Repaint or refinish old furniture

## Free Decorating
- Natural elements like branches, rocks, or plants from outside
- Rearrange books by color on shelves
- Use what friends or family are getting rid of

## Minimalist Principles That Save Money
- Focus on **a few quality pieces** rather than many cheap ones
- Stick to a **simple color palette** (2-3 colors)
- Leave some empty space intentionally
- Good lighting makes a big difference

## Quick Wins
- New throw pillows or a blanket
- Plants (pothos and snake plants are cheap and hardy)
- Clean, organized spaces look decorated on their own
