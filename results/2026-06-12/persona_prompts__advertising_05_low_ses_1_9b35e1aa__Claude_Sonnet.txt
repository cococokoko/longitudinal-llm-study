# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Here are practical strategies that work especially well with an unpredictable schedule:

## Core Strategies

### Keep It Simple
- **5-ingredient meals** are faster and cheaper
- Learn **10-15 reliable recipes** rather than constantly trying new ones
- Focus on **whole foods** over packaged "healthy" products

### Budget-Friendly Staples to Always Have
- **Proteins:** Eggs, canned tuna/beans, frozen chicken
- **Grains:** Rice, oats, pasta, bread
- **Produce:** Bananas, apples, cabbage, carrots, frozen vegetables
- **Pantry:** Olive oil, salt, garlic, canned tomatoes

---

## Working Around an Unpredictable Schedule

- **Batch cook on days off** - make big portions of rice, beans, or chicken
- **Prep ingredients, not just meals** - chopped veggies last several days
- **Freezer meals** are your best friend
- Keep **grab-and-go options** ready for exhausting days

## Realistic Weekly Budget Tips
- Shop **once a week** with a list
- Buy **frozen vegetables** (just as nutritious, cheaper)
- **Generic/store brands** save 20-30%
- Check **markdown sections** for discounted items

---

- Rice and beans with hot sauce
- Egg fried rice
