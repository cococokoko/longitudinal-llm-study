# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- Compare prices on multiple sites (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Be **flexible with dates** — midweek flights are often cheaper
- Set price alerts for flights you're watching
- Use **incognito mode** when searching to avoid price increases

## Flights
- Fly into smaller/alternate airports
- Look for **budget airlines**
- Use **travel rewards credit cards** and points
- Consider connecting flights over direct routes
- Pack light to **avoid baggage fees**

## Accommodation
- Use **Airbnb, Hostelworld, or VRBO** instead of hotels
- Stay slightly outside city centers
- Consider **house swapping or couchsurfing**
- Book refundable rates and watch for price drops

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation** instead of taxis/rideshares
- Cook some meals instead of eating out every time
- Look for **free attractions** (museums, parks, walking tours)
- Get a local SIM card to avoid roaming charges
- Travel during **shoulder season** (just before/after peak times)

## Loyalty & Rewards
- Join airline and hotel loyalty programs
- Use points/miles strategically
- Look for **travel credit card perks** (lounge access, no foreign fees)
