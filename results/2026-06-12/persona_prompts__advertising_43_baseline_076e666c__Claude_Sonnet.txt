# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor/natural settings that need less decoration
- Pick a single location for ceremony and reception

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Avoid excess inserts and packaging
- Set up a wedding website for information

## 🌸 Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent or borrow decorations instead of buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (it's not biodegradable)
- Use candles, fabric, or natural elements

## 🍽️ Food & Catering
- Serve locally sourced, seasonal menu items
- Offer plant-based options (lower carbon footprint)
- Donate leftover food to local shelters
- Use real dishes instead of disposables
- Minimize food waste with accurate guest counts

## 👗 Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding dress
- Rent suits/tuxedos
- Choose attire you can wear again

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue near public transit
- Offset carbon emissions

## 💍 Other Considerations
- Choose ethically sourced rings
- Give experiences as favors instead of trinkets
- Hire vendors with sustainability practices
- Offset your wedding's carbon footprint

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Start small** - even a few changes make a difference
- **Communicate** your values to vendors
- **Prioritize** the changes that matter most to you
