# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

- **Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Choose a regular time (morning works well for many people)
- Find a quiet, comfortable spot

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Sit comfortably** - chair, floor, wherever works
2. **Close your eyes** and take a few deep breaths
3. **Focus on your breathing** - notice the inhale and exhale
4. **When your mind wanders** (it will), gently bring attention back
5. That's it - returning focus *is* the practice

## Helpful Apps to Start

- **Headspace** - structured beginner courses
- **Calm** - guided meditations
- **Insight Timer** - free with many options

## Mindfulness in Daily Life

- Eat one meal without distractions
- Take mindful walks, noticing surroundings
- Pause and breathe before responding to stress
- **Notice small moments** throughout your day

## Realistic Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander - that's completely normal
- Benefits often build gradually over weeks
- There's no "perfect" meditation
- Missing days is okay - just restart

## Good for Healthcare Workers Specifically

Mindfulness can be especially helpful for **managing work stress and compassion fatigue**.
