# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks** – core strength
- **Burpees** – full body + cardio
- **Dips** – using a chair or bench

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Jump rope
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Dancing or aerobics videos

## Home Equipment Worth Considering
| Item | Cost | Benefit |
|------|------|---------|
| Resistance bands | Low | Versatile strength training |
| Dumbbells | Moderate | Progressive overload |
| Pull-up bar | Low | Upper body strength |
| Yoga mat | Low | Comfort for floor work |

## Everyday Activity
- Take stairs instead of elevators
- Walk or bike for errands
- Active hobbies (hiking, sports, gardening)

## Key Principles
- **Consistency** matters more than intensity
- **Progressive overload** – gradually increase difficulty
- **Rest and recovery** are essential
- **Nutrition** supports your fitness goals

## Free Resources
- YouTube workout channels
- Fitness apps (Nike Training Club, etc.)
- Online communities for accountability
