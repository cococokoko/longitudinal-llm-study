- **Designate a workspace** - even a corner with a specific chair signals "work mode"

- **Work during natural quiet windows** - nap times, early mornings, or after bedtime
- Block your most demanding tasks when kids are most settled

- Prepare independent activities for children before starting focused work
- Accept that some interruptions are unavoidable and plan accordingly

## Staying Focused

- Keep a running task list so you can quickly resume after interruptions
- Batch similar tasks together

## Practical Daily Habits
- Prepare the night before (lunches, clothes, work priorities)
- Set clear start and end times to prevent burnout
- **Ask for help** - family, neighbors, childcare swaps with other parents
