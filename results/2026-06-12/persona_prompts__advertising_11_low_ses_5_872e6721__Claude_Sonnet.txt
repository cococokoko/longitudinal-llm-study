# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises at Home

**Strength Training**
- Push-ups (many variations for different difficulty levels)
- Squats and lunges
- Planks and core work
- Dips using a chair
- Glute bridges

**Cardio Options**
- Jumping jacks, burpees, high knees
- Jump rope (inexpensive and effective)
- Dancing to music
- Stair climbing if available

---

## Outside the House

- **Walking/jogging** in the neighborhood
- **Cycling** for errands or recreation
- **Park workouts** using benches and open space
- **Sports** with family or friends

---

## Practical Tips for Busy People

- **Break it up** - three 10-minute sessions work as well as one 30-minute workout
- **Involve the family** - walks, active games, backyard activities
- **Use what you have** - water jugs as weights, stairs for cardio
- **Morning routines** - exercise before the day gets busy
- **Consistency over intensity** - regular movement matters most

---

## Low-Cost Equipment Worth Considering
- Resistance bands
- Jump rope
- Yoga mat

**The biggest factor is simply moving regularly** - even 20-30 minutes most days makes a significant difference.
