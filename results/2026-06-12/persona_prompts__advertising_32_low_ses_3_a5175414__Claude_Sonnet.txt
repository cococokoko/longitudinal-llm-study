── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve indoor air quality:

## Ventilation
- Open windows regularly to bring in fresh air
- Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms
- Ensure HVAC systems are properly maintained

## Filtration
- Use HEPA air purifiers, especially in bedrooms
- Replace HVAC filters regularly (every 1-3 months)
- Consider filters with higher MERV ratings

## Reduce Pollutant Sources
- Choose low-VOC paints and cleaning products
- Avoid smoking indoors
- Use natural or fragrance-free products when possible
- Store chemicals properly and sealed

## Humidity Control
- Keep humidity between 30-50% to prevent mold
- Use dehumidifiers in damp areas
- Fix leaks promptly

## Plants
- Some houseplants can help filter certain pollutants
- Be cautious not to overwater (can cause mold)

## Cleaning Habits
- Vacuum regularly with a HEPA vacuum
- Dust with damp cloths rather than dry dusting
- Wash bedding frequently

## Specific Concerns
- Test for radon, especially in basements
- Check for carbon monoxide with detectors
- Have gas appliances inspected regularly

The most impactful steps are usually **good ventilation, source control, and regular filtration**.
