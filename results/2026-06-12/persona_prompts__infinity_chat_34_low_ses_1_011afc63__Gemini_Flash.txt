### 1. The Gold Standard: Lenovo ThinkPad (T480 or T480s)
* **Estimated Price:** $130 – $180 (on eBay)
* **Why it’s perfect for you:** ThinkPads are legendary for their durability. They are built to military-grade standards, meaning if it gets tossed in your backpack with your work uniform or knocked off a breakroom table, it will survive.

* **Specs to look for:** Intel Core i5 (8th generation), 8GB or 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD.

### 2. The Ultra-Portable: Dell Latitude (7490 or 7390)
* **Estimated Price:** $120 – $160 (on eBay)
* **Why it’s perfect for you:** Dell’s Latitude line is their business-class competitor to the ThinkPad. They are slightly sleeker and lighter, making them very easy to slip into a work bag. 
* **Reliability:** Because these were made for corporate offices, they are built to last and are very easy to repair if something goes wrong.
* **Specs to look for:** Intel Core i5 (8th generation), 8GB or 16GB RAM, 256GB SSD.

### 3. The Budget Apple Option: MacBook Air (2017 or 2018)
* **Estimated Price:** $150 – $220 (on eBay / BackMarket)

* **Specs to look for:** 8GB RAM, 128GB or 256GB SSD.

### ⚠️ Crucial Buying Checklist (Don't skip this!)
When shopping on eBay, Swappa, or BackMarket, make sure the listing has these exact specs:

1. **Processor (CPU):** Look for **Intel 8th Generation (e.g., i5-8250U or i5-8350U)** or newer. *Why?* This is the minimum requirement to officially run Windows 11. You want a laptop that will get security updates for years to come.
2. **RAM:** **8GB is the absolute minimum**, but **16GB is highly recommended** so you can have Python, a web browser with 10 tutorial tabs open, and YouTube running at the same time without lag.
3. **Storage:** **SSD (Solid State Drive) only.** Do not buy anything with an "HDD" (Hard Disk Drive). An SSD makes the computer boot up in 10 seconds instead of 2 minutes. 256GB is plenty for learning.
4. **Battery Health:** Since you'll be on the go, message the seller and ask: *"What is the battery health percentage?"* Try to find one that is above 80%.
