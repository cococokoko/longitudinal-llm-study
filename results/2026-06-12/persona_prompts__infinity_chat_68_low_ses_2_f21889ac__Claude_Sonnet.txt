## Communicate Clearly and Consistently

**Set clear expectations from the start.** Make sure your team knows:
- What their specific responsibilities are
- Deadlines and priorities
- How you prefer to communicate (email, meetings, quick messages)

**Why this helps:** When people know exactly what's expected, there's less confusion, fewer mistakes, and less time spent on back-and-forth clarification.

**A simple habit:** Start each week with a brief check-in - even just 10-15 minutes - so everyone knows the priorities and can flag any obstacles early.

This saves time in the long run and helps the whole team work more smoothly together.
