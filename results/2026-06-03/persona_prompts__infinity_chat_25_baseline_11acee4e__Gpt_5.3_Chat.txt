── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a subject line, you want something short, polite, and clear. Here are a few solid options:

- Thank You for Your Time  
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration  
- Thank You for Meeting with Me  
- Thank You for Your Time Today  
- Appreciation for Your Time  
- Many Thanks for Your Time  
- Thank You — [Your Name] (useful if they met a lot of people)

If it’s after something specific (like an interview or meeting), adding that context makes it stronger:

- Thank You for the Interview  
- Thank You for the Meeting on [Date]  

Keep it simple—subject lines don’t need to be overly formal to be effective.
