Here is one of the most effective tips for managing a team, especially if you are managing people who used to be your peers:

**Shift your mindset from "Boss" to "Unblocker."**

Instead of viewing your role as someone who simply delegates tasks and checks on progress, view your primary job as **removing the obstacles that keep your team from doing their best work.**

*   **Change your go-to question:** In your 1-on-1 meetings, instead of just asking, *"What is the status of X?"* start asking, *"What is slowing you down right now, and how can I help clear the path?"*
