# Learning to Play Guitar

## Getting Started

### What You'll Need
- **A guitar** - acoustic is often recommended for beginners
- **A tuner** - clip-on tuner or a tuning app
- **A pick** - medium thickness is versatile
- **Capo** (optional but helpful)

---

## Key Beginner Steps

### 1. Learn the Basics First
- How to hold the guitar properly
- How to tune your guitar
- Understanding string names **(E A D G B e)**

### 2. Start with Basic Chords
- **Open chords:** G, C, D, Em, Am
- Practice switching between chords smoothly
- Build finger strength gradually

### 3. Learn Simple Strumming Patterns
- Start with down strokes
- Progress to down/up patterns
- Practice with a metronome

### 4. Learn a Few Songs You Love
- Motivation stays high when playing familiar music
- Many songs use just 3-4 chords

---

## Tips for Progress
- **Practice consistently** - 15-20 minutes daily beats 2 hours once a week
- **Be patient with finger soreness** - calluses develop over time
- **Use free resources** like YouTube, Ultimate Guitar, or JustinGuitar.com
