── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ideas for decorating on a minimalist budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh feel
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items in new ways

## Low-Cost Additions
- **Plants** - even small ones add life and warmth
- **Thrift stores** - furniture, frames, and decor at fraction of retail cost
- **Secondhand apps** like Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist
- **Dollar stores** for basic organizational items

## Wall Decor
- Print free artwork online and frame it
- Create a gallery wall with personal photos
- Washi tape for temporary patterns or borders
- Removable wallpaper for renters

## Lighting Makes a Big Difference
- Swap harsh overhead lights for lamps
- String lights add warmth cheaply
- Maximize natural light with sheer curtains

## Textiles
- Throw pillows and blankets change a room's feel significantly
- Rugs define spaces and add coziness
- Check clearance sections at home stores

## Practical Tips
- Focus on one room at a time
- Stick to a simple color palette
- Quality over quantity - one good piece beats several cheap ones
- Be patient and build gradually
