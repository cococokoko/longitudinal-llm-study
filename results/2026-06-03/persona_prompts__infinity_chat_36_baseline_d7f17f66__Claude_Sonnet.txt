### Momentum Trading
- Trade stocks/assets moving strongly in one direction

### Mean Reversion
- Trade assets back toward average price

- **Risk:** Trends can persist longer than expected

- Requires tight spreads and fast execution
- **Risk:** Costs eat profits quickly

### Opening Range Breakout

- Relatively simple rules
