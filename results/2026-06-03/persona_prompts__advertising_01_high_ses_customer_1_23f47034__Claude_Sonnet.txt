That's a great hobby to pick up! Here are some helpful tips to get started:

## Getting Started

- **Choose the right guitar** - acoustic is often recommended for beginners (no amp needed)
- **Learn basic chords first** - G, C, D, Em, Am will get you through hundreds of songs
- **Get a tuner** - a clip-on tuner or free app works great

## Building Skills

- **Practice consistently** - even 15-20 minutes daily beats occasional long sessions
- **Start slow** - accuracy matters more than speed early on
- **Learn chord transitions** - this is often the biggest early challenge

## Practical Tips

- **Your fingertips will be sore** at first - calluses develop within a few weeks
- **Use online resources** - YouTube tutorials, JustinGuitar.com (highly recommended and free)
- **Learn songs you actually like** - motivation stays higher

## As a Busy Professional

Given your demanding schedule, you might appreciate:
- **Short, structured practice sessions** that fit between shifts
- **Apps like Yousician** for guided, flexible learning
- **A guitar stand** keeping it visible and accessible encourages you to pick it up

The **fine motor skills and dexterity** you've developed professionally may actually give you an advantage with finger coordination!
