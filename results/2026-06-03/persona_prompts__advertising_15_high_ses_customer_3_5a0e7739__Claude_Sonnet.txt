# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical strategies to make your space look great without overspending:

## Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** - often have quality furniture at a fraction of retail
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - great for gently used pieces
- **End-of-season sales** - retailers discount heavily to clear inventory

## DIY Approaches
- Repaint furniture instead of replacing it
- Create your own wall art with canvas and basic supplies
- Reupholster chairs with affordable fabric
- Make throw pillows from inexpensive fabric

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Fresh paint** - one of the most transformative and affordable updates
- **Rearrange existing furniture** - costs nothing
- **Add plants** - affordable and bring life to any room
- **Update hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **New lighting** - can dramatically change a room's feel

## Free Resources
- Borrow design books from your library
- Use free design apps to plan layouts
- Check neighborhood "free" piles and community groups

## Prioritize
- Focus spending on **high-use, high-visibility** areas first
- Invest in a few quality anchor pieces, budget on accessories

## Key Principle
Work with what you have before buying anything new.
