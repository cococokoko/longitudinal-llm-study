# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Smart Strategies to Get Started

- **Set a realistic budget** before you begin shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most often
- **Plan before you buy** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Shop sales and clearance** sections regularly

---

## Budget-Friendly Decorating Ideas

### 🎨 Paint
- One of the **biggest impact, lowest cost** changes you can make
- Refresh walls, furniture, or cabinets
- Costs roughly $30–$50 per room

### 🛋️ Thrift & Secondhand Shopping
- Thrift stores, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist
- Estate sales and garage sales
- Habitat for Humanity ReStores

### 🌿 Add Plants
- Affordable and bring life to any space
- Low-maintenance options like pothos or snake plants

### 🖼️ DIY Art & Decor
- Frame fabric, wallpaper samples, or personal photos
- Create gallery walls with mixed frames

### 💡 Update Lighting
- Swap outdated fixtures for modern ones
- Add lamps to create warmth and ambiance

### 🔄 Rearrange What You Have
- **Costs nothing** and can completely transform a space

---

## Places to Shop Affordably
- IKEA
- Target clearance
- Amazon
- Dollar stores
- TJ Maxx / HomeGoods

---

## Key Tip
> **Less is often more.** A few well-chosen pieces look better than a cluttered space full of cheap items.
