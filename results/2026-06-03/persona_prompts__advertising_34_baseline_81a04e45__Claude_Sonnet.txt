# Effective Online Teaching Techniques

## Engagement Strategies
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minute chunks work better than long lectures)
- **Use interactive polls and quizzes** (Mentimeter, Kahoot, Poll Everywhere)
- **Ask frequent questions** rather than lecturing continuously
- **Incorporate multimedia** - videos, infographics, simulations

## Building Community
- Create structured discussion forums with clear expectations
- Use breakout rooms for small group collaboration
- Establish regular office hours and be responsive
- Encourage peer-to-peer interaction, not just student-to-teacher

## Clear Communication
- Post detailed instructions and rubrics upfront
- Use multiple formats (written + video explanations)
- Establish consistent communication channels
- Set clear expectations about response times

## Assessment Approaches
- Use varied assessment types (projects, discussions, quizzes)
- Provide timely, specific feedback
- Consider open-book or application-based assessments
- Use formative checks throughout, not just summative tests

## Technical Considerations
- Keep technology simple and accessible
- Have backup plans for technical failures
- Ensure content works on mobile devices
- Record sessions when possible

## What Research Suggests Works Best
- **Active learning** consistently outperforms passive watching
- **Regular low-stakes feedback** improves retention
- **Social presence** significantly affects student satisfaction
