# CUBICLE OF THE DAMNED
### A Play in Three Acts
*In the Style of Scott Adams*

---

## DRAMATIS PERSONAE

**DILBERT** — An engineer, still wearing his tie, which now curls upward from radiation  
**DOGBERT** — A small dog with delusions of godhood, which are now largely justified  
**THE POINTY-HAIRED BOSS (PHB)** — Somehow still employed. Somehow still pointy-haired.  
**ALICE** — Engineer. Fist of Death now literally glows.  
**WALLY** — Has not noticed the apocalypse. Still has coffee.  
**ASOK** — Intern. Has survived things that would destroy lesser men, yet still cannot get a parking pass.  
**CATBERT** — Evil HR Director. Thriving.  

---

## ACT ONE: THE SYNERGY OF EXTINCTION

*A burned-out office building. Three walls remain. The fourth opens onto a smoldering wasteland. Mutant creatures howl in the distance. A motivational poster reading "HANG IN THERE" dangles from a single screw. DILBERT sits at his desk, which is partially melted. He is on a conference call.*

**DILBERT:**
...so if I understand the new requirements correctly, you want the shelter to support ten thousand survivors, be completed by Friday, use no materials that currently exist, and come in under the budget of a medium pizza?

**VOICE ON SPEAKERPHONE:**
And it needs to be scalable.

**DILBERT:**
*(writing this down)*
Scalable. Right.

**VOICE ON SPEAKERPHONE:**
And synergistic.

**DILBERT:**
With what?

**VOICE ON SPEAKERPHONE:**
With itself.

*(The call disconnects. A mushroom cloud blooms pleasantly in the distance. DILBERT stares at it.)*

**DILBERT:**
That's actually the most reasonable thing I've seen today.

*(PHB enters, stepping over a skeleton in a business casual outfit.)*

**PHB:**
Dilbert! Great news. Corporate has decided that the apocalypse represents a significant market opportunity.

**DILBERT:**
Corporate is gone, sir. They were vaporized in the first wave.

**PHB:**
Their spirit lives on in their quarterly objectives.

**DILBERT:**
Their quarterly objectives included "leverage AI to disrupt disruption."

**PHB:**
*(nodding slowly)*
They were visionaries.
