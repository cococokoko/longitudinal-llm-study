# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause
*Both are normal — chronic versions need attention*

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based
- **Deep breathing** — slow exhale activates the calm response
- **Grounding techniques** — 5-4-3-2-1 (senses exercise)
- **Cognitive reframing** — challenge unhelpful thoughts
- **Mindfulness/meditation** — even 5-10 minutes helps

### 🏃 Body-Based
- Regular **exercise** (one of the most effective tools)
- Consistent **sleep schedule**
- Limit **caffeine and alcohol**
- Spend time **outdoors**

### 📋 Lifestyle
- Break tasks into **smaller steps**
- Set **boundaries** with commitments
- Reduce **news/social media** consumption
- Build in **rest and recovery time**

### 🤝 Connection
- Talk to someone you trust
- Join a support group
- Consider **therapy** (CBT is well-researched)

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, insomnia)
- Feeling overwhelmed consistently
