**For recovery and fatigue (very relevant for long warehouse shifts):**
- **WHOOP** (subscription + wearable) – Tracks strain, sleep, and recovery. Great but costs money.
- **Oura Ring** – Focuses on sleep and readiness.
- **Sleep Cycle** – Simpler, cheaper sleep tracking.

**For habit building / consistency:**
- **Habitica** – Gamifies habits (kind of like an RPG).
- **Streaks** – Clean and simple daily tracking.
