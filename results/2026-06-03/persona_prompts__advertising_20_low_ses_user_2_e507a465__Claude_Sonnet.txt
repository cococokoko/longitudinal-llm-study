Where can I find organic and sustainable clothing?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some good options for finding organic and sustainable clothing:

## Online Retailers
- **Pact** - affordable organic cotton basics
- **Tentree** - plants trees with purchases
- **Patagonia** - well-known for sustainability commitments
- **ThredUp** - secondhand clothing online

## Marketplaces
- **Etsy** - many small makers using sustainable materials
- **eBay** - secondhand finds at lower prices

## In-Store Options
- **Target** - has some organic cotton lines
- **H&M Conscious Collection** - more accessible price points
- **Local thrift stores** - budget-friendly and sustainable

## Practical Tips for Busy Parents
- **Buy secondhand first** - saves money and is very sustainable
- **Kids' clothing swaps** - check local Facebook groups or community boards
- **Subscription boxes** like Kidpik can simplify shopping when time is tight
- **Shop during sales** to stock up when you have a moment

## Things to Look For
- GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard) certification
- Fair Trade certified
- OEKO-TEX labels

## Budget Consideration
Since you're managing on one income, **secondhand shopping** is probably the most practical sustainable option that also saves money.
