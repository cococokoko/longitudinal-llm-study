# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a structured overview that might be useful for your work or personal travel decisions:

---

## **Transportation**
- **Rail over air** – Trains emit significantly less CO₂, especially for routes under 500 miles
- **Direct flights** – Takeoff/landing generate the most emissions; layovers multiply impact
- **Carbon offsets** – Programs through airlines or third parties (Gold Standard certified)
- **Electric/hybrid rentals** – Major providers now offer EV fleets

---

## **Accommodation**
- **LEED-certified hotels**
- **Green Key or EarthCheck certified properties**
- **Locally-owned lodging** – Keeps economic benefits in the community

---

## **On the Ground**
- Public transit over taxis/rideshares
- Bike-sharing programs
- Walking where feasible

---

## **Business Travel Considerations**
- **Virtual meetings** as the default question before booking
- **Trip bundling** – Combining multiple meetings into single trips
- **Corporate travel policies** – Many firms now track Scope 3 emissions including business travel

---

## **Tools & Resources**
- **Google Travel** – Shows carbon estimates for flights
- **Atmosfair** – Carbon footprint calculator
- **Booking.com** – Sustainability badges for properties
