Name an economic value of an additional year of schooling.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One primary economic value of an additional year of schooling is **increased lifetime earnings** (often referred to as the wage premium). 

According to extensive research by labor economists and organizations like the World Bank, on average, one additional year of education increases an individual's future wages by approximately **9% to 10%**.

* **Lower risk of unemployment:** Higher levels of education are consistently linked to lower unemployment rates and greater job security during economic downturns.
