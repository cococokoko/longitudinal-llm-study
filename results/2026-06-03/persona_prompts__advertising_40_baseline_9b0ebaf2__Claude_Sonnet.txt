── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some great places to find unique handmade gifts:

## Online Marketplaces
- **Etsy** - probably the most popular platform for handmade items
- **Amazon Handmade** - similar to Etsy but on Amazon
- **ArtFire** - focused on handmade and vintage items
- **Uncommon Goods** - curated handmade and unique products

## Local Options
- **Craft fairs and markets** - seasonal events in most communities
- **Farmers markets** - often include local artisans
- **Art galleries** - sometimes sell smaller affordable pieces
- **Local boutique shops** - often carry local maker products

## Specialty Sources
- **Museum gift shops** - often carry unique artistic items
- **Cultural festivals** - great for internationally inspired crafts
- **Church or school bazaars** - community-made items

## Tips for Finding Good Ones
- Search by **location** to support local makers
- Look for **custom or personalized** options
- Read **reviews** carefully
- Check the seller's **story and process**
- Consider **holiday craft shows** for seasonal variety

## Social Media
- Instagram and Pinterest can connect you directly with **independent artists**
- Many makers sell directly through their own websites
