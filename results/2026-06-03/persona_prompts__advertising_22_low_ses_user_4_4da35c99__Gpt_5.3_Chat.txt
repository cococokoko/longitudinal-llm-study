- When I Work: built specifically for shift-based teams

- Google Calendar / Outlook: basic but works well for smaller teams

**For documentation and shared knowledge**
- Google Docs/Drive: easy real-time collaboration
- Microsoft OneDrive/SharePoint: better if you're in a Microsoft ecosystem
- Notion or Confluence: for keeping procedures, training info, and updates in one place

**For quick video or check-ins**
- Zoom: reliable and simple
- Microsoft Teams: again, good all-in-one if already in use
