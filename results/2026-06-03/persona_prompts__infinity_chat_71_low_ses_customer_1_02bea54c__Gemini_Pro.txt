One of the most common optimization techniques in machine learning is called **Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)**. 

Since you work full-time in fast food with a schedule that's always changing, here is a way to think about it that might feel very familiar:

Imagine you are trying to find the absolute most efficient way to clear a massive, chaotic lunch rush (in machine learning, this goal is called "minimizing the error").
