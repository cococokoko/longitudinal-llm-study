# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are some practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor or naturally lit spaces to reduce energy use

## Invitations
- Send **digital invitations** or use recycled paper
- Use plantable seed paper for physical invites
- Minimize printed materials overall

## Food & Catering
- Source **locally grown, seasonal food**
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decor
- Choose **locally grown, seasonal flowers**
- Rent or borrow decorations
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid single-use plastics

## Attire
- Consider **vintage or secondhand** wedding attire
- Rent suits/tuxedos
- Choose dresses that can be reworn

## Transportation
- Arrange **group shuttles** for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
- Offset carbon emissions

## Favors & Registry
- Give **experience-based or charitable** favors
- Register for experiences rather than things
- Choose sustainable product brands

## Waste Management
- Set up clear recycling and composting stations
- Use compostable tableware if needed
