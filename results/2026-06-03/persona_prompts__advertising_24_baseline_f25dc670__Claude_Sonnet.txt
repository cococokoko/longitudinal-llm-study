What are the best ways to save for retirement?

# Best Ways to Save for Retirement

## Tax-Advantaged Accounts (Start Here)

**Employer-Sponsored Plans**
- **401(k) or 403(b)** - contribute enough to get full employer match (it's free money)
- 2024 contribution limit: $23,000 ($30,500 if 50+)

**Individual Retirement Accounts**
- **Traditional IRA** - tax deduction now, pay taxes in retirement
- **Roth IRA** - pay taxes now, tax-free withdrawals later
- 2024 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+)

## General Principles

- **Start early** - compound interest rewards time significantly
- **Be consistent** - automate contributions
- **Increase contributions** when income rises
- **Avoid early withdrawals** - penalties plus lost growth

## Investment Approach

- Diversify across stocks, bonds, and other assets
- Consider low-cost index funds
- Adjust risk level as you get closer to retirement
- Keep fees low

## Other Options

- HSA accounts (triple tax advantage if used for healthcare)
- Real estate
- Taxable brokerage accounts after maxing tax-advantaged options

## Key Factors to Consider

- Your current tax bracket
- Expected retirement age
- Employer benefits available
- Income limits for certain accounts
