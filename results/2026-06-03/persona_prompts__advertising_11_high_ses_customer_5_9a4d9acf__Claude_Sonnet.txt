# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are practical, efficient approaches that fit a busy executive schedule:

## High-Impact Options

**Bodyweight Training**
- Push-ups, pull-ups, squats, lunges, planks
- 20-30 minutes delivers real results
- No equipment needed

**Running/Walking**
- Easy to fit around travel
- Morning runs are a common executive habit
- Combines cardio with thinking time

**HIIT Workouts**
- 15-20 minutes, high efficiency
- Apps like Nike Training Club or Peloton (no bike needed)

## Practical Strategies

- **Hotel room workouts** - consistent routine while traveling
- **Walking meetings** - productive and active
- **Stairs over elevators** - simple daily habit
- **Desk stretching/standing desk** - reduces sedentary time

## Minimal Equipment Worth Having at Home
- Resistance bands (~$20)
- Kettlebell (one versatile weight)
- Pull-up bar (doorframe)

## Structure That Works
| Goal | Approach |
|------|----------|
| Strength | 3x/week bodyweight circuits |
| Cardio | Daily 30-min walks/runs |
| Flexibility | 10-min morning mobility |

## Key Principle
**Consistency beats intensity.** Short, regular sessions outperform occasional long workouts.
