── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for minimalist apartment decorating on a budget:

## Core Principles
- **Less is more** - focus on a few quality pieces rather than lots of clutter
- Choose a simple, cohesive color palette (2-3 colors works well)

## Budget-Friendly Strategies

**Repurpose what you have**
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh feel
- Declutter first - removing things often improves a space dramatically

**Affordable sources**
- Thrift stores and Facebook Marketplace for furniture
- IKEA for clean, simple pieces
- Dollar stores for basic organizational items

**High-impact, low-cost touches**
- Plants add life without visual clutter
- Simple white or neutral curtains brighten rooms
- Mirrors create the illusion of more space
- Remove unnecessary items from surfaces

**DIY options**
- Frame simple prints or photos
- Create gallery walls with consistent frames
- Use washi tape for temporary wall designs

## Smart Priorities
- Invest slightly more in one or two focal pieces
- Keep surfaces clear
- Good lighting makes a huge difference (swap bulbs for warmer tones)

## Free Options
- Borrow or swap items with friends
- Check "free" sections on Craigslist or neighborhood apps
