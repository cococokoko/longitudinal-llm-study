Here are some practical tips for decorating on a tight budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh feel
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks more intentional
- Clean and polish what you already own

## Low-Cost Decorating Ideas
- **Thrift stores and Facebook Marketplace** for furniture and decor
- **Dollar stores** for basic frames, candles, and small accents
- **DIY art** - simple prints, painted canvases, or framed photos
- **Plants** - even small succulents add life and are inexpensive

## Free Options
- Swap items with friends or family
- Download and print free art from sites like Unsplash
- Collect interesting rocks, branches, or pinecones for natural decor
- Rearrange books by color for visual interest

## Smart Shopping
- Check clearance sections at Target or IKEA
- Watch for seasonal sales
- Buy secondhand whenever possible
- Focus on one room at a time rather than everything at once

## Practical Priorities
- Good lighting makes a huge difference (inexpensive string lights work well)
- A few throw pillows can transform a couch
- Mirrors make spaces feel larger

**Given your schedule**, shopping online during breaks or using apps like OfferUp lets you browse without needing dedicated shopping time.
