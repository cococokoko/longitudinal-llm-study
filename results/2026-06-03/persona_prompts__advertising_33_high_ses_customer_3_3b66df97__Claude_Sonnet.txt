# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options with simple recipes:

## Skincare

**Basic Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, and cool in a clean jar

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil + lemon juice
- Mix and store in an airtight container

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (simmer rose petals, strain, cool)

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioner**
- Avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil + tea tree oil
- Massage in, leave overnight

## Lip Care

**Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Key Tips

- **Sanitize** all equipment and containers thoroughly
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- **Label** with ingredients and date made
- Most products last **1-4 weeks** without preservatives
- Store in **cool, dark places**
- Research any essential oils before use (some cause irritation)
