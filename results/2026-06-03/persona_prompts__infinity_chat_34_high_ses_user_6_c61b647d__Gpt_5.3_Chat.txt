- Models: 7490, 7400, 7410
- Why: understated, solid build, widely available from corporate resale

**HP EliteBook (800 series)**
- Models: 840 G5, G6, G7
- Why: often the best value in bulk resale markets, decent screens, solid performance

**If you’re open to macOS:**
- MacBook Air M1 (used/refurbished) — this is the outlier
- Why: extremely fast for Python, silent, amazing battery life
- Downside: usually pricier than the Windows options, less upgradeable

- CPU: Intel i5/i7 8th gen or newer (or AMD Ryzen 4000+)
- RAM: 16 GB preferred (8 GB is okay but you’ll outgrow it quickly)
- Storage: 256 GB SSD minimum (512 GB ideal)
- Screen: 1080p minimum (avoid older 1366×768 panels)

---
