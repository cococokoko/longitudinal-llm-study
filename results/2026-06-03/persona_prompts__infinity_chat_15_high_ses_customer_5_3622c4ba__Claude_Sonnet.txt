- Realists focus on relative power regardless of economic development levels

**Honest complications:**

- Economic interdependence sometimes constrains powerful nations too
- Inequality within countries may matter as much as between them for stability

- Whether China's rise represents system change or adaptation
- How much inequality drives conflict versus other factors
