## Economic Value of an Additional Year of Schooling

A clear economic value is **increased earning potential / higher wages**.

### How this applies to your situation:
In healthcare, an additional year of schooling could allow you to advance from a **patient care aide** to a role like:
- Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN)
- Medical Assistant (certified)
- Phlebotomist

Each step typically comes with a **measurable salary increase**.

### The broader concept:
This is often called the **"returns to education"** - economists estimate that each additional year of schooling can increase earnings by roughly **8-13%** on average.

This is also related to **human capital theory** - the idea that investing in education increases your productivity and market value as a worker.
