# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Here's a practical guide to help you begin:

## Start Simple
- **Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Choose a regular time (morning often works well before the day gets busy)
- Find a quiet, comfortable spot

## Basic Meditation Technique
1. Sit comfortably with your back relatively straight
2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze
3. Focus on your natural breathing
4. When your mind wanders, **gently return attention to your breath**
5. That's it - the returning is the practice

## Helpful Beginner Apps
- **Headspace** - structured beginner courses
- **Calm** - variety of guided sessions
- **Insight Timer** - free with thousands of options

## Types to Explore
- **Breath awareness** - simplest starting point
- **Body scan** - good for stress relief
- **Guided meditation** - helpful if you struggle alone
- **Mindful walking** - great if sitting feels difficult

## Realistic Expectations
- Your mind **will** wander - this is completely normal
- Benefits often build gradually over weeks
- Even "bad" sessions have value

## Quick Daily Mindfulness Tips
- Eat one meal without screens
- Take three conscious breaths before meetings
- Notice your surroundings during commutes
