Starting a new job is an exciting milestone, but being on probation can also bring a lot of underlying pressure, stress, and a desire to prove yourself. Here is a beginner-friendly guide to getting started, tailored to fit into a busy, transitional time in your life:

### 1. Understand the Difference
*   **Mindfulness** is the act of being fully present in whatever you are doing right now (e.g., actively listening to a colleague without planning what you'll say next).
*   **Meditation** is the dedicated practice or "workout" where you train your brain to be mindful.
