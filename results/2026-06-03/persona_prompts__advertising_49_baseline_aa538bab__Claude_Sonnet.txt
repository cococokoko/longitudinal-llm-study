# How to Improve Home WiFi

## Quick Fixes First
- **Restart your router** (unplug for 30 seconds)
- **Update router firmware**
- **Check for interference** from microwaves, cordless phones, baby monitors

---

## Router Placement
- Place it **centrally** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not floor)
- Avoid walls, cabinets, and metal objects
- Keep away from other electronics

---

## Settings to Adjust
- **Change WiFi channel** (use apps like WiFi Analyzer to find less crowded channels)
- **Use 5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby
- **Use 2.4GHz band** for longer range
- **Change DNS servers** (try Google 8.8.8.8 or Cloudflare 1.1.1.1)

---

## Hardware Upgrades
| Option | Best For |
|--------|----------|
| **WiFi extender/repeater** | Eliminating dead zones cheaply |
| **Mesh network system** | Large homes, seamless coverage |
| **New router** | Outdated equipment |
| **Powerline adapters** | Wired connection through outlets |

---

## Other Tips
- **Limit connected devices** when possible
- **Use ethernet cable** for gaming/streaming
- **Secure your network** so neighbors aren't using your bandwidth
- **Contact your ISP** if speeds are consistently low
