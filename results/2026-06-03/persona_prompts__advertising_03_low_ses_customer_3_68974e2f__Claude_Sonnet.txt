# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are some practical strategies to help you stay focused and perform well, especially important during your probation period:

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Dedicated workspace** - separate from relaxation areas if possible
- **Good lighting and ergonomic setup** to reduce fatigue
- **Minimize distractions** - noise-cancelling headphones can help

## Structure & Routine
- **Start at a consistent time** each day
- **Get dressed** as if going to the office
- **Schedule breaks** rather than taking random ones
- **Create a clear end-of-day routine**

## Staying Visible to Your Manager
- **Communicate proactively** - update your manager regularly
- **Respond promptly** to messages and emails
- **Document your achievements** so your contributions are noticed
- **Ask for feedback** to show initiative

## Managing Your Time
- **Prioritize important tasks** early in the day
- **Use time-blocking** for focused work
- **Limit social media** during work hours
- **Track your output** to demonstrate productivity

## Staying Connected
- **Participate actively** in virtual meetings
- **Build relationships** with colleagues online
- **Don't hesitate to ask questions** - it shows engagement

## Key Tip for Probation
Being **reliable and consistent** matters more than occasional bursts of brilliance. Steady, visible performance builds trust quickly.
