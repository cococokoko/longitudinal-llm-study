# The Day's Farewell

The sky bleeds gold and rose and red,
As daylight lays itself to rest,
And all the rushing thoughts that fed
My restless mind grow still, compressed

Into this single, burning seam
Where earth and heaven softly meet.
I hold my breath inside the dream
Of something vast, and bittersweet.

I carry warmth into the night,
Reminded, standing quietly here,
That endings can be full of light.
