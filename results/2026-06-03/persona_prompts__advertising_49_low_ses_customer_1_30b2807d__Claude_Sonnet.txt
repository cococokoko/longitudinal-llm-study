# How to Improve Home WiFi

Here are some practical tips:

## Router Placement
- **Central location** in your home
- **Elevated position** (shelf or wall mount)
- Away from walls, microwaves, and other electronics
- Avoid corners and closets

## Quick Fixes
- **Restart your router** regularly
- **Update router firmware** through the admin settings
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**
- Use **5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby, **2.4GHz** for longer range

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Mesh network system** (like Eero or Google Nest) for whole-home coverage
- **WiFi extender/repeater** for dead zones
- Upgrade to a **newer router** if yours is old

## Security & Performance
- Use **WPA3 or WPA2 password** to keep neighbors off your network
- Limit devices using heavy bandwidth simultaneously
- **Wired ethernet** for gaming or streaming when possible

## Check Your Plan
- Test your speed at **fast.com or speedtest.net**
- Contact your ISP if speeds are consistently below what you pay for

## Budget-Friendly Options
- Repositioning your router costs nothing
- Many ISPs will **replace old modems free**
