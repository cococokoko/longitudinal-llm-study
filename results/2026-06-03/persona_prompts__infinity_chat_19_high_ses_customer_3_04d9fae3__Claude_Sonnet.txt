- Potential benefit: reduced misunderstanding across languages/cultures

**Relationship formation**
- AI matchmaking becoming more sophisticated and dominant
- Possible displacement of organic relationship development
- Questions about whether AI-optimized relationships are actually better

## Genuine uncertainties worth acknowledging
