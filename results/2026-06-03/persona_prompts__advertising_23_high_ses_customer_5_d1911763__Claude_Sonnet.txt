- **Sheet pan meals** - roast protein and vegetables together at once
- **One-pot/one-pan cooking** reduces both cooking and cleanup time
- **Stir-frying** is fast and preserves nutrients

- Buy pre-cut vegetables or frozen vegetables (nutritionally comparable to fresh)
- Use rotisserie chicken as a protein base
- Keep hard-boiled eggs ready for quick protein
- Learn 5-6 reliable recipes really well rather than constantly trying new ones

- Start with simple recipes (3-5 ingredients)
- Watch cooking videos for technique
- Focus on flavor basics: acid, salt, fat, heat

- Sharp knives (dramatically speeds up prep)
- Food processor for chopping
- Instant Pot or air fryer
