# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition information
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical guide with recipes
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - affordable approach

## Websites & Organizations
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based, free information
- **PCRM.org** (Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine)
- **Oldwayspt.org** - cultural food traditions including plant-based patterns

## Apps
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-friendly restaurants
- **Forks Over Knives** - meal planning

## Practical Tips for Starting
- Start gradually rather than all at once
- Learn a few reliable recipes first
- Pay attention to **protein, B12, iron, and omega-3s**
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian**

## YouTube Channels
- Pick Up Limes
- Mic the Vegan (research-focused)
- Cheap Lazy Vegan

## Worth Noting
- Some resources have advocacy perspectives, so cross-referencing is helpful
- Individual nutritional needs vary
