# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a straightforward, effective routine:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
4. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (non-drying formula)

## Evening Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
3. **Rich moisturizer** - heavier than your daytime cream
4. **Facial oil** *(optional)* - squalane or rosehip oil to seal in moisture

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - repairs skin barrier
- **Glycerin** - humectant
- **Shea butter** - deep moisturizing

## Tips
- Use **lukewarm water** (hot water strips moisture)
- Apply moisturizer while skin is **slightly damp**
- Consider a **humidifier** at home
- **Drink plenty of water**

- CeraVe, La Roche-Posay, Neutrogena Hydro Boost
