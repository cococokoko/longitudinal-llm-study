── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn quick healthy cooking:

## Start with Simple Skills
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Master knife skills to speed up prep time
- Practice cooking proteins simply (baked chicken, pan-seared fish)

## Build Good Habits
- **Meal prep** on weekends or one day a week
- Keep a stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, frozen vegetables)
- Plan meals before shopping to avoid wasted time deciding

## Learn Efficiently
- Follow recipe websites like **Budget Bytes** or **Minimalist Baker**
- Watch YouTube channels (Joshua Weissman, Ethan Chlebowski cover healthy basics)
- Start with 15-30 minute recipes before attempting complex ones

## Focus on Reliable Meal Formulas
- Grain + protein + roasted vegetables
- Stir-fry with whatever you have
- Sheet pan meals (everything on one pan, minimal cleanup)

## Practical Tips
- Use a few quality tools (sharp knife, sheet pan, good skillet)
- Batch cook grains and proteins to mix and match
- Frozen vegetables are nutritious and save prep time
- Spices make simple food taste good

## Track Progress
- Try cooking 2-3 new recipes per week
- Note what worked and what you'd change
