- **TrainHeroic** – More advanced programming; useful if you follow structured plans or a coach.

**Passive tracking / health data (minimal effort)**
- **Apple Health + Apple Watch** (or **Whoop / Oura**)

  - **Oura Ring** – Great sleep + readiness tracking, less fitness detail.
