# Best Fitness & Nutrition Apps for Warehouse Workers

Given your long hours and physical work, here are solid options:

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - Large food database, easy barcode scanning, free tier is useful
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking, good if you want precision
- **Lose It!** - Simple interface, good for quick logging

## Fitness/Activity Tracking
- **Google Fit** or **Apple Health** - Free, built-in, tracks steps automatically
- **Fitbit app** - Works well if you have the device
- **Strava** - Better for structured workouts outside work

## Things Worth Considering for Your Situation

**Warehouse work is already physically demanding**, so you might want to track:
- Calories burned vs. consumed (you likely need more than desk workers)
- Hydration reminders
- Recovery and sleep

**Practical tips:**
- MyFitnessPal + Apple/Google Health together covers most bases for free
- Quick barcode scanning matters when you're tired after a shift
- Look for apps with **meal prep planning** since cooking after long shifts is hard

## My Honest Recommendation
**Start simple** - MyFitnessPal for food and your phone's built-in health app for activity is enough for most people before adding complexity.
