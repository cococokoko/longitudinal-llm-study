── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Proper nutrition** - age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - daily walks, playtime, or activity suited to your pet
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm protection
- **Spay/neuter** if appropriate

## Mental Wellbeing
- **Enrichment activities** - toys, puzzles, and new experiences
- **Social interaction** - time with you and other animals when appropriate
- **Training** - helps dogs feel confident and understand boundaries
- **Consistent routine** - pets generally feel secure with predictability

## Environment
- **Safe space** - a comfortable place to rest and feel secure
- **Clean living area** - regular cleaning of bedding, litter boxes, cages
- **Appropriate temperature** - protection from extreme heat or cold

## Monitoring
- Watch for changes in **eating, drinking, or behavior**
- Check for **unusual lumps, wounds, or signs of pain**
- Know the **signs of stress** specific to your pet species

## Emotional Connection
- Spend **quality time** together
- Learn your pet's **individual personality and needs**
- Handle them **gently and respectfully**
