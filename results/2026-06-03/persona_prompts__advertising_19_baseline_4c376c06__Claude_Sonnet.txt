── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based ways to improve mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep regularly** - aim for 7-9 hours consistently
- **Exercise** - even 20-30 minutes of walking helps significantly
- **Eat nutritiously** - diet affects mood and brain function
- **Limit alcohol and substances**

## Social Connection
- Maintain relationships with people you trust
- Reach out when you're struggling rather than isolating
- Consider community involvement or volunteering

## Mental Practices
- **Mindfulness or meditation** - reduces stress and anxiety
- **Journaling** - helps process emotions
- **Set realistic goals** - builds sense of accomplishment
- **Limit news/social media** if it increases anxiety

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** (especially CBT) is highly effective for many conditions
- Talk to a doctor if symptoms are persistent
- Support groups can help with specific challenges

## Stress Management
- Identify your stress triggers
- Build in regular breaks and downtime
- Learn to set boundaries

## Things Worth Knowing
- Small consistent changes often work better than big overhauls
- Mental health fluctuates - bad periods don't mean permanent failure
- Asking for help is a strength, not weakness
