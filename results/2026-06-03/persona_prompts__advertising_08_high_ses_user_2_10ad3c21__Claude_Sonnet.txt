# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some practical approaches to help you find great hiking trails:

## Online Resources
- **AllTrails** - Most comprehensive database with reviews, difficulty ratings, and maps
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed trail data with community contributions
- **Recreation.gov** - For national parks and federal lands

## Key Factors to Consider
- **Difficulty level** - Beginner, moderate, or strenuous
- **Distance & elevation gain** - Match your fitness level
- **Scenery** - Mountains, forests, coastal, desert
- **Crowd levels** - Popular vs. off-the-beaten-path

## Tips for Your Search
- **Filter by location** and how far you're willing to travel
- **Read recent reviews** for current trail conditions
- **Check seasonal availability** - some trails close in winter
- **Look for permit requirements** for popular destinations

## Great Starting Points
- National and State Parks in your region
- Local hiking clubs often share hidden gems
- REI and outdoor stores frequently have local trail guides

## Practical Considerations
- Cell service availability on trail
- Parking and trailhead access
- Pet-friendly options if applicable
