# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Dedicated workspace** - separate from relaxation areas when possible
- **Good lighting** - natural light boosts mood and focus
- **Minimize clutter** - a tidy space reduces mental distraction
- **Reliable equipment** - fast internet, comfortable chair, proper monitor height

## Managing Your Time
- **Set consistent work hours** - start and end at the same time daily
- **Use time-blocking** - schedule specific tasks in defined periods
- **Follow the 2-minute rule** - do quick tasks immediately
- **Try the Pomodoro technique** - 25 minutes focused work, 5-minute break

## Reducing Distractions
- **Silence non-essential notifications**
- **Communicate boundaries** to family/housemates
- **Use website blockers** (Freedom, Cold Turkey) during focus time
- **Keep your phone in another room** when deep work is needed

## Maintaining Energy & Focus
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Take **real breaks** away from screens
- Schedule movement/exercise
- Eat proper meals rather than snacking constantly

## Staying Connected
- Over-communicate with your team
- Use video calls strategically
- Schedule virtual social time

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Overworking without clear stop times
- Isolation and loneliness
- Blurring work/personal boundaries
