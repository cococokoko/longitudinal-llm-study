# Team Management Tip: **Have Regular One-on-One Meetings**

Schedule brief, consistent one-on-one meetings with each team member. This helps you:

- **Build trust** by giving each person dedicated attention
- **Catch problems early** before they affect the whole team
- **Understand individual motivations** and career goals
- **Provide personalized feedback** rather than only group feedback

**Practical suggestion:** Even 15-30 minutes every week or two can make a significant difference in how connected and supported your team members feel.

The key is to **listen more than you talk** during these meetings - let the team member set much of the agenda so they feel heard.
