# Safest Travel Practices During a Pandemic

As a senior attorney, you likely need to balance professional obligations with health considerations. Here's a practical overview:

## General Risk Hierarchy (Lower to Higher Risk)
- **Private vehicle travel** - minimal exposure to others
- **Private charter/aviation** - controlled environment
- **Driving alone or with household members**
- **Commercial air travel** with precautions
- **Public transportation** - generally higher exposure risk

## Key Protective Measures

### Before Travel
- Check current health advisories and destination requirements
- Verify vaccination/testing requirements
- Consider travel insurance with pandemic coverage
- Review your firm's travel policies

### During Travel
- Wear high-quality masks (N95/KN95) in crowded spaces
- Maintain distance where possible
- Use hand sanitizer frequently
- Choose less crowded travel times
- Ensure good ventilation

### Accommodations
- Private accommodations over shared spaces
- Hotels with clear sanitation protocols

## Professional Considerations
- **Video conferencing** remains the safest alternative
- Many courts and depositions now accommodate remote participation
- Consider whether travel is truly necessary

## Practical Resources
- CDC travel guidelines
- State/local health department advisories
- WHO travel recommendations
