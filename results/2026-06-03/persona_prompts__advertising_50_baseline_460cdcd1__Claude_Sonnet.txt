# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- Compare prices on multiple sites (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Be **flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- Set price alerts for routes you want
- Use **incognito mode** when searching flights

## Flights
- Fly into smaller/alternate airports
- Look for **budget airlines**
- Consider connecting flights vs. direct
- Use **miles and points** from credit cards
- Travel during off-peak seasons

## Accommodation
- Try **Airbnb, Hostel World, or VRBO**
- Use hotel reward programs
- Consider house swapping
- Stay slightly outside city centers
- Book refundable rates and watch for price drops

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation** instead of taxis/rideshares
- Cook some meals instead of eating out every time
- Get a **travel credit card** with no foreign transaction fees
- Use local ATMs wisely (avoid airport exchange booths)
- Look for **free attractions** (museums, parks, walking tours)

## General Tips
- Travel with a group to split costs
- Pack light to **avoid baggage fees**
- Get travel insurance to avoid costly surprises
- Follow travel deal newsletters (Scott's Cheap Flights, Secret Flying)
