### Minimum Specs to Look For
- **RAM:** 8GB (most important)
- **Storage:** 256GB SSD (avoid old spinning hard drives)
- **Processor:** Intel i5 or better
- **OS:** Windows 10/11 or Linux ready

- **Newegg Refurbished**
- **Your hospital's IT department** sometimes sells retired equipment
