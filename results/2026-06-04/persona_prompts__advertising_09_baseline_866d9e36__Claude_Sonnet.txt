# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What's your core expertise or perspective?
- Who is your target audience?
- What problem do you solve or value do you provide?
- What makes your point of view distinct?

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B content |
| Instagram | Visual storytelling, lifestyle |
| X/Twitter | Thought leadership, commentary |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video, education, entertainment |
| Substack | Long-form writing, newsletters |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Principles

- **Be consistent** - posting schedule matters more than perfection
- **Be specific** - niche audiences are more valuable than broad ones
- **Show your thinking** - not just conclusions, but reasoning
- **Mix content types** - teach, share opinions, tell stories, engage

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Only posting promotional content
- Copying others' style instead of developing your own
- Disappearing for weeks, then over-posting
- Chasing trends that don't fit your brand

---

## What Actually Builds a Brand

✅ Consistent voice and values
✅ Genuine engagement with your audience
✅ Documenting your real journey
✅ Collaborating with others in your space
