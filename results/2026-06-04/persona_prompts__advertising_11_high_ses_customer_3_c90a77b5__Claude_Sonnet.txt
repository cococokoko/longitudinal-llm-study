# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are practical, evidence-based approaches organized by category:

## Bodyweight Training
- **Push-up variations** (standard, wide, diamond, pike) target chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** for lower body strength
- **Planks and core work** for stability
- **Pull-ups** using a doorframe bar or sturdy branch

## Cardiovascular Options
- Running or brisk walking
- Cycling
- Jump rope (highly efficient, low cost)
- Swimming if accessible
- Stair climbing

## Structured Programs Worth Considering
- **HIIT** (High-Intensity Interval Training) - time-efficient

- Calisthenics progressions

## Practical Habits
- Walk or cycle for transportation
- Take stairs consistently
- Set movement breaks during long work sessions
- Aim for **150+ minutes of moderate activity weekly** (standard recommendation)

## Minimal Equipment Worth Owning
- Resistance bands (~$15-30)
- Kettlebell or adjustable dumbbells
- Jump rope

## Key Principles
- **Consistency matters more than intensity**
- Progressive overload still applies - gradually increase difficulty
- Recovery and sleep are part of fitness
