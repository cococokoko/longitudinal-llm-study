- The threat of ultimate consequences may discourage potential offenders from committing the most serious crimes

- Eliminates risk of escape or future release

- Reflects the moral weight society places on human life

- Some argue lengthy death row proceedings aside, permanent removal eliminates lifetime incarceration costs
