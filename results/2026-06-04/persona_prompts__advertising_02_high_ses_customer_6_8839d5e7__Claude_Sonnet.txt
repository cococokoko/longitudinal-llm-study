# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a straightforward, effective routine:

## Morning
1. **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming)
2. **Hydrating toner** - with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - look for ceramides, shea butter, or squalane
4. **SPF 30+** - preferably with moisturizing properties

## Evening
1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
3. **Rich moisturizer/night cream** - heavier than your daytime formula
4. **Facial oil** *(optional)* - rosehip or jojoba to seal moisture in

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Squalane
- Shea butter

## Quick Tips
- **Lukewarm water only** - hot water strips moisture
- Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp
- Consider a **humidifier** in your workspace/bedroom
- Drink adequate water throughout the day
- Exfoliate gently **1-2x per week** max

## Recommended Brands
- CeraVe, La Roche-Posay *(drugstore)*
- Tatcha, Drunk Elephant *(premium)*
