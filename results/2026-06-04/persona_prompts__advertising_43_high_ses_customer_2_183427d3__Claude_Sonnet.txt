# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical strategies to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a venue close to most guests to minimize travel
- Look for LEED-certified or sustainably operated venues
- Consider outdoor venues that require less artificial lighting
- Opt for a single location for ceremony and reception

## Invitations & Paper
- Use digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Choose recycled or FSC-certified paper if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-forward or vegetarian menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Décor
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent décor rather than purchasing
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral arrangements

## Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Consider renting suits/tuxedos

## Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
- Offset carbon emissions through reputable programs

## Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets, plants, or charitable donations
- Request experiences rather than physical gifts

## Vendors
- Prioritize vendors with documented sustainability commitments
