# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great ways to travel more sustainably:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Walking or cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Public transit** - buses, trains, trams significantly reduce per-person emissions
- **Rail travel** - one of the most efficient options for longer distances
- **Carpooling/ridesharing** - splits emissions among multiple passengers

## If You Need a Vehicle
- **Electric vehicles (EVs)** - especially effective where renewable energy is available
- **Hybrid cars** - better fuel efficiency than conventional vehicles
- **Fuel-efficient rentals** - choose smaller, newer models

## Air Travel Considerations
- **Fly less frequently** - aviation has a significant carbon footprint
- **Choose direct flights** - takeoff and landing use the most fuel
- **Economy class** - uses less space and proportionally fewer emissions
- **Carbon offset programs** - imperfect but can help compensate

## Practical Tips
- Pack light to reduce fuel consumption
- Stay in eco-certified accommodations
- Support local businesses at your destination
- Use reusable water bottles and bags

## Planning Resources
- Google Maps shows transit and cycling routes
- Rome2Rio compares transport emissions
- Various carbon footprint calculators available online
