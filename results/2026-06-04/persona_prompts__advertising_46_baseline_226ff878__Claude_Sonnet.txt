# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die"** by Dr. Michael Greger - evidence-based nutrition
- **"The China Study"** by T. Colin Campbell - research-focused
- **"Forks Over Knives"** companion cookbook - practical recipes
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget"** by Toni Okamoto - affordable approach

## Websites & Organizations
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based, free information
- **PCRM.org** (Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine)
- **Oldwayspt.org** - cultural food traditions
- **Forks Over Knives** website

## Apps
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-based restaurants
- **Forks Over Knives** app - meal planning

## Practical Starting Tips
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian**
- Focus on **whole foods** rather than processed substitutes
- Pay attention to **B12, iron, calcium, omega-3s, and vitamin D**
- Start gradually if an immediate switch feels overwhelming

## YouTube Channels
- Mic the Vegan
- Physicians Committee (PCRM)

## Worth Noting
- Individual nutritional needs vary
- Some people do better with professional guidance
- Be somewhat critical of sources with strong commercial interests
