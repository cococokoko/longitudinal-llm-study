Briefly explain the potential uses of biofuels in 2-3 sentences.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Biofuels can serve as a renewable alternative to fossil fuels in transportation, powering cars, trucks, and aircraft with lower net carbon emissions since the crops used to produce them absorb CO₂ as they grow. They can also be used to generate electricity and heat in power plants, providing energy security by reducing dependence on imported oil. Additionally, biofuels can be blended with conventional fuels, allowing existing engines and infrastructure to be used without significant modification.
