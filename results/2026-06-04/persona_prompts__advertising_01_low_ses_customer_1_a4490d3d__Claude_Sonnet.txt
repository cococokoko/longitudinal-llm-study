# Learning Guitar - Practical Tips for a Busy Schedule

Given your unpredictable work schedule, here's how to make guitar learning work for you:

## Starting Out
- **Get a decent beginner guitar** - acoustic or electric, whichever style interests you
- **Consider a used guitar** to save money initially
- **Essential accessories**: picks, tuner app (free), extra strings

## Learning Approaches That Fit Your Schedule

### Flexible Options
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar is highly recommended and completely free
- **Apps** - Yousician, Fender Play, or Simply Guitar
- **Short sessions work great** - even 10-15 minutes daily beats long irregular practice

## Building Skills Gradually

1. **Start with basic chords** - G, C, D, Em, Am
2. **Learn simple strumming patterns**
3. **Practice chord transitions** (this takes time - be patient)
4. **Learn songs you actually like** to stay motivated

## Realistic Expectations
- Fingertips will be sore at first - this passes
- Progress feels slow at first, then clicks
- **Consistency matters more than long sessions**

## Tips for Your Situation
- Keep the guitar **out and visible** so you pick it up during downtime
- Practice during breaks or before/after shifts
- **Don't stress missed days** - just pick it back up
