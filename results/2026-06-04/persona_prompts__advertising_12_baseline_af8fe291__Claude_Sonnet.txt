# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

## 🚆 Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Train travel** - significantly lower emissions than flying
- **Bus/coach** - one of the most efficient per-passenger options
- **Cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Walking** - the most sustainable option locally
- **Electric vehicles** - better than gas, especially with clean energy grids

## ✈️ If You Must Fly
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Choose airlines with **fuel efficiency commitments**

## 🏨 Sustainable Accommodation
- **Eco-certified hotels** (look for Green Key, LEED certification)
- **Locally owned guesthouses**
- **Camping** with leave-no-trace principles
- **Hostels** (shared resources = lower impact)

## 🌍 Responsible Travel Habits
- Pack light (less weight = less fuel)
- Use reusable water bottles/bags
- Eat local, seasonal food
- Support local businesses
- Respect wildlife and natural habitats
- Use public transport at your destination
