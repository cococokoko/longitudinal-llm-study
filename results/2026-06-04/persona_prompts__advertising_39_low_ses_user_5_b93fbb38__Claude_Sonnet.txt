# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting others, this is a great investment in your wellbeing.

## Start Small and Realistic

- **Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - not 20-30 like many guides suggest
- Same time each day builds the habit faster
- Morning often works well before household demands begin

## Simple Starting Techniques

**Basic Breath Awareness**
- Sit comfortably, close eyes
- Notice your natural breathing
- When your mind wanders, gently return attention to breath
- That's literally the whole practice

**Body Scan**
- Mentally check in from head to toe
- Notice tension without trying to fix it immediately

## Practical for Your Situation

- Use existing moments: washing dishes, waiting for coffee
- Even 3 mindful breaths counts
- Apps like **Insight Timer** (free) or **Headspace** offer guided sessions

## Honest Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander constantly - this is normal, not failure
- Benefits typically build over **weeks**, not days
- Consistency matters more than duration

## Especially Relevant for Caregivers

Mindfulness can help with:
- Reducing resentment and burnout
- Responding rather than reacting to stress
- Feeling more present with family

**What feels like the biggest barrier for you** - time, knowing what to do, or something else? That might help narrow down the best approach.
