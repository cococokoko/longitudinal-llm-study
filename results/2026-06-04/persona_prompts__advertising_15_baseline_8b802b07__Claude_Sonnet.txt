# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines

---

## Low-Cost, High-Impact Ideas

### Paint
- One of the **cheapest ways to transform a room**
- Try an accent wall instead of painting everything
- Don't overlook ceilings and trim

### Rearrange What You Have
- Move furniture around for a fresh feel
- **Costs nothing** and can completely change a space

### Declutter
- Less stuff often looks more intentional and stylish
- Sell unwanted items to **fund new purchases**

---

## Smart Shopping Tips
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** for unique finds
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** for furniture deals
- **Discount stores** like IKEA, HomeGoods, or TJ Maxx
- Wait for **seasonal sales**
- Check **clearance sections** first

---

## DIY Projects
- Reupholster old chairs
- Make your own artwork
- Refinish or paint old furniture
- Create gallery walls with personal photos

---

## Small Details That Make a Big Difference
- New throw pillows and blankets
- Plants and greenery
- Updated light fixtures or bulbs
- Mirrors to make spaces feel larger
