## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) - critical for local search
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood-level reach
- Facebook local groups and a business page
- Word of mouth and referral programs

**Community Presence**
- Sponsor local events or sports teams
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Partner with complementary businesses
- Attend farmers markets or community events

**Practical Digital Steps**
- Collect and respond to Google reviews
- Post consistently on relevant social media
- Create simple flyers for local bulletin boards
- Email list for repeat customers

**Relationship Building**
- Offer loyalty programs
- Follow up with existing customers
- Ask satisfied customers for referrals directly

## What Actually Works Best
Honestly, **Google Business Profile + genuine customer reviews** tends to have the highest return for most local businesses, since people search locally before buying.

The best strategy really depends on:
- What type of business you have
- Your target customer age group
- Your budget
