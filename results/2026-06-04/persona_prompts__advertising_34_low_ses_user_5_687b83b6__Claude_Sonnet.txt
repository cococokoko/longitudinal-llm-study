## Effective Online Teaching Techniques

**Engagement Strategies**
- Use varied formats (video, discussion, quizzes) to maintain attention
- Break content into shorter segments (10-15 minutes max)
- Ask questions regularly rather than lecturing continuously
- Use polls and interactive tools like Mentimeter or Kahoot

**Clear Communication**
- State learning objectives explicitly at the start
- Provide written instructions alongside verbal ones
- Create consistent routines so students know what to expect
- Give specific, timely feedback

**Technical Foundations**
- Ensure good lighting and audio quality
- Have a backup plan for technical failures
- Record sessions when possible for review
- Use a stable platform your students can access easily

**Building Connection**
- Start with relationship-building activities
- Create small group opportunities
- Be available through clear office hours or messaging
- Acknowledge students by name

**Assessment**
- Use frequent low-stakes checks rather than rare high-stakes tests
- Vary assessment formats
- Make expectations transparent with rubrics

**Practical tip:** Given your busy schedule managing family responsibilities, **batching your preparation** (creating multiple lessons at once) can save significant time.
