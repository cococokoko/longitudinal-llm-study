# One High-Impact Tip for Teacher Organization

## Use a **Weekly Planning Template**

Create a simple, consistent template you fill out every Sunday or Monday morning that includes:

- **Daily lesson objectives** for each class period
- **Materials needed** (copies, supplies, tech)
- **Deadlines coming up** (assignments due, grading)
- **One priority task** for each day

---

### Why this works for teachers specifically:

- Your schedule changes constantly with assemblies, field trips, and meetings
- Having everything in **one place** prevents the "I forgot to make copies" panic
- It takes only **15-20 minutes** but saves hours of mental energy during the week

---

### Simple ways to do it:

- A **Google Doc or Sheet** you reuse each week
- A **physical planner** kept on your desk
- A **whiteboard** in your workspace

---

The biggest benefit is that it **reduces decision fatigue** during the school day, so you can focus your energy on actually teaching rather than remembering what comes next.
