# Improving Your Credit Score Quickly

Here are practical steps organized by impact and speed:

## Fastest Impact (Days to Weeks)
- **Pay down credit card balances** - lowering your utilization ratio below 30% can show results quickly
- **Ask for a credit limit increase** - reduces utilization without paying more
- **Become an authorized user** on someone else's good-standing account
- **Dispute errors on your credit report** - get free reports at AnnualCreditReport.com

## Medium-Term Actions (1-3 Months)
- **Pay all bills on time** - payment history is the biggest factor (~35%)
- **Set up autopay** for at least minimum payments
- **Avoid applying for multiple new credit accounts** at once

## Things to Avoid
- Closing old credit cards
- Missing any payments
- Maxing out cards

## As a Busy Single Parent
- **Automate everything possible** to reduce mental load
- Many banks offer **free credit monitoring** through their apps
- Consider **credit builder loans** from credit unions if you need to establish credit

## Realistic Expectations
- Small improvements: **1-3 months**
- Significant improvement: **6-12 months**
- Major recovery: **1-2+ years**

## Free Resources
- Credit Karma or Credit Sesame for monitoring
- Nonprofit credit counseling (NFCC.org)
