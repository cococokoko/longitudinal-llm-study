# Best Ways to Save for Retirement

## Tax-Advantaged Accounts (Start Here)

**Employer-Sponsored Plans**
- **401(k) or 403(b)** - contribute enough to get full employer match (it's free money)
- 2024 contribution limit: $23,000 ($30,500 if 50+)

**Individual Retirement Accounts**
- **Traditional IRA** - tax deduction now, pay taxes later
- **Roth IRA** - pay taxes now, tax-free growth and withdrawals
- 2024 limit: $7,000 ($8,000 if 50+)

## General Priority Order
1. Get full employer 401(k) match
2. Max out HSA (if eligible) - triple tax advantage
3. Max out Roth IRA
4. Max out 401(k)
5. Taxable brokerage account

## Key Principles
- **Start early** - compound interest is powerful
- **Invest consistently** regardless of market conditions
- **Keep fees low** - index funds typically work well
- **Increase contributions** when income rises
- **Avoid early withdrawals** (penalties + lost growth)

## Common Investment Approaches
- Target-date funds (simple, automatic rebalancing)
- Low-cost index funds
- Diversified portfolio matching your risk tolerance

## Things to Consider
- Your expected retirement age
- Social Security timing strategy
- Healthcare costs in retirement
