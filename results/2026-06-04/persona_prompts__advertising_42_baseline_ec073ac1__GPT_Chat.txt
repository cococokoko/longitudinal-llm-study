The best fitness and nutrition tracking app depends on your goals, budget, and how much detail you want. Here are some of the top options:

For nutrition tracking

• MacroFactor
  - Excellent for tracking calories and macros
  - Uses your weight trends to estimate your metabolism and adjust targets
  - Strong food database and barcode scanning
  - Subscription required

• Cronometer
  - Best for detailed nutrient tracking
  - Tracks vitamins, minerals, and other micronutrients
  - Popular among athletes and people with specific dietary goals
  - Free version is quite capable

• MyFitnessPal
  - One of the largest food databases
  - Easy barcode scanning and meal logging
  - Integrates with many fitness apps and devices
  - Some features require a premium subscription

• Lose It!
  - User-friendly and focused on weight management
  - Good food database and goal-setting tools
  - Often less overwhelming than MyFitnessPal

For fitness tracking

• Strava
  - Great for running and cycling
  - Excellent GPS tracking and social features
  - Popular with endurance athletes

• Garmin Connect
  - Best if you use a Garmin watch
  - Comprehensive training, recovery, and health metrics
  - Free with Garmin devices

• Apple Fitness / Apple Health
  - Strong ecosystem for iPhone and Apple Watch users
  - Integrates activity, workouts, and health data

• Fitbit
  - Good for general activity, sleep, and health tracking
  - Easy-to-understand metrics and trends

For combining fitness and nutrition

• MacroFactor + Apple Health, Garmin, or Fitbit
  - One of the strongest combinations for serious tracking

• MyFitnessPal
  - Tracks both food and exercise in one place
  - Broad device integration

• Lifesum
  - Attractive interface
  - Combines nutrition, habits, and activity tracking
  - Good for users who want guidance rather than deep analytics

For strength training

• Strong
  - Simple and effective workout logging
  - Tracks sets, reps, and progress over time

• Hevy
  - Popular strength-training tracker
  - Includes social and community features

• Fitbod
  - Generates workout recommendations based on equipment and recovery
  - Subscription-based

Recommendations by goal

• Weight loss: MacroFactor, Lose It!, or MyFitnessPal
• Muscle gain: MacroFactor + Strong or Hevy
• Endurance sports: Strava + Garmin Connect
• Detailed nutrition analysis: Cronometer
• General health and habits: Fitbit or Apple Health
• Beginner-friendly all-in-one solution: MyFitnessPal or Lifesum
