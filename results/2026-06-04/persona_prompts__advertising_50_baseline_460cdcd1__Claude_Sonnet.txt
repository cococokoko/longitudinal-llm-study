# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- Compare prices on multiple sites (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Be **flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- Set price alerts for flights you're watching
- Use **incognito mode** when searching to avoid price increases

## Flights
- Fly into smaller/alternate airports
- Look for **budget airlines**
- Consider connecting flights vs. direct
- Use **travel rewards credit cards** and miles
- Check if nearby departure cities offer better prices

## Accommodation
- Use **Airbnb, Hostel World, or VRBO**
- Stay slightly outside city centers
- Consider **house swapping**
- Book refundable rates and watch for price drops
- Use hotel loyalty programs

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation** instead of taxis/rideshares
- Cook some meals instead of eating out every meal
- Look for **free attractions** (museums, parks, walking tours)
- Get a local SIM card or international phone plan
- Travel during **shoulder season** (just before/after peak times)

## General Tips
- Travel with a group to split costs
- Set a daily budget and track spending
- Look for **package deals**
- Use a **no foreign transaction fee** credit card
