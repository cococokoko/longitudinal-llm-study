── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Learning to cook healthy meals quickly is very achievable! Here are some practical strategies:

## Start with the Basics
- Learn a few **fundamental techniques** (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Master simple knife skills to speed up prep
- Understand basic flavor combinations

## Build a Smart Kitchen Setup
- Keep healthy staples stocked (olive oil, canned beans, whole grains, frozen vegetables)
- Have a few reliable tools (sharp knife, sheet pan, good skillet)
- Use time-saving appliances like an Instant Pot or air fryer

## Practical Cooking Strategies
- **Meal prep** on weekends - batch cook grains, proteins, vegetables
- Learn **one-pan/sheet pan meals** - minimal cleanup, easy execution
- Use the **formula approach**: protein + vegetable + grain + sauce
- Keep frozen vegetables on hand - they're nutritious and fast

## Learning Resources
- YouTube channels (Joshua Weissman, Ethan Chlebowski focus on practical cooking)
- Apps like Yummly or Budget Bytes for simple recipes
- Start with recipes labeled "30 minutes or less"

## Build Gradually
- Master **5-10 reliable recipes** before expanding
- Repeat recipes until they feel automatic
- Gradually add new dishes

## Quick Meal Ideas to Start
- Stir-fries
- Sheet pan dinners
- Buddha bowls
- Egg-based meals
