- Difficulty setting healthy boundaries

## Behavioral Signs
- Seeking external validation constantly
- Difficulty being alone
- Overworking to prove your worth
- Difficulty accepting care from others

## Important Considerations
- These patterns can have **multiple causes**, not just maternal relationships
- Many people show some of these without childhood neglect
- A therapist can help distinguish what's actually driving these patterns
- **These are not permanent** - they can be worked through
