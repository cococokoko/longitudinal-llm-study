# Learning a New Language Effectively

Here's a practical, evidence-based approach:

## Core Principles

- **Comprehensible input** is the primary driver of acquisition (Krashen's hypothesis holds up reasonably well)
- **Spaced repetition** dramatically improves vocabulary retention
- **Active production** accelerates fluency beyond passive consumption
- **Consistency beats intensity** - daily practice outperforms weekend marathons

## High-Leverage Methods

### Vocabulary
- **Anki** with spaced repetition flashcards
- Learn the most frequent 1,000-2,000 words first (covers ~80-90% of common speech)

### Listening/Speaking
- Immersion apps (Pimsleur, Language Transfer)
- Conversation partners via **iTalki** or **Tandem**
- Shadowing technique for pronunciation

### Reading/Writing
- Graded readers matched to your level
- LingQ for reading with integrated vocabulary lookup
