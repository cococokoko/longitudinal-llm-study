# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Shea butter / cocoa butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds (exfoliants)

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Moisturizing Body Lotion
- 1/2 cup shea butter
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil
- Whip together and store in a jar

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 2 tbsp beeswax
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Melt together, pour into small containers, let cool

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- Few drops essential oil
- Mix and use in the shower

### 🌿 Face Toner
- 1 part apple cider vinegar
- 2 parts water
- Apply with cotton pad

### 🍯 Face Mask
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp oats (ground)
- Mix, apply 10-15 minutes, rinse

---

## Tips for Success
- **Sanitize** all tools and containers
- **Patch test** before using on your face
- **Label** products with date made
- Store in **cool, dark places**
- Products **without water** last longer
- Add **vitamin E oil** as a natural preservative

---

## Things to Be Careful About
- Essential oils can irritate skin if not diluted
- Water-based products can grow bacteria quickly
- Some ingredients can cause allergic reactions
- Citrus oils can make skin sun-sensitive
