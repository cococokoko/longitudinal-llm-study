# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are some effective ways to maintain fitness without a gym membership - which can also be helpful when you're managing a budget during a new job's probation period.

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** - if you have a doorframe bar

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Brisk walking
- Swimming (public pools are affordable)

## Home Workout Resources
- **YouTube channels** - many free workout videos
- **Apps** - Nike Training Club, FitOn (free options available)
- **Podcasts** with guided workouts

## Everyday Activity
- Take stairs instead of elevators
- Walk or cycle to work
- Active breaks during the day
- Household chores count too

## Low-Cost Equipment Worth Considering
- Resistance bands (~$10-15)
- Jump rope (~$10)
- Yoga mat (~$15-20)

## Tips for Consistency
- Set a regular schedule
- Find a workout buddy
- Track your progress
- Start small and build up gradually
