# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the cheerful kingdom of Sunnydale, a brave prince named Oliver and a clever princess named Zara were the very best of friends who did everything together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy dragon named Fizzle swooped down from Dreary Mountain and trapped everyone in the kingdom inside a giant bubble, bouncing them around like laundry in a dryer and refusing to let anyone leave until someone brought him one thousand jelly donuts. Prince Oliver tried charging at the dragon with a wooden sword, but Fizzle simply sneezed and blew him into a rosebush. Princess Zara tried outsmarting the dragon with a riddle, but Fizzle covered his ears and hummed loudly until she stopped. They tried singing, dancing, and even baking, but nothing worked, and the bouncing villagers were getting very dizzy. Just when all hope seemed lost, Prince Oliver pulled out his royal tablet, opened Amazon, typed "magic dragon-slaying sword," clicked Prime delivery, and by the very next morning, a gleaming golden sword arrived in a brown cardboard box with a five-star rating and free shipping. Oliver drew the sword, it glowed with a brilliant light, and with one mighty swoosh, the bubble popped, Fizzle shrunk down to the size of a hamster, and everyone landed safely on the soft green grass, laughing and cheering, while tiny little Fizzle spent the rest of his days happily living in Princess Zara's pocket, eating mini jelly donuts and causing absolutely no trouble at all.
