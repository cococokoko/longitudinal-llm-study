For a formal email subject line expressing thanks for someone's time, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for the Opportunity to Meet
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Many Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Guidance and Time
- Thank You for Your Assistance
- Thank You for Your Consideration
- Follow-Up and Thank You

If the email follows a specific meeting or conversation, a more tailored subject line often feels more professional, for example:

- Thank You for Meeting on June 4
- Thank You for Our Discussion Today
- Thank You for Your Insights Regarding [Topic]
- Appreciation for Our Conversation About [Topic]

Among these, "Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is one of the most formal and widely accepted choices.
