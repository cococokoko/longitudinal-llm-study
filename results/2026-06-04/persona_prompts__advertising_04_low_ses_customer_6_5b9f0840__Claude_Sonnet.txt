# Starting a Small Online Business

Here are some key areas to focus on:

## Business Foundation
- **Choose your niche** - products, services, or digital goods
- **Research your market** - competitors, target customers, pricing
- **Write a simple business plan** - goals, costs, revenue projections

## Setup Essentials
- Register your business name/structure (LLC, sole proprietor)
- Get an EIN (free from IRS)
- Open a separate business bank account
- Choose an e-commerce platform (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, etc.)

## Practical Considerations
Since you work long warehouse hours, consider:
- **Starting lean** - minimal upfront investment
- **Products that don't require constant attention** - digital downloads, dropshipping, or print-on-demand
- **Automating where possible** - scheduled posts, automated emails
- **Building gradually** on days off or after shifts

## Getting Customers
- Social media (free to start)
- Word of mouth
- SEO for your website
- Marketplace listings (Amazon, eBay, Etsy)

## Keep Costs Low Initially
- Free tools where possible
- Start with one platform
- Reinvest early profits

## Good Starting Questions
- What will you sell?
- How much startup money do you have?
- How many hours per week can you dedicate?

What type of business are you considering?
