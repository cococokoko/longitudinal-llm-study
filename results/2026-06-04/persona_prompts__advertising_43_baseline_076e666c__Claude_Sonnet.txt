# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to reduce guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Pick a single location for ceremony and reception

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Avoid excess inserts and packaging

## 🌸 Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent or borrow decorations instead of buying new
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (it's not biodegradable)

## 🍽️ Food & Catering
- Serve locally sourced, seasonal menu items
- Offer plant-based options (lower carbon footprint)
- Donate leftover food to local shelters
- Use real dishes instead of disposables

## 👗 Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Rent suits/tuxedos
- Choose a dress you can wear again

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit

## 💍 Rings & Gifts
- Consider recycled metal or lab-grown stones
- Create a registry with sustainable brands
- Ask for experience gifts or charitable donations

## ♻️ Waste Management
- Set up clear recycling and compost stations
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Carbon offset** your event
- **Hire vendors** who share your values
- **Keep the guest list** intentionally sized
