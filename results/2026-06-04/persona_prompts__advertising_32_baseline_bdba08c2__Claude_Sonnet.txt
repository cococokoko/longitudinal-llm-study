What are the best ways to improve indoor air quality?

# Improving Indoor Air Quality

Here are the most effective approaches:

## Ventilation
- **Open windows regularly** when outdoor air quality is good
- Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms
- Ensure HVAC systems are properly maintained

## Filtration
- Use **HEPA air purifiers** in frequently used rooms
- Replace HVAC filters regularly (every 1-3 months)
- Consider higher MERV-rated filters for your system

## Reduce Pollution Sources
- **Avoid smoking indoors**
- Use low-VOC paints and cleaning products
- Store chemicals properly or outside
- Limit use of candles and wood burning
- Remove shoes at the door

## Control Humidity
- Keep humidity between **30-50%**
- Fix leaks promptly to prevent mold
- Use dehumidifiers in damp areas

## Common Pollutant Sources to Address
- Test for **radon** (especially in basements)
- Check for **carbon monoxide** with detectors
- Address any mold growth immediately
- Vacuum regularly with HEPA vacuums

## Plants
- Some houseplants may help modestly, though **evidence is limited** for significant air cleaning

## Priority Order
Most experts suggest **source control** is more effective than filtration alone - removing the problem beats filtering it out.
