Getting started with meditation and mindfulness is much simpler than most people think. You don’t need to clear your mind completely, sit in uncomfortable positions, or chant for hours.

*   **Meditation** is the *training ground*. It is a dedicated time you set aside to practice focusing your mind (like going to the gym).
