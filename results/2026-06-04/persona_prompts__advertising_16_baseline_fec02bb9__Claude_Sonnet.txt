# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** - typically triggered by external pressures (work, relationships, finances)
**Anxiety** - often involves worry about future threats, sometimes without clear cause

Both are normal human experiences, but they can become overwhelming.

---

## Practical Strategies

### Immediate Relief
- **Deep breathing** - slow exhale activates your calm response
- **Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)** - notice 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, etc.
- **Cold water** on your face or wrists
- **Physical movement** - even a short walk helps

### Daily Habits
- **Regular sleep schedule** - poor sleep amplifies anxiety significantly
- **Limit caffeine and alcohol** - both worsen anxiety
- **Exercise** - consistently one of the most effective tools
- **Reduce news/social media** consumption

### Mental Approaches
- **Challenge worried thoughts** - ask "is this likely? what's the evidence?"
- **Focus on what you can control**
- **Journaling** to process feelings
- **Mindfulness/meditation** - even 5-10 minutes daily

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily functioning
- Persistent symptoms lasting weeks
- Physical symptoms (chest pain, panic attacks)
- Therapy (especially CBT) and/or medication can be very effective
