# Mastering Digital Photography

Here's a practical guide tailored to fit around a busy, unpredictable schedule:

---

## Starting Simple
- **Learn your camera's basic modes** - Auto, Aperture Priority, Shutter Priority
- **Understand the exposure triangle** - ISO, aperture, shutter speed
- **Practice during breaks or commute** - even smartphone photography builds skills

---

## Core Skills to Build
### Composition
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines
- Framing subjects

### Lighting
- Golden hour (sunrise/sunset) - often before/after shifts
- Window light for indoor shots
- Avoiding harsh midday shadows

### Settings
- Shooting in RAW vs JPEG
- White balance adjustment
- Focus modes

---

## Learning on Your Schedule
- **YouTube tutorials** - watch during downtime, 10-15 minutes at a time
- **Practice daily** - photograph things around you, even at work
- **Free resources** - Cambridge in Colour, Digital Photography School

---

## Budget-Friendly Gear
- Start with what you have
- Consider used cameras
- A 50mm lens is affordable and versatile

---

## Quick Wins
- Delete bad photos ruthlessly
- Study photos you admire
- Shoot consistently, even briefly
