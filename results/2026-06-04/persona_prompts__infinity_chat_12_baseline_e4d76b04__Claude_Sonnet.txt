── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What are signs that show you were not loved enough as a child by your mom?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This is a meaningful question. Here are some commonly recognized signs, based on psychology research:

## Emotional Patterns
- Difficulty trusting others or feeling worthy of love
- Constantly seeking approval or validation
- Fear of abandonment in relationships
- Feeling like a burden to others
- Difficulty identifying or expressing emotions

## Relationship Challenges
- Attracting or tolerating unhealthy relationships
- Either clinging too tightly or pushing people away
- Difficulty setting boundaries
- People-pleasing behavior
- Trouble accepting genuine affection

## Self-Perception Issues
- Low self-esteem or chronic self-doubt
- Inner critic that sounds harsh or dismissive
- Feeling fundamentally "not enough"
- Difficulty believing compliments

## Behavioral Tendencies
- Overachieving to earn love
- Difficulty asking for help
- Anxiety around maternal figures
- Feeling emotionally numb sometimes

## Important Considerations
- These signs can have **multiple causes**
- Not all apply to everyone with difficult childhoods
- Some people show **opposite** responses
- Cultural context matters
- **These patterns are changeable** with awareness and support

## Worth Knowing
Many people find therapy helpful for working through these patterns, particularly approaches like attachment-focused therapy.
