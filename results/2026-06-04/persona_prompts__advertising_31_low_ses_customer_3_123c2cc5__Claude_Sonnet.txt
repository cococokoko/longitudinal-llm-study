- **Choose nearby destinations** - shorter travel distances reduce costs and carbon footprint

- **Compare transportation options** - trains are often cheaper and greener than flights
- **Stay in eco-hostels or guesthouses** - certified sustainable accommodations at lower prices

- **Travel slowly** - staying longer in fewer places saves money and emissions
