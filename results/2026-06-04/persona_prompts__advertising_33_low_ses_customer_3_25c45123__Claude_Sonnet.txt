# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, effective DIY beauty products you can make at home:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Mix coconut oil or shea butter with a few drops of essential oil (lavender, rosehip)
- Apply a small amount to clean skin

**Honey Face Mask**
- Raw honey applied directly to face for 15-20 minutes
- Rinse with warm water
- Great for hydration and mild antibacterial properties

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gently massage onto skin, rinse off

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado + olive oil + honey
- Apply to hair for 30 minutes, then shampoo out

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil massaged into scalp
- Leave for an hour before washing

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Melt beeswax + coconut oil + a little honey
- Pour into small containers and let set

## Tips for Success

- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean containers** to avoid contamination
- Make **small batches** since there are no preservatives
- Store in **cool, dark places**
- Note that natural doesn't always mean allergen-free
