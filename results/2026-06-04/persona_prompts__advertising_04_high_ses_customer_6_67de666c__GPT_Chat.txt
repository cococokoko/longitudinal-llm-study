2. Validate demand
- Talk to potential customers.
- Search forums, social media groups, and reviews of competing products.

- Digital products (templates, courses, guides, software)

- Subscription-based offerings
- Affiliate marketing or content-driven businesses

4. Build a simple online presence
- Create a website with a clear value proposition.
- Set up payment processing.
- Establish profiles on relevant social platforms.

5. Acquire customers
- Content marketing
- Search engine optimization (SEO)
- Paid advertising
- Partnerships and referrals
- Email marketing

6. Measure and improve
- Track traffic, conversion rates, customer acquisition cost, and retention.
- Double down on channels that work.
- Gather customer feedback continuously.

- Spending months building before validating demand.
- Trying to serve everyone.
- Overinvesting in branding before securing customers.
