# Benefits of Hardwood Flooring

## Durability & Longevity
- Can last **decades or even a lifetime** with proper care
- Can be **sanded and refinished** multiple times to restore appearance
- Handles normal wear well in most rooms

## Aesthetics
- Natural, warm appearance that many find attractive
- Wide variety of **wood species, stains, and finishes**
- Adds a classic, timeless look to homes

## Home Value
- Generally **increases resale value**
- Considered a desirable feature by many homebuyers

## Practical Advantages
- **Easier to clean** than carpet
- Doesn't trap allergens, dust mites, or pet dander as much as carpet
- Doesn't hold odors as readily

## Versatility
- Works with many design styles
- Can be refinished to change color or finish over time

## Considerations / Trade-offs
- **More expensive** upfront than many alternatives
- Can be scratched or damaged by water
- Can feel hard underfoot compared to carpet
- Not ideal for basements or high-moisture areas
- Requires some maintenance
