── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I make my home more energy efficient?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to make your home more energy efficient:

## Heating & Cooling
- Seal air leaks around windows, doors, and outlets
- Add insulation to attic, walls, and basement
- Use a programmable or smart thermostat
- Service your HVAC system regularly
- Use ceiling fans to reduce AC/heating needs

## Windows & Doors
- Install weatherstripping
- Consider double or triple-pane windows
- Use thermal curtains or blinds

## Appliances & Lighting
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Replace old appliances with Energy Star certified models
- Unplug devices when not in use (they draw "phantom" power)
- Use power strips to easily cut standby power

## Water Heating
- Lower water heater temperature to 120°F
- Insulate water heater pipes
- Consider a tankless or heat pump water heater

## Bigger Investments
- Solar panels
- Better insulation upgrades
- Energy efficient windows replacement
- Heat pump systems

## Simple Habits
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Run dishwasher only when full
- Air dry dishes and clothes when possible

## Getting Started
- Consider getting a **home energy audit** to identify your biggest opportunities
- Check for **utility rebates** and **tax credits** that can offset upgrade costs
