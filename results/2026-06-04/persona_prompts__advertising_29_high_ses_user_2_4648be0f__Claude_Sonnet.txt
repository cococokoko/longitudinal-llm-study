I am a senior attorney at a well-established law firm.

How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
That's a nice question to step away from legal work for a moment!

Here are some straightforward tips for keeping pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups and staying current on vaccinations
- **Proper nutrition** - quality food appropriate for their species, age, and size
- **Exercise** - regular walks, playtime, or activity suited to your pet
- **Dental care** - brushing or dental treats to prevent issues

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - toys, puzzles, and activities that engage their minds
- **Social interaction** - quality time with you and appropriate socialization
- **Consistent routine** - pets generally thrive on predictability
- **Safe space** - a comfortable area where they feel secure

## Practical Considerations
- Keep up with **parasite prevention** (fleas, ticks, heartworm)
- **Spay/neuter** if appropriate
- Ensure **proper identification** (microchip, tags)
- **Pet-proof your home** to prevent accidents

## Given Your Busy Schedule
- Consider a **reliable pet sitter or dog walker**
- Look into **pet insurance** to manage unexpected costs
- Build a relationship with a **trusted veterinarian**
