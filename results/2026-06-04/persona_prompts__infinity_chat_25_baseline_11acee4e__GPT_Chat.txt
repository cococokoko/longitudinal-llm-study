── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for Your Assistance
- Thank You for Your Support and Guidance
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Sincere Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You Following Our Meeting
- Thank You for the Opportunity
- Grateful for Your Time and Consideration

If the email is after an interview or meeting, a more specific subject line is often stronger, for example:

- Thank You for Meeting on [Date]
- Thank You for the Interview – [Your Name]
- Thank You for Discussing [Topic] with Me
- Appreciation for Our Conversation Regarding [Topic]

"Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is one of the most professional and widely accepted options.
