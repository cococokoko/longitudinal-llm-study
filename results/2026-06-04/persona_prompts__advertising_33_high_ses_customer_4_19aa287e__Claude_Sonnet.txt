# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some popular DIY options that are straightforward to make:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, cool, and store in a clean jar

**Sugar Scrub**
- Coconut oil + granulated sugar + lemon juice
- Mix and use immediately or store short-term

**Clay Face Mask**
- Kaolin or bentonite clay + water/rose water
- Mix to paste consistency before each use

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Treatment**
- Coconut oil + honey + optional egg yolk
- Apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Scrub**
- Brown sugar + olive oil + tea tree oil

## Lip Care
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Important Considerations

- **Preservation** - water-based products need preservatives to prevent microbial growth
- **Patch testing** - always test on a small skin area first
- **Sanitation** - sterilize all equipment and containers
- **Shelf life** - natural products expire faster than commercial ones
- **Essential oil dilution** - typically 1-2% concentration for skin safety
