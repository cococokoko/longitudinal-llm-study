# Starting a Small Online Business

Here are the key areas to focus on:

## Foundation
- **Business idea/niche** - identify your product or service
- **Market research** - validate demand and understand competition
- **Business model** - how you'll make money (products, services, subscriptions, etc.)

## Legal & Financial Setup
- Register your business (LLC is common for liability protection)
- Get an EIN (free from IRS)
- Open a separate business bank account
- Understand basic tax obligations

## Online Presence
- Choose a domain name
- Build a website (Shopify, Squarespace, WordPress, etc.)
- Set up payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, Square)

## Marketing
- Define your target customer
- Choose 1-2 marketing channels to start (social media, email, SEO, ads)
- Build an email list early

## Operations
- Decide on fulfillment/delivery of your product or service
- Set up basic customer service processes
- Track finances from day one

---

## Practical Advice
- **Start lean** - don't over-invest before validating
- **Get your first customer quickly** - real feedback beats planning
- **Focus** - avoid trying to do everything at once
