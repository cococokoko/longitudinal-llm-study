Here are 3 short, actionable tips for self-development:

**1. Focus on 1% Daily Growth**
Instead of trying to overhaul your life overnight, aim for small, consistent improvements. Building tiny, sustainable habits (like reading for 10 minutes or drinking an extra glass of water) compounds into massive, lasting change over time. Consistency always beats intensity.

**2. Embrace a "Growth Mindset"**
Train yourself to view failures and setbacks as data, not defeats. When you shift your perspective to see challenges as opportunities to learn rather than threats to your ego, you become much more resilient and open to trying new things. 

**3. Practice Regular Self-Reflection**
You cannot improve what you do not measure. Dedicate 5–10 minutes at the end of each day to unplug and review your progress. Simply asking yourself, *"What went well today, and what can I do better tomorrow?"* will keep your daily actions aligned with your long-term goals.
