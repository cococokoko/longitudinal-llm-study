# The Day's Farewell

The sky bleeds gold and rose and red,
As daylight lays itself to rest,
And all the rushing thoughts that fed
My mind grow quiet in my chest.

The horizon drinks the burning sun,
A slow and wordless letting go,
Reminding me that what is done

Watching light become the dark,
