# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Classical has nylon strings (easier on fingers), Electric requires an amp but has lighter strings
- **Get the basics** - Picks, tuner, capo, and a strap are helpful accessories

## First Steps
1. **Learn to tune your guitar** - Use a clip-on tuner or a tuning app
2. **Practice proper posture** - Sit comfortably, keep your back straight
3. **Build calluses** - Fingertip soreness is normal at first

## Essential Skills to Learn
- **Open chords** - Start with G, C, D, Em, Am
- **Chord transitions** - Practice switching smoothly
- **Basic strumming patterns** - Down strokes first, then combinations
- **Simple scales** - E minor pentatonic is a great starting point

## Practice Tips
- **Practice daily** - Even 15-20 minutes is effective
- **Go slow** - Speed comes with muscle memory
- **Learn songs you love** - Keeps motivation high
- **Use resources** like YouTube, apps (Yousician, Justin Guitar), or a teacher

## Realistic Timeline
- Basic chords: **1-3 months**
- Playing simple songs: **3-6 months**
- Comfortable playing: **1-2 years**
