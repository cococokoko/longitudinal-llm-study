# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

## Core Principles

- **Plan before you shop** — reduces impulse buying and waste
- **Cook at home** — restaurant meals cost 3-5x more
- **Embrace whole foods** — less processed = cheaper + healthier
- **Use everything** — minimize food waste

---

## Budget-Friendly Healthy Foods

### Proteins
- Eggs
- Canned tuna/salmon
- Dried or canned beans/lentils
- Chicken thighs (cheaper than breasts)
- Greek yogurt

### Grains & Starches
- Oats
- Brown rice
- Whole wheat pasta
- Sweet potatoes

### Vegetables & Fruits
- Frozen vegetables (just as nutritious)
- Cabbage, carrots, onions
- Bananas, apples, seasonal produce

---

## Practical Strategies

**Shopping**
- Buy store brands
- Shop sales and plan meals around them
- Buy in bulk for staples
- Check unit prices, not just package prices

**Cooking**
- Batch cook on weekends
- Make large pots of soup, grains, or beans
- Repurpose leftovers creatively

**Reducing Waste**
- Store food properly
- Use vegetable scraps for broth
- Freeze bread, meat, and leftovers

- ✅ Eat before grocery shopping
- ✅ Make a list and stick to it
- ✅ Compare fresh vs. frozen vs. canned
- ✅ Learn 5-6 versatile base recipes
