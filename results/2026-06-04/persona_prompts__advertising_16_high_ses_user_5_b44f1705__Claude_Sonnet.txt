# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

## Why Your Role Creates Unique Pressures
- **High-stakes decisions** with significant consequences
- **Always-on culture** and constant connectivity
- **Board/investor expectations** alongside team demands
- **Rapid change** requiring constant adaptation
- **Visibility** - your mood affects entire organizations

---

## Practical Strategies

### Immediate Relief
- **Box breathing** (4-4-4-4 counts) before high-pressure meetings
- **Brief walks** between back-to-back sessions
- **Phone-free blocks** during the day

### Structural Habits
- **Protect sleep** - non-negotiable, even during crunch periods
- **Exercise consistently** - even 20-30 minutes matters significantly
- **Calendar boundaries** - block recovery time like important meetings
- **Delegate deliberately** - holding everything increases anxiety

### Mental Approaches
- Separate **what you control vs. what you don't**
- **Reframe pressure** as information, not threat
- Regular **journaling** to process decisions and concerns

---

## Worth Considering
- **Executive coach** - confidential, experienced with your context
- **Therapist** - particularly if anxiety is persistent
- **Peer groups** with other executives who understand the role

---

## A Honest Note
Many executives normalize unhealthy stress levels. **Chronic stress impairs the judgment your role demands.**
