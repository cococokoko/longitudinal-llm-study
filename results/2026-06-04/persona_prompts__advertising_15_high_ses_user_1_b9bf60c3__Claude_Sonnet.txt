# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to refresh your space without breaking the bank:

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Repurpose items** from other rooms
- **Declutter** - less can look more intentional

## Low-Cost High-Impact Changes
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest ways to transform a room
- **New throw pillows or blankets** - swap seasonally
- **Swap hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **Update light fixtures** or just the bulbs (warm vs. cool tones matter)

## Smart Shopping
- Thrift stores and estate sales
- Facebook Marketplace / Craigslist
- End-of-season sales
- IKEA hacks and DIY modifications

## Free or Nearly Free
- **Plants** - propagate from cuttings
- **Printable art** - frame inexpensive downloads
- **Natural elements** - branches, stones, pinecones

## DIY Projects
- Gallery walls with personal photos
- Painted or stained furniture
- Homemade curtains (basic sewing)

## Focus Your Budget
- Prioritize **high-traffic, high-visibility** areas
- One **statement piece** per room
