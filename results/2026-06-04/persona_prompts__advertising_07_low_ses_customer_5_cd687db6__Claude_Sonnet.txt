# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

Here are practical strategies that can make a real difference, especially for a busy household:

## Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- **Adjust thermostat** - 7-10°F lower when sleeping or away saves ~10% on heating/cooling
- **Unplug devices** when not in use (TVs, chargers, appliances draw standby power)
- **Run full loads** in dishwasher and washing machine
- **Use cold water** for laundry when possible
- **Switch to LED bulbs** if you haven't already

## Managing a Larger Household
- **Set clear habits** with family members about lights, doors, and thermostat
- **Use power strips** with switches to cut multiple devices at once
- **Cook efficiently** - batch cooking, pressure cookers, and microwaves use less energy than ovens
- **Air dry dishes and laundry** when practical

## Bigger Impact Changes
- **Smart thermostat** - programs automatically around your schedule
- **Seal drafts** around doors and windows
- **Insulate water heater** and set it to 120°F
- **Energy audit** - many utilities offer free assessments

## Tracking Progress
- Monitor your utility bills monthly
- Many utilities have free apps showing usage patterns

**Starting with thermostat adjustments and unplugging unused devices typically gives the fastest results.**
