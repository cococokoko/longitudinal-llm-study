## Sneaker Decision: Zara vs. Used Adidas

Here's a straightforward breakdown to help you decide:

---

### Consider **Used Adidas** if:
- **Budget is tight** - likely cheaper
- **Quality matters** - Adidas generally has better build quality and durability
- **Comfort is priority** - Adidas has established athletic/comfort technology
- **Sustainability** - buying used is more eco-friendly
- **Brand reputation** - stronger sneaker credibility

### Consider **New Zara** if:
- **Condition matters** - brand new, no wear
- **Specific style** - you love that particular design
- **Hygiene concerns** - uncomfortable with used footwear
- **Warranty/returns** - easier to return if needed

---

### Key Questions to Ask:
1. **What's the price difference?**
2. **What condition is the used Adidas in?**
3. **What will you use them for?** (fashion vs. sport/walking)
4. **How long do you need them to last?**

---

### **General Recommendation:**
> **Used Adidas** often wins on **value and quality** - IF the condition is good (check sole wear, no odor, minimal creasing).
