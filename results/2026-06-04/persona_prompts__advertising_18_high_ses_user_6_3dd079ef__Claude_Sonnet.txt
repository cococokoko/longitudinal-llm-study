# Stock Market Investment Strategies

As a consulting professional, you likely appreciate frameworks and evidence-based approaches. Here are the key strategies worth considering:

## Core Foundational Strategies

**Passive Index Investing**
- Low-cost index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- Consistently outperforms most active managers long-term
- Minimize expense ratios (target <0.1%)

**Asset Allocation**
- Diversify across stocks, bonds, and asset classes
- Align with your time horizon and risk tolerance
- Rebalance periodically

## More Active Approaches

- **Value investing** - buying undervalued companies (Buffett model)
- **Growth investing** - prioritizing earnings growth potential
- **Dividend investing** - income-focused, often lower volatility
- **Factor investing** - targeting specific risk premiums (momentum, quality)

## Practical Principles That Matter Most

- **Dollar-cost averaging** reduces timing risk
- **Tax efficiency** - maximize 401k, IRA contributions first
- **Keep costs low** - fees compound against you
- **Avoid emotional decisions** during volatility

- Most active stock picking underperforms indexes after fees
- Your income level likely makes **tax-advantaged accounts** especially valuable
- A fee-only fiduciary financial advisor may be worth consulting
