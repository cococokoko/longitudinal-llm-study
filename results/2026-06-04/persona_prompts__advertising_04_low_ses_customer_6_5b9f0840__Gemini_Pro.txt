### 1. Best Business Models for Your Schedule
Since you deal with inventory and logistics all day, you probably want to avoid handling physical products at home. Consider these "hands-off" models:
*   **Print-on-Demand (POD):** You design graphics for t-shirts, mugs, or phone cases. When a customer orders, a third-party company prints and ships it. You never touch the inventory.
*   **Dropshipping:** You set up an online store, but a supplier holds the inventory and ships it directly to the customer. Your main job is just marketing and customer service.
*   **Digital Products:** Create something once and sell it infinitely. This could be budget templates, planners, digital art, or how-to guides. 
*   **Affiliate Marketing:** You promote other companies' products (via a blog, TikTok, or Instagram) and earn a commission on sales. No customer service or shipping required.
