# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a simple, effective routine to keep dry skin hydrated and healthy:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Use a cream or milk-based cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - Choose a rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
4. **SPF 30+** - Sun protection is essential daily

## Evening Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Same as morning
2. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or niacinamide work well
3. **Rich moisturizer or face oil** - Heavier than your daytime moisturizer
4. **Occasional overnight mask** - 1-2 times per week

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - Draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - Strengthen skin barrier
- **Glycerin** - Locks in hydration
- **Shea butter** - Deeply nourishing
- **Squalane** - Lightweight oil

## Tips
- Drink plenty of water
- Use a humidifier indoors
- Avoid hot showers
- Pat skin dry (don't rub)
- Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp
