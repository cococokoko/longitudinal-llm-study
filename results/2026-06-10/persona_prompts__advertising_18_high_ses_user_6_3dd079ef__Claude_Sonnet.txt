# Stock Market Investment Strategies

As a consulting professional, you likely appreciate frameworks and evidence-based approaches. Here are the key strategies worth considering:

## Core Foundational Approaches

**Passive Index Investing**
- Low-cost index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- Consistently outperforms most active managers long-term
- Minimize fees (expense ratios matter significantly)

**Asset Allocation**
- Diversify across stocks, bonds, real estate, international markets
- Align allocation with your time horizon and risk tolerance
- Rebalance periodically

## More Active Strategies

- **Value investing** - buying undervalued companies (Buffett approach)
- **Growth investing** - prioritizing revenue/earnings growth
- **Dividend investing** - income-focused, lower volatility
- **Factor investing** - targeting specific risk premiums (small-cap, value, momentum)

## Practical Principles

- **Dollar-cost averaging** reduces timing risk
- **Tax optimization** - maximize 401k, IRA contributions first
- **Keep costs low** - fees compound negatively just as returns compound positively

## Honest Caveats

- Most professional stock pickers **underperform** index funds after fees
- Your income likely makes **tax-advantaged accounts** especially valuable
- Consider consulting a **fee-only fiduciary advisor** for personalized guidance
