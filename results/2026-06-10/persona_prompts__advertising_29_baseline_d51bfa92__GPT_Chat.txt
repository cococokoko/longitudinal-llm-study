  - Dogs generally benefit from daily walks, play, and training.
  - Cats often enjoy interactive toys, climbing structures, and opportunities to explore safely.

  - Use toys, puzzles, training sessions, and enrichment activities.
  - Rotate toys periodically to keep them interesting.
  - Offer opportunities for natural behaviors, such as sniffing, foraging, climbing, or exploring.

- Create a safe and comfortable environment
  - Provide a clean living space with suitable shelter, bedding, and temperature control.
  - Keep toxic plants, foods, medications, and household chemicals out of reach.
  - Ensure enclosures and outdoor areas are secure.

- Support emotional well-being
  - Spend quality time with your pet and learn their preferences and body language.
  - Provide predictable routines when possible.
  - Give them places to rest and retreat when they want quiet time.

- Maintain grooming and hygiene
  - Brush coats as needed.
  - Trim nails when appropriate.
  - Follow species-specific recommendations for bathing, dental care, and grooming.

  - Obesity can contribute to many health problems.
