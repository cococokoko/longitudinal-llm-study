── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for Your Assistance
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Support
- Sincere Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You for the Opportunity
- Thank You for Speaking with Me
- Thank You for Your Guidance and Time

If the email follows an interview or meeting, a more specific subject line is often best, such as:
- Thank You for the Interview – [Your Name]
- Thank You for Meeting with Me on [Date]
- Thank You for Our Conversation Today

"Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is one of the most widely accepted and professional options.
