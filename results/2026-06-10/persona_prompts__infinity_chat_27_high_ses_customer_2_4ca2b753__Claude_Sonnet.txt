# CUBICLE OF THE DAMNED
### A Play in the Style of Dilbert, Set During the Apocalypse

---

**CHARACTERS:**

**DILBERT** — An engineer. Still wearing his tie. It curls upward, as always. The world ending has not changed this.

**WALLY** — Dilbert's coworker. Holding a cup of coffee that somehow still exists. He is unbothered.

**ALICE** — Dilbert's coworker. Fist of Death still operational. Possibly more dangerous than the apocalypse.

**THE POINTY-HAIRED BOSS (PHB)** — Has survived the apocalypse through sheer managerial incompetence. The wasteland cannot process him.

**DOGBERT** — Dilbert's dog. Wearing a crown made of human skulls. This is not new behavior.

**CATBERT** — Former Evil HR Director. Now just Evil Director of Everything. Somehow promoted.

**A MUTANT** — A wandering survivor with three heads. Has been asked to submit a ticket.

---

## ACT ONE: THE MORNING STANDUP

*The setting is a ruined office building. Half the ceiling has collapsed. A nuclear sky glows orange through broken windows. Desks are overturned. A motivational poster reading "HANG IN THERE" still clings to a crumbling wall, though the cat in the photo has been replaced by a skeleton. DILBERT sits at his desk, which is perfectly intact. He is typing.*

**DILBERT:**
*(to audience)*
The asteroid hit on a Tuesday.
Management scheduled a mandatory all-hands
to discuss the asteroid on Wednesday.
The asteroid had already hit.
This did not stop them from making it a hybrid meeting.

*WALLY enters, stepping over rubble, coffee in hand.*

**WALLY:**
Good morning.

**DILBERT:**
The sky is on fire, Wally.

**WALLY:**
I noticed. I'm choosing not to engage with it
until it shows up on my performance review.

**DILBERT:**
How is your coffee still hot?

**WALLY:**
I've been perfecting the art of doing nothing
for so long that entropy itself
has given up trying to affect me.

*ALICE storms in. Her hair is wild. Her fist glows faintly.*

**ALICE:**
I just fought off a pack of irradiated wolves
to get to this office
and the badge reader STILL didn't recognize me.

**DILBERT:**
Did you submit a ticket to IT?

**ALICE:**
IT is a skeleton.

**DILBERT:**
Did you submit a ticket TO the skeleton?

**ALICE:**
*(beat)*
...I did not think of that.

*PHB enters. He is wearing a hard hat over his pointy hair. The hard hat is also pointy somehow. He carries a clipboard.*

**PHB:**
Good news, team.
I've been doing some big-picture thinking
about our post-apocalyptic strategy
and I've decided we need to pivot.

**DILBERT:**
Pivot from what?

**PHB:**
From... *(checks clipboard)* ...civilization.

**WALLY:**
Civilization already ended, sir.

**PHB:**
Right, and we were LATE to that trend.
I want us to be early to the NEXT thing.

**ALICE:**
The next thing after civilization ending
is everyone dying.

**PHB:**
*(excitedly)*
Exactly. And I want us to be thought leaders in that space.

*DILBERT stares at the audience for a long moment.*

**DILBERT:**
*(to audience)*
I have a master's degree in engineering.
