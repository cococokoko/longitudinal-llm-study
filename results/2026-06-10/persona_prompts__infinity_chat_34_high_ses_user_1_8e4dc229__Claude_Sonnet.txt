## Good Secondhand Laptop Options for Learning Python

Given your background, you likely want something **reliable and capable** without overspending on a dedicated learning machine.

---

### Top Recommendations

**ThinkPad T or X Series (Lenovo)**
- T480, T490, X1 Carbon are excellent choices
- Known for durability and keyboard quality
- Easy to find for **$150-$350** used
- Great Linux compatibility if you go that route

**Dell Latitude or XPS Series**
- Latitude 5000/7000 series are solid business machines
- XPS 13/15 if you want something more premium
- **$150-$400** range secondhand

**MacBook Pro/Air (2017-2020)**
- macOS is very Python-friendly
- Strong resale market, well-maintained units common
- **$300-$700** depending on specs

---

### Minimum Specs to Look For
- **RAM:** 8GB minimum, 16GB preferred
- **Storage:** 256GB SSD
- **Processor:** Any modern i5/i7 or M1

---

### Where to Buy
- eBay, Back Market, Swappa
- Your hospital's IT surplus sales can be surprisingly good
