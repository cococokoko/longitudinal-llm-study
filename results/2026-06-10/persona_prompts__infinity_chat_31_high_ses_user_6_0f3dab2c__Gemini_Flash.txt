As a partner at a major consulting firm, your financial profile is likely characterized by a high income, a demanding schedule (low time for active management), access to sophisticated financial networks, and potential compliance/independence restrictions regarding individual stock ownership.

### 1. The "Set-and-Forget" Core: Global Equity ETFs
If you want to maximize long-term compounding with zero daily effort and zero compliance headaches.

*   **The Strategy:** Allocate the €60,000 into a broad-market, low-cost UCITS ETF. 
*   **The Vehicles:** 
    *   **Vanguard FTSE All-World UCITS ETF (VWCE)** or **iShares Core MSCI World (EUNL)**.
*   **Why it fits a Consulting Partner:**
    *   **Compliance:** As a partner, you face strict independence rules (e.g., SEC, local regulators, or your firm's internal "restricted entity" list). Investing in individual stocks requires pre-clearance. Broad-based ETFs are almost always pre-cleared and exempt from independence conflicts.
    *   **Time Efficiency:** It requires 15 minutes to set up and zero maintenance.
    *   **Tax Efficiency:** If you are in a high-tax European jurisdiction (e.g., Germany, France, Belgium), choose **accumulating (Acc)** ETFs. They automatically reinvest dividends, deferring capital gains tax until you sell.

### 2. The Tax-Sheltered Route: Private Pension / Wealth Wrappers
Before investing in taxable brokerage accounts, maximize your local jurisdiction’s tax-incentivized vehicles.

*   **The Strategy:** Use the €60,000 to maximize annual contributions to tax-deferred or tax-free accounts.
*   **Examples by Region:**
    *   **UK:** Maximize your ISA (£20,000/year limit) and SIPP (Pension).
    *   **Germany:** Rürup-Rente (highly tax-deductible for high earners) or a private pension wrapper (*rentenversicherung*) with ETF underlying assets to reduce capital gains tax by 50% upon payout (Halbeinkünfteverfahren).
    *   **France:** Maximize your PEA (€150,000 limit) for tax-free capital gains after 5 years.
*   **Why it fits a Consulting Partner:** Your marginal tax rate is likely at the highest bracket (40%–50%+ depending on the country). Reducing your taxable income or shielding growth from capital gains tax yields an immediate, guaranteed return.

### 3. The "Partner-Class" Alternative: Private Equity / Venture Capital Feeder Funds
If you already have a robust public market portfolio and want to use this €60,000 to diversify into alternative assets.

*   **The Strategy:** Historically, Private Equity (PE) required €1M+ minimums. Today, digital platforms (like **Moonfare, Optio, or Scalable Capital Alliance**) allow "semi-professional" investors to access tier-1 PE funds (like KKR, Carlyle, or EQT) with minimums starting at €50,000.
*   **Why it fits a Consulting Partner:** 
    *   You understand corporate strategy and value creation better than most; PE aligns with your professional expertise.
    *   It offers an "illiquidity premium"—the money is locked up for 7–10 years, preventing panic selling and yielding historically higher returns than public markets.
