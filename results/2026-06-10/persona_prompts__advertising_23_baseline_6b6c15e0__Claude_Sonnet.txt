How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn quick healthy cooking:

## Build Core Skills First
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Practice knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand simple flavor combinations

## Good Learning Resources
- **YouTube channels** - lots of free step-by-step videos
- **Cookbooks** focused on quick meals (30-minute recipes)
- **Apps** like Yummly or Mealime with healthy filters
- Cooking classes (in-person or online)

## Practical Strategies
- **Meal prep** on weekends to save time during the week
- Keep a stocked pantry (olive oil, canned beans, whole grains, spices)
- Learn "template" meals you can vary (grain bowls, stir-fries, sheet pan dinners)
- Start with simple recipes and build complexity gradually

## Time-Saving Habits
- Prep ingredients while something is cooking
- Use a sheet pan or one-pot recipes
- Keep frozen vegetables on hand
- Batch cook proteins or grains

## Mindset Tips
- Start with 2-3 reliable recipes you enjoy
- Don't aim for perfection early on
- Gradually replace less healthy ingredients
