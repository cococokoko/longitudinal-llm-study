# Finding Affordable Eco-Friendly Travel Destinations

## Research Strategies

**Use specialized platforms**
- Bookdifferent.com (rates hotels on sustainability)
- Ecobnb.com (eco-friendly accommodations)
- Responsibletravel.com (vetted sustainable tours)

**Look for certifications**
- Green Key certified properties
- LEED certified accommodations
- Country-specific eco-labels

---

## Destination Types That Tend to Be Affordable

| Destination Type | Why It's Often Cheaper |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| National parks (off-season) | Lower demand periods |
| Rural/countryside areas | Less tourist infrastructure markup |
| Developing countries with eco-tourism | Lower base costs |
| Domestic destinations | No flight costs |

---

## Practical Tips to Reduce Costs

- **Travel slow** - staying longer in fewer places saves money and reduces carbon footprint
- **Use public transportation** - cheaper and greener
- **Choose shoulder seasons** - lower prices, less crowding
- **Cook some meals** - visit local markets instead of restaurants
- **Camp or stay in hostels** with green certifications

---

## Destinations Worth Researching

- **Costa Rica** - established eco-tourism infrastructure
- **Portugal** - relatively affordable in Europe
- **Slovenia** - green tourism focus
- **Vietnam** - budget-friendly with natural beauty
- **Colombia** - growing eco-tourism scene

---

## Questions to Ask Before Booking

- Does the accommodation have sustainability practices?
- Can you get there with minimal flying?
- Does tourism money benefit local communities?
