# Effective Online Teaching Techniques

## Engagement & Interaction
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minutes max for video lectures)
- Use polls, quizzes, and chat features regularly
- Ask questions throughout rather than just at the end
- Create discussion forums with clear prompts
- Use breakout rooms for small group work

## Clear Communication
- Set explicit expectations for participation and deadlines
- Provide detailed instructions (students can't ask questions as easily)
- Use multiple formats - video, text, audio
- Establish regular office hours and response time commitments

## Active Learning Strategies
- Flipped classroom approaches
- Case studies and problem-solving activities
- Peer review assignments
- Project-based learning
- Real-world application tasks

## Technical Best Practices
- Keep technology simple and consistent
- Provide technical support resources upfront
- Record sessions for students in different time zones
- Test your setup before class

## Building Community
- Create informal social spaces
- Learn student names and backgrounds
- Acknowledge contributions publicly
- Use icebreakers and relationship-building activities

## Assessment
- Vary assessment types to reduce academic dishonesty concerns
- Provide frequent low-stakes feedback
- Use rubrics consistently

## What Works Best
Research suggests **regular feedback** and **social presence** have the strongest impact on student success.
