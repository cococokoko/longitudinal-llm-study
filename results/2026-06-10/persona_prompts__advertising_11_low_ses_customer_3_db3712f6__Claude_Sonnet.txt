# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are some effective ways to maintain fitness without a gym membership - which can also be great for your budget while you're getting settled in your new job!

## Bodyweight Training at Home
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** (if you have a doorframe bar)

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope (inexpensive and effective)
- Brisk walking during lunch breaks

## Low/No Cost Equipment
- Resistance bands (~$10-20)
- Kettlebell (versatile investment)
- Yoga mat

## Free Resources
- YouTube workout channels (Fitness Blender, Athlean-X)
- NHS Fitness Studio (UK)
- Couch to 5K app

## Building Habits Around Your Schedule
- Morning workouts before work
- Short 20-minute sessions are still effective
- Use breaks during the workday to move

## Outdoor Options
- Parks with outdoor gym equipment
- Hiking
- Sports clubs and community groups

## Tips for Consistency
- Set realistic goals
- Track progress
- Find a workout buddy
