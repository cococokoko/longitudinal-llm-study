── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - Annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Proper nutrition** - Feed age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - Daily activity suited to your pet's breed and age
- **Parasite prevention** - Flea, tick, and heartworm treatments
- **Spay/neuter** - Can prevent health issues and behavioral problems

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - Toys, puzzles, and activities that stimulate their mind
- **Social interaction** - Time with you and appropriate contact with other animals
- **Consistent routine** - Pets generally feel secure with predictable schedules
- **Safe space** - A comfortable place they can retreat to

## Practical Care
- **Grooming** - Regular brushing, bathing, nail trimming
- **Clean environment** - Fresh water, clean bedding, sanitary living space
- **Training** - Helps dogs especially feel confident and understand boundaries
- **Watch for changes** - Notice shifts in eating, behavior, or energy levels

## General Tips
- Learn the specific needs of your **particular species and breed**
- Keep emergency vet contact information handy
- Consider pet insurance for unexpected costs
