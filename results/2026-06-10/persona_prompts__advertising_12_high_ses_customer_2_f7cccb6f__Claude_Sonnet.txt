# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a practical overview of sustainable travel choices:

## Transportation
- **Train travel** - Significantly lower carbon footprint than flying
- **Electric vehicles** - For road trips, with growing charging infrastructure
- **Cycling/walking** - For shorter distances or urban exploration
- **Carpooling** - Reduces per-person emissions substantially
- **Direct flights** - When flying is necessary (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)

## Accommodation
- **Certified eco-lodges** - Look for LEED or Green Key certification
- **Locally-owned properties** - Keeps money in the community
- **Glamping** - Often lower environmental impact than traditional hotels

## Practical Habits
- Pack light (reduces fuel consumption)
- Use refillable water bottles
- Choose public transportation locally
- Eat at farm-to-table restaurants
- Offset carbon emissions through reputable programs

## Useful Resources
- **Google Flights** - Shows carbon estimates per route
- **Atmosfair** - Carbon offset calculator
- **Booking.com** - Filters for sustainable properties

## Worth Considering
