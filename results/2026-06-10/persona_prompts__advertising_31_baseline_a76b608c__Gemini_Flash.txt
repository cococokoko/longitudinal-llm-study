── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to find affordable eco-friendly travel destinations?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Finding affordable, eco-friendly travel destinations requires looking beyond mainstream tourism hotspots and focusing on places that prioritize conservation, community-based tourism, and low-impact living. 

Here is a comprehensive guide on how to find these destinations, followed by some of the best budget-friendly, eco-conscious spots in the world.

---

### 1. How to Find Affordable Eco-Destinations

To find these hidden gems, you need to change *how* and *where* you search. Use these strategies:

#### A. Look for "Under-tourism" and Secondary Destinations
Popular eco-destinations like Costa Rica or the Galápagos Islands are beautiful but highly commercialized and expensive. 
* **The Strategy:** Look for neighboring countries or secondary regions. Instead of Costa Rica, look at **Nicaragua** or **Guatemala**. Instead of Bali, look at **Lombok** or **Sumatra** in Indonesia. These places offer similar biodiversity and eco-lodges at a fraction of the cost.

#### B. Use Reliable Green Certifications
Greenwashing is common in the travel industry. To find truly eco-friendly destinations and accommodations that won't break the bank, look for these certifications:
* **Global Sustainable Tourism Council (GSTC):** The gold standard for sustainable tourism.
* **Green Key / EarthCheck:** Excellent for finding certified budget hotels and hostels.
* **Bookdifferent.com:** A hotel booking site that calculates the carbon footprint of your stay and highlights green-certified accommodations.

#### C. Prioritize Countries with Strong National Parks Systems
Countries that invest heavily in their national parks often offer incredibly cheap entry fees for world-class nature. 
* Camping or staying in state-run cabins inside national parks (e.g., in Chile, Poland, or the US) is often the cheapest
