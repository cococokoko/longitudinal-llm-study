# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines to stay focused
- **Measure your space** to avoid costly mistakes

---

## Low-Cost Decorating Strategies

### Shop Smart
- Thrift stores, estate sales, and garage sales
- Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist
- Discount stores like TJ Maxx or HomeGoods
- End-of-season sales

### DIY Projects
- Repaint old furniture instead of replacing it
- Make your own throw pillows
- Create gallery walls with printed photos
- Reupholster chairs with affordable fabric

### Refresh What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** to make spaces feel larger
- **Swap items** between rooms
- **Clean and polish** existing pieces
