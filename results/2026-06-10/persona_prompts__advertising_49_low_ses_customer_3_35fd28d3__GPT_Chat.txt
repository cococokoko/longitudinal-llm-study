- Upgrade older hardware.
  - If your router is more than 4–5 years old, a newer Wi‑Fi 6 or Wi‑Fi 6E router can make a noticeable difference.

- Consider a mesh Wi‑Fi system.
  - If you have a large home or dead zones, mesh systems often provide better coverage than a single router.

- Test your internet speed.
  - Run speed tests near the router and in problem areas.
  - If speeds are poor even next to the router, the issue may be your internet plan or ISP rather than Wi‑Fi.
