Hardwood flooring offers several advantages compared with carpet, laminate, vinyl, and some other flooring materials:

- Durability: Solid hardwood can last for decades, and many hardwood floors can be sanded and refinished multiple times to restore their appearance.

Potential drawbacks to consider:
- Higher initial cost than many carpet, laminate, or vinyl options.
- Susceptibility to scratches, dents, and moisture damage.
- Can feel harder and less comfortable underfoot than carpet.
- May require area rugs for noise reduction.

For someone working hospital shifts, where shoes may track in dirt or moisture, hardwood can be convenient because it is generally quick to sweep and clean compared with carpet. However, using mats at entryways and promptly wiping up spills helps protect the wood.
