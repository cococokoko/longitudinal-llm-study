── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective formal subject line options for thanking someone for their time:

## Direct Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time Today"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You for the [Date] Meeting"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Grateful for Your Time and Consideration"**

## Professional Variations
- **"Many Thanks for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for the Opportunity to Connect"**
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time"**

## Tips for Choosing
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the meeting topic or date)
- **Keep it concise** - short subject lines are easier to read
- **"Thank You for Your Time"** is generally the most universally appropriate option

The best choice depends on the **context** of your email, such as a job interview, business meeting, or professional conversation.
