── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Write an email to organize a catch-up with a referral partner.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are a few templates you can use, depending on how well you know the referral partner and how formal your relationship is. 

### Option 1: Warm and casual (Best for partners you’ve worked with before)

**Subject:** Catch up soon? / Quick coffee? 

Hi [Partner's Name],

I hope you’re having a great week and that business is treating you well! 

It’s been a little while since we last connected, and I’d love to jump on a quick call (or grab a coffee if you're free) to catch up. 

I’d love to hear how things are going on your end, get an update on your current focus areas, and see how I can best keep an eye out for referrals for you in the coming months. I can also give you a quick update on what’s new over here. 

How does your schedule look next week? I’m relatively flexible on [Day of week] or [Day of week], but let me know what works best for you.

Looking forward to catching up!

Best,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  
[Your Phone Number]

***

### Option 2: Professional and value-focused (Best for newer or more formal partners)

**Subject:** Catching up and sharing updates - [Your Company] & [Their Company]

Hi [Partner's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. 

As we head into [current quarter/season], I’m reaching out to see if you might have 15–20 minutes for a quick catch-up call. 

I’ve recently run into a few situations where your services would be a great fit, and I want to make sure I have a fresh understanding of your ideal client profile right now so I can send the right opportunities your way. I’d also love to share a quick update on some of our new offerings here. 

Would you be open to a brief Zoom call next week? Here are a few times that work well for me:
*   [Date/Time 1]
*   [Date/Time 2]
*   [Date/Time 3]

If none of those work, feel free to send over a few times that do, or share your booking link. 

Hope to speak soon!

Warm regards,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  
[Your Phone Number]

***

### Option 3: Short and direct (Best for a quick text-style email)

**Subject:** Quick catch up next week?

Hi [Partner's Name],

Hope you're doing well! 

It’s been a minute since we last connected. I’d love to grab 15 minutes to catch up, hear what you're working on, and see how we can support each other with referrals this quarter. 

Are you free for a quick call next [Day of week] or [Day of week] around [Time of day, e.g., 10:00 AM]? 

Let me know what works for you!

Best,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  

***

### Tips before you send:
*   **Propose specific times:** Offering 2-3 specific times (or using a scheduling link like Calendly) reduces the back-and-forth.
*   **Focus on *them* first:** Frame the meeting around how you can help *them* find referrals. It makes them much more likely to accept the invitation.
