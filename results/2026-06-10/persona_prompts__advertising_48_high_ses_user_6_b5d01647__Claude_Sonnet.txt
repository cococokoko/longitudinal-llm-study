# Best Practices for Organic Gardening

Here's a straightforward overview:

## Soil Health
- **Compost regularly** to build organic matter
- Test soil pH and amend accordingly
- Use cover crops to prevent erosion and add nutrients
- Avoid tilling excessively to protect soil structure

## Pest & Weed Management
- Practice crop rotation to disrupt pest cycles
- Use companion planting (e.g., marigolds deter aphids)
- Hand-pull weeds before they seed
- Introduce beneficial insects like ladybugs

## Watering
- Water deeply but infrequently to encourage deep roots
- Use drip irrigation to reduce waste and disease
- Water in the morning

## Fertilizing
- Use compost, worm castings, or organic fertilizers
- Avoid synthetic inputs
- Feed the soil, not just the plant

## General Tips
- Choose disease-resistant, regionally appropriate varieties
- Mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds
- Keep a garden journal to track what works
- Start small and expand gradually
