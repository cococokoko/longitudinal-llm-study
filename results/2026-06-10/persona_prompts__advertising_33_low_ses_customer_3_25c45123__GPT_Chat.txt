Making your own natural beauty products can be simple, but it's important to keep safety and hygiene in mind because homemade products don't usually contain preservatives and can spoil quickly.

Some beginner-friendly options:

• Simple facial oil
  - 2 tablespoons jojoba oil or sweet almond oil
  - Optional: 1–2 drops lavender essential oil
  - Mix in a clean glass bottle.
  - Apply a few drops to damp skin.

• Sugar lip scrub
  - 1 teaspoon sugar
  - 1 teaspoon honey or coconut oil
  - Mix and gently massage onto lips.
  - Rinse off after a minute.

• Oatmeal face mask
  - 1 tablespoon finely ground oats
  - 1 tablespoon plain yogurt or water
  - Mix into a paste.
  - Leave on for 10–15 minutes, then rinse.

• Body scrub
  - 1/2 cup sugar or fine sea salt
  - 1/4 cup coconut oil or olive oil
  - Optional: a few drops of essential oil
  - Store in a clean container and use within a few weeks.

• Simple hair mask
  - 1 ripe banana
  - 1 tablespoon honey
  - Blend thoroughly to avoid chunks.
  - Apply to hair for 15–20 minutes before washing out.

Safety tips:
• Sanitize containers and utensils before use.
• Patch-test any new product on a small area of skin for 24–48 hours.
• Be cautious with essential oils; some can irritate skin and should be heavily diluted.
• Avoid adding water to products unless you plan to use them immediately, as water encourages microbial growth.
• Discard products if they develop an unusual smell, color, or texture.
