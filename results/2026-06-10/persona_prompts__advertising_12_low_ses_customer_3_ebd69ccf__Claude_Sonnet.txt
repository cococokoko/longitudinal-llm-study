# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great sustainable travel choices:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Train travel** - significantly lower emissions than flying
- **Bus/coach** - one of the most efficient per-passenger options
- **Cycling** - zero emissions for shorter distances
- **Walking** - the most sustainable option locally
- **Electric vehicles** - especially when charged from renewable sources

## If You Must Fly
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Choose airlines with **newer, fuel-efficient fleets**

## Accommodation
- **Eco-certified hotels** and lodges
- **Locally owned** guesthouses
- **Camping** with minimal impact
- Properties with **renewable energy** and water conservation

## General Tips
- **Travel slower** - stay longer in fewer places
- Use **public transport** at your destination
- **Pack light** - less weight means less fuel
- Support **local businesses** and restaurants
- Avoid **single-use plastics**

## Planning Resources
- Rome2Rio for comparing transport emissions
- Google Flights carbon estimates
- Responsible Travel for eco-certified options
