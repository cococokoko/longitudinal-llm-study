## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) - critical for local search

- Ask satisfied customers for online reviews

**Networking**
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Attend community events and farmers markets
- Partner with complementary local businesses
- Word of mouth referrals with a simple incentive program

**Visible Presence**
- Yard signs or vehicle signage
- Flyers on community bulletin boards
- Sponsor local sports teams or events
- Local newspaper or radio ads

**Digital Targeting**
- Facebook/Google ads targeted by zip code
- Local SEO (using city/neighborhood names in your content)
- Email list of local customers

- **Referral programs** leverage existing happy customers
