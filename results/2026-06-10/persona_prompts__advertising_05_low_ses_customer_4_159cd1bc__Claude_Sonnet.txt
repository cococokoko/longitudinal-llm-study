# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Great topic, especially working shifts at a hospital - you need **reliable, nutritious food** that fits an unpredictable schedule!

## Core Budget-Friendly Strategies

### 🛒 Smart Shopping
- **Buy in bulk** - rice, oats, beans, lentils
- Shop **store brands** over name brands
- Use **frozen vegetables** - just as nutritious, much cheaper
- Plan meals around **weekly sales**

### 🍽️ Affordable Protein Sources
| Food | Approx. Cost | Benefit |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Eggs | Very low | Versatile, complete protein |
| Canned tuna/salmon | Low | Quick, no cooking needed |
| Dried beans/lentils | Very low | High protein + fiber |
| Chicken thighs | Moderate | Cheaper than breasts |

## Shift Worker Specific Tips

- **Batch cook on days off** - prep 3-4 days of meals at once
- Pack meals to **avoid vending machines** at the hospital
- Keep **grab-and-go snacks** ready (boiled eggs, fruit, nuts)
- Invest in a **good insulated bag**

## Simple Weekly Framework
- **Grains** - rice, oats, whole wheat bread
- **Protein** - rotate eggs, beans, chicken
- **Vegetables** - frozen mix + whatever's on sale
