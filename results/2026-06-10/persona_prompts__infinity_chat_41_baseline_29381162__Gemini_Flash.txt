Here are 3 short, actionable tips for self-development:

1. **Embrace the "Two-Minute Rule"**
If a task takes less than two minutes to do (like replying to an email, washing a dish, or putting away shoes), do it immediately. This builds momentum, prevents procrastination, and keeps your environment and mind clutter-free.

2. **Practice Active Reflection Daily**
Spend just 5 minutes at the end of each day asking yourself two questions: *What went well today?* and *What is one thing I can improve tomorrow?* This shifts your mindset from passive living to conscious growth.

3. **Protect Your First Hour**
Avoid checking your phone, social media, or emails for the first 60 minutes of your day. Use this time to read, exercise, meditate, or plan your day. How you spend your morning sets the tone and focus for your entire day.
