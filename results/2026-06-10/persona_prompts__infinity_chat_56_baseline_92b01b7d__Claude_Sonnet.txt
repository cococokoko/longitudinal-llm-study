Name an economic value of an additional year of schooling.

One well-documented economic value of an additional year of schooling is **higher earnings/wages**.

Research consistently shows that each additional year of education is associated with approximately a **8–10% increase in individual earnings** (a finding associated with economist George Psacharopoulos and others using Mincer earnings equations).
