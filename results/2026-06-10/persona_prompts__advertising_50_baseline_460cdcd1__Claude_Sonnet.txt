# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- Compare prices on multiple sites (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- Be **flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- Set price alerts for flights you're watching
- Use **incognito mode** when searching to avoid price increases

## Flights
- Fly into smaller/alternate airports
- Consider **budget airlines**
- Look for connecting flights vs. direct
- Use **travel rewards credit cards** and miles
- Check if flying one-way on different airlines is cheaper

## Accommodation
- Use **Airbnb, Vrbo, or hostels** instead of hotels
- Stay slightly outside the city center
- Consider **house swapping**
- Book refundable rates and watch for price drops
- Travel during **off-season**

## On the Ground
- Use public transportation
- **Cook some meals** instead of eating out every time
- Look for free attractions and activities
- Get a local SIM card to avoid roaming charges
- Use apps like **Too Good To Go** for cheap food

## Money
- Avoid airport currency exchange
- Use a **no foreign transaction fee** card
- Travel with a group to split costs
