# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause
*Both are normal — chronic versions need attention*

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based
- **Deep breathing** — slow exhale activates the nervous system's calm response
- **Mindfulness/meditation** — even 5-10 minutes daily makes a difference
- **Journaling** — writing worries down reduces their mental weight
- **Challenging negative thoughts** — ask "is this actually likely?"

### 🏃 Body-Based
- Regular **exercise** (one of the most effective tools)
- Consistent **sleep schedule**
- Limiting **caffeine and alcohol**
- Spending time **outdoors/in nature**

### 🤝 Social/Behavioral
- Talking to someone you trust
- Setting realistic **boundaries**
- Breaking tasks into **smaller steps**
- Reducing **news/social media** consumption

---

## When to Seek Help
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, insomnia)
- Feeling overwhelmed consistently
- **Therapy (especially CBT) and/or medication can be very effective**
