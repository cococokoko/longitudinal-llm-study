# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle cleanser** - Cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - Look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or vitamin C
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **SPF 30+** - Preferably with moisturizing properties

## Evening Routine

1. **Oil cleanser or micellar water** - To remove makeup/sunscreen
2. **Gentle cleanser** - Second cleanse if needed
3. **Hydrating toner**
4. **Treatment serum** - Peptides or niacinamide
5. **Rich moisturizer or face oil** - Squalane or rosehip oil work well
6. **Occasional retinol** - 1-2x per week (builds collagen)

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Shea butter
- Squalane

## Weekly Additions
- **Hydrating mask** (1-2x per week)
- **Gentle exfoliation** (1x per week max)

## Quick Tips
- Lukewarm water only
- Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp
- Consider a humidifier
- Stay well hydrated
