## The Honest Answer

**There is no single "best and most profitable day trading strategy of all time."** Anyone claiming otherwise is likely selling something.

## What the Evidence Actually Shows

- **~70-80% of day traders lose money** consistently (studies from FINRA, SEC, and academic research)
- Most "profitable strategies" suffer from **survivorship bias** - we hear about winners, not the majority who failed
- Strategies that worked historically often **stop working** once widely known (alpha decay)
- Transaction costs, spreads, and taxes **erode returns significantly**

## Strategies With Documented Track Records

Some approaches with legitimate research behind them:
- **Momentum trading** (short-term price continuation)
- **Mean reversion** (statistical arbitrage)
- **Market making** (requires institutional infrastructure)

## What Actually Works Consistently

Frankly, the most reliably profitable "strategies" involve:
- **Institutional advantages** (speed, data, capital)
- **Low-cost index investing** (not day trading)
