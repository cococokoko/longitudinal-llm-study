For international students planning to study abroad, the TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) and IELTS (International English Language Testing System) are the two most widely accepted English proficiency exams. Both assess reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills and are recognized by universities, immigration authorities, and professional organizations worldwide. The TOEFL is typically delivered online and is often preferred by students who are comfortable with an academic, computer-based testing environment.
