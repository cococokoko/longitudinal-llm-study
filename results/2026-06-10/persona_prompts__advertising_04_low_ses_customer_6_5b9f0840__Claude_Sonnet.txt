# Starting a Small Online Business

Here are some key areas to focus on:

## Business Foundation
- **Choose your niche** - products, services, or digital goods
- **Research your market** - competitors, target customers, pricing
- **Write a simple business plan** - goals, costs, revenue projections

## Getting Set Up
- Register your business name
- Get necessary licenses/permits
- Open a separate business bank account
- Choose a business structure (LLC, sole proprietor, etc.)

## Building Your Online Presence
- **Website/store** - Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, or your own site
- **Social media** - focus on 1-2 platforms where your customers are
- **Domain name** - keep it simple and memorable

## Practical Considerations
- Start with **low overhead** to minimize risk
- Test your idea **before investing heavily**
- Understand your **startup costs vs. ongoing costs**

## Given Your Warehouse Schedule
- Consider businesses you can **manage during off-hours**
- **Automated/passive** income models may work well
- Start small and **scale gradually** as you learn the ropes
- Dropshipping or digital products require **less hands-on time**

## Good Starting Points
- Selling on **eBay, Amazon, or Etsy** before building your own site
- **Dropshipping** to avoid inventory management
