# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some simple, popular DIY options:

## Skin Care

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Coconut oil or shea butter as a base
- Add a few drops of essential oil (lavender, rosehip)
- Apply sparingly to face

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coconut or olive oil
- Optional: lemon juice, vanilla extract

**Clay Face Mask**
- Bentonite or kaolin clay
- Mix with water, apple cider vinegar, or honey
- Apply for 10-15 minutes

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado
- Coconut oil
- Honey
- Leave in 20-30 minutes

**Dry Shampoo**
- Arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- Cocoa powder (for darker hair)

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax pellets
- Coconut oil
- Shea butter
- Melt together, pour into containers

## General Tips

- ✅ **Patch test** everything first
- ✅ Store in clean, airtight containers
- ✅ Use within a few weeks (no preservatives)
- ⚠️ Be cautious with essential oils - dilute properly
- ⚠️ Some ingredients can cause reactions
