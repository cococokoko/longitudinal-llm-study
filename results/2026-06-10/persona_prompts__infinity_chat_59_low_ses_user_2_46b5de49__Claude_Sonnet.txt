# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write tomorrow's top 3 priorities** on a sticky note
- **Clear your desk** to a clean starting point
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing

### Why This Works Well for Your Situation

As a single parent, your mornings are likely **already chaotic** with getting kids ready. When you arrive at school, you want to **hit the ground running** instead of spending mental energy figuring out where you left off.

Coming back to an **organized desk and a clear plan** means you can transition faster from parent mode to teacher mode.

### Bonus Benefit

It also helps you **mentally leave work at work** when you pick up your kids, because you know tomorrow is already handled.

---

This takes minimal time but can make a **real difference** on those days when everything feels overwhelming.
