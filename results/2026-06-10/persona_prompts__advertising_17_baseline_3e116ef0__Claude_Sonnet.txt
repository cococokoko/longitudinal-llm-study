── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I make my home more energy efficient?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to make your home more energy efficient:

## Heating & Cooling
- Seal air leaks around windows, doors, and outlets
- Add insulation to attic, walls, and basement
- Use a programmable or smart thermostat
- Service your HVAC system regularly
- Use ceiling fans to reduce AC/heating needs

## Windows & Doors
- Install weatherstripping
- Consider double-pane windows
- Use thermal curtains or blinds

## Lighting
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Use motion sensors or timers
- Take advantage of natural light

## Appliances & Electronics
- Choose Energy Star certified appliances
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Use power strips to eliminate standby power
- Run dishwashers and washing machines with full loads

## Water Heating
- Lower water heater temperature to 120°F
- Insulate water heater pipes
- Consider a tankless water heater

## Bigger Investments
- Solar panels
- Heat pump systems
- Energy-efficient windows replacement

## Simple Habits
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Air dry dishes and laundry
- Keep refrigerator coils clean

**Starting point:** Consider getting a **home energy audit** to identify where you're losing the most energy - many utility companies offer these free or at low cost.
