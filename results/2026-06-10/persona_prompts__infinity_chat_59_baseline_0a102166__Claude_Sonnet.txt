# Organization Tip for Teachers: The **Weekly Reset Routine**

Set aside **15-20 minutes every Friday afternoon** (or Sunday evening) to:

## What to Do:
- **Clear your desk** and put everything in its place
- **Review next week's lessons** so you're not scrambling Monday morning
- **Update your grade book** so it doesn't pile up
- **Write 3 priorities** for the upcoming week
- **Restock supplies** you'll need (copies, handouts, etc.)

## Why It Works:
- You start each week with a **clean slate mentally and physically**
- Small weekly maintenance prevents **overwhelming buildup**
- You feel more **in control** walking into Monday

---

## Bonus Practical Tip:
Use a **simple color-coding system** for your different class periods or subjects - different colored folders, bins, or sticky notes so papers never get mixed up between classes.

---

The biggest challenge for teachers is usually **paper management**, so having a clear system for *incoming work* (student assignments) vs. *outgoing materials* (handouts) can make a huge difference.
