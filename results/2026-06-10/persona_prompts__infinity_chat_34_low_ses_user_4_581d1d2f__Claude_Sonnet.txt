## Good Secondhand Laptops for Learning Python

For Python development, you don't need a powerful machine. Here are solid options by budget:

---

### **Budget (~$50-100)**
- **Lenovo ThinkPad T430/T440** - Very durable, easy to upgrade RAM
- **Dell Latitude E6440/E6540** - Reliable, widely available

### **Mid-Range (~$100-200)**
- **Lenovo ThinkPad T460/T470** - Great keyboards, good battery life
- **Dell Latitude 5480/5490** - Solid performance
- **HP EliteBook 840 G3/G4** - Business grade, reliable

---

### **What to Look For**
- **RAM:** At least **8GB** (16GB preferred)
- **Storage:** SSD strongly recommended over HDD
- **CPU:** Any Intel i5 or i7 from 2015 onward is fine
- **OS:** Linux runs great on older hardware and is Python-friendly

---

### **Where to Buy**
- eBay
- Facebook Marketplace
- Local computer repair shops
- Craigslist

---

### **Practical Tips**
- ThinkPads are generally the **most recommended** for beginners
- Avoid anything with less than 4GB RAM
- An SSD upgrade (~$30) makes a huge difference if it has an HDD
