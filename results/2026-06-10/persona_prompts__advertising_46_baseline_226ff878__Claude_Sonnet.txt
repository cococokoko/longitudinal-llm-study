# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition guidance
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - companion to the documentary
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - practical and affordable approach

## Websites & Apps
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based information from Dr. Greger
- **Forks Over Knives** (forksoverknives.com) - recipes and meal plans
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients to ensure you're meeting needs
- **Oh She Glows** - popular recipe blog

## Documentaries
- *What the Health*
- *Forks Over Knives*
- *The Game Changers*

## Practical Starting Tips
- Focus on **whole foods** rather than processed substitutes
- Watch key nutrients: **B12, iron, calcium, omega-3s, vitamin D**
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian**
- Start gradually if needed

## Communities
- Reddit: r/PlantBasedDiet, r/vegan
- Local meetup groups

**Worth noting:** Some resources have specific viewpoints, so cross-referencing with a healthcare provider is a good idea.
