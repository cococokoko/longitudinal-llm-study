### 1. Executive-Level Meal Delivery (Zero-Prep Nutrition)
If your primary constraint is time, outsourcing meal preparation ensures you get nutritionally complete, gourmet plant-based meals without the cognitive load of cooking.

*   **Sakara Life (Organic Meal Delivery):** The gold standard for high-performing professionals. It offers ready-to-eat, organic, plant-rich meals designed to improve digestion, boost energy, and enhance skin clarity. 
*   **Splendid Spoon or Daily Harvest:** Excellent for quick, nutrient-dense, plant-based smoothies, soups, and grain bowls that fit into a packed meeting schedule.
*   **Methodology:** A premium, customized meal service (available in select metro areas) that offers high-protein, plant-based options tailored to specific dietary goals.

### 2. The Science & Data (For Informed Decision-Making)
As a technology leader, you likely value data-driven insights. These resources provide the clinical and scientific backing for plant-based nutrition.

*   **NutritionFacts.org (Dr. Michael Greger):** A non-profit, science-based public service that provides free updates on the latest in nutrition research via bite-sized videos. It is entirely commercial-free and strictly data-driven.
*   **The Proof Podcast with Simon Hill:** A highly analytical podcast where the host (a nutritionist and physiotherapist) interviews world-class doctors, researchers, and athletes. The focus is heavily on longevity, cardiovascular health, and brain function.
*   **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger:** The definitive book on how a whole-food, plant-based diet can prevent and reverse the leading causes of disease. 

### 3. High-Performance & Longevity Blueprints
These resources focus on how plant-based nutrition fuels elite physical and mental performance.

*   **The Rich Roll Podcast:** Hosted by a plant-based ultra-endurance athlete and wellness advocate. Rich Roll interviews top performers, neuroscientists, and tech founders about optimization, mindfulness, and plant-based living.
*   **The Game Changers (Documentary):** Available on Netflix, this film focuses on elite athletes (including NFL players, UFC fighters, and Special Forces governement operatives) who transitioned to plant-based diets to improve recovery times and performance.
*   **Peter Attia, MD (The Drive Podcast):** While not strictly plant-based, Dr. Attia’s deep dives into longevity and metabolic health provide excellent frameworks for how to structure any diet (including plant-based) for lifespan and healthspan extension.

### 4. Frictionless Tech & Apps (For Your Stack)
Leverage these digital tools to track your nutrition and simplify grocery shopping.

*   **Cronometer (App):** The most precise tracker for biometrics and micro/macronutrients. It is highly valued by the biohacking and tech communities for its granular data on amino acid profiles and micronutrient intake.
*   **Dr. Greger’s Daily Dozen (App):** A simple, free checklist app that tracks the 12 essential foods you should consume every day for optimal health. It takes 30 seconds a day to use.
*   **PlateJoy:** An app that uses custom algorithms to generate personalized plant-based meal plans and automatically sends the ingredient list to Instacart or Amazon Fresh.

### 5. Culinary Inspiration (For When You Do Cook)

*   **Forks Over Knives:** Offers a massive database of whole-food, plant-based recipes, a meal-planning app, and cooking courses.
*   **The Korean Vegan (Joanne Lee Molinaro):** For incredibly rich, flavorful, and sophisticated plant-based meals that are perfect for hosting.
