# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor/natural settings that need less decoration
- Select a location accessible by public transportation

## Invitations & Paper
- Use digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Choose recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-forward or vegetarian menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Décor
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent décor rather than buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral alternatives

## Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Consider renting suits/tuxedos

## Practical Logistics
- Arrange group transportation for guests
- Choose carbon offset options for travel
- Use biodegradable confetti or bubbles
- Select a photographer who uses digital delivery

## Gifts
- Create a registry with sustainable brands
- Request charitable donations instead of gifts

Would you like more detail on any specific aspect?
