# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces (parks, gardens, farms)
- Pick a location accessible by public transit

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations (Paperless Post, Greenvelope)
- Use recycled or seed paper if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## 👗 Attire
- Rent or borrow wedding attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage
- Choose a dress you can rewear
- Support sustainable fashion brands

## 🌸 Flowers & Décor
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Rent décor instead of buying
- Use candles, fabric, or natural elements

## 🍽️ Food & Drink
- Serve locally sourced, seasonal menu
- Offer plant-based or vegetarian options
- Work with caterers to minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to shelters

## ♻️ Waste Reduction
- Use real dishes instead of disposables
- Set up clear recycling/compost stations
- Avoid single-use plastics

## 🎁 Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets, plants, or edible favors
- Request charitable donations instead of gifts
- Create a registry with sustainable brands

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Encourage carpooling
- Choose an electric or hybrid vehicle

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Carbon offset** your wedding emissions
- **Donate flowers** to hospitals after the event
- **Hire vendors** who share your values
