# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, effective recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, argan)
- Beeswax or shea butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, rosemary)
- Raw honey
- Apple cider vinegar
- Aloe vera gel
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds (exfoliants)

---

## Simple Recipes

### **Face Moisturizer**
- Mix jojoba oil + a few drops of lavender essential oil
- Apply a small amount to clean skin

### **Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gently scrub and rinse

### **Hair Mask**
- 1 egg + 2 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp honey
- Apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

### **Lip Balm**
- Melt beeswax + coconut oil + shea butter
- Pour into small containers, let set

### **Toner**
- Dilute apple cider vinegar with water (1:3 ratio)

---

## **Important Tips**
- **Patch test** everything first
- Use clean, sterilized containers
- Note that homemade products have **shorter shelf lives**
- Research any essential oil safety before use
