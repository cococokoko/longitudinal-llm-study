# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Dedicated workspace** – Use a specific area only for work to mentally separate work from home life
- **Minimize distractions** – Reduce noise, clutter, and visual interruptions
- **Good lighting and ergonomics** – Proper chair, monitor height, and lighting reduce fatigue
- **Reliable tech setup** – Stable internet, necessary tools, and backup plans

## Managing Your Time
- **Set consistent work hours** – Start and end at the same time daily
- **Time blocking** – Schedule specific tasks in defined time slots
- **Use the Pomodoro Technique** – 25 minutes focused work, 5-minute break
- **Prioritize tasks** – Tackle high-impact work when your energy is highest

## Staying Focused
- **Turn off non-essential notifications**
- **Use website blockers** (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- **Batch similar tasks** together
- **Set clear daily goals** each morning

## Maintaining Boundaries
- Communicate availability to household members
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Take real lunch breaks
- Log off completely at end of day

## Staying Connected
- Regular check-ins with colleagues
- Overcommunicate progress and updates
- Use video calls to reduce isolation

## Health Habits
- Schedule movement breaks
- Stay hydrated
- Protect sleep schedule
