# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Classical has nylon strings (easier on fingers), Electric requires an amp but has lighter strings
- **Get the basics** - picks, tuner, capo, extra strings
- **Tune your guitar** - Standard tuning: **E A D G B E** (low to high)

## First Things to Learn
1. **How to hold the guitar** properly
2. **Basic chord shapes** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
3. **Strumming patterns** - Simple down strokes first
4. **Finger placement** - Press close to the fret, use fingertips

## Building Skills
- Learn **simple songs** you enjoy early on
- Practice **chord transitions** smoothly
- Develop a **consistent practice routine** (even 15-20 min/day helps)
- Learn basic **music theory** gradually

## Common Beginner Challenges
- **Sore fingertips** - Normal at first, builds calluses over time
- **Buzzing strings** - Usually finger placement or pressure
- **Slow chord changes** - Improves with repetition

## Resources
- YouTube channels (JustinGuitar is highly recommended)
- Apps like Yousician or Fender Play
- Local lessons for personalized feedback
