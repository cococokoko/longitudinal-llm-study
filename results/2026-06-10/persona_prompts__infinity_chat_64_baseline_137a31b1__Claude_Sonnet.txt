**When advising international students on English proficiency testing, the choice between TOEFL and IELTS often comes down to destination, learning style, and institutional requirements.** The TOEFL iBT is predominantly preferred by universities in the United States and Canada, and it assesses academic English through an entirely computer-based format, making it well-suited for students comfortable with typing and reading on screen.
