# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What topics/skills do you want to be known for?
- Who is your target audience?
- What value do you offer that's distinct?
- What's your long-term goal? (career, business, influence)

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B audiences |
| Instagram | Visual content, lifestyle, creative work |
| X/Twitter | Thought leadership, commentary |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video-first, broad reach |
| Substack | Long-form writing, newsletters |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Pillars

Build around **3-5 consistent themes** related to your niche:
- Educational content
- Personal stories/experiences
- Behind-the-scenes
- Opinions/hot takes
- Curated insights

---

## Practical Growth Tips

- **Post consistently** over posting perfectly
- **Engage genuinely** - comment, reply, collaborate
- **Show your face/voice** - people connect with people
- **Document your journey** - not just expertise
- **Repurpose content** across platforms

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Copying others' style completely
- Disappearing for weeks, then posting in bursts
- Focusing only on follower count vs. engagement
- Ignoring your audience's responses

---

## What Actually Builds a Brand

> **Consistency + Authenticity + Value = Trust**

The strongest personal brands feel *recognizable* - people know what to expect from you.
