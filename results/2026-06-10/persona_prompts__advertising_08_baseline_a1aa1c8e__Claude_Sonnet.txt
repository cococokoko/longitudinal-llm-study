# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great ways to discover trails that match your needs:

## Online Resources
- **AllTrails** - Most popular app/website with reviews, maps, and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed trail data with community input
- **TrailLink** - Great for rail-trails and paved paths
- **Recreation.gov** - For national parks and federal lands

## What to Consider When Choosing a Trail

### Difficulty Factors
- **Distance** - Total mileage round trip
- **Elevation gain** - Steeper = harder
- **Terrain** - Rocky, rooted, or smooth
- **Exposure** - Sun, wind, or weather risk

### Personal Factors
- Your current fitness level
- Experience with hiking
- Who's coming (kids, dogs, elderly)
- Time available

## Tips for Finding Hidden Gems
- Ask at **local outdoor/REI stores**
- Join **local hiking clubs or Facebook groups**
- Check **state park websites**
- Talk to **park rangers**
- Look at **Google Maps satellite view**

## Safety Considerations
- Check recent trail conditions/reviews
- Look up weather forecasts
- Note cell service availability
- Read about wildlife in the area
