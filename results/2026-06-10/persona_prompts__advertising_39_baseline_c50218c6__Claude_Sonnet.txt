# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration. Many people quit because they start too ambitious.

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting in a chair works fine, no need for lotus pose
2. **Set a timer** so you're not checking the clock
3. **Focus on your breath** - notice the sensation of breathing in and out
4. **When your mind wanders** (it will), gently return attention to your breath
5. **Repeat** without judging yourself

## Practical Tips

- **Same time daily** builds the habit (morning often works well)
- **Quiet space** helps when starting out
- **Eyes closed or soft gaze** - whatever feels natural
- **Don't try to stop thoughts** - just observe them

## Common Beginner Mistakes
- Thinking you're "doing it wrong" when thoughts arise
- Expecting immediate calm
- Skipping days and giving up entirely

## Helpful Resources

- **Apps:** Headspace, Calm, Insight Timer (free)
- **YouTube:** search guided meditations for beginners
- **Books:** *Wherever You Go, There You Are* by Jon Kabat-Zinn

## Mindfulness Beyond Meditation
Practice paying full attention during everyday activities like eating, walking, or washing dishes.

**The goal isn't an empty mind - it's noticing where your mind goes.**
