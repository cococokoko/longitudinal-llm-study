Here are some effective approaches for learning a musical instrument online:

## Structured Learning Platforms
- **Yousician, Simply Piano, Fender Play** - app-based with immediate feedback
- **Coursera or Skillshare** - more structured course formats
- **YouTube channels** - many high-quality free lessons (search instrument-specific channels)

## Key Practices for Success

**Build consistency**
- Short daily sessions (15-30 minutes) beat occasional long ones
- Schedule it like a meeting you won't cancel

**Get quality instruction**
- Consider live online lessons with a real teacher via Zoom
- A teacher catches bad habits that apps miss

**Use deliberate practice**
- Focus on specific weak spots rather than playing through pieces
- Slow down difficult passages significantly

**Track progress**
- Record yourself periodically
- It reveals improvement you might not notice otherwise

## Practical Considerations
- Invest in decent equipment - a poor instrument makes learning harder
- Use a metronome consistently from the start
- Join online communities (Reddit has active instrument-specific groups)

## Honest Expectations
- Progress feels slow initially but compounds significantly
- Most adults learn faster conceptually but need more repetition for muscle memory
- Having a specific goal (a song you want to play) helps maintain motivation
