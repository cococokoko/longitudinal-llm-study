Here are some practical ways to find affordable eco-friendly travel destinations:

## Research Tools & Resources
- **Websites**: EcoTourism.org, Responsible Travel, Green Destinations
- **Certifications to look for**: LEED, Green Globe, Rainforest Alliance certified accommodations
- **Travel blogs** focused on sustainable budget travel

## Destination Strategies
- **Choose less-visited countries** - often cheaper and lower environmental impact from overtourism
- **Travel in shoulder seasons** - lower prices, reduced crowd pressure on ecosystems
- **Consider Eastern Europe, Southeast Asia, or Central America** for value + natural beauty

## Cost-Saving Approaches
- Stay in **eco-hostels or community-run lodges** rather than large resorts
- Use **slow travel** (fewer destinations, longer stays) - reduces flights and costs
- **Overland transport** where possible instead of multiple flights
- Cook locally sourced food rather than eating out constantly

## Practical Tips
- **Carbon offset programs** can be surprisingly affordable
- National parks often offer **annual passes** with good value
- **Volunteer programs** sometimes offset accommodation costs

- What region interests you most?
- Are you prioritizing wildlife, culture, or adventure?
- What's your rough budget range?
