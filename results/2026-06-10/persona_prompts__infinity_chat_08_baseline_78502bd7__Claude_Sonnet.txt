- **Antimicrobial** - it can inhibit bacterial growth

- **Natural sweetener** - produced through a fascinating biological process by bees

**Things to be realistic about:**
- Many health claims about honey are **exaggerated** or lack strong scientific evidence
- It's still essentially **sugar** and should be consumed in moderation
- "Superfood" marketing often overstates its benefits

**Culturally:**
- Honey has appeared in mythology, religion, and folklore across many cultures, sometimes associated with divine or special qualities
