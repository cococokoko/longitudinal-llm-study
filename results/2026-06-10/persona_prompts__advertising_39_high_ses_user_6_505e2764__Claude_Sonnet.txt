# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Here's a practical, straightforward guide:

## Start Simple

- **Begin with just 5-10 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Pick a consistent time (morning works well for many busy professionals)
- Find a quiet space where you won't be interrupted

## Basic Techniques to Try First

**Breath Focus**
- Sit comfortably, close your eyes
- Focus attention on your breathing
- When your mind wanders, gently return focus to breath

**Body Scan**
- Mentally scan from head to toe
- Notice tension without judgment

**Simple Counting**
- Count breaths from 1-10, then repeat

## Practical Tools

- **Apps:** Headspace, Calm, or Waking Up (Sam Harris) are well-regarded
- **Timer:** Use your phone so you're not clock-watching

## Realistic Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander - that's normal, not failure
- Benefits typically build over **weeks**, not days
- Research suggests even brief practice improves focus and stress management

## Fitting It Into a Demanding Schedule

- Try micro-sessions between meetings
- Use commute time for guided meditation
- Even **mindful breathing for 2 minutes** has measurable benefits

## What to Expect

- Reduced stress response
- Improved focus and decision-making
- Better emotional regulation
