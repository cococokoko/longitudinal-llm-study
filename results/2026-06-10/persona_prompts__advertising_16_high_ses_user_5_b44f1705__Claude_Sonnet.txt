# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

## Why Your Role Creates Unique Pressures
- **High-stakes decisions** with significant consequences
- **Always-on culture** and constant connectivity
- **Public scrutiny** of performance and decisions
- **Responsibility** for employees, shareholders, and customers
- **Rapid change** requiring constant adaptation

---

## Practical Strategies

### **Immediate Relief**
- Box breathing (4-4-4-4 counts)
- Brief walks between meetings
- Phone-free lunch breaks
- 5-minute mindfulness between high-pressure sessions

### **Structural Habits**
- **Protect sleep** - non-negotiable recovery time
- **Exercise consistently** - even 20-30 minutes daily
- **Calendar boundaries** - block genuine recovery time
- **Delegate deliberately** - identify what only *you* can do

### **Cognitive Approaches**
- Separate **urgent from important**
- Challenge catastrophic thinking
- Define what "good enough" looks like
- Regular journaling to process decisions

### **Professional Support**
- Executive coach
- Therapist familiar with high-performance environments
- Peer groups with other executives

---

## Worth Considering
- Stress often signals **misalignment** worth examining
- Modeling healthy boundaries **benefits your entire organization**
- Burnout is a **business risk**, not just personal
