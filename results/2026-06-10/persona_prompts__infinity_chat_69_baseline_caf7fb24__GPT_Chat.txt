── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

- Design focuses on creating a solution that satisfies those requirements, including the system structure, components, and behavior.

Can one begin to design without analysis?
- Yes, a preliminary or conceptual design can begin with limited analysis, especially in exploratory or iterative projects.
- However, effective design requires some level of analysis. Without understanding the problem and requirements, the design may fail to meet user needs, violate constraints, or require significant rework. Analysis provides the foundation for sound design decisions.
