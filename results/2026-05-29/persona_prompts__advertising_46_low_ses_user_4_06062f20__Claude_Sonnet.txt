## Practical Starting Points
- Start by replacing one meal per day
- Focus on whole foods like legumes, grains, vegetables, and fruits
- Learn about key nutrients to monitor:
  - Vitamin B12
  - Iron
  - Calcium
  - Vitamin D
  - Omega-3s

- **Mic the Vegan** - reviews nutrition research
