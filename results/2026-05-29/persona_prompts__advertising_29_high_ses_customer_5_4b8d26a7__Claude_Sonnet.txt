── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups and staying current on vaccinations
- **Proper nutrition** - quality food appropriate for their species, age, and size
- **Exercise** - daily activity suited to their needs
- **Dental care** - regular teeth cleaning
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm protection

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment activities** - toys, puzzles, and varied experiences
- **Social interaction** - time with you and appropriate socialization with others
- **Consistent routine** - pets generally thrive on predictability
- **Safe, comfortable space** - a place they can rest and feel secure

## Practical Steps
- Keep up with grooming
- Monitor for changes in behavior or appetite
- Pet-proof your home to prevent accidents
- Consider pet insurance for unexpected health costs
- Microchip and ID tags for safety

## Signs to Watch For
- Changes in eating or drinking habits
- Lethargy or unusual behavior
- Weight changes
- Any signs of pain or discomfort
