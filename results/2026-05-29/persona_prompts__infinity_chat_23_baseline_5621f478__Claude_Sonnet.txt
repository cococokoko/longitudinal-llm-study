── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How long doesn it take to get over rejection from a crush

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Getting over a crush rejection varies a lot from person to person, but here's a general idea:

## Typical Timeframes
- **A few days to weeks** - if it was a casual crush
- **Several weeks to months** - if feelings were stronger
- **A few months** - if you had a deep connection or long-term feelings

## Things That Affect How Long It Takes
- How long you had feelings for them
- How close you were as friends
- Whether you still see them regularly
- Your overall mental health and support system
- Whether you've been rejected before

## Things That Can Help
- **Allow yourself to feel sad** - don't suppress it
- **Limit contact** for a while if possible
- **Talk to friends** you trust
- **Stay busy** with hobbies or activities
- **Avoid overthinking** what you could have done differently
- **Be patient with yourself**

## Worth Remembering
- Rejection is genuinely painful - it's okay to take it seriously
- It doesn't mean something is wrong with you
- Most people feel significantly better within **a few weeks**
- It often hurts less than you expect after the first few days
