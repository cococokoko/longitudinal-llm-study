# Best Books for Design and Analysis of Algorithms (2023)

## 🏆 Top Tier (Most Recommended)

### 1. **Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)**
- *Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein* — 4th Edition (2022)
- The **gold standard** textbook
- Covers sorting, graphs, dynamic programming, NP-completeness
- Very rigorous with proofs
- ⚠️ Dense for beginners

---

### 2. **Algorithm Design**
- *Kleinberg & Tardos*
- Excellent **intuitive explanations**
- Great for greedy, divide & conquer, network flow
- More readable than CLRS

---

### 3. **Algorithms** *(free online)*
- *Jeff Erickson*
- **Free PDF** at jeffe.cs.illinois.edu
- Modern, well-written, highly recommended

---
