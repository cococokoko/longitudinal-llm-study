## 🌱 Organic Gardening Best Practices

### **Soil Health**
- Compost regularly to add nutrients
- Use mulch to retain moisture and suppress weeds
- Avoid tilling excessively to protect soil structure
- Test soil pH and amend naturally

### **Pest & Weed Management**
- Use companion planting (e.g., marigolds deter pests)
- Introduce beneficial insects like ladybugs
- Hand-pull weeds before they seed
- Use neem oil or insecticidal soap for pests

### **Watering**
- Water deeply but less frequently
- Water at the base of plants, not leaves
- Use drip irrigation when possible
- Collect rainwater

### **Fertilizing**
- Use compost, worm castings, or fish emulsion
- Avoid synthetic fertilizers
- Feed soil, not just plants

### **Planning**
- Rotate crops each season
- Choose disease-resistant varieties
- Start with what grows well in your climate
