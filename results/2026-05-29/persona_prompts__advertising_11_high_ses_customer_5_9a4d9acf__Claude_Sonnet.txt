# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are practical, effective approaches that fit a busy executive's schedule:

## High-Impact, Time-Efficient Options

**Bodyweight Training**
- Push-ups, squats, lunges, planks
- 20-30 minutes delivers real results
- No equipment, do it anywhere

**HIIT Workouts**
- Maximum results in minimal time (15-20 min)
- Numerous free apps and YouTube programs
- Hotel-room friendly for travel

## Building Activity Into Your Day

- **Walking meetings** - effective and underutilized
- **Stairs over elevators** - consistent habit
- **Standing desk** - reduces sedentary time significantly
- **10-minute movement breaks** between calls

## Equipment Worth Having at Home

| Item | Cost | Benefit |
|------|------|---------|
| Resistance bands | ~$20 | Versatile, portable |
| Kettlebell | ~$40-80 | Full-body efficiency |
| Pull-up bar | ~$30 | Upper body strength |

## Structured Programs to Consider

- **Peloton/Apple Fitness+** - structured, on-demand
- **7-Minute Workout** - research-backed, minimal time
- **Running** - simple, scalable, clears the mind

## The Key Principle

**Consistency beats intensity.** Even 20-30 minutes daily outperforms occasional long sessions.
