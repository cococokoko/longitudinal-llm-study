# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are some practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor or naturally beautiful spaces that need less decoration

## Invitations
- Use digital invitations or recycled paper
- Seed paper invitations guests can plant afterward
- Minimize paper RSVPs

## Food & Catering
- Source locally and seasonally
- Choose plant-forward menus
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (not biodegradable)

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose natural fiber fabrics
- Plan to rewear or donate afterward

## Favors
- Skip favors or choose consumable/plantable options
- Donate to an environmental charity in guests' names

## Logistics
- Organize group transportation
- Choose vendors who share your values
- Use a digital wedding website

## After the Wedding
- Compost floral arrangements
- Donate decorations
