## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) - critical for local search
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood-level reach
- Facebook/Instagram local community groups
- Ask satisfied customers for online reviews

**Networking**
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Attend community events and farmers markets
- Partner with complementary local businesses
- Word of mouth referrals with a simple incentive program

**Visible Presence**
- Yard signs or vehicle wraps if applicable
- Flyers on community bulletin boards
- Sponsor local sports teams or events
- Local newspaper or community newsletter ads

**Digital Basics**
- Simple website with your location and contact info
- Consistent social media posting
- Email list of past customers

**What tends to work best depends on:**
- Your specific type of business
- Your target customer age group
- Your budget
- Whether customers find you online vs. in person

**The most important starting point** is usually claiming and optimizing your Google Business Profile since most people search locally before buying.
