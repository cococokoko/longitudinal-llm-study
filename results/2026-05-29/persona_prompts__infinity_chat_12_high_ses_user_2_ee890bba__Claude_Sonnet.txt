# Comparing Expat Insurance: A Senior Attorney's Guide

As a senior attorney, you likely have specific professional and personal considerations that make thorough due diligence on expat insurance particularly important.

---

## 🔍 Core Coverage Elements

### Medical Coverage
- **Inpatient vs. outpatient** coverage distinctions
- **Annual coverage limits** (look for $1M+ for serious jurisdictions)
- **Pre-existing condition** exclusions and waiting periods
- **Maternity coverage** terms and limitations
- **Mental health** coverage parity
- **Dental and vision** riders

### Geographic Coverage
- **Coverage territory** definitions (regional vs. worldwide)
- **USA coverage** inclusion (significantly raises premiums)
- **Home country** visit coverage duration limits
- **War/conflict zone** exclusions relevant to travel patterns

---

## ⚖️ Professional Considerations

### Liability & Professional Risks
- Coordination with your **professional liability/malpractice** coverage
- **Business travel** vs. personal travel distinctions
- **Employer-sponsored vs. individual** policy gaps
- Coverage during **client meetings abroad**

### Income Protection
- **Disability coverage** while working internationally
- **Medical evacuation** that accounts for professional obligations
- **Business interruption** implications

---

## 📋 Policy Language & Legal Terms

### Critical Clauses to Review
- **Definition of "emergency"** - often narrowly construed
- **Subrogation rights** language
- **Choice of law and jurisdiction** provisions
- **Arbitration clauses** - binding vs. non-binding
- **Rescission provisions** for material misrepresentation
- **Notice requirements** for claims
- **Coordination of benefits** language with other policies

### Red Flags
- Vague **"reasonable and customary"** charge definitions
- Broad **"experimental treatment"** exclusions
- Ambiguous **pre-authorization** requirements
- **Unilateral modification** rights by insurer

---

## 💰 Financial & Underwriting Factors

### Insurer Stability
- **AM Best or S&P ratings** (look for A- or better)
- **Reinsurance arrangements**
- **Claims payment history** and reputation
- **Regulatory jurisdiction** of the insurer
- **Years in expat market** specifically

### Premium Structure
- **Deductible options** and their impact
- **Co-insurance percentages**
- **Premium escalation** clauses with age
- **Currency denomination** of premiums and benefits
- **Renewal guarantees** vs. discretionary renewal

---

## 🏥 Practical Claims Considerations

### Network & Access
- **Direct billing** arrangements with hospitals
- **Quality of provider network** in your specific location
- **24/7 assistance line** quality and language support
- **Pre-authorization** process efficiency
- **Claims processing** timeline standards

### Evacuation & Repatriation
- **Medical evacuation** triggers and coverage limits
- **Repatriation of remains** coverage
- **Security evacuation** (political/civil unrest) - separate from medical
- **Accompanying family member** provisions

---

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Family & Dependent Coverage

- **Dependent eligibility** definitions
- **Child age limits** for coverage
- **Spouse/partner** coverage terms
- **Domestic partner** recognition
- **Educational interruption** benefits for children

---
