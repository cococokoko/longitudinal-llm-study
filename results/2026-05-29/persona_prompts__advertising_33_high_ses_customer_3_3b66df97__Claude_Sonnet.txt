# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options with simple recipes:

## Skincare

**Basic Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, and cool in a clean jar

**Simple Toner**
- Witch hazel + rose water (1:1 ratio)
- Apply with cotton pad after cleansing

**Exfoliating Scrub**
- Brown sugar + coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gentle enough for face and body

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil + tea tree essential oil
- Massage in, leave overnight

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Practical Tips

- **Sanitize** all equipment and containers thoroughly
- **Patch test** new products before full use
- **Label** everything with ingredients and date
- **Refrigerate** products without preservatives
- **Small batches** reduce waste and spoilage
- Research any essential oils before use (some cause irritation)
