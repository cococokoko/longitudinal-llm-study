# Social Media Tips for Hospital Patient Care Aides

Here are some important points to keep in mind regarding social media in your role:

## ⚠️ Critical Boundaries to Maintain

- **Never share patient information** - names, conditions, photos, room numbers, or anything identifiable
- **HIPAA compliance** is legally required - violations can result in termination and legal consequences
- **No photos on the unit** - even seemingly innocent background details can expose patient privacy

## ✅ What You Can Generally Share

- General experiences about your work environment (without patient details)
- Professional achievements and certifications
- Advocacy for healthcare workers
- General wellness and caregiving tips

## 🤔 Things to Consider

- **Your employer likely has a social media policy** - worth reviewing carefully
- Posts made on personal time can still affect your employment
- Venting about difficult shifts can be misinterpreted
- Colleagues and patients may see your posts

## 💡 Best Practices

- When in doubt, **don't post**
- Use privacy settings on personal accounts
- Separate professional and personal online presence
- Think before posting - *"Would my supervisor be comfortable seeing this?"*
