# Comparing Expat Insurance as a Single Parent

Here are the **key things to prioritize** given your specific situation:

---

## 👶 Child Coverage Essentials

- **Age limits** for dependent children coverage
- Whether children are **automatically included** or cost extra
- **Pediatric care** coverage quality
- **Vaccinations and routine checkups** included
- Coverage for **child-specific conditions**

---

## 🏥 Core Medical Coverage

- **Inpatient vs outpatient** coverage levels
- **Maternity/ongoing care** if relevant
- **Mental health support** - important for stressed single parents
- **Emergency evacuation** coverage
- **Pre-existing conditions** policy

---

## 💼 Work & Childcare Specific Needs

- **24/7 customer support** - you may need help outside business hours
- **Direct billing** to hospitals so you handle less paperwork
- **Telemedicine options** - easier when you cannot easily leave home
- **Simple claims process** - you have limited admin time

---

## 💰 Financial Considerations

- **Premium vs deductible** balance
- **Annual limits** per person or per family
- **Co-payment amounts**
- Whether **single parent family rates** are available

---

## ✅ Practical Tips

- Get **quotes showing total family cost**
- Read reviews about **claims experience**
- Check **network hospitals** near your location
- Ask specifically about **solo parent scenarios**
