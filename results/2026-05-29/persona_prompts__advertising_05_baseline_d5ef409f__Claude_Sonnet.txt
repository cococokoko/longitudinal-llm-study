# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

## Core Principles

- **Plan before you shop** — reduces impulse buying and waste
- **Cook at home** — restaurant meals cost 3-5x more
- **Embrace whole foods** — less processed = cheaper + healthier
- **Use everything** — minimize food waste

---

## Budget-Friendly Healthy Foods

### Proteins
- Eggs
- Canned tuna/salmon
- Dried or canned beans & lentils
- Chicken thighs (cheaper than breasts)
- Tofu

### Grains & Starches
- Oats
- Brown rice
- Whole wheat pasta
- Sweet potatoes

### Produce
- Frozen vegetables (just as nutritious)
- Seasonal fresh produce
- Bananas, apples, cabbage, carrots

---

## Practical Strategies

**Batch cooking**
- Cook large portions on weekends
- Portion into meals for the week

**Stretch ingredients**
- One rotisserie chicken → 3 different meals
- Big pot of beans → soups, tacos, salads

**Shop smart**
- Buy store brands
- Check unit prices
- Shop sales and freeze extras

---

| Day | Meal Idea |

| Monday | Lentil soup + bread |
| Tuesday | Rice & bean bowls |

- ✅ Eat before grocery shopping
- ✅ Make a list and stick to it
- ✅ Compare fresh vs. frozen vs. canned
- ✅ Reduce meat portions — add beans instead
