How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for minimalist apartment decorating on a budget:

## Core Principles
- **Less is more** - focus on a few quality pieces rather than lots of items
- Choose a simple, cohesive color palette (2-3 colors works well)

## Budget-Friendly Ideas

**Furniture & Layout**
- Thrift stores and Facebook Marketplace for quality secondhand pieces
- Multifunctional furniture (storage ottomans, fold-out desks)
- Arrange what you have thoughtfully before buying anything new

**Walls & Decor**
- Simple framed prints (even free printable art works)
- One or two plants add life without clutter
- Mirrors create space and light inexpensively

**Lighting**
- Swap harsh overhead lights for warm-toned bulbs
- Inexpensive floor or table lamps dramatically change atmosphere

**Practical Steps**
- Declutter first - minimalism costs nothing
- Use what you already own creatively
- Shop sales at IKEA, Target, or similar stores
- DIY simple projects (painted pots, simple shelving)

## Free Resources
- Pinterest for inspiration boards
- YouTube tutorials for DIY projects
- Local "buy nothing" groups for free items

The biggest minimalist win is usually **removing things** rather than buying them. What rooms are you focusing on?
