Here are some practical tips for cooking healthy meals quickly, especially given your busy household responsibilities:

## Planning & Prep
- **Meal plan weekly** so you know exactly what you're making
- **Batch cook on weekends** - grains, proteins, and roasted vegetables store well
- **Prep ingredients ahead** - chop vegetables, marinate proteins the night before

## Smart Cooking Methods
- Sheet pan meals - everything roasts together with minimal cleanup
- One-pot dishes like soups, stews, and stir-fries
- Pressure cooker or Instant Pot for fast cooking of beans and tough cuts
- Stir-frying cooks most meals in under 15 minutes

## Efficient Strategies
- Keep a well-stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, frozen vegetables)
- Learn 5-6 reliable base recipes you can vary with different ingredients
- Double recipes and freeze portions for busy nights
- Use rotisserie chicken as a time-saving protein base

## Scaling for Multiple People
- Invest in larger pots and sheet pans
- Simple dishes like grain bowls, tacos, and pasta are easy to scale up
- Let family members customize toppings to satisfy different preferences

## Good Starting Resources
- YouTube cooking channels focused on weeknight meals
- Budget Bytes website has practical, family-friendly recipes
