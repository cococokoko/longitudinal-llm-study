# Mastering Digital Photography

Here's a structured guide to help you develop strong photography skills:

---

## **Core Technical Foundations**

### Exposure Triangle
- **Aperture** (f-stop) — controls depth of field and light intake
- **Shutter Speed** — freezes or blurs motion
- **ISO** — sensor sensitivity; higher = more noise

### Key Modes to Master
- **Manual (M)** — full creative control
- **Aperture Priority (Av)** — great for portraits/landscapes
- **Shutter Priority (Tv)** — ideal for action shots

---

## **Composition Principles**
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines
- Framing within frames
- Negative space
- Light direction and quality

---

## **Post-Processing**
- **Lightroom** — organization and color grading
- **Photoshop** — detailed editing and compositing
- Shoot in **RAW** for maximum editing flexibility

---

## **Practice Strategies**
- Shoot daily with intentional goals
- Study photographers you admire
- Join critique communities
- Recreate shots you love to understand technique

---

## **Gear Priorities**
- Invest in **lenses** over camera bodies
- Prime lenses teach discipline and deliver sharpness
