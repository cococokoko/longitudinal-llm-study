── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn cooking healthy meals quickly:

## Build Core Skills First
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Practice knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand how to season food well

## Start Simple
- Begin with 3-5 ingredient recipes
- Master a few reliable meals before expanding
- Focus on one cuisine style at a time

## Use Good Resources
- YouTube channels (Joshua Weissman, Ethan Chlebowski, Budget Bytes)
- Apps like Yummly or Mealime
- Books like *Salt Fat Acid Heat* by Samin Nosrat

## Work Smarter
- **Meal prep** on weekends to save weekday time
- Keep a stocked pantry (olive oil, canned beans, whole grains, spices)
- Use sheet pan or one-pot recipes to reduce complexity
- Batch cook proteins and grains to mix and match

## Healthy Shortcuts
- Frozen vegetables are nutritious and fast
- Rotisserie chicken saves time
- Pre-washed salad greens
- Canned fish (salmon, tuna, sardines)

## Stay Consistent
- Cook the same meals repeatedly until they feel easy
- Gradually try new recipes as confidence grows

**The biggest factor is just practicing regularly** - even 2-3 times a week builds skills quickly.
