── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Basic Health Care
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm treatments
- **Spay/neuter** if appropriate
- **Keep up with medications** as prescribed

## Nutrition
- Feed **quality food** appropriate for their species, age, and size
- Provide **fresh water** always
- Avoid overfeeding - obesity is a common health problem
- Know which **human foods are dangerous** for your pet

## Exercise & Mental Stimulation
- Regular **walks and playtime**
- Toys and puzzles to keep them mentally engaged
- Social interaction with people and other animals when appropriate

## Environment
- Safe, comfortable **shelter**
- Clean living spaces
- Remove **household hazards** (toxic plants, chemicals, small objects)

## Emotional Wellbeing
- Spend **quality time** with them daily
- Learn to read their **body language**
- Provide **routine** - pets generally thrive on consistency
- Give them **attention and affection**

## Watch for Warning Signs
- Changes in eating, drinking, or behavior
- Unusual lethargy or aggression
- Physical symptoms like limping or discharge
