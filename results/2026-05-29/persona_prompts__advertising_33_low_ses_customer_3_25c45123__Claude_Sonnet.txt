# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options that are simple and effective:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Coconut oil or shea butter as a base
- Add a few drops of essential oils (lavender, tea tree)
- Mix and store in a clean jar

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coconut oil
- Optional: lemon juice or vanilla extract

**Honey Face Mask**
- Raw honey applied directly
- Leave on 15-20 minutes
- Rinse with warm water

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado
- Coconut oil
- Apply, leave 30 minutes, rinse

**Dry Shampoo**
- Arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- Cocoa powder (for darker hair)

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax
- Coconut oil
- Shea butter
- Melt, mix, pour into containers

## General Tips

- **Start simple** with few ingredients
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- **Patch test** before full use
- Store properly to avoid spoilage
- Research any **allergies** beforehand
- Add **vitamin E oil** as a natural preservative
