# Best Fitness & Nutrition Apps for Warehouse Workers

Great question! Since you're doing physical work with long hours, your needs are a bit different from typical gym-goers.

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - Most popular, huge food database, easy barcode scanning
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking, good for making sure you're replacing what you burn
- **Lose It!** - Simple interface, good for quick logging during breaks

## Fitness & Activity Tracking
- **Google Fit** or **Apple Health** - Free, tracks steps and activity automatically
- **Fitbit app** - Great if you wear a Fitbit device
- **Strava** - Better for structured workouts outside work

## Recovery & Wellness (Important for Physical Jobs)
- **Whoop** - Tracks recovery and strain, helpful for demanding physical work
- **Sleep Cycle** - Sleep quality matters a lot when you're physically active all day

## Practical Tips for Your Situation
- **Hydration tracking** is especially important in warehouse environments
- Look for apps with **quick-log features** since you may have limited break time
- Consider tracking **protein intake** specifically to support muscle recovery

## My Honest Recommendation
**MyFitnessPal + Apple/Google Health** is a solid free starting point that covers most bases.
