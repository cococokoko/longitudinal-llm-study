── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I am a partner at a major consulting firm.

I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some good free options for hosting your HTML resume site:

## Easiest Options

**GitHub Pages**
- Free and reliable
- Create a free GitHub account
- Upload your HTML files to a repository
- Enable GitHub Pages in settings
- Your site will be at `username.github.io`

**Netlify**
- Very beginner friendly
- Go to netlify.com and create a free account
- Simply **drag and drop your folder** of HTML files
- Get a live URL instantly

**Google Drive** *(simplest but limited)*
- Less professional looking URL

---

## My Recommendation

**Netlify is probably your best bet** because:
- No coding or command line required
- Takes about 2 minutes
- Looks professional
- You can connect a custom domain later if you want (like yourname.com)

---

## Quick Netlify Steps
1. Go to **netlify.com**
2. Sign up for free
3. Click **"Add new site"**
4. Drag and drop your HTML folder
5. Share the link

---

**One tip** - if you want a more professional URL like `yourname.com`, you can buy a domain for ~$10-15/year and connect it to any of these free hosts.
