── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Getting over a crush rejection varies a lot by person, but here's what's generally true:

## Typical Timeline
- **A few days to weeks** for a casual crush
- **Several weeks to months** for someone you had strong feelings for
- **Longer** if you have to see them regularly (work, school, etc.)

## Factors That Affect It
- How long/intense the feelings were
- Whether you have to keep seeing them
- Your support system
- How you process emotions generally

## Things That Actually Help
- **Letting yourself feel it** rather than suppressing it
- Staying socially connected
- Keeping busy with meaningful activities
- Avoiding excessive social media checking on them
- Giving yourself some distance if possible

## What Doesn't Help Much
- Replaying conversations looking for what went wrong
- Hoping they'll change their mind (usually prolongs it)
- Jumping immediately into another situation to distract yourself

## Honest Reality
Most people feel significantly better within **4-8 weeks**, though some lingering feelings can last longer. It rarely feels as permanent as it does in the first few days.
