# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical strategies to make your space look great without overspending:

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Repurpose items** from other rooms
- **Declutter first** - less can look more intentional

## Low-Cost Updates with High Impact
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest, most transformative changes
- **New throw pillows or blankets** to update color schemes
- **Swap hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **Update lighting fixtures** or add lamps

## Smart Shopping
- Thrift stores and estate sales
- Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist
- End-of-season sales
- Dollar stores for small decorative items

## DIY Options
- Frame interesting prints or personal photos
- Create gallery walls
- Refinish or paint old furniture
- Make simple curtains

## Free Resources
- **Library books** for design inspiration
- **Pinterest** for planning
- **Freecycle groups** in your community

## Key Principles to Keep in Mind
- Focus on **one room at a time**
- Prioritize **high-traffic areas**
- Stick to a **cohesive color palette**
