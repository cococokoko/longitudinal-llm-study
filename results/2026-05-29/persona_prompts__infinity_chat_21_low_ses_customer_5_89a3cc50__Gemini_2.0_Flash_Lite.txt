**Option 1: One-Pan Roasted Chicken and Veggies (Easiest)**

*   **Why it works:** Minimal cleanup, cooks itself, and is a complete meal.
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Whole chicken (or chicken pieces like thighs/drumsticks for faster cooking)
    *   Potatoes (cut into chunks)
    *   Carrots (chopped)
    *   Onion (wedged)
    *   Broccoli or other veggies you like (optional)
    *   Olive oil
    *   Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, herbs (rosemary, thyme are great)
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
    2.  Toss veggies with olive oil and seasonings on a large baking sheet.
    3.  Place chicken on top of the veggies. Season chicken generously.
    4.  Roast for about 1 hour (or until chicken is cooked through and veggies are tender).
*   **Make-ahead:** Chop veggies and prep seasonings earlier in the day.

**Option 2: Slow Cooker Pulled Pork or Chicken Sandwiches (Easy & Great for a Crowd)**

*   **Why it works:**  Set it and forget it!  Great for feeding a lot of people.
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Pork shoulder (for pulled pork) or chicken breasts/thighs
    *   BBQ sauce (or your favorite sauce)
    *   Onion (optional)
    *   Buns
    *   Coleslaw (pre-made or easy to make)
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Place meat in slow cooker. Add onion if using.
    2.  Pour BBQ sauce over meat.
    3.  Cook on low for 6-8 hours (pork) or 4-6 hours (chicken), or until meat is very tender.
    4.  Shred meat with two forks.
    5.  Serve on buns with coleslaw.
*   **Make-ahead:**  This is perfect for making ahead!  Cook the meat in the slow cooker the day before and store it in the fridge. Reheat and shred before serving.

**Option 3: Pasta with Meat Sauce (Classic & Customizable)**

*   **Why it works:**  Everyone loves pasta!  You can easily adjust the ingredients to your preferences and what you have on hand.
*   **Ingredients:**
    *   Pasta (spaghetti, penne, etc.)
    *   Ground meat (beef, turkey, or a mix)
    *   Onion, garlic
    *   Canned crushed tomatoes or tomato sauce
    *   Italian seasoning
    *   Salt, pepper
    *   Optional:  Veggies like carrots, bell peppers, mushrooms
    *   Optional:  Grated Parmesan cheese
*   **Instructions:**
    1.  Cook pasta according to package directions.
    2.  Brown ground meat in a pan. Drain off any excess grease.
    3.  Add chopped onion and garlic and cook until softened.
    4.  Add crushed tomatoes, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for at least 15 minutes (longer is better for flavor).
    5.  Stir in cooked pasta.
    6.  Serve with Parmesan cheese.
*   **Make-ahead:**  Make the meat sauce ahead of time and store it in the fridge. Cook the pasta just before serving.
