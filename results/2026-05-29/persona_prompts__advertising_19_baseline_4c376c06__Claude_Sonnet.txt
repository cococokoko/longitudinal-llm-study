── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based approaches to improving mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep** - Aim for 7-9 hours consistently
- **Exercise** - Even 20-30 minutes of walking helps
- **Nutrition** - Balanced diet affects mood significantly
- **Limit alcohol** - It's a depressant despite feeling relaxing

## Social Connection
- Maintain relationships, even when it feels hard
- Talk to trusted friends or family about struggles
- Reduce isolation

## Mental Practices
- **Mindfulness or meditation** - Reduces anxiety and stress
- **Journaling** - Helps process emotions
- **Limit news/social media** if it increases anxiety

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** - Especially CBT, which has strong evidence
- **Psychiatry** - If medication might help
- **Your doctor** - Rule out physical causes of mood issues

## Practical Strategies
- Set realistic goals and boundaries
- Spend time in nature
- Engage in meaningful activities or hobbies
- Practice self-compassion

## When to Seek Help Urgently
If you're experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please contact:
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** (call or text 988 in the US)
- Emergency services
