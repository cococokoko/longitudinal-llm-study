How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for decorating your apartment on a minimal budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh look
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Shopping Options
- **Thrift stores and secondhand shops** - great for unique pieces
- **Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist** - free or cheap furniture
- **Dollar stores** - for small decorative items
- **Discount stores** like TJ Maxx or HomeGoods

## Simple DIY Ideas
- Paint an accent wall (one can of paint goes far)
- Make your own art with simple prints or framing photos
- Use washi tape for temporary wall designs
- Create shelving from basic boards and brackets

## Free Decorating
- Collect interesting branches, rocks, or pinecones
- Ask friends/family if they have unwanted items
- Check neighborhood "free" piles

## Smart Priorities
- Good lighting makes a huge difference - swap bulbs or add lamps
- Plants add life and are relatively cheap
- Focus on one room at a time rather than everything at once

## Practical Considerations
- Since your schedule varies, tackle projects on days off when you have energy
- Start with small, quick wins that don't require much time
