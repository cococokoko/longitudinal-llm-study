# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are some practical strategies to help you stay focused and perform well, especially important during your probation period:

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Dedicate a workspace** - separate from relaxation areas if possible
- Keep it tidy and free from distractions
- Ensure good lighting and a comfortable setup

## Managing Your Time
- **Stick to consistent hours** - mirror your office schedule
- Use time-blocking to structure your day
- Take regular short breaks (Pomodoro technique works well)

## Staying Visible to Your Team
- **Communicate proactively** - update your manager regularly
- Respond promptly to messages and emails
- Join video calls with camera on when appropriate

## Minimizing Distractions
- Set boundaries with household members during work hours
- Use website blockers if needed (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- Put your phone on Do Not Disturb

## Tracking Your Output
- Keep a daily task list and **document what you accomplish**
- This is especially useful for demonstrating your value during probation
- Use tools like Trello, Notion, or simple spreadsheets

## Maintaining Wellbeing
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Build in a "commute" routine to mentally start/end the day
- Stay connected socially with colleagues

**Key tip for your situation:** Making your work visible and staying communicative with your manager can really help build trust during your probation period.
