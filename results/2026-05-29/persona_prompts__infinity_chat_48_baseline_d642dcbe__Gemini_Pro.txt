**A Short Note on the Problem of Overpopulation**

Overpopulation occurs when the human population exceeds the carrying capacity of Earth’s environment and resources. It is currently one of the most pressing global challenges, threatening ecological balance and human well-being. 

**Primary Causes:**
*   **Medical Advancements:** The primary driver of population growth is not necessarily an increase in birth rates, but a drastic decline in death rates. Better healthcare, vaccines, and sanitation have significantly increased life expectancy.
*   **Lack of Family Planning:** In many developing regions, there is a lack of access to contraceptives and reproductive education.
*   **Poverty and Cultural Factors:** In some societies, large families are seen as an economic necessity for labor or a cultural norm, often exacerbated by a lack of education, particularly for women.
