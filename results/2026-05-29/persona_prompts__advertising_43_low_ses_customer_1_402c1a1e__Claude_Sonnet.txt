# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to make your wedding more sustainable:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to reduce guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Minimize printed programs

## Food & Catering
- Choose seasonal, locally sourced menu items
- Offer plant-based or reduced-meat options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations instead of buying
- Use biodegradable confetti

## Attire
- Consider renting or buying secondhand
- Choose dresses/suits that can be worn again

## Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets or small plants
- Request charitable donations instead of gifts
- Skip favors entirely

## Practical Tips Given Your Schedule
- **Plan in batches** during days off
- Use online planning tools you can access anytime
- Hire a coordinator if budget allows to reduce your planning burden
- Book vendors early to avoid rushed decisions

## Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
