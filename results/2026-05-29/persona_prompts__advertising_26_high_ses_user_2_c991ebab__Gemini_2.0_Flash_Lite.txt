    *   Password security best practices.

    *   Reporting security incidents.

*   **Access Control:**
    *   **Least Privilege:** Grant employees only the minimum access necessary to perform their job duties.
    *   **Strong Passwords & Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):** Enforce strong password policies and require MFA for all accounts, especially those with access to sensitive data.
    *   **Regular Password Changes:**  Implement a policy for regular password changes.
    *   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):**  Assign access based on job roles, not individual employees.
*   **Network Security:**
    *   **Firewall:**  Use a firewall to protect the network from unauthorized access.

*   **Endpoint Security:**
