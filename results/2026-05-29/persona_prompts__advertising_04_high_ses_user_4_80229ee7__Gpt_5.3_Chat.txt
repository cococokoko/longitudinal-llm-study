The biggest constraint you’ll face isn’t ideas, it’s time and cognitive bandwidth. So the goal is to pick something that either:

A few directions that tend to work well for someone in your position:

If you want it tied to your expertise:

- Conflict of interest: be very clear about institutional policies and disclosure requirements.
- Reputation risk: anything public-facing will reflect on you professionally—avoid anything that could be seen as overstated claims or “guru” positioning.
- Scalability vs. attention: avoid models that require constant 1:1 interaction unless priced very high.

If you’re just getting started, a simple path that works:
