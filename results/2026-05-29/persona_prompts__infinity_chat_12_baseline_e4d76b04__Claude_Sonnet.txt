# What to Look for When Comparing Expat Insurance

## 📋 Coverage Essentials

### Medical Coverage
- **Maximum coverage limit** (look for $1M+ for serious conditions)
- **Inpatient vs outpatient** coverage differences
- **Pre-existing conditions** – are they covered or excluded?
- **Chronic illness** coverage
- **Mental health** treatment coverage
- **Maternity coverage** (if relevant)
- **Dental and vision** coverage options

---

## 🌍 Geographic Coverage

- Which **countries are included**
- Whether your **home country** is covered
- **USA coverage** (significantly raises premiums)
- **Travel coverage** between countries
- **Worldwide vs regional** plans

---

## 💰 Cost Factors

| Factor | What to Check |
|--------|--------------|
| **Premium** | Monthly/annual cost |
| **Deductible** | Amount you pay first |
| **Co-payments** | Your share per visit |
| **Out-of-pocket maximum** | Annual spending cap |
| **Currency** | USD, EUR, GBP options |

---

## 🏥 Network & Access

- **Direct billing** hospitals vs reimbursement
- **Size of hospital network** in your location
- **Quality of network hospitals**
- **GP/specialist access** without referrals
- **Telemedicine** availability

---

## 📞 Claims & Support

- **24/7 emergency helpline**
- **Claims process** – online, app, or paper
- **Reimbursement speed**
- **Multilingual support**
- **Local support** in your country

---

## ⚠️ Policy Exclusions

Watch out for exclusions like:
- Adventure sports or hazardous activities
- Pre-existing conditions waiting periods
- Specific treatments or procedures
- War/civil unrest zones
- Alcohol/drug-related incidents

---

## 📄 Policy Details

- **Renewal terms** – can they cancel or raise rates?
- **Age limits** for coverage
- **Portability** if you move countries
- **Waiting periods** for certain conditions
- **Policy flexibility** to upgrade/downgrade

---

## 🏢 Insurer Reputation

- **Financial stability rating** (AM Best, S&P)
- **Customer reviews** and complaints
- **Years in expat insurance market**
- **Claim approval rate**

---

## 🔍 Top Questions to Ask

1. Is my specific country covered?
2. What happens if I need emergency evacuation?
3. Can I keep the policy if I move?
4. How do I get pre-authorization for treatment?
5. What is the claims reimbursement timeline?

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

- **Compare at least 3-5 providers**
- Use **expat insurance brokers** (often free)
- Read the **fine print** on exclusions
- Consider **local insurance** as a supplement
- Check if your **employer offers** group coverage
