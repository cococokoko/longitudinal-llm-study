# Marathon Training Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you prepare and succeed:

## Training
- **Build mileage gradually** - increase weekly distance by no more than 10% per week
- **Follow a structured plan** - most plans are 16-20 weeks long
- **Include long runs** - build up to 20-22 miles before race day
- **Rest days matter** - recovery is when your body actually gets stronger

## Nutrition & Hydration
- **Practice fueling during training** - don't try new foods on race day
- **Carb load 2-3 days before** the race
- **Stay hydrated** consistently, not just race week
- **Learn the race course aid stations** so you know when to fuel

## Practical Considerations
- **Train in your race-day shoes** well in advance
- **Prepare gear the night before** to reduce morning stress
- **Plan your race day logistics** early - parking, timing, etc.
- **Have a backup plan** if something goes wrong

## Race Day
- **Start slower than you feel like you should** - most people go out too fast
- **Break the race into segments** mentally
- **Trust your training**

## Recovery
- **Plan for 2-4 weeks of easy recovery** after the race
- **Sleep and nutrition** are your best recovery tools
