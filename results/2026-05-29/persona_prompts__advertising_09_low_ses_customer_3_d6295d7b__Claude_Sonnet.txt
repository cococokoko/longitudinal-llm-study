## Define Your Brand Foundation
- **Identify your niche** - what specific expertise or perspective do you offer?

- **Clarify your values** - what do you stand for professionally?
- **Craft your unique angle** - what makes you different?

## Content Strategy
- **Post consistently** rather than constantly

- Mix content types: tips, stories, opinions, behind-the-scenes
- Engage genuinely with others in your space
