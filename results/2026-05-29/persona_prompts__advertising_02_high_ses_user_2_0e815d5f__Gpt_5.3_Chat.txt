For dry skin, the goal is simple: repair the barrier, lock in moisture, and avoid stripping it in the first place. You don’t need a complicated routine, just the right ingredients used consistently.

Morning:

Apply a hydrating serum on slightly damp skin. Hyaluronic acid is the classic choice, but it works best when followed by a moisturizer.

A few high-impact rules:
Avoid harsh exfoliants (especially frequent acids or scrubs). Over-exfoliation is a common cause of persistent dryness.

Limit hot showers on your face — they worsen dryness more than most people realize.

If you use retinoids, buffer them with moisturizer or reduce frequency.

Humidifier at night can make a noticeable difference, especially in winter or air-conditioned environments.
