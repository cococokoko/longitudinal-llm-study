## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute) for better deals
- **Use incognito mode** when searching flights to avoid price tracking
- **Be flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- **Set price alerts** on Google Flights, Kayak, or Hopper
- **Compare nearby airports** for lower fares

## Flights
- Use **miles and points** from credit card rewards
- Fly **budget airlines** when appropriate
- Look for **error fares** on sites like Secret Flying
- Consider **layover flights** instead of direct routes
- Travel during **off-peak seasons**

## Accommodation
- Try **Airbnb, Hostel World, or VRBO**
- Use **hotel loyalty programs**
- Consider **house swapping**
- Stay slightly **outside city centers**
- Look into **work exchanges** (Workaway, HelpX)

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation** instead of taxis
- Cook some meals instead of eating out every time
- Get a **travel credit card** with no foreign transaction fees
- Use **free walking tours**
- Visit **free attractions** (parks, museums, markets)

- Use ATMs wisely to minimize fees
