── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Congratulations on building your own resume website! Since your website consists of `.html` files (and likely some CSS or images), it is called a **"static website."** 

There are several excellent, completely free services designed specifically for hosting static websites. 

Before you start, **make sure your main resume file is named exactly `index.html`**. All hosting platforms look for this specific file to know what the homepage is. Put this file, along with any images or CSS files, into a single folder on your computer.
