── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can social media cultivate extremism

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This is a well-documented area of research. Here are the key mechanisms:

## Algorithmic Factors
- **Recommendation systems** optimize for engagement, which often means emotionally provocative content
- **Rabbit holes** - platforms progressively suggest more extreme content to maintain attention
- **Filter bubbles** limit exposure to challenging perspectives

## Social/Psychological Mechanisms
- **Echo chambers** reinforce existing beliefs without pushback
- **Social validation** through likes/shares rewards increasingly extreme positions
- **Outgroup dehumanization** spreads more easily at scale
- **Competitive outrage** - users escalate rhetoric to stand out

## Platform Design Issues
- Anonymity can reduce accountability
- Virality rewards shocking content over nuanced content
- **Engagement metrics** don't distinguish healthy vs. harmful interaction

## Organizational Exploitation
- Extremist groups deliberately use platforms for recruitment
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior amplifies fringe views
- Private groups allow radicalization away from moderation

## Research Findings
- Studies show mixed results - some find platforms accelerate radicalization, others find pre-existing vulnerabilities matter more
- The **pathway** often involves gradual normalization rather than sudden conversion

## Countermeasures researchers discuss
- Algorithm transparency
- Friction in sharing
- Exposure to diverse viewpoints
