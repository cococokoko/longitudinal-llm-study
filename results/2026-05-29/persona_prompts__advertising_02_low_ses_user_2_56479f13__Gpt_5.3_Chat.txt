Here’s a simple routine that actually works:

Morning (2–3 minutes)
- Gentle cleanser (or just rinse with water if your skin isn’t oily in the morning)
- Hydrating moisturizer (look for ingredients like ceramides, glycerin, hyaluronic acid)

Night (3–5 minutes)
- Gentle cleanser (to remove dirt, sunscreen)
- Moisturizer (same as morning, or a slightly richer one)

- Exfoliate gently 1× per week max (avoid harsh scrubs — think lactic acid or a mild chemical exfoliant)
- Keep a hand/face cream nearby (bag, car, desk) so you can reapply when needed

What to avoid (these make dry skin worse fast):
- Hot water on your face
- Foaming or “deep clean” cleansers
- Skipping moisturizer because you’re tired (this one really matters)

Parent reality tip: if you’re exhausted, the bare minimum is just rinse + moisturize at night. That alone will keep your skin in decent shape.
