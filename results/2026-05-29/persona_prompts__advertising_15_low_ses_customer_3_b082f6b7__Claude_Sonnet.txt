# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** - a tidy space instantly looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Updates
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations for little money
- Swap out **cushion covers** instead of buying new cushions
- Update **light switches and outlet covers** for a polished look

## Smart Shopping
- **Charity shops and thrift stores** for unique pieces
- **Facebook Marketplace and Freecycle** for free or cheap furniture
- **End of season sales** for soft furnishings
- DIY projects using affordable materials

## Free Ideas
- Foraged branches or dried flowers as natural decor
- Rearrange artwork you already have
- Print free downloadable art online

## Practical Priorities
- Focus on **high-impact areas** like entryways and living rooms first
- Fix things that are broken before buying new items
- **One room at a time** to avoid overspending

## Things to Avoid
- Impulse purchases
- Buying everything at once
- Trends that date quickly

Since you're currently in a new job and building financial stability, taking a **gradual approach** makes a lot of sense - small changes over time can add up to a beautifully decorated home without financial stress.
