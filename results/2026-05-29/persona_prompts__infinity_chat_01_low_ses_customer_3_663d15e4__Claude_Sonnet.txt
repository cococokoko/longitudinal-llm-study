# Finding a Job in Scandinavian Countries

Here are the main ways to find employment in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, or Finland:

## Online Job Portals

- **LinkedIn** - widely used across all Scandinavian countries
- **Indeed.se / Indeed.no / Indeed.dk**
- **Finn.no** (Norway)
- **Jobindex.dk** (Denmark)
- **Blocket Jobb** (Sweden)
- **Monster.fi** (Finland)

## Official Resources

- **EURES** (European Job Mobility Portal) - eures.europa.eu
- Public employment services like **NAV** (Norway), **Arbetsförmedlingen** (Sweden)

## Practical Tips

- **Learn the local language** - greatly improves your chances
- **Network actively** - Scandinavian hiring relies heavily on connections
- **Tailor your CV** to local standards (usually concise, 1-2 pages)
- **Research work permit requirements** if you're outside the EU/EEA

## In-Demand Sectors

- IT and technology
- Healthcare
- Engineering
- Construction
- Finance

## Things to Consider

- Cost of living is high
- Strong worker rights and benefits
- English is widely spoken, especially in tech
