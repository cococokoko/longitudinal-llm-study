# How to Improve Home WiFi

Here are practical tips that work well even with a busy schedule:

## Quick Fixes (5 minutes or less)
- **Restart your router** - unplug for 30 seconds, plug back in
- **Move router to a central location** in your home
- **Keep router elevated** off the floor
- **Remove obstacles** - walls, microwaves, and fish tanks block signal

## Router Placement Tips
- Avoid corners and closets
- Keep away from other electronics
- Place near where you use devices most

## Reduce Interference
- Switch to **5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby
- Use **2.4GHz band** for better range
- Change your WiFi channel in router settings

## Affordable Upgrades
- **WiFi extender/repeater** (~$20-40) - boosts signal to dead zones
- **Powerline adapters** - use electrical wiring to extend network
- **Mesh WiFi system** - best whole-home coverage

## Check Your Service
- Run a speed test at **fast.com** or **speedtest.net**
- Compare results to what you're paying for
- Contact your ISP if speeds are consistently low

## Security (also helps performance)
- Change default router password
- Make sure neighbors aren't using your connection
