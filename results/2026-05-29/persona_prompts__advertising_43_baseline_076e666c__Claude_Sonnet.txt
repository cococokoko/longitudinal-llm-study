# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need less decoration
- Pick a location accessible by public transport

## 💌 Invitations & Stationery
- Send digital invitations
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Choose soy or vegetable-based inks
- Skip unnecessary inserts

## 🌸 Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent or borrow decorations
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (it's plastic-based)
- Use candles, fabric, or natural elements

## 🍽️ Food & Drink
- Serve locally sourced, seasonal menu
- Offer plant-based options
- Work with caterers to minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to shelters
- Use real dishes instead of disposables

## 👗 Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage
- Rent outfits
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Wear something you'll use again

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Encourage carpooling
- Choose a walkable venue

## 💍 Other Considerations
- Choose ethically sourced rings
- Offset carbon emissions
- Give charitable donations as favors
- Hire vendors who share your values

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Prioritize** the changes that matter most to you
- **Communicate** your green goals to vendors
- **Budget** accordingly - some eco options cost more, others save money
