# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Great topic! Here's a practical guide to help you begin:

## Start Simple
- **Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Choose a regular time (morning often works well before the day gets busy)
- Find a quiet, comfortable spot

## Basic Meditation Technique
1. Sit comfortably with your back relatively straight
2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze
3. Focus on your **natural breath**
4. Notice when your mind wanders (it will!)
5. Gently bring attention back - without judgment

## Helpful Beginner Apps
- **Headspace** - structured beginner courses
- **Calm** - guided meditations and sleep content
- **Insight Timer** - free library of guided sessions

## Mindfulness in Daily Life
- Pay full attention while eating, walking, or doing dishes
- Take **three conscious breaths** before meetings or tasks
- Notice your surroundings with curiosity

## Realistic Expectations
- Your mind **will** wander - that's completely normal
- Benefits often build gradually over weeks
- Even short sessions count

## Why It Might Help You Right Now
Starting a new job can be stressful, and mindfulness can genuinely help with **managing anxiety, improving focus, and staying calm** under pressure - useful during a probation period.
