Okay, here's a fun and diet-friendly snack idea that skips the yogurt:

**Cucumber & Hummus Bites with Everything Bagel Seasoning**

This snack is refreshing, crunchy, and packed with flavor!

**Ingredients:**

*   1 large cucumber
*   Hummus (choose your favorite flavor, but plain or roasted red pepper are great)
*   Everything Bagel Seasoning
*   Optional: A sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a little heat

**Instructions:**

1.  **Prep the Cucumber:** Wash and slice the cucumber into thick rounds (about 1/2 inch thick). You can peel the cucumber if you prefer, but the skin adds some nice texture and nutrients.
2.  **Assemble the Bites:** Spread a generous dollop of hummus on top of each cucumber slice.
3.  **Season:** Sprinkle the hummus with a generous amount of everything bagel seasoning.
4.  **Optional Heat:** If you like a little kick, add a tiny pinch of red pepper flakes on top.
5.  **Enjoy!**

**Why it's great for a diet:**

*   **Low in Calories:** Cucumber is mostly water and very low in calories.
*   **Fiber-Rich:** Cucumber has some fiber, which helps you feel full. Hummus also provides fiber.
*   **Protein & Healthy Fats:** Hummus is made from chickpeas, which provide protein and healthy fats.
*   **Flavorful & Satisfying:** The combination of the cool cucumber, creamy hummus, and savory seasoning is delicious and satisfying, helping to curb cravings.
*   **Customizable:** You can adjust the amount of hummus and seasoning to your liking.

**Variations:**

*   **Add a protein boost:** Add a small piece of cooked chicken or turkey on top of the hummus.
*   **Use different veggies:** Try using bell pepper slices or celery sticks instead of cucumber.
*   **Make it a dip:** Instead of individual bites, you can cut the cucumber into sticks and use them to dip into a bowl of hummus.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious snack!
