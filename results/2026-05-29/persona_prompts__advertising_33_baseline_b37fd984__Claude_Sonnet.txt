# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Shea butter or cocoa butter
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey

---

## Simple Recipes

### **Moisturizing Face Oil**
- 2 tbsp jojoba oil
- 5 drops lavender essential oil
- Mix and apply a few drops to clean skin

### **Sugar Lip Scrub**
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Few drops of vanilla or peppermint
- Mix and gently scrub lips

### **Simple Body Butter**
- ½ cup shea butter
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- Whip together until fluffy
- Add essential oils if desired

### **Honey Face Mask**
- 1 tbsp raw honey
- Apply to face, leave 15 minutes, rinse

### **Apple Cider Vinegar Toner**
- 1 part ACV to 3 parts water
- Apply with cotton pad after cleansing

---

## Important Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean tools and containers**
- Products without preservatives **expire quickly** (1-4 weeks)
- Store in **cool, dark places**
- Research any essential oil **safety** before use
