# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks** – core strength
- **Burpees** – full body + cardio
- **Dips** – using a chair or sturdy surface

## Cardio Options
- Walking or jogging outdoors
- Jump rope
- Cycling
- Dancing
- Stair climbing

| Item | Cost | Benefit |

## Practical Tips
- **Be consistent** – short daily workouts beat occasional long ones
- **Progressive overload** – gradually increase reps, sets, or difficulty
- **Use what you have** – water jugs, backpacks, stairs
- **Follow routines** – YouTube has free structured workouts
- **Track progress** – keeps you motivated

- Prioritize **sleep** for recovery

- Eat **whole foods** when possible
- Reduce prolonged **sitting**
