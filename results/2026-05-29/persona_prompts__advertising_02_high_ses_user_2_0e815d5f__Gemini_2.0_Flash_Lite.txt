I am a senior attorney at a well-established law firm.

Best skin routine for dry skin.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Okay, as a senior attorney, you likely appreciate efficiency and results. Here's a skin routine tailored for dry skin, focusing on effective ingredients and a streamlined approach. Remember, consistency is key!

**Morning Routine (Quick & Effective):**

1.  **Gentle Cleanser:**
    *   **Why:** Avoid harsh cleansers that strip away natural oils.
    *   **Recommendations:**
        *   **Cream or Milk Cleanser:** Look for formulas with ingredients like ceramides, hyaluronic acid, or glycerin.
        *   **Examples:** CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser, Cetaphil Gentle Skin Cleanser, La Roche-Posay Toleriane Hydrating Gentle Cleanser.
    *   **How:** Wet your face with lukewarm water. Apply a small amount of cleanser, gently massage in circular motions, and rinse thoroughly. Pat dry with a soft towel.

2.  **Hydrating Serum:**
    *   **Why:** Delivers a concentrated dose of moisture and active ingredients.
    *   **Recommendations:**
        *   **Hyaluronic Acid (HA) Serum:** HA attracts and holds moisture. Apply to damp skin for best results.
        *   **Examples:** The Ordinary Hyaluronic Acid 2% + B5, Paula's Choice Hyaluronic Acid Booster.
        *   **Peptide Serums:** Peptides can help with collagen production and skin elasticity.
        *   **Examples:** The Ordinary Matrixyl 10% + HA, Drunk Elephant Protini Polypeptide Cream.
    *   **How:** Apply a few drops to your face and neck, gently patting it in.

3.  **Moisturizer:**
    *   **Why:** Seals in hydration and protects the skin barrier.
    *   **Recommendations:**
        *   **Rich, Emollient Moisturizer:** Look for ingredients like ceramides, shea butter, squalane, and occlusives (like petrolatum or mineral oil) to create a protective barrier.
        *   **Examples:** CeraVe Moisturizing Cream, La Roche-Posay Toleriane Double Repair Face Moisturizer UV SPF 30, First Aid Beauty Ultra Repair Cream.
    *   **How:** Apply a generous amount to your face and neck, massaging gently until absorbed.

4.  **Sunscreen (Essential!):**
    *   **Why:** Protects against UV damage, which can worsen dryness and accelerate aging.
    *   **Recommendations:**
        *   **Broad-Spectrum SPF 30 or higher:** Choose a formula that is hydrating and suitable for dry skin.
        *   **Examples:** EltaMD UV Clear Broad-Spectrum SPF 46 (if you have acne-prone skin), La Roche-Posay Anthelios Melt-In Sunscreen Milk SPF 60, Supergoop! Unseen Sunscreen (for a lightweight option).
    *   **How:** Apply liberally to your face and neck at least 15 minutes before sun exposure. Reapply every two hours, or more frequently if sweating or swimming.

**Evening Routine (Slightly More Involved):**

1.  **Makeup Removal (if applicable):**
    *   **Why:** Removes makeup, dirt, and oil that can clog pores and exacerbate dryness.
    *   **Recommendations:**
        *   **Oil-Based Cleanser or Balm:** Dissolves makeup effectively without stripping the skin.
        *   **Examples:** DHC Deep Cleansing Oil, Farmacy Green Clean Makeup Removing Cleansing Balm.
    *   **How:** Massage onto dry skin, emulsify with water, and rinse thoroughly.

2.  **Gentle Cleanser:** (Same as morning)

        *   **Examples:** The Ordinary Retinol 0.2% in Squalane, Paula's Choice 1% Retinol Treatment.
        *   **Important:** If using retinol, avoid using other harsh actives (like AHAs/BHAs) on the same night.
        *   **Alternate:** If retinol is too irritating, consider a serum with peptides or antioxidants.
