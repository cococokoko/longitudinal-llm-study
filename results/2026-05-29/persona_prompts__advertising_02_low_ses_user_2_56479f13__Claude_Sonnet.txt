# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a **simple, effective routine** that works even with a busy schedule:

---

## Morning (5 minutes)
1. **Gentle cleanser** - avoid harsh soaps that strip moisture
2. **Hydrating toner** - optional but helpful
3. **Moisturizer with SPF** - combines two steps in one *(time saver!)*

---

## Evening (5 minutes)
1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Serum** - look for **hyaluronic acid** or **glycerin**
3. **Rich night moisturizer** - thicker creams work well overnight

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - repairs skin barrier
- **Shea butter** - seals moisture
- **Glycerin** - hydrating and affordable

---

## Budget-Friendly Tips
- **CeraVe, Cetaphil, Vanicream** are affordable and effective
- A **good moisturizer matters most** - you can skip fancy serums
- Apply moisturizer on **slightly damp skin** for better absorption

---

## Quick Tip for Busy Days
If you only have **2 minutes**, just:
- Rinse face
- Apply moisturizer with SPF

**Consistency beats complexity!**
