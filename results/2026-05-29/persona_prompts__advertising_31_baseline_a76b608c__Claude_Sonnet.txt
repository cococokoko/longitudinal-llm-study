# Finding Affordable Eco-Friendly Travel Destinations

## Research Strategies

**Use specialized platforms**
- Bookdifferent.com (rates hotels on sustainability)
- Ecobnb.com (eco-friendly accommodations)
- Responsibletravel.com
- Green Destinations (certified sustainable spots)

**Look for budget-friendly eco-certified regions**
- Costa Rica (well-developed eco-tourism infrastructure)
- Portugal (relatively affordable, strong sustainability focus)
- Slovenia (Green European Destination certified)
- Vietnam (growing eco-tourism, lower costs)

## Ways to Reduce Costs

**Transportation**
- Travel overland instead of flying when possible
- Use trains (lower carbon + often cheaper)
- Travel during shoulder seasons for better prices

**Accommodation**
- Volunteer programs (Workaway, WWOOF) - work for free lodging
- Eco-hostels instead of eco-resorts
- Camping in national parks

**Activities**
- Free hiking and nature experiences
- Community-based tourism (often cheaper than commercial tours)

## Practical Tips

- **Stay longer in fewer places** (reduces transport costs and emissions)
- **Eat local food** (cheaper and supports local economy)
- **Check for carbon offset programs** if flying is necessary
- **Travel with a small group** to share costs

## Red Flags to Avoid
- "Greenwashing" - vague sustainability claims without certification
- Expensive "eco" labels that are mostly marketing
