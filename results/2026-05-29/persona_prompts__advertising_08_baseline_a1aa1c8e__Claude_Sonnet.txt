# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great ways to discover trails that match your needs:

## Online Resources
- **AllTrails** - Most popular app/website with reviews, photos, and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed maps and community-contributed trails
- **Recreation.gov** - For national parks and federal lands
- **TrailLink** - Great for rail-trails and paved paths

## What to Consider When Choosing
- **Difficulty level** - Beginner, intermediate, or advanced
- **Distance & elevation gain** - Know your limits
- **Terrain type** - Rocky, forested, desert, coastal
- **Scenery goals** - Waterfalls, summits, wildlife, wildflowers

## Practical Tips
- **Read recent reviews** - Conditions change seasonally
- **Check weather forecasts** before heading out
- **Look at trail photos** to set realistic expectations
- **Consider crowds** - Weekday vs. weekend timing

## Safety Planning
- Download offline maps in case of no cell service
- Tell someone your plans
- Check if permits are required
- Know the trailhead parking situation

## Local Options
- Visit a **local REI or outdoor shop** for staff recommendations
- Contact **ranger stations** for insider knowledge
- Join **local hiking groups** on Facebook or Meetup
