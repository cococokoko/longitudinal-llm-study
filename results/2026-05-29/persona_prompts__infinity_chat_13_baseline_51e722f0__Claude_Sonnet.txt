## Cucumber "Sushi" Rolls

**What you need:**
- Cucumber slices (cut into thin strips or rounds)
- Cream cheese (light)
- Smoked salmon or deli turkey
- A little everything bagel seasoning

**How to make it:**
- Spread a thin layer of cream cheese on the cucumber
- Add a small piece of salmon or turkey
- Sprinkle the seasoning on top
- Roll or stack and enjoy!

**Why it's great:**
- 🥒 Super low calorie
- Feels fancy and fun to eat
- Crunchy and satisfying
- High in protein to keep you full

It feels like a treat but is totally diet friendly! 😄
