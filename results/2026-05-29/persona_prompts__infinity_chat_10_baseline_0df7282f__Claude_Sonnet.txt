── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
## Free Ways to Host Your Resume Website

Here are the best options for hosting static HTML files:

---

### **GitHub Pages** *(Most Popular)*
1. Create a free account at **github.com**
2. Create a new repository named `yourusername.github.io`
3. Upload your HTML files
4. Your site goes live at `https://yourusername.github.io`

**Pros:** Free, reliable, custom domain support

---

### **Netlify** *(Easiest)*
1. Go to **netlify.com** and sign up free
2. Simply **drag and drop** your folder onto their dashboard
3. Get an instant URL like `yoursite.netlify.app`

**Pros:** Extremely easy, no technical knowledge needed

---

### **Cloudflare Pages**
- Similar to Netlify, free and fast
- Go to **pages.cloudflare.com**

---

### **Google Drive** *(Quick but limited)*
- Less ideal since Google removed direct HTML hosting

---

## My Recommendation
- **Quickest:** Netlify drag-and-drop
- **Best long-term:** GitHub Pages (also looks good on a resume itself)

---

### Tips
- Make sure your main file is named **index.html**
- All file paths should be **relative** (not full computer paths)
