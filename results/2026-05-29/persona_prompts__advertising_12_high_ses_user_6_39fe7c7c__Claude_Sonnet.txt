# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a structured overview that might be useful whether you're advising clients or optimizing your own firm's travel:

---

## **Transportation**
- **Rail over air** for routes under 500 miles (significantly lower carbon footprint)
- **Direct flights** when flying is necessary (takeoff/landing = highest emissions)
- **Electric/hybrid rentals** at destinations
- **Public transit** over taxis/rideshares where practical

---

## **Accommodation**
- Hotels with **LEED certification** or recognized sustainability credentials
- Properties with **local sourcing** and waste reduction programs

---

## **Business Travel Specifically**
- **Carbon offset programs** (though quality varies - verify credibility)
- **Trip consolidation** - combining multiple client meetings into single trips
- **Virtual meetings** as the default, travel as the exception
- **Sustainability travel policies** firms increasingly adopt for ESG reporting

---

## **Measurement & Reporting**
- Track Scope 3 emissions (business travel is often a major component)
- Tools like **Salesforce Sustainability Cloud** or **SAP Green Ledger**
