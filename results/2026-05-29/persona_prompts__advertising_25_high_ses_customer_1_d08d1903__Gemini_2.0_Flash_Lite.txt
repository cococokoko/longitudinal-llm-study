**I. The Core Principles of Minimalist Decorating (and Budgeting):**

*   **Less is More:** This is the foundation. Focus on quality over quantity. Each item should serve a purpose and bring you joy.
*   **Declutter Ruthlessly:** Before you buy anything, get rid of what you don't need, use, or love. This is the *most* important step and the cheapest!
*   **Functionality First:** Prioritize items that are useful and serve a clear purpose.
*   **Neutral Color Palette:** This creates a sense of calm and allows for easy updates later. Think whites, grays, beiges, and natural wood tones.
*   **Strategic Storage:** Maximize space with smart storage solutions to keep things tidy.
*   **DIY and Upcycling:** Embrace your creativity! Repurpose items and create your own decor.
*   **Patience:** Don't feel pressured to furnish everything at once. Build your space gradually.
