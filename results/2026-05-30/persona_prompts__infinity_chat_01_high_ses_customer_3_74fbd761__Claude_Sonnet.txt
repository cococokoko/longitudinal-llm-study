# Finding a Job in Scandinavian Countries

Here are practical steps to help you in your job search:

## Online Job Portals
- **Denmark:** jobnet.dk, ofir.dk, jobindex.dk
- **Sweden:** arbetsformedlingen.se, linkedin.com, blocket.se/jobb
- **Norway:** finn.no, nav.no, jobbnorge.no
- **Finland:** mol.fi, oikotie.fi/tyopaikat

## Key Steps

### 1. **Prepare Your Documents**
- Update your CV (Scandinavian style - concise, 1-2 pages)
- Write a strong cover letter
- Gather academic credentials and references

### 2. **Language Considerations**
- Many positions accept **English**, especially in academia and tech
- Learning basic Swedish/Norwegian/Danish is a significant advantage

### 3. **Networking**
- Use **LinkedIn** actively
- Attend academic conferences in your field
- Contact universities directly

### 4. **University/Academic Positions**
- Check university websites directly
- **EURAXESS** (euraxess.ec.europa.eu) - excellent for academic jobs
- **Jobs.ac.uk** also lists Scandinavian positions

### 5. **Work Permit**
- EU/EEA citizens have free movement
- Non-EU citizens need a work/residence permit

## Helpful Tips
- Scandinavian work culture values **work-life balance**
- Flat organizational hierarchies are common
- Networking is highly valued
