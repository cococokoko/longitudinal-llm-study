── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I am a partner at a major consulting firm.

I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some great **free options** to host your HTML resume website:

## Easiest Options

### **GitHub Pages** *(Most recommended)*
- Create a free account at github.com
- Upload your HTML files to a repository
- Enable GitHub Pages in settings
- Get a free URL like `yourname.github.io`

### **Netlify**
- Go to netlify.com
- Simply **drag and drop** your folder of HTML files
- Get a free URL instantly
- Very beginner friendly

### **Google Drive** *(Simplest but limited)*
- Upload files to Google Drive
- Less ideal since Google deprecated direct hosting

## My Recommendation

**Netlify** is probably the fastest for your situation:
1. Go to **netlify.com**
2. Sign up free
3. Drag your HTML folder onto their dashboard
4. Share the link immediately

You can also later connect a **custom domain** (like `yourname.com`) for a more professional appearance, which typically costs around **$10-15/year**.
