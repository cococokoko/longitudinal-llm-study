How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for minimalist apartment decorating on a budget:

## Core Principles
- **Less is more** - focus on a few quality pieces rather than lots of items
- Choose a simple, cohesive color palette (2-3 colors works well)

## Budget-Friendly Ideas

**Furniture & Layout**
- Thrift stores and Facebook Marketplace for quality secondhand pieces
- Multi-functional furniture (storage ottomans, fold-out tables)
- Arrange what you have thoughtfully before buying anything new

**Walls & Decor**
- A few large pieces of art rather than many small ones
- Simple frames with free printable art or personal photos
- Mirrors to create space and light

**Plants**
- Low-maintenance plants like pothos or snake plants are inexpensive and add life
- Propagate from cuttings (often free)

**Lighting**
- Swap harsh overhead lights for warm-toned bulbs
- Thrifted lamps can transform a space

**Decluttering First**
- Removing clutter is free and often the biggest improvement
- Donate or sell what you don't need

## Smart Shopping
- Wait for sales rather than impulse buying
- IKEA basics work well for minimalist aesthetics
- Focus spending on high-visibility items (couch, rug)
