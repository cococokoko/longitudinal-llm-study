# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a **simple, effective routine** that works even with a busy schedule!

---

## Morning Routine (5 minutes)
1. **Gentle cleanser** - cream or milk-based, avoid foaming
2. **Hydrating toner** - look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - something rich with ceramides
4. **SPF 30+** - protects and prevents further dryness

---

## Evening Routine (5 minutes)
1. **Cleanse** - remove the day's buildup
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid works great for dry skin
3. **Rich moisturizer/night cream** - this is when skin repairs itself

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - locks moisture in
- **Shea butter** - deeply nourishing
- **Glycerin** - gentle hydration

---

## Quick Tips for Busy Parents
- **Keep products on your bathroom counter** so they're easy to grab
- **Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp** for better absorption
- A **simple 2-step routine** (cleanser + moisturizer) is better than skipping entirely

---

**Budget-friendly brands:** CeraVe, Cetaphil, and Vanicream work really well for dry skin.
