# Learning to Play Guitar

## Getting Started

### What You'll Need
- **A guitar** - acoustic is often recommended for beginners
- **A tuner** - clip-on tuner or a free app (GuitarTuna, etc.)
- **A pick** - medium thickness is versatile
- **Optional:** capo, extra strings, strap

---

## Beginner Roadmap

### 1. **Learn the Basics First**
- How to hold the guitar properly
- How to tune your guitar
- Understanding string names **(E A D G B e)**
- Basic finger positioning

### 2. **Start with Chords**
Common beginner chords:
- **Em, Am** - easiest to start
- **G, C, D** - very common in songs
- **E, A** - great foundations

### 3. **Practice Techniques**
- Chord transitions
- Strumming patterns
- Fingerpicking basics

---

## Tips for Success

- ✅ Practice **15-30 minutes daily** rather than long sessions
- ✅ Your fingertips will be sore at first - **it gets better**
- ✅ Learn songs you **actually enjoy**
- ✅ Use free resources like **YouTube, JustinGuitar.com**
