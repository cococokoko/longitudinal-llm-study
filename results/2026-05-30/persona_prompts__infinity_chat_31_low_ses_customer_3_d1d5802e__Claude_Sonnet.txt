- Business degrees (MBA is common)

- **Entrepreneurship** - founding your own company
- **Specialist expertise** - becoming indispensable in a niche area
- **Lateral moves** - switching companies for senior roles
- **Headhunting** - being recruited based on reputation

## Important Qualities Often Cited
- Strategic thinking
- Communication skills
- Resilience and adaptability
- Emotional intelligence
- Decision-making ability
