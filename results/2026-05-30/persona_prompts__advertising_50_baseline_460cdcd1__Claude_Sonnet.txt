# Ways to Save on Travel

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or last minute for some deals)
- **Use flight comparison sites** (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- **Be flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- **Set price alerts** for routes you want
- **Clear browser cookies** or use incognito mode when searching

## Accommodation
- Consider **hostels, Airbnb, or vacation rentals**
- Use **hotel loyalty programs**
- Stay slightly **outside city centers**
- Try **house swapping or Couchsurfing**
- Book **refundable rates** and watch for price drops

## Transportation
- Use **public transit** instead of taxis/rideshares
- Look into **rail passes** for multi-city trips
- **Walk or rent a bike** when possible
- Compare **rental car** deals in advance

## Food & Activities
- Eat where **locals eat**
- Visit **free museums and attractions**
- Look for **city tourist cards** for bundled savings
- Cook some meals if you have a kitchen

## Credit Cards & Money
- Use a **travel rewards credit card**
- Avoid **foreign transaction fees**
- Use **ATMs over currency exchange kiosks**

## Other Tips
- Travel during **shoulder season**
- Consider **travel insurance** to avoid costly surprises
- Pack light to **avoid baggage fees**
