Here are some practical daily habits to reduce your carbon footprint:

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Consider carpooling to work

- Eat more plant-based meals
- Reduce food waste by planning meals
- Buy local and seasonal produce when practical
- Compost food scraps

## Energy at Home
- Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use
- Use a programmable thermostat
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Air dry laundry when possible

## Shopping & Consumption
- Buy secondhand when possible
- Choose quality items that last longer
- Bring reusable bags and containers
- Avoid single-use plastics

## Digital Habits
- Reduce unnecessary streaming and cloud storage
- Unsubscribe from emails you don't read
- Video call instead of traveling for meetings

## Quick Wins
- Take shorter showers
- Eat less beef and dairy specifically
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Use a reusable water bottle and coffee cup

## Higher Impact Changes (when possible)
- Switch to renewable energy providers
- Consider an electric vehicle eventually
- Improve home insulation

**Starting small matters** - picking 2-3 habits and building from there is more sustainable than trying to change everything at once.
