# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY options:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Coconut oil or shea butter as a base
- Add a few drops of essential oils (lavender, tea tree)
- Mix and store in a clean jar

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coconut oil
- Optional: lemon juice or vanilla extract

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (steep rose petals in distilled water)

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply for 20-30 minutes, rinse well

**Dry Shampoo**
- Arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- Add cocoa powder for darker hair

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + shea butter
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## General Tips

- **Use clean containers** to prevent bacteria
- **Patch test** new products before full use
- **Label everything** with ingredients and date
- **Refrigerate** products without preservatives
- Start with **small batches**
