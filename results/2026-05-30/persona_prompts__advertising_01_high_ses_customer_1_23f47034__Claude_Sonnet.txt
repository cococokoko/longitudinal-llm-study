That's a great hobby to pick up! Here are some helpful tips to get started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - acoustic is often recommended for beginners (no amp needed)
- **Consider your goals** - classical, rock, folk, fingerpicking all have different approaches

## Fundamentals First
- Learn proper **hand position and posture** - prevents injury and bad habits
- Start with **basic chords** - G, C, D, Em, Am cover hundreds of songs
- Practice **chord transitions** - often more important than individual chords

## Building Skills
- Use a **metronome** - developing rhythm early is crucial
- Learn to **read chord charts** and basic tablature (tabs)
- Practice **fingertip toughening** - expect soreness at first

## Practical Tips
- **Short, consistent sessions** (15-30 min daily) beat long occasional ones
- Apps like **Yousician, Justin Guitar, or Fender Play** are excellent for beginners
- **Learn songs you love** - motivation matters enormously

## Realistic Expectations
- Basic songs: **weeks**
- Comfortable playing: **several months**
- Real fluency: **1-2+ years**

Given your demanding schedule, even **10-15 minutes of daily practice** can yield real progress over time.
