# Best Apps for Fitness & Nutrition Tracking

Great question! For warehouse work specifically, you're likely on your feet a lot, so tracking both activity and recovery matters.

## Fitness Tracking Apps

**MyFitnessPal**
- Tracks steps, calories burned, and exercise
- Large food database for nutrition too

**Fitbit App** (works without Fitbit device)
- Good step and activity tracking

**Google Fit / Apple Health**
- Free, built-in options
- Aggregate data from other apps

## Nutrition Apps

**Cronometer**
- Very detailed micronutrient tracking
- Good for making sure you're eating enough for physical work

**MyFitnessPal**
- Huge food database
- Easy barcode scanning for quick logging

**Lose It!**
- Simple interface
- Good meal planning features

## Practical Tips for Warehouse Workers

- **Calorie needs are higher** with physical labor, so don't undereat
- Track **hydration** - physical work increases needs significantly
- Monitor **protein intake** for muscle recovery
- Consider apps with **meal prep planning** features

## My Honest Recommendation

**MyFitnessPal + your phone's built-in health app** is probably the simplest starting combination that covers most needs without being overwhelming.
