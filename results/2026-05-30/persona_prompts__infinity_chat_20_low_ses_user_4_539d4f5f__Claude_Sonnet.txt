# Social Media Tips for Hospital Patient Care Aides

Here are some important points to keep in mind regarding social media in your role:

## ⚠️ Critical Boundaries to Maintain

- **Never share patient information** - names, conditions, photos, room numbers, or anything identifiable

- Complaints about specific patients or coworkers

- Medical details, even without names

- General posts about enjoying your career
- Sharing public health awareness information
- Celebrating milestones like certifications
- Connecting with other healthcare professionals professionally

- **Assume your employer can see everything** you post
- Check your hospital's **specific social media policy**
- When in doubt, **don't post it**
- Be careful even in **private groups**
