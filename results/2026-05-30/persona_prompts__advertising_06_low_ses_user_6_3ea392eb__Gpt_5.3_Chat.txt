If your goal is to really get good at digital photography, think in terms of momentum over intensity.

First, tighten your fundamentals so every shot counts. Focus on exposure (ISO, shutter speed, aperture), composition, and light. - Low-light shooting  
- High contrast scenes

- “One photo a day for 30 days”
- “Document my workplace”
- “50 photos focused only on light”

Constraints will keep you moving even when tired.
