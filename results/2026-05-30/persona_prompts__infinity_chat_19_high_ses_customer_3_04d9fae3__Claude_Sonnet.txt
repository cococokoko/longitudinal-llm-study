# Best Books for Design and Analysis of Algorithms (2023)

## 🏆 Top Tier / Essential

### 1. **Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)**
- Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein — 4th Edition (2022)
- The **gold standard** for algorithm courses
- Rigorous mathematical treatment, comprehensive coverage
- Best for: formal proofs, pseudocode, broad coverage

### 2. **Algorithm Design** — Kleinberg & Tardos
- Excellent narrative and intuition-building
- Strong on **greedy, DP, network flow, NP-completeness**
- Best for: understanding *why* algorithms work

---

## 📘 Strong Alternatives

### 3. **Algorithms** — Dasgupta, Papadimitriou, Vazirani
- Free PDF available legally
- Concise, elegant, mathematically mature
- Great supplementary text

### 4. **The Algorithm Design Manual** — Skiena (3rd Ed.)
- Practical perspective with real-world war stories
- Excellent **catalog of problems**
- Best for: applied intuition
