- Reduce food waste: plan meals, use leftovers, and store food properly.

- Walk, bike, or use public transit when you can.
- Combine errands into one trip instead of multiple drives.

- Repair instead of replacing when you can.

**Water use**
- Take shorter showers.
- Run full loads in dishwashers and washing machines.

**Digital footprint (often overlooked)**
- Stream in lower resolution when HD isn’t necessary.
- Delete unused files/emails and avoid unnecessary cloud storage.
