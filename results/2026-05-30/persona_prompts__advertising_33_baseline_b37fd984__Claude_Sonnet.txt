# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Shea butter or cocoa butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds (exfoliants)

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Moisturizing Body Lotion
- 1/2 cup shea butter
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil
- Whip together until fluffy

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 2 tbsp beeswax
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Melt together, pour into containers, let cool

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- Few drops essential oil
- Mix and store in a jar

### 🧖 Face Mask
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp oats (ground)
- Apply for 15 minutes, rinse

---

## Safety Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- Add **vitamin E oil** as a natural preservative
- Label with **date made**
- Most products last **1-3 months**
- Be cautious with essential oils if pregnant
