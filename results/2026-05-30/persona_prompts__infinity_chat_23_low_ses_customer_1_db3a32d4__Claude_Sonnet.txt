── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Getting over a crush rejection varies a lot by person, but here's a realistic picture:

## Typical Timeframes
- **A few days to weeks** - for casual crushes
- **Several weeks to months** - for deeper feelings or longer crushes
- **A few months** - if you have to see them regularly

## What Affects How Long It Takes
- How long/intense the feelings were
- Whether you still see them often
- How the rejection happened
- Your support system
- How busy and distracted you can stay

## Things That Actually Help
- **Let yourself feel it** - don't suppress it
- **Stay busy** - work, hobbies, friends
- **Limit contact** if possible, especially on social media
- **Talk to someone you trust**
- **Be honest with yourself** about the feelings rather than pretending you're fine

## Things That Make It Worse
- Constantly checking their social media
- Replaying conversations
- Isolating yourself
- Hoping they'll change their mind

## Honest Reality
Most people feel significantly better within **4-8 weeks** if they're actively moving forward. It rarely just disappears on its own.
