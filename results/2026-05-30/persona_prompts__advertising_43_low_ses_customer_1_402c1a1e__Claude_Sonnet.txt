# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Pick a location accessible by public transport

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Minimize printed programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Choose seasonal, locally sourced menu items
- Offer plant-based or reduced meat options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent or borrow decorations
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid single-use plastic decorations

## Attire
- Consider renting or buying secondhand
- Choose natural fiber fabrics
- Look into dress rental services

## Practical Tips for Your Situation
Since your schedule is unpredictable, consider:
- **Planning in smaller chunks** when you have free time
- Using **online planning tools** you can access anytime
- Hiring a coordinator to handle details between your shifts
- Keeping the guest list smaller to simplify logistics

## Budget-Friendly Green Options
- DIY decorations from natural materials
- Digital everything where possible
- Potluck-style elements from family
