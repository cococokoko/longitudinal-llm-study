# Best Freelance Writing Platforms for Single Parents

Here are solid options, organized by what might work best for your situation:

## Flexible, Work-When-You-Can Platforms
- **Upwork** - Large marketplace, set your own hours, wide variety of projects
- **Fiverr** - Create service packages clients come to you for
- **Freelancer.com** - Bid on projects that fit your schedule

## Content-Specific Platforms
- **Contena** - Curated writing jobs, good for finding higher-paying work
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Blog and content writing focused
- **BloggingPro** - Similar to ProBlogger, regularly updated listings

## Quick-Pay Options
- **Textbroker** - Lower rates but consistent work and fast payment
- **WriterAccess** - Steady content work with flexible timing

## Higher-Paying Opportunities
- **LinkedIn** - Direct client outreach can land better rates
- **Clearvoice** - Portfolio-based matching with brands

## Practical Tips for Your Situation
- **Start with 2-3 platforms** rather than spreading thin
- **Set realistic availability** upfront with clients
- **Async communication** clients are often more flexible
- **Build toward retainer clients** for predictable income

## Worth Knowing
Upwork and Fiverr have the largest volume of work, making them good starting points while you build a client base.
