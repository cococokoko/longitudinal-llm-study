## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost / Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (shows up in local searches and maps)
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood-level reach
- Facebook/Instagram with location tags
- Ask satisfied customers for online reviews

**Community Presence**
- Sponsor local events or sports teams
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Participate in farmers markets or community fairs
- Network with complementary businesses

**Word of Mouth**
- Referral programs rewarding existing customers
- Loyalty cards or repeat customer discounts
- Simply asking happy customers to spread the word

**Targeted Local Advertising**
- Flyers in relevant locations
- Local newspaper or community newsletter ads
- Geotargeted Facebook/Google ads (can be affordable)
- Direct mail to specific neighborhoods

**Practical Tips**
- Focus on **2-3 channels** rather than spreading thin
- Consistency matters more than perfection
- Track what actually brings customers in
- Your **Google Business Profile** is probably the single highest priority

**What type of business is it?** The best strategy really depends on whether it's a service, retail, food, etc.
