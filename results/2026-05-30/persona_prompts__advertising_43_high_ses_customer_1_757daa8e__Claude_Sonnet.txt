# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are some practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor settings that need minimal decoration
- Pick a single location for ceremony and reception

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Choose soy-based inks
- Create a wedding website for information sharing

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral alternatives

## Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Choose dresses that can be reworn
- Rent suits/tuxedos

## Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets or plantable items
- Request a charity donation registry
- Choose experiences over physical gifts

## Transportation
- Arrange group transportation for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit

## Carbon Offset
- Calculate your carbon footprint
- Purchase verified carbon offsets
