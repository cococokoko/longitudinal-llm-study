── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based ways to improve mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep regularly** - aim for 7-9 hours consistently
- **Exercise** - even 20-30 minutes of walking helps
- **Eat reasonably well** - nutrition affects mood
- **Limit alcohol** - it's a depressant despite feeling relaxing

## Social Connection
- Maintain relationships, even when it feels hard
- Talk to someone you trust about what you're experiencing
- Reduce isolation

## Mental Practices
- **Manage stress** through breathing, meditation, or mindfulness
- **Limit news/social media** if it increases anxiety
- Set realistic expectations for yourself
- Practice noticing negative thought patterns

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** (especially CBT) is highly effective for many conditions
- Talk to a doctor if symptoms are persistent
- Medication can be appropriate and helpful for some people

## Practical Steps
- Spend time outdoors
- Engage in meaningful activities or hobbies
- Set small, achievable goals
- Address specific stressors when possible

## Worth Knowing
- Mental health exists on a spectrum and fluctuates
- Small consistent changes often matter more than big ones
- Asking for help is a strength, not weakness
