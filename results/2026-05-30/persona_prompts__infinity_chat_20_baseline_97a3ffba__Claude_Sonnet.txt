# Points of Interest in Social Media

## 📍 Definition
**Points of Interest (POIs)** in social media refer to specific locations, places, or topics that users tag, check in to, or engage with on social platforms.

---

## 🗺️ Types of POIs in Social Media

### Location-Based POIs
- **Check-ins** (Facebook, Foursquare/Swarm)
- **Geotagged posts** (Instagram, Twitter/X)
- **Location stories** (Snapchat, Instagram)
- **Place tags** on photos and videos

### Content-Based POIs
- **Trending topics** and hashtags
- **Viral posts** and threads
- **Popular accounts** or influencers
- **Event pages**

---

## 📱 Platforms & Features

| Platform | POI Feature |
|----------|-------------|
| Instagram | Location tags, Place pages |
| Facebook | Check-ins, Place pages |
| TikTok | Location hashtags |
| Snapchat | Snap Map |
| Twitter/X | Trending locations |
| Google Maps | User reviews & photos |

---

## 💡 Why POIs Matter

- **Marketing** – Businesses target local audiences
- **Tourism** – Travelers discover new places
- **Analytics** – Track engagement by location
- **Community building** – Connect local users
- **Event promotion** – Drive foot traffic

---

## ⚠️ Considerations
- **Privacy risks** from location sharing
- **Data collection** concerns
- **Misinformation** about places
