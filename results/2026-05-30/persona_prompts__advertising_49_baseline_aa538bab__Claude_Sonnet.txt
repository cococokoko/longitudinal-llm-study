# How to Improve Home WiFi

## Quick Fixes First
- **Restart your router** - unplug for 30 seconds, then plug back in
- **Check for interference** - move router away from microwaves, cordless phones, baby monitors
- **Update router firmware** - check manufacturer's app or website

## Router Placement
- Place it **centrally** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not floor)
- Avoid **thick walls, metal objects, and appliances** nearby
- Position antennas **vertically**

## Improve Your Signal
- **Change WiFi channel** - reduce congestion from neighbors (use apps like WiFi Analyzer)
- **Use 5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby, **2.4GHz** for longer range
- **Reduce connected devices** that aren't in use

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Upgrade your router** if it's more than 4-5 years old
- **Add a WiFi extender/repeater** for dead zones
- **Mesh WiFi system** (like Eero, Google Nest, or Orbi) for whole-home coverage
- **Powerline adapters** to extend network through electrical wiring

## For Best Performance
- Use a **wired ethernet connection** for gaming/streaming when possible
- **Limit bandwidth-heavy activities** during peak times
- Consider upgrading your **internet plan**
