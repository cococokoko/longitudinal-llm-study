── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Safe Travel During a Pandemic for Single Parents

Here are practical, evidence-based options to consider:

## Lowest Risk Options
- **Driving your own vehicle** - minimal contact with others
- **Staying local** - reduces exposure significantly
- **Virtual alternatives** - video calls instead of visiting family

## If You Must Travel

### Before Traveling
- Check current transmission rates at your destination
- Ensure everyone is up to date on vaccinations
- Pack a health kit (masks, hand sanitizer, disinfecting wipes)
- Have a backup childcare plan if someone gets sick

### During Travel
- **Flying:** Choose direct flights, use airport less-busy times, wear well-fitted masks
- **Public transit:** Travel off-peak hours when possible
- **Hotels:** Request rooms away from high-traffic areas

### Practical Single Parent Considerations
- **Travel insurance** is especially important since you're the primary caregiver
- Keep children's medical information accessible
- Know healthcare locations at your destination
- Have emergency contacts ready

## Key Habits Regardless of Transport
- Frequent handwashing
- Avoiding crowded indoor spaces
- Maintaining distance when possible
- Monitoring symptoms before and during travel

## Worth Checking
- CDC travel guidelines
- Your destination's current health requirements
- Your employer's travel policies
