# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a straightforward, effective routine:

## Morning
1. **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
4. **SPF 30+** - sunscreen is essential daily

## Evening
1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide work well
3. **Rich moisturizer or face oil** - squalane or rosehip oil are good options
4. **Occasional retinol** - 1-2x per week if desired (start slowly)

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Shea butter
- Squalane

## Tips
- **Lukewarm water only** - hot water strips moisture
- Apply moisturizer while skin is slightly damp
- Consider a **humidifier** in your home/office
- Stay well hydrated

- CeraVe, La Roche-Posay (accessible)
- Tatcha, Drunk Elephant (premium)
