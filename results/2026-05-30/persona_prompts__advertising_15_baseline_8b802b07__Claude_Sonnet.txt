# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** to make spaces feel larger and cleaner
- Repurpose items from other rooms

---

## Low-Cost Updates with Big Impact

### Paint
- One of the **most affordable transformations**
- Try an accent wall instead of painting entire rooms
- Paint old furniture for a new look

### Lighting
- Swap outdated fixtures for modern ones
- Add floor or table lamps for warmth
- Use **LED bulbs** to save money long-term

### Textiles
- New throw pillows, blankets, or curtains can refresh a room
- Shop discount stores or thrift shops

---

## Smart Shopping Tips
- **Thrift stores & garage sales** for unique finds
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** for furniture deals
- Shop **end-of-season sales**
- Check **IKEA, Target, or Amazon** for affordable basics

---

## DIY Ideas
- Create your own wall art
- Build simple shelving
- Reupholster chairs with fabric
- Make your own throw pillow covers

---

## Free Resources
- **Pinterest** for inspiration
- YouTube tutorials for DIY projects
- Library books on interior design
