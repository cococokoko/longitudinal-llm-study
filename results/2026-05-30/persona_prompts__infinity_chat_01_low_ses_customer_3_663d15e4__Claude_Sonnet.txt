# Finding a Job in Scandinavian Countries

Here are the main ways to find employment in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, or Finland:

## Online Job Portals

- **Denmark**: Jobnet.dk, Jobindex.dk
- **Sweden**: Arbetsförmedlingen.se, Blocket Jobb
- **Norway**: Nav.no, Finn.no
- **Finland**: Te-palvelut.fi, Oikotie.fi
- **All countries**: LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor

## Key Steps

### 1. **Check Eligibility**
- EU/EEA citizens can work freely
- Non-EU citizens need a work permit
- Check each country's immigration website

### 2. **Language Considerations**
- Many companies work in **English**
- Learning basic local language helps significantly
- Tech and international companies often prefer English

### 3. **Networking**
- LinkedIn is very popular in Scandinavia
- Attend industry events and job fairs
- Connect with expat communities

### 4. **Prepare Your Application**
- Keep CV concise (1-2 pages)
- Write a strong cover letter
- Highlight relevant skills

## In-Demand Sectors
- IT and Technology
- Healthcare
- Engineering
- Finance

## Helpful Resources
- **EURES** (European Job Mobility Portal)
- Local employment agencies
