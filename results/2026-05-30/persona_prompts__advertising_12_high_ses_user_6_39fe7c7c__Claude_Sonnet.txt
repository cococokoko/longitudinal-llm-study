# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a structured overview that might be useful whether you're advising clients or optimizing your own firm's travel practices:

---

## **Transportation**
- **Rail over air** – Significantly lower carbon footprint for routes under ~500 miles
- **Direct flights** – Takeoff/landing generate the most emissions
- **Electric/hybrid rentals** – Major providers now offer EV fleets
- **Public transit** at destination vs. taxis/rideshares

---

## **Accommodation**
- **LEED-certified hotels**
- Properties with **Green Key or EarthCheck** certification
- Hotels with **local sourcing** and energy reduction commitments

---

## **Business Travel Specifically**
- **Carbon offset programs** (though quality varies significantly)
- **Virtual-first policies** – Replacing lower-value trips entirely
- **Trip bundling** – Combining multiple meetings into single trips
- Tracking with tools like **Thrust Carbon or Squake**

---

## **Firm-Level Considerations**
- Travel policy integration with **ESG commitments**
- **Supplier scorecards** including sustainability metrics
- Employee **incentive programs** for choosing greener options
