# Comparing Expat Insurance as a Single Parent

Here are the key things to prioritize given your specific situation:

---

## 🏥 Coverage Priorities

### For Your Children
- **Pediatric care** coverage included
- **Vaccinations** and routine check-ups
- **Dental and vision** for kids
- Whether children are covered **at the same premium level** as adults

### For You
- **Mental health support** - single parenting is stressful
- **Maternity coverage** if relevant
- Emergency care without large out-of-pocket costs

---

## ⚡ Practical Considerations

| Factor | Why It Matters to You |
|--------|----------------------|
| **24/7 helpline** | You may need help outside business hours |
| **Direct billing** | Avoid paying upfront when cash is tight |
| **Simple claims process** | Less admin time for busy parents |
| **English-speaking support** | Faster resolution |

---

## 💰 Financial Protection

- **Maximum out-of-pocket limits**
- **No claim gaps** between policies
- What happens if **you are hospitalized** - who covers childcare emergencies

---

## 🔍 Questions to Ask Insurers

- Are **both parent and child** on one policy?
- Is there a **family discount**?
- What is the **pre-existing condition** policy?
- Can you **add dependents** easily?

---

## ⚠️ Watch Out For

- Policies that **exclude children** by default
- High **deductibles per person**
- Limited **mental health sessions**
