# Best Fitness & Nutrition Tracking Apps

## Fitness Tracking

**Comprehensive Tracking**
- **Strava** - Great for running, cycling, and cardio activities
- **Garmin Connect** - Best if you use Garmin devices
- **Apple Fitness+** - Good for iPhone/Apple Watch users

**Workout Planning**
- **Strong** - Simple, effective weightlifting tracker
- **JEFIT** - Detailed exercise library and planning
- **Nike Training Club** - Free guided workouts

## Nutrition Tracking

- **MyFitnessPal** - Largest food database, very popular
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - User-friendly calorie counting
- **Noom** - Combines tracking with coaching (subscription)

## All-in-One Options

- **MyFitnessPal** - Covers both fitness and nutrition
- **Fitbit app** - Works well without the device too
- **HealthifyMe** - Popular, especially in some regions

## Things to Consider When Choosing

- **Cost** - Many have free tiers with paid upgrades
- **Device compatibility** - iOS vs Android vs wearables
- **Your specific goal** - Weight loss, muscle gain, endurance
- **How detailed** you want to get

## Honest Caveats
- Calorie counts are estimates, not exact
- Consistency matters more than which app you pick
