# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What's your core expertise or perspective?
- Who specifically are you trying to reach?
- What problem do you solve or value do you provide?
- What makes your angle different from others in your space?

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B audiences |
| Twitter/X | Thought leadership, networking |
| Instagram | Visual brands, lifestyle, creative |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video-first, broader reach |
| Substack | Deep expertise, writing |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content That Actually Works

**The 3 content types you need:**
- **Educational** - teach something useful
- **Personal** - share your story and perspective
- **Engaging** - ask questions, spark conversation

**Practical rules:**
- Post consistently over posting perfectly
- Have a recognizable format or style
- Take clear positions (vague content gets ignored)
- Show your thinking process, not just conclusions

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Only posting promotional content
- Copying others' style exactly
- Disappearing for weeks, then posting in bursts
- Chasing trends that don't fit your brand

---

## Growth Tactics That Work

- **Engage genuinely** with others in your space
- **Collaborate** with complementary creators
- **Repurpose** content across platforms
- **Build an email list** early (you own it)
- **Be consistent** for at least 6 months before judging results

---

## Measuring Progress

Track what actually matters:
- Are the **right people** following you?
- Are you getting **real opportunities** (jobs, clients, speaking)?
- Is your **network quality** improving?
- Do people associate you with your topic?

---

## The Honest Reality

- Results typically take **6-18 months**
- Authenticity outperforms performance long-term
- Your brand is built by **showing up repeatedly**, not one viral post
