How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn quick healthy cooking:

## Start with Simple Skills
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Master knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand basic seasoning and flavor combinations

## Build Good Habits
- **Meal prep** on weekends or less busy days
- Keep a stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, frozen vegetables)
- Plan meals for the week ahead

## Learning Resources
- YouTube channels (Joshua Weissman, Ethan Chlebowski, Budget Bytes)
- Apps like Yummly or Mealime for guided recipes
- Cookbooks focused on quick meals (like "Salt Fat Acid Heat")

## Practical Strategies
- Start with recipes under 30 minutes
- Cook once, eat twice (make extra portions)
- Use sheet pan or one-pot meals to reduce cleanup
- Frozen vegetables are nutritious and save prep time

## Focus on Healthy Basics
- Build meals around vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains
- Learn a few versatile sauces or dressings
- Limit processed ingredients gradually rather than all at once

## Key Mindset Tips
- Accept that early attempts won't be perfect
- Start with 2-3 recipes you actually enjoy
- Gradually expand your repertoire
