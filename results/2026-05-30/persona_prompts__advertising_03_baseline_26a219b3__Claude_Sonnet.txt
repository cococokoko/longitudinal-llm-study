# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Common Challenges First
- Distractions from family, pets, or household tasks
- Difficulty separating work and personal life
- Lack of structure or routine
- Feelings of isolation
- Overworking or underworking

---

## Key Strategies

### 🏠 Set Up Your Space
- **Dedicate a specific area** for work only
- Keep it **organized and clutter-free**
- Ensure good **lighting and ergonomics**
- Minimize background noise or use headphones

### ⏰ Build a Routine
- Start and end work at **consistent times**
- Get dressed as if going to an office
- Schedule **regular breaks** (try the Pomodoro technique: 25 min work / 5 min break)
- Create a "commute" ritual to signal work mode

### 📋 Manage Your Tasks
- Plan your day **the night before**
- Prioritize using methods like **MIT (Most Important Tasks)**
- Use tools like Todoist, Notion, or a simple notebook
- Tackle hard tasks during your **peak energy hours**

### 📵 Reduce Distractions
- Silence non-essential notifications
- Use website blockers (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- Communicate boundaries to people you live with
- Set specific times to check email/messages

### 🤝 Stay Connected
- Schedule regular check-ins with colleagues
- Use video calls when possible
- Join virtual coworking sessions

### 🧠 Protect Your Energy
- Take a real lunch break
- Move your body regularly
- Know when to **log off completely**

---

## Quick Wins to Try Today
1. Clear your workspace
2. Write down your top 3 tasks for tomorrow
3. Set a firm end time for your workday
