Second, minimize friction.  
With a small account, fees and spreads matter more than your strategy.
- Use a broker with zero commissions and tight spreads

- Don’t use high leverage—it can wipe you out instantly

- Breakout from a clear range

- Risk maybe $1–$2 per trade max (1–2%)

- If you lose 10 trades in a row, you’re still in the game

You’re not trying to make meaningful income from $100. You’re:
- Learning execution
- Understanding how price moves
- Building discipline under real (but small) pressure
