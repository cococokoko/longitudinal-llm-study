# What to Look for When Comparing Expat Insurance

## 🏥 Coverage & Benefits

- **Medical coverage limits** (annual maximum payout)
- **Inpatient vs outpatient** coverage
- **Emergency evacuation** and repatriation
- **Dental and vision** coverage
- **Mental health** coverage
- **Maternity coverage** (if needed)
- **Pre-existing conditions** handling

---

## 💰 Cost Considerations

- Monthly/annual **premium costs**
- **Deductibles and co-pays**
- **Out-of-pocket maximums**
- Whether premiums increase with age
- Currency of payments

---

## 🌍 Geographic Coverage

- **Countries covered** (worldwide vs regional)
- **USA coverage** (significantly raises premiums)
- Home country coverage included
- Travel between countries

---

## 🏨 Network & Access

- Size of **hospital/doctor network**
- **Direct billing** vs reimbursement
- Quality of hospitals in your location
- 24/7 customer support availability

---

## 📋 Policy Details

- **Exclusions and limitations**
- Waiting periods
- Policy renewal guarantees
- Cancellation terms
- Claims process ease

---

## 🏢 Insurer Reputation

- Financial stability ratings
- Customer reviews
- Claims payment history
- Experience with expats

---

## ✅ Quick Tips

- **Compare at least 3-5 providers**
- Read the fine print on exclusions
- Consider your specific destination's healthcare costs
- Check if your employer offers group coverage
