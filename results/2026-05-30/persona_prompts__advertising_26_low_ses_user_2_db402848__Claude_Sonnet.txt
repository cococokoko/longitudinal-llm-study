# Data Security Best Practices for Small Businesses

Here are the key practices organized by priority:

## Essential Foundations
- **Use strong, unique passwords** with a password manager (Bitwarden, LastPass)
- **Enable two-factor authentication** on all business accounts
- **Keep software updated** - patches fix security vulnerabilities
- **Regular data backups** using the 3-2-1 rule (3 copies, 2 formats, 1 offsite)

## Employee & Access Management
- Give employees **minimum necessary access** to data
- Have clear **offboarding procedures** when staff leave
- Train employees to **recognize phishing emails**

## Network Security
- Use a **business-grade router** with firewall
- Separate **guest WiFi** from business network
- Consider a **VPN** for remote work

## Data Protection
- **Encrypt sensitive files** and devices
- Know **what data you collect** and store only what's necessary
- Have a clear **privacy policy** for customer data

## Practical Planning
- Create a simple **incident response plan** - what to do if breached
- Consider **cyber liability insurance**
- Use reputable **cloud services** with strong security reputations

## Low-Cost Starting Points
- Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 have built-in security tools
- Many banks offer **fraud monitoring** for business accounts
