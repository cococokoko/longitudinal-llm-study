Here are some practical tips for decorating your apartment on a minimal budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh look
- Repurpose items you already own
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better

## Low-Cost Shopping Options
- **Thrift stores and secondhand shops** - great for unique pieces
- **Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist** - furniture for very little
- **Dollar stores** - for small decorative items
- **Discount stores** like TJ Maxx or HomeGoods

## Simple DIY Ideas
- Paint an accent wall (one can of paint goes far)
- Create gallery walls with printed photos
- Make your own throw pillow covers
- Use washi tape for temporary wall designs

## Free or Nearly Free Options
- Natural elements like branches, rocks, or plants from cuttings
- Printable art you find online
- Rearranging furniture seasonally
- Borrowing items from family

## Smart Priorities
- Focus on **lighting** - it changes everything
- Add **plants** - affordable and impactful
- Use **mirrors** to make spaces feel larger
- Invest in a few quality textiles (rugs, throws)

## Practical Tip
Since your schedule can be unpredictable, **weekend thrift store visits** when you have free time can be really rewarding without requiring much planning.
