# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration. You can gradually increase over time.

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting, lying down, or even walking
2. **Focus on your breath** - notice the inhale and exhale
3. **When your mind wanders** - gently bring attention back (this is normal and expected)
4. **Repeat** without judging yourself

## Practical Tips for Beginners

- **Same time each day** builds the habit (morning works well for many people)
- **Quiet space** helps initially, though not strictly necessary
- **Use an app** like Headspace, Calm, or Insight Timer for guided sessions
- **Start with guided meditations** if sitting in silence feels difficult

## Types to Explore

| Type | Good For |
|------|----------|
| Breath focus | Beginners, stress relief |
| Body scan | Relaxation, sleep |
| Loving-kindness | Emotional wellbeing |
| Walking meditation | People who struggle sitting still |

## Realistic Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander - that's not failure
- Benefits often take **weeks** to notice
- There's no "perfect" meditation

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Expecting instant results
- Giving up after a few days
- Thinking you're "bad at it"
