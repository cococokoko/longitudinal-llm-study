── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep pets healthy and happy:

## Basic Health Care
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm treatments
- **Spay or neuter** if appropriate
- **Keep up with medications** as prescribed

## Nutrition
- Feed **age-appropriate, quality food**
- Provide **fresh water** always
- Avoid overfeeding to prevent obesity
- Know which **human foods are toxic** to your pet

## Exercise & Mental Stimulation
- Daily walks or playtime
- Toys and enrichment activities
- Training and learning new skills
- Social interaction with people and other animals

## Emotional Wellbeing
- Spend **quality time** with your pet
- Provide a **safe, comfortable space**
- Learn to recognize signs of stress or anxiety
- Be consistent with routines

## Grooming
- Regular brushing and bathing
- Nail trimming
- Ear cleaning when needed

## Safety
- ID tags and microchipping
- Pet-proof your home
- Keep toxic plants and chemicals out of reach

## Things to Watch For
- Changes in eating, drinking, or behavior
- Unusual lumps or weight changes
- Signs of pain or discomfort
