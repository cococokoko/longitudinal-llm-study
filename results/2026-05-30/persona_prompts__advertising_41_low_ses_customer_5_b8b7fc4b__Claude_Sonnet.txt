How can I improve my credit score quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve your credit score, with your situation in mind:

## Highest Impact Actions

**Pay down credit card balances**
- Aim to keep utilization below 30% (ideally under 10%)
- This can show results within 1-2 billing cycles

**Pay all bills on time**
- Set up automatic payments for minimums
- Payment history is the biggest factor (~35% of score)

## Practical Steps for Busy Households

- **Become an authorized user** on a family member's account with good history
- **Ask for credit limit increases** without spending more (lowers utilization ratio)
- **Dispute any errors** on your credit report at AnnualCreditReport.com

## Things to Avoid

- Don't close old accounts
- Avoid applying for multiple new cards at once
- Don't miss payments even during tight months

## Given Managing Multiple People's Finances

- Consider a **secured credit card** if rebuilding from scratch
- **Experian Boost** can add utility/phone payments to your report
- Keep emergency funds separate so credit cards aren't your backup

## Realistic Timeline

- Small improvements: **1-3 months**
- Significant changes: **6-12 months**
