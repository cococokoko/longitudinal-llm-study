── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I work in a warehouse position that requires long hours.

I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
## Free Ways to Host Your Resume Website

Here are the best options for hosting static HTML files:

---

### **GitHub Pages** (Most Popular)
- Completely free
- Go to **github.com** and create a free account
- Create a new repository
- Upload your HTML files
- Go to Settings → Pages → enable it
- Your site will be at **username.github.io/repository-name**

---

### **Netlify** (Easiest)
- Go to **netlify.com** and create a free account
- You can literally **drag and drop your folder** of HTML files
- Get a free URL like **yourname.netlify.app**
- This is probably the fastest option for you

---

### **Google Drive** *(not recommended anymore)*
- Google removed public hosting support

---

## My Recommendation

**Use Netlify** since it is the simplest:
1. Create account
2. Drag and drop your files
3. Done in about 5 minutes

---

## Tips for Your Resume Site
- Make sure your main file is named **index.html**
- Keep all files in one folder before uploading
- You can share the link directly on job applications
