# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces (parks, gardens, farms)
- Pick a location accessible by public transport

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send **digital invitations** or save-the-dates
- Use recycled or seed paper if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus
- Create a wedding website for information

## 👗 Attire
- Rent, borrow, or buy secondhand wedding attire
- Choose dresses that can be worn again
- Support sustainable fashion brands
- Donate or resell after the wedding

## 🌸 Flowers & Décor
- Use **locally grown, seasonal flowers**
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Rent décor instead of buying
- Use natural or biodegradable decorations

## 🍽️ Food & Drink
- Serve **locally sourced, seasonal food**
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Donate leftover food to shelters
- Use real dishes instead of disposables

## ♻️ Waste Reduction
- Set up recycling and compost stations
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue near hotels
- Offset carbon emissions

## 💝 Extras
- Give **eco-friendly favors** (seeds, local honey, plants)
- Register for experiences rather than things
- Donate to environmental charities in guests' honor
