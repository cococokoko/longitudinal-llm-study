# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Classical has nylon strings (easier on fingers), Electric requires an amp but has lighter strings
- **Get essential accessories** - Picks, tuner, capo, extra strings
- **Learn proper posture** - Sitting position, how to hold the neck, strumming hand position

## First Things to Learn
1. **Tune your guitar** - Use a clip-on tuner or tuning app (GuitarTuna is popular)
2. **Basic chords** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
3. **Chord transitions** - Practice switching smoothly between chords
4. **Basic strumming patterns** - Down strokes first, then up/down combinations
5. **Simple songs** - Apply what you learn right away

## Tips for Progress
- **Practice consistently** - 15-30 minutes daily beats 3 hours once a week
- **Be patient with finger pain** - Calluses develop over time
- **Use resources** - YouTube (Justin Guitar is highly recommended), apps like Yousician
- **Learn songs you love** - Keeps motivation high

## Common Beginner Mistakes
- Skipping fundamentals
- Practicing too fast
- Neglecting music theory basics
