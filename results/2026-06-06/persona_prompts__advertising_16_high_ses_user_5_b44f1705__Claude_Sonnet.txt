# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

Given your role, you're likely dealing with **high-stakes decisions, constant pressure, and always-on expectations**. Here are practical approaches:

---

## Immediate Stress Relief
- **Box breathing** (4 counts in, hold, out, hold) - usable in meetings
- Brief walks between calls
- Single-tasking rather than context-switching constantly

---

## Structural Habits
- **Protect recovery time** - calendar block it like a board meeting
- Set clear boundaries on after-hours communication
- Delegate decisions that don't require your level
- Regular exercise - non-negotiable for executive performance

---

## Cognitive Approaches
- Distinguish between **what you control vs. what you don't**
- Separate your identity from company performance
- Challenge catastrophic thinking about outcomes
- Regular journaling to process decisions

---

## Professional Support Worth Considering
- **Executive coach** - normalized at your level
- **Therapist or psychologist** - genuinely effective, not a weakness
- Peer groups with other executives who understand the context

---

## Worth Monitoring
Chronic executive stress can lead to **burnout, poor decision-making, and health consequences** - all of which affect your effectiveness.
