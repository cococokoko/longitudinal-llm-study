── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Eco-friendly travel options.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Eco-friendly travel (or sustainable travel) is all about making simple choices that lessen your environmental impact and support local communities. 

Here is a comprehensive guide to eco-friendly travel options, broken down by the different stages of your trip:

### 1. Transportation: Getting There & Around
Transportation is usually the largest source of carbon emissions in travel.
*   **Take the Train:** Trains are one of the most eco-friendly ways to travel long distances. They emit significantly less carbon per passenger than planes or cars.
*   **Travel by Bus:** Modern coaches are highly fuel-efficient and a great low-emission option for regional travel.
*   **If You Must Fly, Fly Smart:** 
    *   **Fly direct:** Takeoffs and landings consume the most fuel.
    *   **Fly economy:** More people in the cabin means a lower carbon footprint per person.
