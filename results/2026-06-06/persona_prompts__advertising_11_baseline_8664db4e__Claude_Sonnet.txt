# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks** – core strength
- **Burpees** – full body + cardio
- **Dips** – using a chair or sturdy surface

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Jump rope
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Dancing or aerobics videos

## Home Equipment (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells or kettlebells
- Pull-up bar (doorframe)
- Yoga mat

## Everyday Activity
- Take the stairs
- Walk or bike instead of driving
- Active chores (gardening, cleaning)
- Stand instead of sitting when possible

## Useful Strategies
- **Follow YouTube workouts** – many are free and structured
- **Set a schedule** – consistency matters more than intensity
- **Progressive overload** – gradually increase reps or difficulty
- **Track your progress** – keeps you motivated

## Key Principles
| Goal | Focus On |
|------|----------|
| Weight loss | Cardio + diet |
| Muscle building | Resistance training |
| Flexibility | Yoga/stretching |
| Endurance | Running/cycling |
