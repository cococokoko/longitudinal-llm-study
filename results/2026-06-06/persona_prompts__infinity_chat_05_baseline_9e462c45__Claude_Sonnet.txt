# Smartwatch Feature for Senior Citizens

## 💊 **Intelligent Medication Management System**

### Core Functionality
A **comprehensive medication reminder and tracking system** that goes beyond simple alarms.

---

### Key Components

**Smart Reminders**
- Gentle vibration + audio alerts with large visual display
- Escalating notifications if doses are missed
- Simple one-tap confirmation ("I took my medication")

**Caregiver Connection**
- Automatically notifies family members or caregivers if doses are consistently missed
- Shared dashboard accessible via smartphone app

**Health Integration**
- Tracks medication adherence over time
- Syncs with doctor's appointments and prescription refill schedules
- Alerts when supply is running low

---

### Why This Feature Makes Sense

| Challenge | Solution |
|-----------|----------|
| Forgetting medications | Persistent, escalating reminders |
| Complex schedules | Pre-programmed by family/pharmacist |
| Independence concerns | Reduces need for constant caregiver check-ins |
| Health outcomes | Improved adherence reduces hospitalizations |

---

### Design Considerations
- **Large, readable text** on display
- **Simple confirmation** requiring minimal dexterity
- **Long battery life** to ensure reliability
- **Loud enough alerts** for hearing impairment

This feature directly addresses one of the **most critical daily health challenges** seniors face while supporting their independence.
