# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Shea butter / cocoa butter
- Essential oils
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Moisturizing Body Lotion
- 1/2 cup shea butter
- 1/4 cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil
- **Whip together** until fluffy

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 2 tbsp beeswax
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- **Melt together**, pour into small containers, let cool

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- Few drops essential oil
- **Mix and store** in a jar

### 🧖 Face Mask
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp oats (ground)
- Apply for 15 minutes, rinse

### 💆 Hair Mask
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Apply to hair, wait 30 minutes, wash out

---

## Safety Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean tools** and containers
- Add **vitamin E oil** as a natural preservative
- Store products **away from heat and light**
- Products **without preservatives** have shorter shelf life
