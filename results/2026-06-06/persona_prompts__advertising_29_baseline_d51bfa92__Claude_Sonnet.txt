── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and preventive care
- **Proper nutrition** - age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - daily activity suited to your pet's species and breed
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm protection
- **Dental care** - regular teeth brushing or dental treats

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - toys, puzzles, and activities that stimulate their mind
- **Social interaction** - time with you and appropriate socialization
- **Consistent routine** - pets generally feel secure with predictable schedules
- **Safe space** - a comfortable place they can retreat to

## Practical Care
- **Grooming** - regular brushing, bathing, and nail trimming
- **Clean environment** - fresh water, clean bedding, sanitary living space
- **Spay/neuter** - can prevent health and behavioral issues
- **ID and microchip** - helps if they get lost

## Watch For Warning Signs
- Changes in eating, drinking, or bathroom habits
- Unusual behavior or lethargy
- Weight changes
- Signs of pain or discomfort

The specific needs vary quite a bit depending on whether you have a **dog, cat, bird, fish, reptile**, etc. Would you like more specific advice for a particular type of pet?
