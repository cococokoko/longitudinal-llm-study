## Be Clear About Expectations

Make sure your team knows:
- **What** they're responsible for
- **When** things are due
- **What success looks like**

Unclear expectations are one of the most common sources of frustration and underperformance on teams. When people know exactly what's expected, they can focus their energy on doing the work rather than guessing what you want.

**Practical ways to do this:**
- Set specific, measurable goals
- Document responsibilities so everyone can reference them
- Check in regularly to address confusion early
- Ask team members to repeat back their understanding of a task

This simple habit can reduce miscommunication, build trust, and improve overall team performance.
