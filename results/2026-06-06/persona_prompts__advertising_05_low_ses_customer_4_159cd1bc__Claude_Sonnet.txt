# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Great topic, especially with your shift work schedule! Here are practical strategies that work well for healthcare workers.

## Core Strategies

**Batch cooking on days off**
- Cook large portions when you're not working
- Portion into grab-and-go containers for busy shift days
- Saves both money and time

**Budget-friendly protein sources**
- Eggs (~$3-4/dozen)
- Canned tuna/salmon
- Dried beans and lentils
- Chicken thighs (cheaper than breasts)
- Greek yogurt

## Shift-Friendly Meal Ideas

**Quick grab before a shift**
- Overnight oats prepared the night before
- Hard-boiled eggs + fruit
- Peanut butter on whole grain toast

**Meals that reheat well**
- Soups and stews
- Rice and bean bowls
- Stir-fry with vegetables

## Money-Saving Tips

- **Shop sales** and plan meals around what's discounted
- **Frozen vegetables** are just as nutritious and cheaper
- **Store brands** save 20-30% typically
- **Reduce food waste** by planning before shopping
