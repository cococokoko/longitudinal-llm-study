# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration. Many people quit because they try too long too soon.

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting, lying down, or even walking
2. **Focus on your breath** - notice the inhale and exhale
3. **When your mind wanders** (it will) - gently bring attention back
4. **That's it** - the returning is the practice, not staying perfectly focused

## Practical Tips for Beginners

- **Same time daily** builds habit (morning often works well)
- **Use an app** like Headspace, Calm, or Insight Timer for guidance
- **Start guided** before trying silent meditation
- **Don't judge** your sessions as good or bad

## Mindfulness Beyond Meditation

You can practice throughout the day:
- Eat one meal without distractions
- Take a few conscious breaths before checking your phone
- Notice sensations during routine tasks like washing dishes

## Realistic Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander constantly - this is normal
- Benefits often take **weeks** to notice
- There's no "perfect" meditation
- Even 2 minutes counts

## Good Free Resources

- YouTube: search "guided meditation for beginners"
- Insight Timer app (free)
- Books: *Wherever You Go, There You Are* by Jon Kabat-Zinn
