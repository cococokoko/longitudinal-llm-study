## Starting a Successful Podcast from Home

### Equipment Basics
- **Microphone** - A USB mic like Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica AT2020 works well
- **Headphones** - To monitor your audio
- **Pop filter** - Reduces harsh sounds
- **Quiet space** - Closets with clothes work surprisingly well

### Planning Your Podcast
- Choose a **specific niche** you're passionate about
- Define your **target audience**
- Decide on episode **format and length**
- Plan your first **10 episode topics** before launching

### Recording & Editing
- Free software like **Audacity** or **GarageBand**
- Record consistently in the same space
- Edit out long pauses and mistakes

### Publishing
- Use hosting platforms like **Buzzsprout, Anchor, or Spotify for Podcasters**
- Submit to Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and Google Podcasts
- Create consistent **cover art**

### Growing Your Audience
- Post consistently on a schedule
- Promote on social media
- Engage with listeners
- Consider **guests** to expand reach

### Realistic Expectations
- Growth takes **6-12 months** typically
- Consistency matters more than perfection
