# Finding Affordable Eco-Friendly Travel Destinations

## Research Strategies

### Online Resources
- **Websites**: EcoTourism.org, Responsible Travel, Green Globe certified destinations
- **Blogs**: Search "budget eco travel" or "sustainable travel on a budget"
- **Reddit communities**: r/solotravel, r/travel for real traveler recommendations

### Certification Programs to Look For
- LEED certified accommodations
- Green Key certified hotels
- B Corp certified tour operators

---

## Destination Types That Tend to Be Affordable

| Type | Examples | Why Affordable |
|------|----------|----------------|
| National Parks | Southeast Asia, Central America | Lower cost of living |
| Rural/off-peak areas | Eastern Europe, rural India | Less tourist infrastructure |
| Volunteer programs | Africa, South America | Work-exchange options |

---

## Practical Tips to Reduce Costs

- **Travel off-season** - lower prices, less environmental crowding
- **Stay longer in fewer places** - reduces transportation emissions AND costs
- **Choose overland travel** over flying when possible
- **Book homestays** (often cheaper and more community-supporting)
- **Eat local food** - cheaper and lower carbon footprint

---

## Budget-Friendly Eco Destinations Worth Considering
- **Costa Rica** (established eco-tourism infrastructure)
- **Portugal** (affordable European option)
- **Vietnam** (low cost, rich nature)
- **Colombia** (biodiversity + budget-friendly)
