- Healthy fats:
  - Peanut butter

  - Olive or canola oil

Simple meal-planning method:
1. Choose 2–3 proteins for the week.
2. Choose 2 grains or starches.
3. Choose 4–5 vegetables.
4. Plan meals that reuse ingredients.
5. Cook larger batches and use leftovers.

Breakfasts:
