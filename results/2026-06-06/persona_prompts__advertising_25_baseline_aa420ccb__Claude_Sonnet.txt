── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to decorate minimally and affordably:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh feel
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Additions
- **Plants** - even small ones add life and warmth
- **Thrift stores** - frames, vases, art, furniture
- **Secondhand apps** like Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist
- **Dollar stores** for basic organizational items

## Free or Nearly Free
- Print your own art (many free downloads online)
- Collect interesting rocks, branches, or pinecones
- Rearrange books by color on shelves
- Use fabric scraps as table runners

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** one accent wall
- **New throw pillows** can transform a couch
- **Better lighting** - swap bulbs or add a lamp
- **Mirrors** make spaces feel larger
- **Curtains** hung high make ceilings look taller

## Practical Tips
- Focus on one room at a time
- Buy secondhand first, new as a last resort
- Stick to a simple color palette so things coordinate
- Quality over quantity - a few nice things beat many cheap ones
