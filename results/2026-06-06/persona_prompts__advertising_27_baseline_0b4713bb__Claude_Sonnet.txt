── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint across different areas of daily life:

## Food & Diet
- Eat less meat, especially beef (biggest single dietary impact)
- Buy local and seasonal produce when possible
- Reduce food waste by planning meals
- Compost food scraps

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit
- Combine errands into single trips
- Work from home when possible
- Consider an electric or hybrid vehicle

## Home Energy
- Lower your thermostat a few degrees
- Use LED lighting
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Improve insulation/weatherstripping

## Shopping & Consumption
- Buy secondhand when possible
- Choose quality items that last longer
- Repair rather than replace
- Reduce single-use plastics

## Higher Impact Actions
- Switch to renewable energy (solar panels or green energy plans)
- Fly less - flights are significant contributors
- Have conversations that influence others

## Realistic Perspective
- **Focus on high-impact changes first** rather than stressing over minor details
- Individual action matters but systemic change also matters
- Progress over perfection is a reasonable approach
