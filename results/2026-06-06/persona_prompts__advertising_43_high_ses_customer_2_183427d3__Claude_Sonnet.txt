# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with existing green certifications
- Consider outdoor settings that need minimal decoration
- Select a location accessible by public transportation

## Invitations & Paper
- Use digital invitations or wedding websites
- Choose recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Choose plant-forward or vegetarian menus
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (contains microplastics)

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose natural fiber fabrics
- Plan to repurpose or donate the dress afterward

## Practical Logistics
- Provide shuttle service for guests
- Choose vendors within close proximity to each other
- Use digital photography over excessive printing

## Offsetting Impact
- Purchase carbon offsets for unavoidable emissions
- Make a donation to an environmental organization

Would you like more detail on any specific aspect of eco-friendly wedding planning?
