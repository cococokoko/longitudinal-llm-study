# Learning a New Language Effectively

## Core Principles

**Consistency beats intensity** — 20 minutes daily outperforms 3 hours once a week. Your brain consolidates language during rest periods.

---

## Key Strategies

### 1. Build a Strong Foundation First
- Learn the **most common 1,000-2,000 words** (covers ~90% of everyday conversation)
- Master basic sentence structures before complex grammar
- Focus on *high-frequency* vocabulary relevant to your goals

### 2. Use Spaced Repetition
- Apps like **Anki** or **Duolingo** schedule review at optimal intervals
- Prevents forgetting by revisiting material just before you'd lose it

### 3. Immerse Yourself
- Change your phone/device language
- Watch shows with subtitles (target language subtitles, not your native language)
- Listen to podcasts, music, and radio

### 4. Speak Early and Often
- **Don't wait until you're "ready"** — you never will be
- Use italki, Tandem, or HelloTalk for conversation partners
- Embrace mistakes as data, not failures

### 5. Comprehensible Input
- Consume content slightly above your current level
- Reading + listening simultaneously is highly effective

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Translating word-for-word in your head
- Studying grammar rules without using them
- Switching apps constantly instead of going deep
