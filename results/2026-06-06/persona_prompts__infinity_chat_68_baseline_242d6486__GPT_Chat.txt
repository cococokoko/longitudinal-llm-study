Give me a tip for managing a team of coworkers.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One effective tip: establish clear ownership for tasks and decisions.

When multiple people are involved in a project, confusion about who is responsible can lead to delays, duplicated work, or missed tasks. For each major task, make sure everyone knows:
- Who is responsible for completing it
- What success looks like
- When it is due
- Who needs to be informed of progress

A simple way to do this is to end meetings with a quick recap of action items, owners, and deadlines. This creates accountability and reduces misunderstandings without requiring micromanagement.
