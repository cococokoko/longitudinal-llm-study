- It can help soothe sore throats and minor wounds

**Things to be realistic about:**
- Some claims about honey's health benefits are exaggerated
- It's still essentially a sugar and should be consumed in moderation
- "Superfood" marketing sometimes overstates its benefits

**Culturally:**
- It has been valued across many cultures and throughout history
- It appears in mythology and religious traditions, which may contribute to a sense of it being special
