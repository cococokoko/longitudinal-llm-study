── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One example of an optimization technique used in machine learning is Gradient Descent.

Gradient Descent is an algorithm that iteratively adjusts a model's parameters to minimize a loss function by moving in the direction of the negative gradient (the direction of steepest decrease). Variants include:
- Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)
- Mini-batch Gradient Descent
- Adam (Adaptive Moment Estimation)
- RMSprop

For example, neural networks are commonly trained using Gradient Descent or one of its variants.
