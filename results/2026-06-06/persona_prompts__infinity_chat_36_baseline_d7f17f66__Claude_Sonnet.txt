# Day Trading Strategies: An Honest Assessment

I'll give you a straightforward answer rather than hype.

## The Reality First
**No single "best" strategy exists** - performance depends on:
- Market conditions
- Trader skill/discipline
- Capital available
- Risk management

## Consistently Cited Effective Strategies

### Momentum Trading
- Trade stocks/assets moving strongly in one direction
- Follow volume and news catalysts
- Relatively straightforward to understand

### Mean Reversion
- Trade assets back toward their average price
- Works well in range-bound markets

### Breakout Trading
- Enter when price breaks key support/resistance
- Clear entry/exit rules

### Scalping
- Many small, quick trades
- Requires significant focus and low commissions

## What Actually Determines Profitability

- **Risk management** (position sizing, stop losses)
- **Consistency** in execution
- **Emotional discipline**
- **Low transaction costs**
- **Realistic expectations**

## Important Honest Facts

- **~70-80% of day traders lose money** (documented by multiple studies)
- Most profitable traders combine **multiple approaches**
- **Edge erodes** as more people use the same strategy

## Bottom Line
The "best" strategy is one you can **execute consistently with discipline** while managing risk.
