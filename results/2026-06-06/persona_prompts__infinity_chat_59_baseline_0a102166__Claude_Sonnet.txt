# A Tip for Teacher Organization: **Use a Weekly "Brain Dump" Planning Session**

Set aside **15-20 minutes every Friday afternoon** (or Sunday evening) to do this:

## What to do:
- **Write down everything** on your mind - assignments to grade, emails to send, copies to make, parent contacts needed
- **Sort items** into categories (urgent this week, can wait, delegate)
- **Prep your Monday** so you walk in with a clear plan

## Why it works for teachers specifically:
- Teaching is mentally exhausting, so carrying tasks in your head adds unnecessary stress
- It creates a **clean mental break** between your work week and personal time
- You stop forgetting small but important tasks like submitting attendance corrections or ordering supplies

## Simple tools to try:
- A **physical notebook** kept on your desk
- A **Google Keep or Trello board**
- Even sticky notes organized by day

---

The biggest benefit is that you **stop relying on memory** during hectic school days and instead trust your system.
