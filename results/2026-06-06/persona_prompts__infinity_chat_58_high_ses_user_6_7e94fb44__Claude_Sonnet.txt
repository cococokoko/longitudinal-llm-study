# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the kingdom of Sunnydale, a brave prince named Oliver and a clever princess named Iris were the very best of friends who loved going on adventures together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy old dragon named Smudge swooped down from Dreary Mountain and trapped everyone in the kingdom inside a giant bubble made of smoke and fire. Prince Oliver tried throwing rocks at the dragon, and Princess Iris tried reasoning with him, but Smudge just sneezed sparks at them and laughed a big, rumbling laugh. The royal knights had run away, the royal wizard was on vacation in Florida, and even the royal dog had hidden under the bed. Just when all hope seemed lost, Prince Oliver remembered he had a Prime membership, quickly pulled out his royal iPhone, and ordered the Legendary Sword of Eternal Brightness with two-day shipping. Sure enough, two days later, a smiling delivery box appeared at the castle door, Oliver unwrapped the glowing golden sword, charged up the mountain with Princess Iris cheering him on, and gave Smudge such a tremendous bop on the nose that the dragon sneezed so hard he blew himself all the way to the next kingdom, and everyone in Sunnydale lived happily — and bubble-free — ever after.
