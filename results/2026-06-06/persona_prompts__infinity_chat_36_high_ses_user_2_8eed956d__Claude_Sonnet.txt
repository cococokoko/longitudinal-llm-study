## The Honest Answer

**There is no single "best and most profitable day trading strategy of all time."** Anyone claiming otherwise is likely selling something.

## What the Evidence Actually Shows

- **~70-80% of day traders lose money** consistently (studies from FINRA, SEC, and academic research)
- Most "profitable strategies" suffer from **survivorship bias** - we hear about winners, not the majority who failed
- Edges that exist tend to get **arbitraged away** quickly, especially by institutional algorithms
- Transaction costs, spreads, and taxes **erode returns significantly**

## Strategies That Have Shown *Some* Historical Evidence

- Momentum trading
- Mean reversion
- Opening range breakouts
- Statistical arbitrage (largely institutional now)

## What Actually Works for the Few Who Succeed

- **Strict risk management** (position sizing, stop losses)
- **Consistency** over big wins
- **Low transaction costs**
- **Psychological discipline**
