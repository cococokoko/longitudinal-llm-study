# Learning to Play Guitar

That's a great hobby to pick up! Here are some practical tips that work well with a busy, unpredictable schedule:

## Getting Started
- **Choose your guitar type** - acoustic (no equipment needed) or electric
- **Learn basic chord shapes first** - C, G, D, Em, Am cover hundreds of songs
- **Get a tuner app** (GuitarTuna is free and easy)

## Practice Tips for a Busy Schedule
Since your work hours can vary, short sessions work great:
- **Even 10-15 minutes daily** beats a 2-hour session once a week
- Practice during **breaks or before/after shifts**
- Keep your guitar **out of its case** so it's easy to grab

## Free Learning Resources
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar is highly recommended for beginners
- **Yousician** - app-based, go at your own pace
- **Ultimate Guitar** - chord charts for songs you love

## Quick Wins to Stay Motivated
- Learn a song you actually **love** early on
- Finger soreness is normal at first - it goes away
- Record yourself occasionally to hear your progress

## Realistic Expectations
- Basic songs: **1-3 months**
- Comfortable playing: **6-12 months**
- Consistency matters more than long sessions
