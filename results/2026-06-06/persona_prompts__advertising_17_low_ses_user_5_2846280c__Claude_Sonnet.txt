── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I support extended family members and manage many household responsibilities.

How can I make my home more energy efficient?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Great goal! Here are practical ways to improve home energy efficiency:

## Quick/Low-Cost Steps
- Switch to LED bulbs throughout the home
- Use power strips to eliminate "phantom" energy drain from electronics
- Seal gaps around doors and windows with weatherstripping or caulk
- Set water heater to 120°F instead of higher

## Heating & Cooling (often biggest savings)
- Change HVAC filters regularly (every 1-3 months)
- Use a programmable or smart thermostat
- Keep vents unblocked by furniture
- Use ceiling fans to reduce AC/heating load

## Appliance Habits
- Run dishwasher and laundry with full loads
- Wash clothes in cold water when possible
- Clean refrigerator coils annually
- Air dry dishes and clothes when practical

## Bigger Investments Worth Considering
- Insulation improvements (attic especially)
- Energy Star appliances when replacing old ones
- Low-flow showerheads and faucet aerators
- Window upgrades if drafty

## Managing a Larger Household
- Set clear household rules about lights/thermostat
- Check for **utility company rebates** - many offer free energy audits
- Look into **LIHEAP** or other assistance programs if costs are a burden
