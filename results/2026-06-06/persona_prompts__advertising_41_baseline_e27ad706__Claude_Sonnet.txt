How can I improve my credit score quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve your credit score:

## Highest Impact Actions
- **Pay down credit card balances** - Lower your credit utilization ratio (aim for under 30%, ideally under 10%)
- **Pay all bills on time** - Payment history is the biggest factor (~35% of your score)
- **Become an authorized user** on someone else's account with good history

## Fix Errors
- Check your credit reports at **AnnualCreditReport.com** (free)
- Dispute any inaccurate negative items
- This can show results relatively quickly

## Avoid Common Mistakes
- Don't close old accounts (hurts your credit history length)
- Don't apply for multiple new cards at once (hard inquiries lower your score)
- Don't miss any payments

## Realistic Timeline
| Action | Potential Timeframe |
|--------|-------------------|
| Paying down balances | 1-2 months |
| Disputing errors | 30-45 days |
| Building payment history | 6-12 months |

## Honest Expectations
- **Quick fixes are limited** - significant improvement usually takes several months
- Be cautious of services promising instant results
- Consistent good habits matter most
