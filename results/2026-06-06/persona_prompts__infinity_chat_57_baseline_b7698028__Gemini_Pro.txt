Name one meaning of life.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One profound meaning of life is **to discover your unique gifts and use them to contribute to the world.** 

This perspective suggests that true fulfillment comes from a two-step process:
1. **Self-discovery:** Looking inward to find what brings you joy, what you are passionate about, and what you are naturally good at.
2. **Connection:** Looking outward to share those abilities with others, whether by creating art, raising a loving family, building a business, or simply being a good friend. 

This idea is beautifully captured in a famous quote often attributed to Pablo Picasso: *"The meaning of life is to find your gift. The purpose of life is to give it away."*
