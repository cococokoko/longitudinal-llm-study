# Effective Online Teaching Techniques

## Engagement Strategies
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minute chunks work better than long lectures)
- **Use interactive polls and quizzes** (Mentimeter, Kahoot, Poll Everywhere)
- **Ask frequent questions** rather than lecturing continuously
- **Incorporate think-pair-share** activities adapted for breakout rooms

## Communication & Presence
- **Establish clear expectations** early about participation and response times
- **Use video when possible** to build human connection
- **Provide regular, specific feedback** on assignments
- **Create announcements and check-ins** to maintain momentum

## Course Design
- **Organize content logically** with clear navigation
- **Offer multiple formats** (video, text, audio) for different learners
- **Build in flexibility** where possible
- **Use rubrics** so students know exactly what's expected

## Technology Tips
- Keep tools **simple and consistent** - avoid overwhelming students with platforms
- **Test everything** before class
- Have a **backup plan** for technical failures
- **Record sessions** for students who miss class

## Building Community
- Discussion boards with meaningful prompts
- Collaborative projects
- Virtual office hours
- Peer review activities

## What Research Suggests Works Best
- Active learning over passive consumption
- Frequent low-stakes assessment
- Timely, constructive feedback
- Clear structure and predictability
