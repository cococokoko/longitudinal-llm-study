# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a practical overview of sustainable travel choices:

## Transportation
- **Train travel** - significantly lower carbon footprint than flying
- **Electric vehicles** - for road trips, with growing charging infrastructure
- **Carpooling/ridesharing** - reduces per-person emissions
- **Cycling or walking** - for local exploration
- **Direct flights** - when flying is necessary (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)

## Accommodation
- **Certified green hotels** - look for LEED certification or Green Key designation
- **Eco-lodges** - often support local conservation
- **Home-sharing** - can reduce resource consumption vs. traditional hotels

## Practical Strategies
- **Carbon offset programs** - through reputable organizations
- **Pack light** - reduces fuel consumption
- **Choose local** - restaurants, guides, and experiences
- **Avoid single-use plastics** - bring reusable items
- **Travel slower** - fewer trips, longer stays

## Useful Resources
- **Google Flights** - shows carbon estimates per route
- **Atmosfair** - carbon footprint calculator
- **Rainforest Alliance** - certified sustainable tourism operators
