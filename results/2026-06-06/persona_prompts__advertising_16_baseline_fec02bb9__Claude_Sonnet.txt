# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures/demands
**Anxiety** = worry often persisting beyond the immediate situation
*Both are normal — chronic versions need attention*

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based
- **Deep breathing** — slow exhale activates calm response
- **Grounding techniques** — 5-4-3-2-1 senses exercise
- **Cognitive reframing** — challenge unhelpful thought patterns
- **Mindfulness/meditation** — even 5-10 minutes daily helps
- **Journaling** — writing reduces mental clutter

### 🏃 Body-Based
- Regular **exercise** (powerful anxiety reducer)
- Consistent **sleep schedule**
- Limit **caffeine and alcohol**
- Spend time **outdoors/in nature**

### 🔗 Lifestyle
- Set **boundaries** with commitments
- **Connect** with supportive people
- Break large problems into **smaller steps**
- Identify and **reduce triggers** where possible

---

## When to Seek Help

Consider talking to a professional if:
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- You feel overwhelmed consistently
- Physical symptoms appear (chest tightness, etc.)
- Coping strategies aren't working
