Here are some effective strategies:

## Planning & Booking
- **Book early** (or sometimes last-minute for deals)
- **Use fare comparison sites** (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)
- **Be flexible with dates** - mid-week flights are often cheaper
- **Set price alerts** for routes you're watching

## Flights
- Use **credit card points/miles**
- Fly into **secondary airports**
- Consider **budget airlines**
- Pack light to **avoid baggage fees**

## Accommodation
- **Hotels.com, Booking.com** for competitive rates
- **Airbnb or VRBO** for longer stays
- **University guest houses** - many offer affordable rates for academics
- **House swapping** programs

## On the Ground
- Use **public transportation**
- **Cook some meals** rather than dining out every meal
- Look for **free attractions** (museums, parks, walking tours)
- Get a **local SIM card** to avoid roaming charges

## Academic-Specific Perks
- **Conference travel funding** through your institution
- **Research travel grants**
- **ISIC or faculty discounts** at many attractions
- **Reciprocal library/university privileges** can reduce costs

## Timing
- Travel in **shoulder season** (just before/after peak times)
- Take advantage of **academic breaks** when flexibility allows
