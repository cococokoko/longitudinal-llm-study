# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are practical, evidence-based approaches:

## Bodyweight Training
- **Push-up variations** (standard, wide, diamond, pike)
- **Squats and lunges** for lower body strength
- **Planks and core work**
- **Pull-ups** using a doorframe bar or sturdy branch

## Cardiovascular Options
- Running or brisk walking
- Cycling
- Jump rope (highly efficient)
- Swimming if accessible

## Structured Programs Worth Considering
- **HIIT** (High-Intensity Interval Training) - time efficient
- Yoga or Pilates for flexibility and strength
- Calisthenics progressions

## Practical Habits
- **Walk more deliberately** - stairs over elevators, walking meetings
- Set movement reminders if you have a sedentary schedule
- Short sessions (20-30 min) are genuinely effective

## Useful Tools (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands
- Kettlebell or adjustable dumbbells
- Yoga mat

## Key Principles
- **Consistency beats intensity** - regular moderate exercise outperforms sporadic intense sessions
- Progressive overload still applies - gradually increase difficulty
- Recovery matters as much as training
