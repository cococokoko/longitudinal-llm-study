• Continuous learning will become more important
  - Career paths may become less linear.
  - Workers may need to update their skills more frequently as technology changes.
  - Learning how to learn may become as important as any specific technical skill.

  - You may not need to become a programmer, but understanding data, automation tools, AI capabilities, and their limitations could become increasingly useful across industries.

• New jobs will emerge
  - Historically, technological revolutions have eliminated some jobs while creating others.
  - Roles related to AI oversight, cybersecurity, human-AI interaction, data governance, compliance, and technology implementation are already growing.

• Employment structures may change
  - Some organizations may operate with smaller teams supported by powerful automation.
  - Freelance, project-based, and hybrid forms of work could become more common in some sectors.
