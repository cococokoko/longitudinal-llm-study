# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some practical approaches to help you find great trails:

## Apps & Websites
- **AllTrails** - Most popular, with reviews, photos, and difficulty ratings
- **Gaia GPS** - Great for detailed topographic maps
- **hikingproject.com** - Curated trails with community input
- **komoot** - Strong for route planning

## Key Factors to Consider
- **Difficulty level** - Match to your fitness and experience
- **Distance & elevation gain** - Be realistic about time available
- **Scenery type** - Mountains, forests, coastal, desert
- **Crowds** - Weekday vs. weekend timing matters

## Tips for Busy Executives
- **Plan ahead** - Book permits early for popular trails
- **Time efficiency** - Look for high reward-to-effort ratios
- **Sunrise hikes** - Beat crowds and enjoy solitude
- **Guided options** - Let someone else handle logistics

## Safety Essentials
- Check weather forecasts
- Tell someone your plans
- Carry the **10 essentials** (water, map, first aid, etc.)
- Download offline maps

## Great Starting Points
- National Park websites
- Local hiking clubs
- REI's trail guides
