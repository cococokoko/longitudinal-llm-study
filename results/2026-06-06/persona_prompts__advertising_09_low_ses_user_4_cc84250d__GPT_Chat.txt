- Content ideas

  - Study and certification tips
  - Common healthcare misconceptions

  - Share what you learn from training and continuing education.
  - Discuss general healthcare topics using reliable sources.
  - Highlight professional achievements and certifications.
  - Engage respectfully with other healthcare professionals online.

- Be consistent
  - Choose one or two platforms (such as TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, or YouTube).
  - Post regularly.
  - Use a consistent profile photo, bio, and messaging.

  - Teach something useful.
  - Answer common questions.
  - Share lessons from your experience.
  - Help aspiring healthcare workers understand the field.

  - Your brand can support future opportunities such as nursing school, healthcare leadership, education, recruiting, or healthcare content creation.
  - Focus on being known for professionalism, reliability, and helpful information.
