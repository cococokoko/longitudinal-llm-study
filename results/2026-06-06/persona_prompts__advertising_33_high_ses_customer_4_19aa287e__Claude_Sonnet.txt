# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some popular DIY options that are straightforward to make:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, cool, and store in a clean jar

**Exfoliating Scrub**
- Sugar or coffee grounds + coconut oil + lemon juice
- Mix and use immediately or store briefly

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (steep rose petals in distilled water)

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil + tea tree oil (diluted ~2%)

## Lip Care
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Key Principles

- **Sanitation** - sterilize all equipment and containers
- **Preservation** - water-based products need preservatives or refrigeration
- **Patch testing** - test on small skin area first
- **Storage** - most last 1-4 weeks without preservatives

## Worth Noting
Essential oils are **bioactive compounds** - dilution matters, and some have documented skin sensitization potential.
