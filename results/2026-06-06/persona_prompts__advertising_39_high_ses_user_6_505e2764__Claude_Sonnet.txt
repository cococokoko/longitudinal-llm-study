# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Here's a practical, straightforward guide:

## Start Simple
- **Begin with just 5-10 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Morning works well for many people before the day gets demanding
- Same time and place helps build the habit

## Basic Technique to Try First
1. Sit comfortably with your back relatively straight
2. Close your eyes or soften your gaze
3. Focus on your breath - just notice it naturally
4. When your mind wanders (it will), gently return attention to breathing
5. That's essentially it

## Practical Tools
- **Apps worth trying:** Headspace, Calm, Waking Up (Sam Harris)
- **Waking Up** is particularly popular with analytically-minded professionals

## What to Realistically Expect
- First weeks feel awkward and frustrating - that's normal
- Benefits like **reduced reactivity and better focus** typically emerge after 4-8 weeks
- You're not trying to empty your mind - just noticing thoughts without chasing them

## Relevant for Your Context
- Useful for **high-pressure decision-making** and managing stress
- Many senior executives use it specifically for **clarity before important meetings**

## Common Mistakes
- Expecting immediate results
- Judging sessions as "good" or "bad"
- Overcomplicating it
