**Behavioral Tendencies**

**Internal Experience**

- Difficulty believing you are genuinely likeable
- Harsh inner self-criticism

**Relationship Patterns**
- Recreating familiar dynamics in adult relationships
- Difficulty with intimacy
- Either avoiding closeness or becoming overly dependent
