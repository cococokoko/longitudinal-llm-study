## 🥇 Top Recommendation: **"Biochar Water Filter Using Rice Husk"**

### The Problem
Vietnam is the **world's 5th largest rice producer**, generating massive rice husk waste, while **millions lack clean drinking water**, especially in rural/Mekong Delta areas.

### The Project

### What You Test

- Test different activation methods
