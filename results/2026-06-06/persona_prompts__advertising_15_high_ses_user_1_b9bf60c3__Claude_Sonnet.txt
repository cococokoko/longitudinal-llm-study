# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to create a great-looking home without spending a lot:

## Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - often deeply discounted items
- **End-of-season sales** - buy holiday and seasonal decor at 50-75% off

## DIY Projects
- Repaint furniture instead of replacing it
- Create your own wall art with canvas and basic paint
- Reupholster chairs with inexpensive fabric
- Make throw pillow covers from fabric remnants

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Fresh paint** - one of the biggest transformations for the money
- **New hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **Rearrange furniture** - costs nothing
- **Add plants** - affordable and lively

## Prioritize Wisely
- Invest more in **high-traffic, high-visibility** areas
- Use budget options in less-seen spaces
- Focus on **lighting** - it dramatically changes atmosphere

## Free Resources
- Pinterest for inspiration
- YouTube tutorials for DIY skills
- Library books on interior design

## General Rule
Aim to **mix price points** - one or two quality anchor pieces with budget accessories around them.
