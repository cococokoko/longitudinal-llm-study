── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write tomorrow's top 3 priorities** on a sticky note
- **Clear your desk** to a workable surface
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing

### Why This Works Well for Your Situation

As a single parent, your **mental energy at home is spoken for** - you shouldn't have to remember work details while managing your evening with your child.

This habit means:
- You arrive the next morning with a **clear starting point**
- You avoid that scattered feeling of "where do I even begin"
- Your **brain can fully switch to parent mode** when you leave

### Bonus Thought

Do this **before pickup or your commute** so the transition home feels cleaner. Many teachers find it actually helps them be **more present with their kids** in the evening because work isn't mentally following them home.

---

The goal is making your school day **self-contained** so your limited time and energy go where they matter most.
