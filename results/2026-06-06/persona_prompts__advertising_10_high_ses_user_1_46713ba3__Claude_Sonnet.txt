# Learning a New Language Effectively

Here's a practical, evidence-based approach:

## Core Principles

- **Consistency beats intensity** - 20 minutes daily outperforms 3 hours weekly
- **Comprehensible input** is the primary driver of acquisition
- **Active production** accelerates fluency beyond passive consumption

## Structured Approach

### Foundation (Months 1-3)
- Learn the **top 1,000-2,000 most frequent words**
- Master basic sentence structures
- Use spaced repetition systems (Anki, Duolingo)

### Intermediate (Months 3-12)
- **Immersion** - podcasts, TV shows, books in target language
- Find a **conversation partner** (iTalki, Tandem)
- Stop translating; start *thinking* in the language

### Advanced
- Read native materials
- Engage in complex discussions on topics you know well
