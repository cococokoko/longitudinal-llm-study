── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some professional subject line options for thanking someone for their time:

## Direct & Clear
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Grateful for Your Time Today"**

## More Specific
- **"Thank You for Our [Date] Meeting"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time and Insights"**

## Slightly More Formal
- **"With Gratitude – [Meeting Topic]"**
- **"Thank You for the Opportunity to Connect"**
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time"**

## Tips to Consider
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the topic or date)
- **Keep it concise** – subject lines are most effective under 50 characters
- **Avoid vague lines** that might look like spam

The most straightforward option, **"Thank You for Your Time,"** is widely considered appropriate for most formal academic or professional contexts.
