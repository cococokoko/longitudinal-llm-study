# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting others, this is a great investment in yourself.

## Start Simple - Really Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily**
- Consistency matters more than duration
- Same time each day builds the habit
- Morning often works well before demands start

## Basic Techniques to Try First

**Breathing focus**
- Sit comfortably, close eyes
- Notice your breath naturally
- When mind wanders, gently return attention to breathing
- That's literally the whole practice

**Body scan**
- Slowly notice sensations from head to toe
- No need to change anything, just observe

## Practical Tips for Busy Caregivers

- **Attach it to existing routines** - morning coffee, before bed
- **Use transitions** - waiting, washing dishes, brief walks
- **Informal mindfulness** - full attention on one task at a time
- **Don't aim for perfect silence** - work with real life conditions

## Free Resources Worth Trying

- **Apps:** Insight Timer (free), Headspace, Calm
- **YouTube:** search "guided meditation for beginners"
- **Podcasts:** many free options available

## Honest Expectation

Your mind **will** wander constantly at first - that's normal, not failure. Noticing the wandering *is* the practice.
