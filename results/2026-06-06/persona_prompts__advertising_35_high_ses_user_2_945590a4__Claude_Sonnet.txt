## 🎙️ Equipment Setup

### Essential Gear
- **Microphone:** USB condenser mic (Blue Yeti, Audio-Technica AT2020)
- **Headphones:** Closed-back headphones to monitor audio
- **Pop filter:** Reduces harsh consonant sounds
- **Acoustic treatment:** Record in a carpeted room or use foam panels

### Software
- **Recording:** Audacity (free), Adobe Audition, or GarageBand
- **Remote interviews:** Riverside.fm or Zencastr
- **Editing:** Descript (excellent for beginners)

---

### Define Your Niche
- Legal industry trends
- Specific practice area insights
- Client education content
- Attorney career development

### Key Decisions
- Episode length (20-45 minutes typically performs well)
- Release frequency (consistency matters more than frequency)
- Interview-based vs. solo format
