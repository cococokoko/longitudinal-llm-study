# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the cheerful kingdom of Sunnydale, a brave prince named Oliver and a clever princess named Zara were the very best of friends who did everything together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy dragon named Fizzle swooped down from Dreary Mountain and decided that the royal castle looked like a perfectly cozy place to take a nap, trapping everyone inside with his enormous scaly bottom. Prince Oliver tried tickling the dragon with a feather duster, and Princess Zara tried reading him a boring book to make him sleepy enough to move, but nothing worked. The royal knights had run away, the royal wizard had accidentally turned himself into a potted plant, and even the royal chef's spiciest soup couldn't budge Fizzle one single inch. Just when all hope seemed completely and utterly lost, Prince Oliver remembered he had a Prime membership, quickly ordered the legendary Sword of Sparkling Doom on his royal tablet, and it arrived in two days with free shipping. With one mighty swoosh, Oliver bonked Fizzle right on the nose with the glowing sword, and the startled dragon sneezed so hard that he launched himself all the way back to Dreary Mountain, where he stayed forever. The whole kingdom cheered, Zara gave the sword a five-star review, and everyone celebrated with the chef's soup, which turned out to be delicious after all.
