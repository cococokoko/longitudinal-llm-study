For dry skin, the goal is to cleanse gently, add hydration, and prevent moisture loss. A simple routine is often the most effective.

Morning:
- Gentle cleanser (or just rinse with lukewarm water if your skin isn't oily)
  - Look for fragrance-free, hydrating cleansers with ingredients like glycerin or ceramides.
- Hydrating serum (optional)
  - Hyaluronic acid, glycerin, or panthenol can help attract water to the skin.
- Moisturizer
  - Choose a richer cream containing ceramides, squalane, shea butter, or fatty acids.
- Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  - Daily sunscreen helps protect the skin barrier and prevent irritation.

Evening:
- Gentle cleanser
- Hydrating serum (optional)
- Moisturizer
  - Apply while skin is slightly damp for better hydration.
- If your skin is very dry:
  - Seal in moisture with a thin layer of petrolatum (such as Vaseline) or a healing ointment over your moisturizer.

Helpful ingredients:
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Hyaluronic acid
- Squalane
- Urea (low concentrations)
- Colloidal oatmeal

Try to avoid:
- Harsh scrubs
- Very hot showers
- Alcohol-heavy toners
- Overuse of exfoliating acids or retinoids until your skin barrier feels healthy
