• Simple facial moisturizer
  - 2 tablespoons jojoba oil or sweet almond oil
  - 1 tablespoon aloe vera gel
  - Mix thoroughly and store in a clean container.

  - 2 tablespoons fine sugar

  - Mix and gently massage onto damp skin.
  - Rinse off. Avoid using harsh scrubs on sensitive or acne-prone skin.

  - 2 tablespoons finely ground oats
  - Enough water or plain yogurt to make a paste
  - Apply for 10–15 minutes, then rinse.

• Lip balm
  - 1 tablespoon beeswax pellets
  - 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  - 1 tablespoon shea butter
  - Melt together, pour into a small container, and allow to cool.

• Hair conditioning treatment
  - 1 ripe avocado
  - 1 tablespoon olive oil
  - Mash and apply to hair for 15–20 minutes before shampooing.

• Use clean utensils and containers.

• Be cautious with essential oils; they can cause irritation and should be properly diluted.
• Discard any product that changes color, smell, or texture.
