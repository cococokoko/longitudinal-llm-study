How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based approaches to improving mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep consistently** - 7-9 hours is strongly linked to mental wellbeing
- **Regular exercise** - even 20-30 minutes has measurable mood benefits
- **Limit alcohol** - it's a depressant despite feeling like stress relief
- **Reduce caffeine** if anxiety is a concern

## Stress Management
- Set clear boundaries between work and personal time
- Practice mindfulness or meditation (apps like Headspace can help)
- Take real breaks during the day, not just checking your phone
- **Delegate more** - executives often carry unnecessary cognitive load

## Social Connection
- Invest in relationships outside work
- Be honest with trusted people about how you're doing
- Consider peer groups with others in similar roles

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** is genuinely effective and not a sign of weakness
- Executive coaches can help with work-related stress specifically
- Your company may have an EAP (Employee Assistance Program)

## Things Worth Examining
- Whether your workload is sustainable
- If your work aligns with your values
- Whether you're measuring your worth primarily by performance

**One honest note:** Executives often have access to resources but less permission (self-imposed) to use them. That's worth reflecting on.
