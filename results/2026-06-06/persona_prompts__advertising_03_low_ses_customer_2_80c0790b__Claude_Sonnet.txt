# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are practical strategies tailored to your situation as a single parent:

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Designate a workspace** - even a corner with a consistent setup signals "work mode"
- Keep essentials within reach to minimize interruptions
- Use noise-canceling headphones during focused work

## Managing Your Schedule Around Childcare
- **Work in focused blocks** during nap times, school hours, or quiet activities
- Identify your **peak energy hours** and protect them for demanding tasks
- Build in realistic buffer time - things will come up
- Communicate your availability windows clearly to colleagues

## Reducing Mental Load
- **Batch similar tasks** together (emails, calls, creative work)
- Use a simple daily priority list - just 3 must-do items
- Prep the night before so mornings run smoother

## Practical Tools
- Time-blocking apps or a simple calendar
- Automation for repetitive tasks
- Templates for common emails or documents

## Setting Boundaries
- Be honest with your employer about your realistic working hours
- **Give yourself grace** on harder days - flexibility is a strength, not a weakness
- Celebrate small wins

## Key Mindset Shift
> Productivity isn't about working more hours - it's about making your available hours count.
