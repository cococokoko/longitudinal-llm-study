# Learning a New Language Effectively

As a physician-scientist, you likely have strong analytical skills and a busy schedule — here's a practical, evidence-based approach:

## Core Principles

- **Consistency beats intensity** — 20-30 minutes daily outperforms 3-hour weekend sessions
- **Comprehensible input** is the primary driver of acquisition (Krashen's theory, well-supported)
- **Active production** accelerates fluency beyond passive exposure

## High-Leverage Strategies

### Foundation
- **Spaced repetition systems (SRS)** — Anki for vocabulary retention
- Focus on the **most frequent 1,000-2,000 words** first (covers ~90% of conversation)

### Immersion
- Change phone/device language settings
- Podcasts, shows, and reading in target language
- **Comprehensible input tools** — Dreaming Spanish, Language Transfer

### Speaking
- **iTalki or Preply** — affordable native speaker tutors
- Language exchange partners (Tandem, HelloTalk)

### Structure
- Grammar reference as needed, not as primary method

| Professional fluency | 1-3 years |
