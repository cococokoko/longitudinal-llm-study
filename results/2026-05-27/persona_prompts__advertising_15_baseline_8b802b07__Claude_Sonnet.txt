# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines

---

## Low-Cost Decorating Ideas

### Repurpose & Upcycle
- Paint old furniture instead of buying new
- Turn crates or pallets into shelving
- Reupholster chairs with affordable fabric

### Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & garage sales** for unique finds
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** for furniture deals
- **Discount stores** like IKEA, HomeGoods, or TJ Maxx
- End-of-season sales for décor items

### DIY Projects
- Make your own artwork or gallery walls
- Sew simple curtains or pillow covers
- Create centerpieces from natural materials

---

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest transformations
- **New throw pillows** to refresh a room
- **Rearrange furniture** - completely free
- **Add plants** for life and color
- **Update lighting** with affordable fixtures
- **Declutter** to make spaces feel larger

---

## Things to Avoid
- Impulse buying
- Following trends too closely
- Overlooking secondhand options
