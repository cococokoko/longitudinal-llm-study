# Best Books for Design and Analysis of Algorithms (2023)

## 🏆 Top Tier / Most Recommended

### 1. **Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)**
- *Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein* — 4th Edition (2022)
- The **gold standard** textbook
- Covers everything comprehensively
- Best for: Deep theoretical understanding

### 2. **Algorithm Design**
- *Kleinberg & Tardos*
- Excellent explanations and intuition
- Great problem sets
- Best for: Conceptual clarity

### 3. **Algorithms** *(free online)*
- *Jeff Erickson*
- Very readable and modern
- Available free at **jeffe.cs.illinois.edu**
- Best for: Students who want clear writing
