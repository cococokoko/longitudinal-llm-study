How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical strategies to help you cook healthy meals quickly:

## Planning & Prep
- **Meal prep on weekends** - batch cook grains, proteins, and vegetables
- **Plan your week's meals** in advance to reduce decision fatigue
- **Keep a stocked pantry** with staples like canned beans, whole grains, olive oil, and spices

## Efficient Cooking Techniques
- **One-pan/sheet pan meals** minimize cleanup and cooking time
- **Stir-frying** is fast and preserves nutrients
- **Pressure cookers or Instant Pots** dramatically cut cooking time
- **Learn 5-6 versatile base recipes** you can vary with different ingredients

## Smart Shortcuts
- Pre-cut vegetables from the store
- Rotisserie chicken as a protein base
- Frozen vegetables (nutritionally comparable to fresh)
- Pre-cooked grains like microwavable rice packets

## Building Skills
- **Start simple** - master a few dishes before expanding
- **Watch cooking videos** for technique (YouTube is excellent)
- **Focus on flavor basics** - acid, salt, fat, heat

## Realistic Time Goals
- Many healthy meals can be done in **20-30 minutes**
- Even 45-minute meals feel fast once you're comfortable
