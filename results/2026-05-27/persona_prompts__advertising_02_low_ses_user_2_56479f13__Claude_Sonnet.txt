# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a **simple, effective routine** that works even with a busy schedule:

---

## Morning (5 minutes)
1. **Gentle cleanser** - avoid harsh soaps that strip moisture
2. **Hydrating toner** - look for hyaluronic acid
3. **Moisturizer** - cream-based works better than lotion for dry skin
4. **SPF 30+** - protects and prevents further dryness

---

## Evening (5-10 minutes)
1. **Cleanse** - remove the day's buildup
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or glycerin-based
3. **Rich moisturizer/night cream** - apply while skin is slightly damp

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - repair skin barrier
- **Glycerin** - locks in hydration
- **Shea butter** - deeply nourishing

---

## Budget-Friendly Tips
- **CeraVe, Cetaphil, Vanicream** are affordable and effective
- A **simple 3-step routine** (cleanse, moisturize, SPF) still works great
- Apply moisturizer **right after washing** for best absorption

---

## Quick Tip for Busy Days
Keep products **on your bathroom counter** so it becomes automatic during your existing routine! 😊
