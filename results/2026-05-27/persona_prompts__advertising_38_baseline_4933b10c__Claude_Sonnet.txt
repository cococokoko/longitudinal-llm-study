What are the best platforms for freelance writers to find work?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some of the most popular platforms for freelance writers:

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large platform with diverse writing jobs
- **Fiverr** - Good for offering specific writing services
- **Freelancer.com** - Competitive bidding on projects

## Writing-Specific Platforms
- **Contently** - Higher-end content work, portfolio-focused
- **ClearVoice** - Content marketing focused
- **Scripted** - Connects writers with businesses
- **Textbroker** - Good for beginners, pays per word

## Content Mills (Good for Starting Out)
- **iWriter**
- **WriterAccess**
- **Constant Content**

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality blogging/content jobs
- **MediaBistro** - Media and journalism focused
- **Journalism Jobs**
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for freelance work

## Direct Pitching
- **Contena** - Curates writing jobs
- Cold pitching publications and businesses directly

## Things to Consider
- **Pay rates vary significantly** across platforms
- Content mills offer volume but lower rates
- Direct clients typically pay best
- Building a **portfolio and niche** helps on most platforms
