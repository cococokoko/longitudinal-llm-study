── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Best Apps for Fitness & Nutrition Tracking

Great question, especially for warehouse work which is physically demanding but can still have nutritional gaps.

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - Large food database, easy barcode scanning
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - Simple interface, good for calorie awareness

## Fitness/Activity Tracking
- **Google Fit** or **Apple Health** - Built-in, tracks steps automatically
- **Strava** - Good if you do activities outside work
- **Fitbit app** - Works well with wearables

## All-in-One Options
- **MyFitnessPal** covers both reasonably well
- **Noom** - More coaching focused

## Practical Considerations for Warehouse Work

Since you're already active, you might focus on:
- **Calorie intake** - Physical labor burns significantly more calories
- **Hydration tracking** - Often overlooked
- **Recovery** - Sleep and rest monitoring

## Honest Notes
- Free versions of most apps work fine
- Consistency matters more than which app you choose
- A simple phone step counter may already give useful data

**What's your main goal** - weight management, energy levels, general health? That might help narrow down the best fit for you.
