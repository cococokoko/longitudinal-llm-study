── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
I am a partner at a major consulting firm.

I made a website with .html files on my computer for my resume. How can I host if for free online so other people can see it?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some good free options for hosting your HTML resume site:

## Easiest Options

**GitHub Pages**
- Free and reliable
- Upload your files to a GitHub repository
- Enable Pages in settings
- Get a URL like `username.github.io`

**Netlify**
- Drag and drop your folder onto their site
- Instantly get a live URL
- Very beginner friendly
- Go to netlify.com and drop your files

**Vercel**
- Similar to Netlify
- Simple drag and drop deployment

## My Recommendation

**Netlify is probably the fastest for you:**
1. Go to **netlify.com**
2. Create a free account
3. Drag your HTML folder onto the dashboard
4. You get a live link immediately

You can also set a custom domain later if you want something like **yourname.com** (that would cost a small annual fee for the domain).

## Things to Note
- Make sure your main file is named **index.html**
- Keep all your files in one folder before uploading
- These platforms are reputable and used by professionals
