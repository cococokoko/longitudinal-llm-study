Here are some practical options for a physician-scientist to earn money quickly:

## Clinical/Medical Options
- **Pick up a clinical shift** - locum tenens, urgent care, or coverage for a colleague
- **Telemedicine** - platforms like Teladoc or MDLive can onboard quickly
- **On-call coverage** - contact your hospital or a staffing agency

## Consulting (Often Quick Payment)
- **Expert witness work** - medical-legal consulting
- **Pharmaceutical/biotech consulting** - many companies pay promptly
- **Insurance company medical review** - utilization review work

## Research-Related
- **Peer review fees** - some journals pay reviewers
- **Grant review panels** - though payment timing varies
- **Speaking honoraria** - if you have a pending engagement

## Immediate Practical Options
- **Sell equipment or supplies** you no longer need
- **Advance from employer** - ask HR about payroll advances
- **Freelance medical writing** - some platforms pay quickly

## Platforms to Consider
- Doximity for locum connections
- UpWork for consulting/writing
- Expert networks (GLG, Guidepoint) for consulting calls
