# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options with simple recipes:

## Skincare

**Basic Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + a few drops of jojoba oil
- Add essential oils like lavender or rosehip (optional)

**Simple Toner**
- Witch hazel + rose water (1:1 ratio)
- Apply with cotton pad after cleansing

**Exfoliating Scrub**
- Brown sugar + coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gentle enough for face and body

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado + honey + olive oil
- Leave on 20-30 minutes before washing

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil + tea tree oil (a few drops)
- Massage in, leave 30 minutes

## Lip Care
- Beeswax + coconut oil + a little honey
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## General Tips
- **Patch test** new products before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- Note that **preservatives matter** - water-based products can grow bacteria
- Store in cool, dark places
- **Shelf life is shorter** than commercial products

## Good Starting Resources
- Books by herbalists like Rosemary Gladstar
- Reputable cosmetic chemistry blogs
