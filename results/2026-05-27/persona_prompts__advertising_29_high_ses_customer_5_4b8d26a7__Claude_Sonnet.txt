How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - Annual checkups and staying current on vaccinations
- **Proper nutrition** - Quality food appropriate for their species, age, and size
- **Exercise** - Daily activity suited to their needs
- **Preventive care** - Flea, tick, and heartworm prevention

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - Toys, puzzles, and activities that stimulate their minds
- **Social interaction** - Time with you and, when appropriate, other animals
- **Consistent routine** - Pets generally thrive with predictability
- **Safe space** - A comfortable place they can retreat to

## Practical Care
- **Grooming** - Regular brushing, nail trims, dental care
- **Clean environment** - Fresh water, clean bedding, sanitary living space
- **Weight management** - Avoiding obesity, which causes many health problems

## Things to Watch For
- Changes in eating, drinking, or behavior
- Unusual lethargy or restlessness
- Signs of pain or discomfort

## A Few Tips
- Build a relationship with a vet you trust
- Learn the specific needs of your particular species and breed
- Consider pet insurance for unexpected costs
