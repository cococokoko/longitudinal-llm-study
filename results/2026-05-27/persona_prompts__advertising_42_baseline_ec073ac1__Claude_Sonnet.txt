## Fitness Tracking

- **MyFitnessPal** - large food database, very popular

## All-in-One Apps
- **MyFitnessPal** - covers both fitness and food
- **Fitbit** - pairs with their devices

## Things to Consider When Choosing
- **Cost** - many have free and premium tiers
- **Device compatibility** - works with your phone/watch
- **Database size** - especially for food logging
- **Your specific goals** - weight loss, muscle gain, endurance
- **How detailed you want to be**

- The best app is one you'll actually use consistently
- Free versions are often sufficient for basic tracking
- Some people find tracking obsessive, so consider your relationship with data
