## Quick & Low-Cost Steps
- **Seal air leaks** around windows, doors, and outlets with weatherstripping or caulk

- Replace older appliances with **Energy Star certified** models
- Consider a **programmable or smart thermostat**
- Upgrade to a **tankless or heat pump water heater**

## Insulation & Structure
- Add or improve **attic and wall insulation**
- Upgrade to **double or triple-pane windows**
- Install **door sweeps** on exterior doors

## Bigger Investments
- Solar panels
- Heat pump systems
- Energy-efficient roofing

## Practical Habits
- Run dishwashers and laundry during **off-peak hours**
- Use **cold water** for laundry when possible
- Keep refrigerator coils clean
