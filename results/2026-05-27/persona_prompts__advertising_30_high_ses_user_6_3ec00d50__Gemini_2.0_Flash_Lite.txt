**1. Define Your "Why" and Set Realistic Goals:**

*   **Why are you learning to code?** This is the most crucial question. Are you:
    *   **Trying to understand your team better?** (e.g., data scientists, developers)
    *   **Wanting to automate tasks and improve your own productivity?** (e.g., data analysis, report generation)
