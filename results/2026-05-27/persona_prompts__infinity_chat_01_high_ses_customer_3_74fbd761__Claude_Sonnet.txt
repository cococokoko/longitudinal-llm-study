# Finding a Job in Scandinavian Countries

Here are practical steps to help you in your job search:

## Online Job Portals
- **Denmark:** jobnet.dk, ofir.dk, jobindex.dk
- **Sweden:** arbetsformedlingen.se, linkedin.com, blocket.se/jobb
- **Norway:** finn.no, nav.no, jobbnorge.no
- **Finland:** mol.fi, oikotie.fi/tyopaikat

## Key Steps

### 1. **Language Considerations**
- Many positions require local language skills
- **English-friendly countries:** Sweden and Denmark have many English-speaking workplaces
- Consider language courses before applying

### 2. **Academic/Professional Networking**
- LinkedIn is widely used across Scandinavia
- Attend international conferences in your field
- Connect with Scandinavian researchers/professionals

### 3. **Work Permits & Visas**
- EU/EEA citizens have free movement rights
- Non-EU citizens need work permits (employer sponsorship often required)
- Check each country's immigration authority

### 4. **University Positions** *(if relevant)*
- Check university websites directly
- **EURAXESS** - excellent for academic positions across Europe
- Research council websites for funded positions

## Tips
- Tailor your CV to Scandinavian standards (concise, factual)
- Scandinavian work culture values **flat hierarchy and work-life balance**
- Networking is highly valued
