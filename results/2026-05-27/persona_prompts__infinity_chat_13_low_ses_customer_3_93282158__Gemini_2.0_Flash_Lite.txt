*   Salt and pepper to taste

3.  **Add Flavor:** Sprinkle with chopped dill.
4.  **Season:** Drizzle with lemon juice and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
5.  **Enjoy!**

**Why it's great for a diet and a new job:**

*   **Quick & Easy:** This snack takes just minutes to assemble, perfect for a busy schedule.
*   **Portable:** You can easily pack these in a container to take to work.
*   **Flavorful:** The combination of cucumber, salmon, dill, and lemon is delicious and satisfying.
