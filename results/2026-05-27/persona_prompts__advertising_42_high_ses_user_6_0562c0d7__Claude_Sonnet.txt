Here are some well-regarded options across categories:

## Fitness Tracking
- **Strava** - Running/cycling, strong social features
- **Whoop** - Recovery and strain focused, popular with executives
- **Apple Fitness+** - Integrated if you're in the Apple ecosystem
- **Garmin Connect** - Excellent if you use Garmin devices

## Nutrition Tracking
- **MyFitnessPal** - Large food database, easy logging
- **Cronometer** - More detailed micronutrient tracking
- **Lose It!** - Clean interface, good for calorie management

## All-in-One Options
- **Noom** - Behavioral coaching approach
- **Levels** - CGM integration, metabolic health focus
- **Eight Sleep app** - If sleep/recovery matters to you

## Practical Considerations
Given a demanding consulting schedule, you might prioritize:
- **Ease of logging** (minimal time investment)
- **Travel-friendly** workouts
- **Wearable integration** to reduce manual entry
- **Whoop or Oura** for passive tracking with minimal effort

## Worth Noting
The best app is honestly the one you'll **actually use consistently** - simplicity often beats features.
