Okay, here's a fun and diet-friendly snack idea that skips the yogurt:

**Cucumber & Hummus Bites with Everything Bagel Seasoning**

This snack is refreshing, crunchy, and packed with flavor!

**Ingredients:**

*   1 large cucumber
*   1/2 cup hummus (choose a flavor you like - plain, roasted red pepper, etc.)
*   Everything bagel seasoning
*   Optional: a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for a little heat

**Instructions:**

1.  **Prep the Cucumber:** Wash and slice the cucumber into 1/2-inch thick rounds.
2.  **Assemble the Bites:** Spread a dollop of hummus on top of each cucumber slice.
3.  **Season:** Sprinkle generously with everything bagel seasoning. Add a pinch of red pepper flakes if you like a little kick.
4.  **Enjoy!**

**Why it's great for a diet:**

*   **Low in Calories:** Cucumbers are mostly water and very low in calories. Hummus is also relatively low in calories, especially if you watch your portion size.
*   **Fiber & Protein:** Hummus provides fiber and protein, which helps you feel full and satisfied.
*   **Flavorful & Satisfying:** The combination of the cool cucumber, creamy hummus, and the savory everything bagel seasoning is delicious and keeps you from feeling deprived.
*   **Customizable:** You can experiment with different hummus flavors and add-ins (like a drizzle of olive oil or a sprinkle of fresh herbs) to keep things interesting.

Enjoy your healthy and delicious snack!
