# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are some practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor or naturally beautiful spaces that need less decoration

## Invitations
- Send digital invitations or use recycled paper
- Choose soy-based or vegetable inks for printing
- Skip unnecessary inserts and extras

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Use reusable or biodegradable materials

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Rent suits/tuxedos rather than buying

## Transportation
- Arrange group transportation for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
- Offset carbon emissions through verified programs

## Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets, plants, or edible favors
- Request charitable donations instead of gifts
- Create a registry with sustainable brands

## Waste Management
- Use compostable or reusable tableware
- Set up clear recycling and compost stations
