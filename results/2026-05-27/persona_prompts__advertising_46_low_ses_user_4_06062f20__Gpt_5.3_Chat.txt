**For quick, no-thinking meal structure**
Think in this simple formula (this is more useful than most diet plans):
- Protein: beans, lentils, tofu, tempeh  
- Carbs: rice, potatoes, oats, whole grains  
- Fats: nuts, seeds, olive oil  
- Veggies: whatever you can get in

You can build almost every meal from that without overthinking.

**Important (don’t skip this part)**
A plant-based diet is healthy, but only if you cover a few basics:
- Vitamin B12 (you’ll need a supplement—non-negotiable)
- Protein (not hard, but be intentional)
- Iron (beans + vitamin C helps absorption)
- Calories (a lot of people undereat at first and feel tired)
