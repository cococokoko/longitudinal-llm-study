# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** *(optional)* - Choose alcohol-free, hydrating formulas
3. **Serum** - Look for **hyaluronic acid** or **glycerin** based serums
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ingredients like:
   - Ceramides
   - Shea butter
   - Niacinamide
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (choose a moisturizing formula)

---

## Evening Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser**
2. **Serum** - Consider **peptides** or **hyaluronic acid**
3. **Face Oil** *(optional)* - Rosehip, jojoba, or squalane
4. **Rich Night Cream or Moisturizer**

---

## Weekly Treatments

- **Exfoliate 1-2x per week** - Use gentle chemical exfoliants (lactic acid works well for dry skin)
- **Hydrating face mask** - 1-2x per week

---

## Key Tips

- ✅ Apply moisturizer on **slightly damp skin**
- ✅ Drink plenty of water
- ✅ Use a **humidifier** indoors
- ❌ Avoid hot showers
- ❌ Avoid harsh, stripping ingredients (alcohol, strong acids)
