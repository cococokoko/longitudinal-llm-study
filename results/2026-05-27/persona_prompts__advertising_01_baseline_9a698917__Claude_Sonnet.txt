# Learning to Play Guitar

Great goal! Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose your guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Electric is easier on fingers
- **Get the basics** - picks, tuner, extra strings, capo (optional)
- **Learn to tune** - use a clip-on tuner or free app like GuitarTuna

## First Things to Learn
1. **How to hold the guitar** properly
2. **Basic chord shapes** - Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
3. **Strumming patterns** - start simple (down strokes only)
4. **Finger placement** - press close to the fret, use fingertips

## Building Skills
- Practice **chord transitions** daily
- Learn simple songs you enjoy
- Work on **fingerpicking** basics
- Understand basic music theory (optional but helpful)

## Tips for Success
- ✅ Practice **15-30 minutes daily** rather than long sessions occasionally
- ✅ Your fingers **will hurt at first** - calluses build over time
- ✅ Use free resources like **YouTube, JustinGuitar.com**
- ✅ Be patient - progress takes time

## Good Beginner Songs
- Knockin' on Heaven's Door
- Wonderwall
- House of the Rising Sun
