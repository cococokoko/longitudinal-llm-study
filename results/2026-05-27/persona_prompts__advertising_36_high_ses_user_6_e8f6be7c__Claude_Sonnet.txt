- **Local print/radio** - Depending on target audience

- **Email list building** - Owned audience, not algorithm-dependent
- **Nextdoor/neighborhood apps** - Highly local targeting

- **Consistency beats volume** - Better to do fewer things well
- **Track what works** - Ask customers how they found you
