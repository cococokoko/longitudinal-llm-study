# Best Books for Design and Analysis of Algorithms (2023)

## 🥇 Top Tier (Essential)

### 1. **Introduction to Algorithms (CLRS)**
- **Authors:** Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, Stein
- **Edition:** 4th Edition (2022)
- **Why:** The gold standard reference — comprehensive coverage of all core topics
- **Best for:** Deep theoretical understanding

### 2. **Algorithm Design**
- **Authors:** Kleinberg & Tardos
- **Why:** Excellent explanations with real-world motivation
- **Best for:** Understanding *why* algorithms work, not just *how*

---

## 🥈 Highly Recommended

### 3. **Algorithms** *(free online)*
- **Author:** Jeff Erickson
- **Why:** Clear writing, free PDF, great problem sets
- **Link:** jeffe.cs.illinois.edu

### 4. **The Algorithm Design Manual**
- **Author:** Steven Skiena
- **Edition:** 3rd Edition
- **Why:** Practical focus, "war stories," excellent catalog of problems

### 5. **Algorithms**
- **Authors:** Sedgewick & Wayne
- **Why:** Strong Java implementations, visual explanations
- **Best for:** Learners who prefer code-first approach

---

## 🥉 Beginner Friendly

### 6. **Grokking Algorithms**
- **Author:** Aditya Bhargava
- **Why:** Visual, fun, easy to start
- **Best for:** Complete beginners

### 7. **Data Structures and Algorithm Analysis in C++/Java**
- **Author:** Mark Weiss
- **Why:** Good balance of theory and implementation
