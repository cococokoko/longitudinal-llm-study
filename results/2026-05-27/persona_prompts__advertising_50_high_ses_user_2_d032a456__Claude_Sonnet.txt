# Travel Cost-Saving Strategies for Senior Attorneys

Here are practical ways to reduce travel expenses while maintaining the professional standards expected at your level:

## Flights
- **Book early** or use fare alert tools (Google Flights, Kayak)
- **Leverage loyalty programs** - consolidate travel on one airline alliance
- **Consider premium economy** as a cost-effective alternative to business class for shorter flights
- **Use corporate travel accounts** negotiated by your firm

## Accommodations
- **Hotel loyalty programs** (Marriott Bonvoy, Hilton Honors) for upgrades and free nights
- **Extended stay options** for longer client matters
- **Negotiate corporate rates** through your firm's travel department

## Ground Transportation
- **Rideshare apps** vs. taxis for cost transparency
- **Airport express trains** where available
- **Rental car loyalty programs** for frequent travel

## Strategic Planning
- **Combine client visits** in the same city/region
- **Video conferencing** to reduce unnecessary travel
- **Travel on off-peak days** (Tuesday/Wednesday flights)
- **Credit card travel benefits** - many premium cards offer lounge access and travel credits

## Firm-Level Considerations
- Review your firm's **travel policy** for reimbursable maximums
- Use **travel management platforms** your firm may already subscribe to
