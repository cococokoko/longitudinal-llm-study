# 13. Time-Saving Tools and Apps

---

## Core Principle
The right tool removes friction. The wrong tool creates more work.

---

## Household Management

**Tody / OurHome**
Distributes chores across family members. Tracks what was cleaned and when. Removes the mental load of remembering.

**Notion / Trello**
One shared board. Everyone sees what needs doing — groceries, appointments, school forms, repairs.

---

## Grocery & Meal Planning

**Mealime / Plan to Eat**
Enter dietary needs once. App generates a weekly plan and consolidated shopping list automatically.

**AnyList / OurGroceries**
Shared list. Anyone in the household adds items in real time. No duplicate purchases. No missed items.

---

## Finances

**YNAB (You Need A Budget)**
Tracks spending across a large household. Assigns every dollar a purpose. Reduces end-of-month surprises.

**Splitwise**
Tracks shared expenses among extended family. Who paid for what. Who owes whom. No awkward conversations.

---

## Scheduling & Coordination

**Cozi Family Organizer**
One calendar. Every family member's appointments visible in one place. Color-coded by person.

**Google Calendar with shared access**
Free. Simple. Works across all devices. Share specific calendars with specific people.

---

## Communication

**Slack or GroupMe**
Dedicated channels for different topics — school pickups, grocery runs, weekend plans. Cleaner than group texts.

---

## Automation Worth Setting Up Once

- Auto-pay recurring bills
- Recurring grocery delivery on a set schedule
- Automatic prescription refills
- Bill reminders 5 days before due dates

---

## Selection Rule

> One tool that everyone uses beats five tools nobody finishes setting up.

Start with one area. Master it. Then expand.
