── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Best skin routine for dry skin.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Dry skin needs two main things: gentle care and serious moisture retention. Think “don’t strip, then seal.”

Here’s a simple routine that actually works:

Morning:
- Use a gentle, non-foaming cleanser (or just rinse with water if you’re very dry)
- Apply a hydrating serum (look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin)
- Use a rich moisturizer with ceramides or shea butter
- Finish with sunscreen (SPF 30+). Look for moisturizing sunscreens

Night:
- Cleanse gently (no harsh foaming or stripping cleansers)
- Optional: hydrating serum again
- Use a thicker moisturizer or night cream
- If you’re very dry, seal it in with a thin layer of an occlusive like petrolatum (Vaseline) or a facial oil

A few key upgrades:
- Look for ingredients like ceramides, hyaluronic acid, glycerin, squalane
- Avoid alcohol-heavy products and overly strong exfoliants
- Exfoliate lightly, max 1–2 times per week (lactic acid is good for dry skin)
- Apply products on slightly damp skin to lock in moisture
- Use a humidifier if your air is dry

If your skin feels tight, flaky, or stings easily, simplify your routine instead of adding more products.
