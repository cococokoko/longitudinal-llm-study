── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for minimalist apartment decorating on a budget:

## Core Principles
- **Less is more** - focus on a few quality pieces rather than lots of clutter
- Choose a simple, cohesive color palette (2-3 colors works well)

## Budget-Friendly Ideas

**Furniture & Layout**
- Rearrange what you already have before buying anything
- Look for secondhand pieces on Facebook Marketplace, thrift stores, or Craigslist
- Multi-functional furniture saves money and space

**Walls & Visual Interest**
- A single large piece of art makes more impact than many small ones
- Simple floating shelves add storage and display space
- Paint is one of the cheapest ways to transform a room

**Plants**
- A few well-placed plants add life without clutter
- Pothos and snake plants are inexpensive and low-maintenance

**Lighting**
- Swap out harsh overhead lights for warmer bulbs
- A simple floor or table lamp changes the whole mood

**Decluttering First**
- Removing things costs nothing and often has the biggest impact
- Storage solutions hide what remains

## Smart Shopping
- IKEA and Target have clean, simple designs at reasonable prices
- Wait for sales on the few pieces you really want
