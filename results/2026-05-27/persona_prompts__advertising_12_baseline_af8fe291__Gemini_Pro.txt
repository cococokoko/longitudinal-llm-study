Eco-friendly travel (often called sustainable or green travel) is all about exploring the world while minimizing your carbon footprint, protecting wildlife, and supporting local communities. 

Here is a comprehensive guide to eco-friendly travel options, broken down by the different stages of your trip:

### 1. Transportation (Getting There & Around)
Transportation is usually the largest contributor to a traveler's carbon footprint. 
*   **Trains over Planes:** Whenever possible, take a train. Trains emit up to 80% less carbon per passenger than flights. Europe and parts of Asia have excellent high-speed rail networks.
*   **Buses and Coaches:** Modern buses are highly fuel-efficient per passenger and are a great budget-friendly, low-carbon option.
