# Finding Affordable Eco-Friendly Travel Destinations

## Research Strategies

**Use specialized platforms**
- Bookdifferent.com (rates hotels on sustainability)
- Ecobnb.com (eco-friendly accommodations)
- Responsibletravel.com (vetted sustainable tours)

**Look for certifications**
- Green Key certified properties
- LEED certified accommodations
- Country-specific eco-labels

---

## Destination Types That Tend to Be Affordable

| Destination Type | Why It's Often Cheaper |
|-----------------|----------------------|
| National parks (developing countries) | Lower entry fees, simpler lodging |
| Rural/off-peak destinations | Less tourist demand |
| Slow travel (staying longer) | Reduces per-day costs |
| Nearby nature destinations | Cuts transportation costs |

---

## Practical Tips to Reduce Costs

- **Travel off-season** - lower prices, less environmental crowding
- **Stay in hostels or guesthouses** with eco practices
- **Use public transportation** - cheaper AND greener
- **Cook some meals** - reduces food waste and costs
- **Travel slower** - fewer flights = cheaper and lower carbon footprint

---

## Good Starting Points to Research

- Costa Rica (established eco-tourism infrastructure)
- Portugal (affordable, strong sustainability focus)
- Slovenia (budget-friendly, very green)
- Southeast Asia (low cost, rich nature)

---

## Questions to Ask Before Booking

- Does the accommodation have genuine sustainability practices?
- Can you get there without flying?
- Does tourism money benefit local communities?
