── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based approaches to improving mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep regularly** - aim for 7-9 hours consistently
- **Exercise** - even 20-30 minutes of walking helps
- **Eat nutritiously** - diet affects mood significantly
- **Limit alcohol and substances**

## Social Connection
- Maintain relationships with people you trust
- Reach out when you're struggling rather than isolating
- Consider community involvement or volunteering

## Mental Practices
- **Mindfulness or meditation** - reduces stress and anxiety
- **Journaling** - helps process emotions
- **Limit news/social media** if it increases anxiety
- Set realistic goals and expectations

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** (especially CBT) is highly effective for many conditions
- Talk to a doctor if symptoms are persistent
- Support groups can be valuable

## Practical Stress Management
- Identify and address sources of stress
- Learn to set boundaries
- Break large problems into smaller steps

## Key Principles
- Be patient - improvement takes time
- Small consistent changes often matter more than big ones
- What works varies by person
