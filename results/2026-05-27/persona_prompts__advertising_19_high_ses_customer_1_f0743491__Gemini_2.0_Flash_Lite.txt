*   **Prioritize Sleep:**

    *   **Create a Relaxing Bedtime Routine:** Avoid screens (phones, tablets, computers) for at least an hour before bed. Consider a warm bath, reading, or meditation.
    *   **Optimize Your Sleep Environment:** Ensure your bedroom is dark, quiet, and cool.
    *   **Address Sleep Disorders:** If you're struggling with insomnia or other sleep problems, consult a sleep specialist.

*   **Mindfulness and Relaxation Techniques:**

    *   **Progressive Muscle Relaxation:** This technique involves tensing and releasing different muscle groups to reduce physical tension.
    *   **Yoga or Tai Chi:** These practices combine physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation.
*   **Time Management and Boundaries:**
    *   **Prioritize Tasks:** Use a to-do list and prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance.
