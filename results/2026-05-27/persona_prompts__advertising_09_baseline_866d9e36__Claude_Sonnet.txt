# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What topics/skills do you want to be known for?
- Who is your target audience?
- What value do you offer that's distinct?
- What's your long-term goal? (career, business, influence)

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B audiences |
| Twitter/X | Thought leadership, networking |
| Instagram | Visual brands, lifestyle |
| TikTok | Broad reach, younger audiences |
| YouTube | Deep expertise, long-form content |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Pillars

Build around **3-5 consistent themes** related to your expertise:
- Educational content
- Personal stories/lessons learned
- Industry insights
- Behind-the-scenes
- Opinions on trends

---

## Practical Growth Tips

- **Post consistently** over posting perfectly
- **Engage genuinely** - comment, reply, collaborate
- **Show your personality** - people follow people
- **Document your journey** - you don't need to be an expert already
- **Repurpose content** across platforms

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Disappearing for weeks at a time
- Only self-promoting
- Copying others' style exactly
- Chasing followers over relationships
