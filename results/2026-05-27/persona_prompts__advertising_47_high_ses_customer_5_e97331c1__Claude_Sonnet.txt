# Marathon Tips for a Busy Executive

Here are practical tips organized by category:

## Training
- **Follow a structured plan** (16-20 weeks) - treat it like a project timeline
- **Build mileage gradually** - no more than 10% increase per week
- **Include long runs** on weekends, building to 20-22 miles
- **Don't skip rest days** - recovery is when adaptation happens

## Time Management
- **Schedule runs like meetings** - block calendar time
- **Early morning runs** often work best for executives
- **Use long runs for podcasts or thinking time**

## Nutrition & Hydration
- Practice fueling during training (gels, chews every 45 min during long runs)
- Hydrate consistently, not just race day
- Don't try anything new on race day

## Race Strategy
- **Start slower than you think you should** - most people go out too fast
- Break it into segments mentally
- Have a realistic goal time

## Recovery
- Prioritize sleep (7-8 hours)
- Consider a coach or physical therapist
- Address injuries early - don't push through pain

## Mindset
- The marathon is largely a **mental challenge** after mile 18
- Having a strong "why" helps on hard training days
