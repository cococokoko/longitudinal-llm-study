# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Common Challenges First
- Distractions from family, household tasks, or entertainment
- Blurred boundaries between work and personal life
- Isolation and lack of accountability
- Difficulty staying focused without office structure

---

## Key Strategies

### 🏠 Set Up Your Space
- Dedicate a specific area **only for work**
- Keep it organized and free of clutter
- Ensure good lighting and a comfortable chair
- Use headphones to block distractions

### ⏰ Structure Your Day
- Start and end work at **consistent times**
- Follow a morning routine before logging on
- Schedule breaks intentionally (try the **Pomodoro technique**: 25 min work / 5 min break)
- Create a "shutdown ritual" to signal the end of the workday

### 📋 Manage Your Tasks
- Write a **prioritized to-do list** each morning
- Tackle your hardest task first (**"eat the frog"**)
- Use tools like Todoist, Notion, or Trello
- Set realistic daily goals

### 🤝 Stay Connected & Accountable
- Check in with colleagues regularly
- Share goals with an accountability partner
- Use video calls to maintain human connection

### 📵 Minimize Distractions
- Silence non-essential notifications
- Use website blockers (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- Communicate boundaries to people you live with

---

## Quick Wins
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Step outside for fresh air daily
- Keep your phone in another room during focus time
