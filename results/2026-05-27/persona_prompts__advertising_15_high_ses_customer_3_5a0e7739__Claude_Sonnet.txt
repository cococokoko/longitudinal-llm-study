# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical strategies to make your space look great without overspending:

## Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - often deeply discounted items
- **End-of-season sales** - retailers discount heavily to clear inventory
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly good for vases, frames, and accents

## DIY Approaches
- Repaint furniture instead of replacing it
- Create your own wall art
- Reupholster chairs with inexpensive fabric
- Make throw pillow covers

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Fresh paint** - one of the biggest transformations per dollar
- **New throw pillows** - instantly refresh a sofa
- **Plants** - add life and color affordably
- **Rearrange existing furniture** - costs nothing

## Principles to Stretch Your Budget
- Focus on a few **statement pieces** rather than many small items
- Stick to a **consistent color palette**
- Prioritize **high-traffic, visible areas** first
- Quality over quantity

## Free Resources
- Swap items with friends
- Check neighborhood "free" listings
- Repurpose items you already own
