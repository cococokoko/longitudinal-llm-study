How long doesn it take to get over rejection from a crush

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Getting over a crush rejection varies a lot by person, but here's what's generally true:

## Typical Timeframes
- **Mild crush** - days to a few weeks
- **Stronger feelings** - 1-3 months
- **Long-term or intense feelings** - several months

## What Affects Recovery Time
- How long you had feelings for them
- How close you were as friends
- Whether you still see them regularly
- Your general emotional resilience
- Whether you have good support around you

## Things That Actually Help
- **Letting yourself feel it** rather than suppressing it
- Staying socially connected with others
- Keeping busy with meaningful activities
- Limiting how much you check their social media
- Giving yourself some distance if possible

## Things That Slow It Down
- Constant contact or hoping they'll change their mind
- Replaying the rejection repeatedly
- Isolating yourself

## Worth Knowing
- The pain is **genuinely real** - rejection activates similar brain regions as physical pain
- Most people feel significantly better within **4-6 weeks**
- It rarely feels as bad at 6 weeks as it did on day one
