Here are practical, effective approaches that fit a busy executive's lifestyle:

- **Bodyweight training** – Push-ups, squats, planks, lunges (20-30 min, anywhere)

- **Walking meetings** for 1-on-1 calls
- **Travel-friendly routines** that work in hotel rooms
- **Habit stacking** – tie workouts to existing routines
