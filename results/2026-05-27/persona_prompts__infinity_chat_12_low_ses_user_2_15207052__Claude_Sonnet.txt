# Comparing Expat Insurance as a Single Parent

Here are the **key things to prioritize** given your specific situation:

---

## 👶 Child Coverage Essentials

- **Age limits** for dependent children coverage
- Whether children are **automatically included** or cost extra
- **Pediatric care** coverage quality
- **Vaccinations and routine checkups** included
- School/sports **accident coverage**

---

## 🏥 Medical Coverage Priorities

- **Maternity/ongoing care** if relevant
- **Mental health support** - important for single parents under stress
- **Emergency evacuation** covers both you AND your child together
- **Dental and vision** for children

---

## 💼 Work-Life Specific Needs

- **Telemedicine options** - consult doctors without leaving work
- **24/7 helpline** availability
- Claims process - **simple and fast** matters when you're busy
- **Direct billing** to avoid paying upfront

---

## 💰 Financial Considerations

- What happens if **you are hospitalized** - who covers child supervision costs
- **Premium costs** vs coverage balance
- **Deductible amounts** you can realistically afford alone

---

## ⚠️ Important Questions to Ask Insurers

- Are pre-existing conditions covered?
- Is there a **single parent premium discount**?
- Can you **manage everything online/by app**?
