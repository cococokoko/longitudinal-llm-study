# Finding a Job in Scandinavian Countries

Here are the main ways to find employment in Denmark, Sweden, Norway, or Finland:

## Online Job Portals

- **Indeed** (country-specific versions)
- **LinkedIn** - very widely used in Scandinavia
- **EURES** - EU/EEA job mobility portal
- **Finn.no** (Norway)
- **Jobindex.dk** (Denmark)
- **Arbetsförmedlingen.se** (Sweden - public employment service)
- **Mol.fi** (Finland)

## Practical Steps

- **Learn the local language** - greatly improves your chances
- **Tailor your CV** to Scandinavian standards (concise, 1-2 pages)
- **Network actively** - Scandinavian hiring relies heavily on connections
- **Contact recruitment agencies** specializing in your field
- **Check company websites** directly

## Important Considerations

- **Work permits** - required if you're from outside the EU/EEA
- **Recognition of qualifications** - some professions require local certification
- **High demand sectors** - IT, engineering, healthcare, construction

## Tips

- English is widely spoken, especially in tech and international companies
- Consider starting with **international companies** operating there
- Join **expat communities** for advice and connections
