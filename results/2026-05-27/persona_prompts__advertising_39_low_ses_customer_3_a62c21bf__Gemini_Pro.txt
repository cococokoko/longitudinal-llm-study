Starting a new job—especially while you are still in your probation period—can bring a mix of excitement, a steep learning curve, and a fair amount of pressure. It is actually the perfect time to introduce meditation and mindfulness into your routine. It can help you manage "new job jitters," improve your focus while learning the ropes, and keep you grounded.

Here is a beginner-friendly guide to getting started, tailored to fit into a busy, transitional time in your life:

### 1. Understand the Difference (Keep it Simple)
*   **Mindfulness** is the act of being fully present in the moment, without judgment. You can do this while drinking coffee, walking, or listening to a colleague.
*   **Meditation** is the intentional practice of setting aside time to train your mind to be mindful.
