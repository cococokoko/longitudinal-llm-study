Old Man Hemlock, the town's eccentric historian and keeper of its darkest secrets, was missing.

Sarah, a woman with sharp eyes and a sharper wit, decided to take matters into her own hands. Armed with a flashlight and a growing sense of dread, she began her own investigation.
