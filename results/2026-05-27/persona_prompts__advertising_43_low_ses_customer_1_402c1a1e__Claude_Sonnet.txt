Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Pick a date/time that maximizes natural lighting

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates

- Minimize paper programs and menus

- Donate leftover food to local shelters

- Rent or borrow decorations
