Start with a problem, not a product  
Pick a specific group of people and a clear problem they already spend money on. “Fitness for busy moms” is better than just “fitness.” The more specific, the easier everything gets.

Validate before building  
Before you invest time or money, test demand:
- Talk to real people in that niche  
- Post about your idea and see if anyone cares  
- Try to get a few pre-orders or signups  

If no one bites, that’s useful data—adjust early.

Keep your first version very small  
Don’t try to launch a perfect brand. Start with:
- A simple offer (1 product or service)  
- A basic landing page or even just a social profile  
- A way to take payment  

You can upgrade later once money is coming in.

- Digital products (guides, templates, courses)
