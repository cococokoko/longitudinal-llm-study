How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical tips for decorating your apartment on a minimal budget:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh look
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks more intentional
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Shopping Options
- **Thrift stores** - great for unique pieces at low prices
- **Facebook Marketplace/Craigslist** - furniture and decor for cheap or free
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly good for basic frames, candles, small items
- **Discount stores** like TJ Maxx or HomeGoods

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Ideas
- Add plants (pothos and snake plants are cheap and hard to kill)
- Use removable wall decals or washi tape for wall interest
- Print free art from sites like Unsplash and frame it
- Swap out throw pillows or add a simple throw blanket
- Good lighting makes a huge difference - try warm bulbs or string lights

## Practical Considerations
- Focus on one room or area at a time
- Buy things gradually rather than all at once
- Look for multipurpose furniture

## Free Resources
- Library books for design inspiration
- YouTube tutorials for DIY projects
- Pinterest for planning before spending
