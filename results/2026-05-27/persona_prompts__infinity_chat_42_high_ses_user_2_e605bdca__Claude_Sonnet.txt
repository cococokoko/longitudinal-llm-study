## An Interesting Note for Legal Professionals

Many English synonym pairs actually come from two different language roots:
- **Anglo-Saxon** (simpler words like *buy, help, ask*)
- **Latin/French** (more formal words like *purchase, assist, request*)

This is why legal documents historically used **both words together** - such as *"null and void"* or *"cease and desist"* - to cover both linguistic traditions and ensure clarity.
