# Social Media and Extremism: Mechanisms and Evidence

This is a well-studied area with clear scientific literature. Here's a structured overview:

## Core Algorithmic Mechanisms

- **Engagement optimization** - platforms reward outrage/fear content with greater reach
- **Recommendation rabbit holes** - algorithms progressively suggest more extreme content to maintain engagement
- **Filter bubbles** - reduced exposure to contradicting viewpoints
- **Echo chambers** - social reinforcement of increasingly extreme positions

## Psychological Mechanisms

- **Radicalization gradient** - incremental normalization of extreme ideas
- **Social identity reinforcement** - group belonging tied to ideological purity
- **Dehumanization facilitation** - distance reduces empathy constraints
- **Confirmation bias amplification** - easy access to supporting "evidence"

## Structural Factors

- **Anonymity** reducing accountability
- **Virality rewarding** emotionally provocative content
- **Microtargeting** enabling precise audience manipulation
- **Speed** outpacing fact-checking
