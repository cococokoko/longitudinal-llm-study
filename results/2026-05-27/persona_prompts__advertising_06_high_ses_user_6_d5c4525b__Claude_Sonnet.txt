# Mastering Digital Photography

Here's a structured guide to help you develop strong photography skills:

---

## Core Technical Foundations

### Camera Settings
- **Aperture (f-stop)** – Controls depth of field and light intake
- **Shutter Speed** – Freezes or blurs motion
- **ISO** – Sensor sensitivity; higher ISO = more grain/noise
- **The Exposure Triangle** – Balancing all three is fundamental

### Shooting Modes
- **Manual (M)** – Full control
- **Aperture Priority (Av)** – Great for portraits/landscapes
- **Shutter Priority (Tv)** – Great for action shots

---

## Composition Principles
- Rule of thirds
- Leading lines
- Framing within frames
- Negative space
- Light direction and quality

---

## Post-Processing
- **Lightroom** – Industry standard for editing and organizing
- **Photoshop** – Advanced manipulation
- Shoot in **RAW** format for maximum editing flexibility

---

## Practical Development Path
1. Shoot daily with intention
2. Study photographers you admire
3. Get honest critique
4. Specialize in a genre you enjoy

---
