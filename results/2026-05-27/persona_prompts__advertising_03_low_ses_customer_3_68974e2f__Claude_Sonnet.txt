# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are some practical strategies to help you stay focused and perform well, especially important during your probation period:

## Structure Your Day
- **Set consistent start and finish times** to maintain routine
- **Create a morning routine** to mentally "switch on" for work
- **Schedule breaks** - short breaks actually improve focus
- **Use time-blocking** to dedicate specific hours to key tasks

## Create Your Workspace
- Designate a specific area for work if possible
- Minimize distractions (phone notifications, household noise)
- Ensure good lighting and a comfortable setup
- Keep your workspace tidy

## Stay Visible to Your Team
- **Communicate proactively** with your manager
- Respond to messages promptly
- Share progress updates regularly
- This is particularly valuable while you're still proving yourself in your new role

## Manage Your Focus
- Try the **Pomodoro Technique** (25 minutes work, 5 minute break)
- Use apps like Focus Mode or website blockers
- Tackle your most important tasks when your energy is highest

## Stay Connected
- Schedule regular check-ins with colleagues
- Don't hesitate to ask questions
- Participate actively in virtual meetings

## Track Your Output
- Keep a daily task list
- Document your achievements
- This helps demonstrate your value during probation
