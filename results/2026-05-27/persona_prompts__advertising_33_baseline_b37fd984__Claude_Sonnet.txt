# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Shea butter or cocoa butter
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey

---

## Simple Recipes

### **Moisturizing Face Oil**
- 2 tbsp jojoba oil
- 2 tbsp rosehip oil
- 5 drops lavender essential oil
- Mix and store in a dark glass bottle

### **Sugar Body Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar
- ½ cup coconut oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil

### **Simple Lip Balm**
- 1 tbsp beeswax
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp shea butter
- Melt together, pour into containers, let cool

### **Hair Mask**
- 1 ripe avocado
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- Apply for 20-30 minutes, rinse well

---

## Important Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- Products **without preservatives** have short shelf life (refrigerate when possible)
- Be cautious with essential oils - **dilute properly**
- Research any ingredient if you have **sensitive skin or allergies**
