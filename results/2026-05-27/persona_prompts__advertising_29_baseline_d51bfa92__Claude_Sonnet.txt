How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - Annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Proper nutrition** - Feed age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - Daily activity suited to your pet's breed and age
- **Parasite prevention** - Flea, tick, and heartworm protection
- **Spay/neuter** - Reduces health risks and behavioral issues

## Mental Wellbeing
- **Enrichment** - Toys, puzzles, and activities that stimulate their mind
- **Social interaction** - Time with you and appropriate animal companions
- **Consistent routine** - Pets generally feel secure with predictable schedules
- **Safe space** - A comfortable place they can retreat to

## Everyday Care
- **Grooming** - Regular brushing, nail trimming, and bathing as needed
- **Clean environment** - Fresh water, clean bedding, and living space
- **Watch for changes** - Notice shifts in eating, behavior, or energy levels

## Emotional Connection
- **Quality time** - Play, cuddle, and engage with them regularly
- **Positive training** - Builds confidence and strengthens your bond
- **Patience** - Understanding their individual personality and needs
