# How to Improve Home WiFi

Here are practical tips that work for most situations:

## Router Placement
- **Central location** in your home
- **Elevated position** (shelf, not floor)
- Away from walls, microwaves, and cordless phones
- Open space, not inside cabinets

## Quick Fixes
- **Restart your router** regularly
- **Update router firmware** (check manufacturer's app/website)
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**
- Use **5GHz band** for faster speeds nearby, **2.4GHz** for longer range

## Hardware Upgrades
- **WiFi extender/repeater** - affordable, extends range
- **Mesh network system** (like Eero, Google Nest) - best for larger homes
- **New router** if yours is 5+ years old

## Simple Settings
- Set a strong password to prevent neighbors from using your bandwidth
- Prioritize devices using **QoS settings**

## Check Your Internet Plan
- Run a speed test at **fast.com** or **speedtest.net**
- Compare results to what you're paying for
- Contact your ISP if speeds are consistently low

## Budget-Friendly Options
- A basic WiFi extender costs **$20-40**
- Powerline adapters work through electrical wiring
