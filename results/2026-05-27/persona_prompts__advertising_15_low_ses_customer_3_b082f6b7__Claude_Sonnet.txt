# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** - a tidy space instantly looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Updates
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations for little money
- Swap out **cushion covers** instead of buying new cushions
- Update **light switches and outlet covers** for a polished look

## Smart Shopping
- **Charity shops and thrift stores** for unique pieces
- **Facebook Marketplace and Freecycle** for free or cheap furniture
- **End of season sales** for significant discounts
- Dollar/pound stores for small decorative items

## DIY Ideas
- Create your own **wall art** with prints or framing fabric
- **Upcycle old furniture** with paint or new handles
- Make your own **cushion covers or curtains**

## Free Touches
- **Plants and greenery** from cuttings
- Rearrange books and ornaments you already have
- Natural elements like pinecones or branches

## Practical Advice
- **Set a clear budget** before you start
- Prioritize the rooms you use most
- Focus on **one room at a time**
