── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How long doesn it take to get over rejection from a crush

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Getting over a crush rejection varies a lot from person to person, but here's a general idea:

## Typical Timeframes
- **A few days to weeks** - if you didn't know them well
- **Several weeks to months** - if you had strong feelings or knew them a long time
- **Longer** - if you have to see them regularly (school, work)

## Things That Affect How Long It Takes
- How strong your feelings were
- How long you liked them
- Whether you still see them often
- Your support system
- Your past experiences with rejection

## Things That Actually Help
- **Let yourself feel it** - don't suppress the emotions
- **Talk to someone** you trust
- **Stay busy** with things you enjoy
- **Limit contact** if possible, at least for a while
- **Avoid checking their social media** constantly
- **Be kind to yourself** - rejection doesn't mean something is wrong with you

## Worth Remembering
- The pain is **real but temporary**
- Rejection is something almost everyone experiences
- It often gets easier faster than you expect
- It doesn't define your worth
