# Marathon Training Tips

Here are some practical tips to help you prepare and succeed:

## Training
- **Build mileage gradually** - increase weekly distance by no more than 10% per week
- **Follow a structured plan** - most plans are 16-20 weeks long
- **Include rest days** - recovery is just as important as running
- **Do long runs** - build up to 20-22 miles before race day

## Nutrition & Hydration
- **Practice fueling during training** - test gels, chews, or real food on long runs
- **Stay hydrated daily**, not just during runs
- **Carb load** in the 2-3 days before the race
- **Don't try anything new on race day**

## Practical Considerations
- **Invest in good shoes** - get fitted at a running store
- **Prevent chafing** - use Body Glide or similar products
- **Train at different times of day** to fit your schedule
- **Batch your long runs on weekends** when you may have more flexibility

## Race Day
- **Start slower than you feel you should**
- **Break the race into segments** mentally
- **Have a support plan** - know where family can cheer you on

## Staying Consistent
- **Prepare runs in advance** (clothes, nutrition) to reduce morning friction
- **Find a running group** for accountability
