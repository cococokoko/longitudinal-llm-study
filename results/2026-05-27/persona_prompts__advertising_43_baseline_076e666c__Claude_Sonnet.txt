# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here's a practical guide to reducing your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue
- Choose a **local venue** to minimize guest travel
- Look for **outdoor or naturally lit spaces** to reduce energy use
- Select venues with **green certifications** or sustainability practices
- Consider parks, farms, or gardens

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send **digital invitations** (Paperless Post, Greenvelope)
- Use **recycled or seed paper** if printing
- Minimize inserts and extra cards

## 🌸 Flowers & Décor
- Choose **seasonal, locally grown flowers**
- Rent décor instead of buying
- Use **potted plants** guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (it's plastic-based)
- Consider dried flowers or greenery

## 🍽️ Food & Drink
- Serve **locally sourced, seasonal menus**
- Offer **plant-based options** (lower carbon footprint)
- Use real dishes instead of disposables
- Donate **leftover food** to local shelters

## 👗 Attire
- Buy **secondhand or vintage**
- Rent your outfit
- Choose designers with ethical practices
- Wear something you'll use again

## 🚗 Transportation
- Choose a **central location** for guests
- Arrange **group shuttles or carpools**
- Offset carbon emissions

## ♻️ Waste Reduction
- Set up **recycling and compost stations**
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## 💝 Gifts & Favors
- Create a **charity registry**
- Give edible or plantable favors
- Request experiences over physical gifts

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Carbon offset** your wedding
- Hire vendors who share your values
- Communicate your green goals to guests
