# Stock Trading Strategies

Here's an honest overview of common approaches:

## Long-Term Strategies (Generally Most Reliable)
- **Buy and Hold** - Purchase diversified index funds, hold for years
- **Dollar-Cost Averaging** - Invest fixed amounts regularly regardless of price
- **Dividend Investing** - Focus on stocks with consistent dividend payments

## Medium-Term Strategies
- **Value Investing** - Buy undervalued companies (Buffett approach)
- **Growth Investing** - Focus on companies with strong earnings growth
- **Sector Rotation** - Shift between sectors based on economic cycles

## Short-Term/Active Trading
- **Swing Trading** - Hold positions days to weeks
- **Momentum Trading** - Follow trending stocks
- **Technical Analysis** - Use chart patterns and indicators
