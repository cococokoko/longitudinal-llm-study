# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures (deadlines, conflicts, demands)
**Anxiety** = worry that persists, sometimes without a clear cause

Both are normal, but they can become overwhelming without good management strategies.

---

## Immediate Relief Techniques

**Breathing exercises**
- Box breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4
- 4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4, hold 7, exhale 8

**Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)**
- Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste

**Physical release**
- Cold water on your face or wrists
- Brief walk or movement
- Progressive muscle relaxation

---

## Longer-Term Strategies

| Area | Approaches |
|------|-----------|
| **Body** | Regular exercise, sleep, limiting caffeine/alcohol |
| **Mind** | Meditation, journaling, therapy |
| **Lifestyle** | Setting boundaries, reducing overcommitment |
| **Connection** | Talking to trusted people |

---

## When to Seek Help

Consider professional support if:
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- You feel hopeless or overwhelmed regularly
- Physical symptoms appear (chest pain, insomnia)
