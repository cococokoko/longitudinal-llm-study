# What to Look for When Comparing Expat Insurance

## 📋 Coverage Essentials

### Medical Coverage
- **Maximum coverage limit** (look for $1M+ for serious conditions)
- **Inpatient vs outpatient** coverage
- **Emergency evacuation** and repatriation
- **Pre-existing conditions** policy
- **Maternity coverage** (if needed)
- **Dental and vision** coverage
- **Mental health** coverage

---

## 💰 Cost Factors

- **Premium costs** (monthly/annual)
- **Deductibles** and co-payments
- **What affects your premium:**
  - Age
  - Nationality
  - Country of residence
  - Coverage area selected

---

## 🌍 Geographic Coverage

- **Worldwide vs regional** plans
- Whether **USA coverage** is included (significantly raises cost)
- **Home country coverage** during visits
- Number of countries covered

---

## 🏥 Network & Access

- Size of **hospital/doctor network**
- **Direct billing** availability
- Access to **private vs public** hospitals
- Quality of network in your specific location

---

## 📝 Policy Details

- **Exclusions** (read carefully)
- **Waiting periods**
- **Claim process** (ease and speed)
- **24/7 support** availability
- Language support

---

## 🔄 Flexibility

- Ability to **add/remove dependents**
- **Renewable** regardless of health changes
- **Portability** if you move countries
- **Customizable** modules

---

## ⭐ Provider Reputation

- **Financial stability** rating
- **Customer reviews**
- **Claims payment** history
- Years in **expat insurance market**

---

## 🔑 Top Tips

> ✅ **Don't just choose the cheapest** — check what's excluded
> ✅ **Compare at least 3-5 providers**
> ✅ **Check local legal requirements** for your host country
> ✅ **Consider your lifestyle** and health needs
> ✅ **Read the fine print** on pre-existing conditions
