Start with a simple weekly structure  
Anchor your meals around 3–4 repeatable options for each category:
- Breakfast: oatmeal with fruit, eggs + toast, or yogurt + granola

Shop with a strategy, not a list of recipes

- A bag of rice becomes stir-fry, burrito bowls, and a side dish

This cuts both cost and effort significantly.

- Fewer impulse takeout decisions
