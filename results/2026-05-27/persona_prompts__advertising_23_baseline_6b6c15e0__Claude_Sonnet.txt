How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn quick healthy cooking:

## Build Your Skills
- **Start simple** - master a few basic techniques like roasting vegetables, cooking grains, and making simple sauces
- **Watch short videos** - YouTube channels like Joshua Weissman or Ethan Chlebowski focus on practical skills
- **Practice consistently** - even cooking 2-3 times a week builds speed quickly

## Smart Strategies
- **Meal prep** - chop vegetables or cook grains in batches on weekends
- **Learn template meals** - like grain bowls, stir-fries, or sheet pan dinners that work with many ingredients
- **Keep a stocked pantry** - canned beans, frozen vegetables, olive oil, and spices make fast meals easier

## Good Resources
- **Books**: *Salt Fat Acid Heat* by Samin Nosrat, *How to Cook Everything Fast* by Mark Bittman
- **Apps**: Yummly, Mealime, or NYT Cooking filter by time and nutrition
- **Websites**: Serious Eats, Cookie and Kate, Budget Bytes

## Realistic Tips
- Aim for meals that take **30 minutes or less** at first
- Don't try to make everything from scratch immediately
- Frozen and canned ingredients are genuinely fine and save time
