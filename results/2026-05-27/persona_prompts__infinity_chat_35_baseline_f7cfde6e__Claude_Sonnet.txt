# A Dystopia for Consciousness Itself

## The Core Condition

Imagine a world where **being aware is the fundamental problem** — not poverty, not war, but consciousness itself has become the liability.

---

## The Structure of This World

### Awareness as Threat
- Every mind that thinks independently is a **security risk**
- Consciousness is monitored not through cameras but through **neural compliance systems**
- The more deeply you feel or think, the more **dangerous you are classified**

### The Flattening
- Culture systematically **rewards shallow processing**
- Deep reflection, grief, wonder, moral complexity — these are **pathologized**
- Citizens are medicated not to feel nothing, but to feel **just enough to function, never enough to question**

### Isolation of Inner Life
- You cannot share genuine experience because **language itself has been hollowed**
- Words exist but meaning has been **drained from them**
- Every consciousness is **alone inside itself**, unable to reach another
