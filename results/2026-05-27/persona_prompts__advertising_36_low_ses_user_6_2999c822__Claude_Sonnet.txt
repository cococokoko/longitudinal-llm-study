## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (shows up in local searches and maps)
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood-level reach
- Facebook local groups and a business page
- Ask satisfied customers for online reviews

**Community Presence**
- Sponsor local events or sports teams
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Partner with complementary businesses
- Attend farmers markets or community events

**Word of Mouth**
- Referral programs rewarding existing customers
- Excellent customer service that people talk about
- Follow up with customers after purchases

**Targeted Advertising**
- Flyers or door hangers in specific neighborhoods
- Local newspaper or community newsletter ads
- Geotargeted Facebook/Instagram ads on a small budget

**Practical Priorities**
- Start with Google Business Profile - it's free and high impact
- Focus on getting reviews early
- Be consistent rather than trying everything at once

**What works best often depends on your specific business type** - a restaurant markets differently than a service business, for example.
