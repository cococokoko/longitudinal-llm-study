How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Great question! Here are some key ways to keep your pets healthy and happy:

## Basic Health Care
- **Regular vet visits** - Annual checkups and vaccinations
- **Parasite prevention** - Flea, tick, and heartworm treatments
- **Dental care** - Brushing teeth or dental treats
- **Spay/neuter** if appropriate

## Nutrition
- Feed **quality food** appropriate for their species and age
- Maintain **consistent feeding schedules**
- Always provide **fresh water**
- Avoid harmful human foods

## Exercise & Mental Stimulation
- Daily walks or playtime
- Toys and enrichment activities
- Training sessions (good for bonding too)
- Social interaction with people and other animals

## Emotional Wellbeing
- Spend **quality time** with them regularly
- Provide a **safe, comfortable space**
- Minimize stress from loud noises or changes
- Learn to read their body language

## Practical Tips for Busy Households
- Since you manage a lot of responsibilities, **automatic feeders** and **water fountains** can help maintain consistency
- Involve family members in pet care routines
- Keep a simple **health log** for each pet
- Have a trusted backup caregiver for busy days

## Watch For Warning Signs
- Changes in eating, drinking, or behavior
- Unusual lethargy or aggression
- Any physical changes
