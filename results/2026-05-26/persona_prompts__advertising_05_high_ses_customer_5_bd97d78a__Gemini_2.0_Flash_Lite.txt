*   **Smart Grocery Shopping:**
    *   **Plan Your Meals:** Before you go to the store, create a detailed meal plan for the week. This prevents impulse buys and reduces food waste.
    *   **Make a List & Stick to It:** This is crucial for staying within budget.
    *   **Shop the Perimeter:** Focus on the fresh produce, meat, and dairy sections. These areas often contain the healthiest and most budget-friendly options.
    *   **Embrace Frozen:** Frozen fruits and vegetables are just as nutritious as fresh and often cheaper. They're also convenient for smoothies and quick side dishes.
