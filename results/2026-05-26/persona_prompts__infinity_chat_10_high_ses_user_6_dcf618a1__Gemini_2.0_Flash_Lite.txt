**Top Free Hosting Options for Your Resume (Prioritized for Professionalism):**

    *   **Cons:**
        *   **Requires a GitHub Account:**  You'll need to create a free GitHub account.

        1.  **Create a GitHub Account:**  Go to [https://github.com/](https://github.com/) and sign up.

        3.  **Upload Your Files:**  You can either:
            *   **Upload Directly:**  Click "Upload files" on the repository page and drag and drop your HTML files.

        5.  **Connect a Custom Domain (Optional):**  If you have a domain, follow GitHub's instructions for connecting it.  This usually involves adding DNS records to your domain registrar.
