**Spiced Chickpea "Popcorn"**

This is a fun, crunchy, and flavorful alternative to traditional popcorn, packed with protein and fiber.

**Here's the recipe:**

1.  **Ingredients:**
    *   1 can (15 ounces) chickpeas, rinsed and thoroughly dried (this is key for crispiness!)
    *   1 tablespoon olive oil (or avocado oil)
    *   1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
    *   1/4 teaspoon cumin
    *   1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    *   Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional, for a little kick)
    *   Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

2.  **Instructions:**
    *   Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
    *   In a bowl, toss the dried chickpeas with the olive oil, smoked paprika, cumin, garlic powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper. Make sure they're evenly coated.
    *   Spread the chickpeas in a single layer on a baking sheet.
    *   Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until they are golden brown and crispy, shaking the pan halfway through to ensure even cooking. Keep a close eye on them towards the end, as they can burn quickly.
    *   Let them cool slightly before enjoying. They'll crisp up even more as they cool.

**Why this is a great snack:**

*   **Nutritious:** Chickpeas are a good source of protein, fiber, and various vitamins and minerals.
*   **Flavorful:** The spices provide a satisfying and complex flavor profile.
*   **Satisfying Crunch:** The roasting process creates a delightful crunch that mimics the experience of popcorn.
*   **Diet-Friendly:** It's naturally low in calories and fat, especially compared to many other snack options.
*   **Customizable:** Feel free to experiment with different spice combinations! Try chili powder, curry powder, or even a sprinkle of nutritional yeast for a cheesy flavor.
