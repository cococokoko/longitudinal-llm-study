# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here's a practical overview of sustainable travel choices:

## Transportation
- **Train travel** - Significantly lower carbon footprint than flying
- **Electric vehicles** - For road trips, with growing charging infrastructure
- **Carpooling/ridesharing** - Reduces per-person emissions
- **Cycling or walking** - Zero emissions for local exploration

- **Eco-lodges** - Often support local conservation efforts

## Practical Strategies
- **Offset carbon emissions** - Through verified programs like Gold Standard

- **Travel slower** - Fewer trips, longer stays

- **National parks and protected areas** - Support conservation funding
- **Off-season travel** - Reduces overtourism pressure
- **Local experiences** - Support community economies
