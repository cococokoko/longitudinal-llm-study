# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), electric is easier on fingers
- **Get essential accessories** - picks, tuner, extra strings, capo
- **Learn proper posture** - sitting position, how to hold the neck, strumming hand position

## First Things to Learn
1. **Tune your guitar** - Use a clip-on tuner or app (GuitarTuna is popular)
2. **Basic chords** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
3. **Chord transitions** - Practice switching smoothly between chords
4. **Basic strumming patterns** - Down strokes first, then up/down combinations

## Practice Tips
- **Practice daily** - Even 15-20 minutes is better than long occasional sessions
- **Start slow** - Speed comes with muscle memory
- **Fingertip soreness is normal** - Calluses develop over a few weeks
- **Use a metronome** - Builds timing and rhythm

## Free Learning Resources
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar (highly recommended for beginners)
- **Apps** - Yousician, Fender Play
- **Websites** - JustinGuitar.com

## Realistic Timeline
- Basic chords: **2-4 weeks**
- Simple songs: **1-3 months**
- Comfortable playing: **6-12 months**
