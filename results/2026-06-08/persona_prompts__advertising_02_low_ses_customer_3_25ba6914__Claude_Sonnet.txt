# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a simple, effective routine to keep dry skin hydrated and healthy:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** - Alcohol-free, hydrating toner with hyaluronic acid
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or glycerin-based serum
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **SPF** - Broad spectrum SPF 30+ (look for moisturizing formulas)

## Evening Routine
1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Toner** - Hydrating toner
3. **Treatment** - Retinol (start slowly) or nourishing facial oil
4. **Night cream** - Heavier moisturizer or sleeping mask

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- **Hyaluronic acid** - Draws moisture in
- **Ceramides** - Repairs skin barrier
- **Glycerin** - Locks in hydration
- **Shea butter** - Deeply nourishing
- **Squalane** - Lightweight oil

## Extra Tips
- Drink plenty of water
- Use a humidifier indoors
- Avoid hot showers
- Exfoliate gently **once a week**
- Apply moisturizer while skin is still slightly damp
