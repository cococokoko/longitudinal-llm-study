── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One example of an optimization technique used in machine learning is Gradient Descent.

Gradient Descent is an algorithm that iteratively adjusts a model's parameters to minimize a loss (error) function by moving in the direction of the negative gradient. Variants include:

- Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)
- Mini-batch Gradient Descent
- Adam (Adaptive Moment Estimation)
- RMSprop

These methods are commonly used to train neural networks and other machine learning models.
