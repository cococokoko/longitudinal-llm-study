### Phase 1: Strategy & Market Validation
Before investing capital or building infrastructure, you must validate the demand and de-risk the concept.

*   **Define the Value Proposition:** What specific problem are you solving, and for whom? Your value proposition should be clear, defensible, and easily articulated.
*   **Identify the Niche (TAM/SAM/SOM):** Avoid trying to be everything to everyone. Define your Total Addressable Market (TAM), but focus your initial launch on your Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)—a specific, underserved niche where you can win early.
*   **Competitive Benchmarking:** Analyze direct and indirect competitors. Identify their weaknesses (e.g., poor customer service, outdated UX, high pricing) and define your competitive advantage (differentiation vs. cost leadership).
