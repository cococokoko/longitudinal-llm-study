* **The tied ankle:** This represents their deeply intertwined economies and global supply chains. They cannot easily separate without causing immense pain to themselves.
* **The race:** They are aggressively trying to outpace each other to prove who is the fastest and strongest (technological and geopolitical supremacy).

They cannot destroy each other, but they must learn to manage the fault line to prevent a catastrophic global tsunami.
