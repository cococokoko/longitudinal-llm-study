# Smartwatch Feature for Senior Citizens

## 🏥 "Wellness Check-In" with Passive Fall Detection & Smart Escalation

### How It Works:

**1. Continuous Passive Monitoring**
- Tracks movement patterns, heart rate, and activity levels throughout the day
- Learns the user's normal daily routine over time

**2. Gentle Check-In Prompts**
- If unusual inactivity is detected (e.g., no movement for 2 hours), the watch **softly vibrates** and displays a simple "Are you okay?" with two large buttons: ✅ **Yes** / 🆘 **Need Help**

**3. Smart Escalation System**
If there's no response within 60 seconds:
- → Notifies a **designated family member**
- → Alerts a **secondary contact**
- → Optionally contacts **emergency services**

### Additional Design Considerations:
| Feature | Benefit |
|---|---|
| Large, high-contrast display | Easy readability |
| One-button SOS | Simple emergency access |
| Medication reminders | Supports independence |
| GPS location sharing | Family peace of mind |

### Why This Matters:
> Many seniors live alone. A **non-intrusive but responsive** system balances **dignity and safety** — avoiding the stigma of traditional medical alert devices.
