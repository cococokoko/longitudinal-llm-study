### 1. Choose the Right Business Model
Since your time is limited, you want a model that minimizes daily manual labor (like packing and shipping boxes late at night after a long shift). 

*   **Digital Products (Highest Leverage):** E-books, templates, guides, or designs. You create them once, and they sell automatically 24/7 with zero inventory or shipping logistics.
*   **Print on Demand (POD):** You design apparel, mugs, or phone cases, and a third-party supplier prints and ships them only when a customer buys. No upfront inventory costs.
*   **Dropshipping / 3PL (Third-Party Logistics):** If you want to sell physical products, look into using a fulfillment partner. They store, pack, and ship your items so you don't have to do it yourself. 
*   *Note:* If you do decide to hold your own inventory, your background in organization and logistics will be a massive superpower. You’ll just want to start very small so it doesn't overwhelm your living space or your free time.
