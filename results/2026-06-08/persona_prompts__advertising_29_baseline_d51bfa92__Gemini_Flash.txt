── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Keeping your pets healthy and happy requires a combination of physical care, mental stimulation, and emotional bonding. While specific needs vary depending on whether you have a dog, cat, bird, or rabbit, the core pillars of pet care remain the same. 

Here is a comprehensive guide to keeping your pets thriving:

---

### 1. Physical Health (The Foundation)
*   **High-Quality Nutrition:** Feed your pet a balanced diet appropriate for their species, age, size, and activity level. Avoid overfeeding; obesity is one of the leading causes of health issues in pets (like arthritis and diabetes).
*   **Constant Access to Fresh Water:** Ensure they always have clean, fresh water. For cats, who often prefer running water, consider a pet water fountain.
*   **Regular Veterinary Care:** 
    *   Schedule annual checkups (bi-annual for senior pets).
    *   Keep up with vaccinations and preventative treatments for fleas, ticks, and heartworms.
    *   **Spay or neuter** your pet to prevent reproductive cancers and unwanted behaviors.
*   **Dental Hygiene:** Dental disease can lead to heart and kidney issues. Brush your pet’s teeth regularly with pet-safe toothpaste, or use dental treats and toys approved by veterinarians.
*   **Grooming:** Regular brushing prevents painful matting, reduces shedding, and allows you to check for unusual lumps, bumps, or skin issues. Keep their nails trimmed to prevent joint pain.
