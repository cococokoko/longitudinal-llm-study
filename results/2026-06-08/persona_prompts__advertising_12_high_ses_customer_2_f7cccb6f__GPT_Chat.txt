  - High-speed rail can be a practical alternative to short-haul flights.

• Shared transportation
  - Carpooling and ridesharing reduce emissions per traveler by increasing vehicle occupancy.
  - Shared shuttle services can be more sustainable than individual taxis.

  - When powered by low-carbon electricity sources, EVs generally produce lower lifecycle emissions than conventional gasoline vehicles.
  - Best suited when public transit is unavailable or impractical.

• Electric bikes and scooters
  - Require relatively little energy and can replace many short car trips.
  - Useful for urban commuting and tourism.
