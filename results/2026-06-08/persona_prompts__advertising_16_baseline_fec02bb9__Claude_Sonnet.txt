# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** = response to external pressures
**Anxiety** = worry often about future/uncertain things
*They often overlap and feed each other*

---

## Immediate Techniques (In the Moment)

**Breathing**
- 4-7-8 breathing: inhale 4 counts, hold 7, exhale 8
- Box breathing: 4 counts each side
- Simply slowing your breath signals safety to your nervous system

**Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)**
- 5 things you see
- 4 you can touch
- 3 you hear
- 2 you smell
- 1 you taste

**Physical reset**
- Cold water on face/wrists
- Brief walk
- Progressive muscle relaxation

---

## Longer-Term Strategies

| Area | Approaches |
|------|-----------|
| **Body** | Regular exercise, sleep consistency, limiting caffeine/alcohol |
| **Mind** | Journaling, meditation, challenging negative thoughts |
| **Lifestyle** | Boundaries, realistic expectations, reducing overcommitment |
| **Connection** | Talking to trusted people, community |

---

## When to Seek Help

Consider professional support if:
- Symptoms interfere with daily life
- You feel overwhelmed consistently
- Physical symptoms appear
- Coping strategies aren't working
