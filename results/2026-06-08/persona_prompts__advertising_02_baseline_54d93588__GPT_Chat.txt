Best skin routine for dry skin.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
If you have dry skin, the goal is to gently cleanse, restore the skin barrier, and lock in moisture. A simple routine is often the most effective.

Morning:
1. Cleanser
   - Use a gentle, non-foaming cleanser.
   - Examples: CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser, La Roche-Posay Toleriane Hydrating Gentle Cleanser, Vanicream Gentle Facial Cleanser.

2. Moisturizer
   - Choose a cream containing ingredients like ceramides, glycerin, hyaluronic acid, squalane, or shea butter.
   - Examples: CeraVe Moisturizing Cream, Vanicream Moisturizing Cream, La Roche-Posay Lipikar Balm AP+M.

3. Sunscreen
   - Use SPF 30 or higher every day.
   - For dry skin, moisturizing sunscreens often feel more comfortable.
   - Examples: EltaMD UV Daily SPF 40, La Roche-Posay Anthelios Melt-in Milk SPF 60.

Evening:
1. Cleanser
   - Use the same gentle cleanser.
   - Avoid hot water, which can worsen dryness.

2. Treatment (optional)
   - If you want anti-aging benefits, try a low-strength retinol 2–3 nights per week and increase gradually.
   - If your skin is very dry or sensitive, focus on moisturizing first before adding active ingredients.

3. Moisturizer
   - Apply a richer cream at night.
   - If extremely dry, apply a thin layer of petroleum jelly (Vaseline) over moisturizer on dry areas to reduce water loss.

Additional tips:
- Apply moisturizer to slightly damp skin.
- Avoid harsh scrubs and alcohol-heavy toners.
- Use a humidifier if indoor air is dry.
- Limit long, hot showers.
- If your skin stings, flakes, or becomes red easily, prioritize barrier-repair products with ceramides and fragrance-free formulas.
