  - Use puzzle feeders, training exercises, enrichment toys, and social interaction.

- Create a safe living environment.
  - Remove hazards such as toxic plants, exposed electrical cords, and accessible medications.
  - Provide comfortable resting areas and protection from extreme temperatures.

- Pay attention to behavior and health changes.

  - Provide positive reinforcement during training.
