Provide an example of the name of an optimization technique used in machine learning.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One of the most common and fundamental optimization techniques used in machine learning is **Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)**. 

### What it is in simple terms:
Think of SGD as a way of finding the fastest path down a foggy mountain. Because of the fog, you can't see the bottom (the perfect solution), so you look at the ground right under your feet, feel which way slopes downward, and take a step in that direction. 

In machine learning, SGD helps the model make small, quick adjustments step-by-step to reduce its errors and find the "sweet spot" where its predictions are as accurate as possible.
