# Best Platforms for Freelance Writers

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large platform, wide variety of clients and projects

- **Contently** - Higher-end clients, portfolio-focused

- **Scripted** - Vetted writers, steady work

- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Blogging and content writing
- **MediaBistro** - Journalism and media
- **Journalism Jobs** - News and editorial work
- **LinkedIn** - Professional networking and job listings

## Content Mills (Lower Pay, Good for Beginners)
- **Textbroker**
- **iWriter**

## Direct Outreach Options
- Cold pitching publications directly
- Building your own website/portfolio
- Networking on LinkedIn or Twitter/X

## Things to Consider
- **Pay rates** vary significantly between platforms
- Content mills offer volume but lower rates
- Direct clients typically pay the most
- Building a **niche specialty** helps command higher rates
