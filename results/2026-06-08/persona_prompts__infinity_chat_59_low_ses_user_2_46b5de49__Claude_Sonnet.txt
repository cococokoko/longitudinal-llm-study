# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "3 Priority Rule" at the Start of Each Day

Before students arrive, **write down only 3 must-do tasks** for that day.

Not 10. Not 15. Just 3.

### Why this works well for your situation:
- Takes only **2-3 minutes** to do
- Keeps you focused when the day gets chaotic
- Gives you a **real sense of accomplishment** when you finish them
- Helps you leave school without carrying mental load home

### Practical examples of your 3 things:
- Grade one set of papers
- Email one parent
- Prep tomorrow's lesson materials

### A simple bonus tip:
Keep a **small notebook on your desk** specifically for this list so it doesn't get lost in papers or your phone.

The reality is that as a single parent your mental energy is **genuinely limited and valuable**. A short focused list respects that, rather than an overwhelming to-do list that follows you home.
