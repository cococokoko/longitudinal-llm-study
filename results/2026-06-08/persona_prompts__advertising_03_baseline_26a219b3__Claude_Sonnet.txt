# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

## Common Challenges First
- Distractions (family, household tasks, TV)
- Blurred work/life boundaries
- Isolation and lack of motivation
- Poor home office setup

---

## Key Strategies

### 🏠 Create a Dedicated Workspace
- Designate a specific area **only for work**
- Keep it organized and separate from relaxation spaces
- Good lighting and ergonomic setup matter

### ⏰ Establish a Routine
- Start and end work at **consistent times**
- Get dressed as if going to the office
- Build in regular breaks (Pomodoro technique: 25 min work / 5 min break)

### 📋 Manage Your Tasks
- Plan your day the night before
- Prioritize using methods like **Eisenhower Matrix** or simple to-do lists
- Tackle difficult tasks during your peak energy hours

### 📵 Minimize Distractions
- Use website blockers (Freedom, Cold Turkey)
- Silence non-essential notifications
- Communicate boundaries to household members

### 🤝 Stay Connected
- Regular check-ins with colleagues
- Use video calls to maintain human connection
- Avoid isolation by scheduling social interactions

### 🧠 Protect Your Energy
- Take real lunch breaks
- Exercise regularly
- Set a clear **end-of-day signal**

---

## Quick Wins
- Make your bed each morning
- Keep a water bottle at your desk
- Use headphones to signal "focus mode"
