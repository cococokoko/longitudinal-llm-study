# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & garage sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - find quality items at low prices
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly good for frames, candles, and small decor

## DIY Projects
- Paint is one of the **cheapest ways to transform a room**
- Repurpose old furniture with new hardware or paint
- Create your own wall art with simple materials

## Rearrange What You Have
- Moving furniture around costs nothing
- Decluttering can make a space feel completely new
- Swap decor between rooms

## Focus on Key Areas
- **Lighting** makes a huge difference - swap bulbs or add lamps
- **Throw pillows and blankets** update a couch quickly
- **Plants** add life and color inexpensively

## Practical Tips
- Set a **clear budget** before you start
- Prioritize rooms you spend the most time in
- Buy one quality piece at a time rather than lots of cheap items

## Free Resources
- Pinterest for inspiration
- YouTube tutorials for DIY projects
