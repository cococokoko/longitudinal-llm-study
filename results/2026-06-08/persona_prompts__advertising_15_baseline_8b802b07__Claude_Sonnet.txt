# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before buying anything
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** to avoid costly mistakes
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines

---

## Low-Cost, High-Impact Ideas

### Paint
- One of the **cheapest ways to transform a room**
- Try an accent wall instead of painting everything
- Don't overlook ceilings and trim

### Rearrange What You Have
- Move furniture around for a fresh feel
- **Costs nothing** and can completely change a space

### Thrift & Secondhand Shopping
- Thrift stores, Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist
- Estate sales and garage sales
- Look for **solid wood furniture** you can refinish

---

## DIY Projects
- Make your own artwork or gallery wall
- Reupholster chairs with affordable fabric
- Build simple shelving
- Repaint or refinish old furniture

## Smart Shopping Tips
- Shop **end-of-season sales**
- Check discount stores like IKEA, HomeGoods, or TJ Maxx
- Use **coupons and cashback apps**
- Buy floor models or open-box items

## Free Decorating
- Use natural elements like branches or stones
- Frame meaningful photos
- Declutter — **less is often more**
