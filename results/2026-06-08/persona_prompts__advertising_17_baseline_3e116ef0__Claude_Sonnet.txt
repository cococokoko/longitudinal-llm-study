── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I make my home more energy efficient?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to make your home more energy efficient:

## Quick & Low-Cost Steps
- Switch to LED light bulbs
- Use smart power strips to reduce standby power
- Seal gaps around doors and windows with weatherstripping or caulk
- Set your thermostat a few degrees lower in winter, higher in summer
- Use ceiling fans to reduce heating/cooling needs

## Appliances & Equipment
- Replace old appliances with Energy Star certified models
- Use a programmable or smart thermostat
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Clean refrigerator coils regularly
- Run dishwashers and washing machines with full loads

## Insulation & Building
- Add insulation to attic, walls, and basement
- Install double or triple-pane windows
- Insulate hot water pipes and water heater

## Bigger Investments
- Install solar panels
- Upgrade to a heat pump for heating/cooling
- Replace an old water heater with a tankless or heat pump model
- Consider an energy audit to identify your biggest opportunities

## Habits
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Use natural light when possible
- Air-dry dishes and clothes when practical

**A professional energy audit** can be a great starting point - it identifies exactly where your home is losing energy and helps prioritize improvements.
