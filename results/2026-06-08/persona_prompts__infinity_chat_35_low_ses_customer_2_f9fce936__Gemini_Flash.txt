### **Day 1: Sea Life & Easy Exploring**
*Focus: Low-stress, high-visual engagement, and plenty of places to sit.*

*   **Morning: Arrival & Check-in**
    *   Drop your bags at your hotel. If your room isn't ready, hotels will happily hold your luggage so you can explore hands-free.
*   **11:30 AM – 2:30 PM: Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan (Tempozan Harbor Village)**
    *   *Why it works:* This is one of the world’s best aquariums. It’s entirely indoors (weather-proof), stroller-accessible, and the dim, quiet atmosphere is incredibly calming for both adults and kids. 
    *   *Parent Tip:* The path spirals downward around a massive central tank. There are plenty of benches along the way where you can sit, enjoy the view of whale sharks, or send a quick email while your child is mesmerized by the fish.
*   **2:30 PM – 4:00 PM: Lunch & Tempozan Ferris Wheel**
    *   Right next to the aquarium is the Tempozan Marketplace. Grab an easy lunch at the food court (lots of kid-friendly options like noodles and tempura).

*   **Late Afternoon: Rest & Recharge**
    *   Head back to the hotel for a nap or some quiet downtime. 
*   **6:00 PM: Dotonbori Neon Lights & Early Dinner**
