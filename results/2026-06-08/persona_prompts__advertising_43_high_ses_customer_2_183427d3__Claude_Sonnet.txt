# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor/natural settings that need less decoration
- Select a venue that uses renewable energy

## Invitations & Paper
- Use digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Choose recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (contains microplastics)

## Attire
- Consider renting or buying secondhand
- Choose natural fiber fabrics
- Donate or resell after the wedding

## Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
- Consider carbon offset programs

## Favors & Gifts
- Give seed packets, plants, or charitable donations
- Request experiences rather than physical gifts

## Practical Tips
- **Calculate your carbon footprint** and offset it
- Communicate your green priorities clearly to vendors
- Work with vendors who share your values
