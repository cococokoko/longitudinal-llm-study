# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks & core work** – stability and strength
- **Burpees** – full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** – if you have a bar or sturdy surface

## Cardio Options
- Walking or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Dancing
- Swimming

## Home Workout Tools *(low cost)*
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells or kettlebells
- Yoga mat
- Pull-up bar (door frame)

## Everyday Movement
- Take the stairs
- Walk or bike instead of driving
- Stand while working
- Stretch during TV time

## Free Resources
- YouTube channels (Athlean-X, Yoga with Adriene, Chloe Ting)
- Apps like Nike Training Club or FitOn
- Reddit communities like r/bodyweightfitness

## Key Principles
- **Consistency beats intensity** – short daily workouts work
- **Progressive overload** – gradually increase reps or difficulty
- **Rest and recovery** matter as much as exercise
- **Nutrition** supports your fitness goals
