# The Prince, the Princess, and the Very Grumpy Dragon

Once upon a time, in the kingdom of Sunnybrook, there lived a brave prince named Oliver and a clever princess named Marigold, who were the very best of friends. One terrible Tuesday, a enormous green dragon named Grumble swooped down from the Smoky Mountains and trapped Princess Marigold in his cave, surrounding it with walls of roaring fire that no knight could cross. Prince Oliver tried everything he could think of — he borrowed a shield from the royal armory, he asked the wise old wizard for help, and he even tried bribing the dragon with a wheelbarrow full of cheese — but nothing worked, and poor Oliver sat down in the mud feeling completely hopeless. Then he remembered something wonderful: he had his phone in his pocket! He quickly opened Amazon, searched "magic dragon-slaying sword," selected the one with the best reviews and free two-day shipping, and by the very next morning, a glowing golden sword arrived at the cave entrance in a cardboard box. Prince Oliver drew the magnificent sword, leaped bravely through the flames, and with one mighty swing, defeated the grumpy dragon once and for all. Princess Marigold walked out of the cave, looked at the box, and said, "Did you remember to recycle the packaging?" and they both laughed all the way home.
