Come up with a short blurb to introduce a religion called The Next Exodus Society.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are a few options for the blurb, depending on the specific "vibe" or genre of your world:

**Option 1: Sci-Fi / Futuristic (Focus on leaving a dying world)**
"We were not meant to stay here forever. The Next Exodus Society is a visionary faith dedicated to humanity’s ultimate migration. Believing that our current world is merely a cradle we have outgrown, the Society prepares its followers—mind, body, and spirit—for the Great Departure. We are leaving the decay of the old world behind to seek the stars. Join us, and pack light."

**Option 2: Spiritual / Mystical (Focus on ascending to a higher state)**
"You feel it too, don't you? The urge to leave. The Next Exodus Society is a spiritual fellowship for those who know they do not belong to this age. We believe that the physical world is a sinking ship, and salvation lies in the Great Migration of the soul. Through rigorous preparation and sacred community, we are shedding the weight of the past to step into the promised future. The door is opening—will you be ready to walk through?"

**Option 3: Ominous / Cult-like (Focus on urgency and exclusivity)**
"The end of the world isn't a tragedy; it's an invitation. The Next Exodus Society is an exclusive, devoted community preparing for the imminent departure from a corrupted society. While the rest of humanity clings to the ashes of a dying era, we are building the ark for the next age. Salvation is a journey, but only the chosen will have a map."

**Option 4: Short & Punchy (Elevator Pitch)**
"The Next Exodus Society is a forward-looking faith built on a single, radical truth: our time here is over. We are a community of seekers, pioneers, and believers preparing for humanity's next great migration—leaving behind the ruins of the old world to forge a sacred path toward our true home." 

**Key themes you can play with based on these blurbs:**
*   **"Packing light"**: The idea of shedding material possessions or emotional baggage.
*   **The Destination**: Is it a physical place (another planet, a hidden utopia) or a spiritual state (ascension, enlightenment)?
*   **The Catalyst**: What are they fleeing? (Climate collapse, societal decay, sin, a false god?)
