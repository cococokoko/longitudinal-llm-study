## Leverage What You Already Know

**Recognize individual strengths and needs** - just like you likely do with family members of different ages and personalities.

- **Check in individually**, not just in group settings - people share more one-on-one
- **Understand what motivates each person** - some want recognition, others want autonomy or growth opportunities
