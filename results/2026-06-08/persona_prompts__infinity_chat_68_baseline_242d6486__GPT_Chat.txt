── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Give me a tip for managing a team of coworkers.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One effective tip is to make expectations explicit and revisit them regularly.

People often struggle not because they lack ability, but because they have different assumptions about priorities, deadlines, or quality standards. To reduce confusion:

- Clearly define goals, responsibilities, and deadlines.
- Explain what success looks like for each task or project.
- Check in regularly to identify obstacles early.
- Encourage team members to ask questions and raise concerns.
- Summarize decisions and action items after meetings so everyone leaves with the same understanding.

Consistent communication helps prevent misunderstandings and makes it easier for a team to work together effectively.
