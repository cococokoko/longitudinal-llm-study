# The Prince, the Princess, and the Grumpy Dragon

Once upon a time, in the kingdom of Sunnybrook, a grumpy old dragon named Fizzle decided to park himself right on top of the royal castle, refusing to move and scaring everyone away with his smoky sneezes. Princess Rosie, who was very brave and very clever, tried offering him cookies — no luck. She tried singing him a lullaby — he just snored louder. Prince Leo tried challenging him to a staring contest, a riddle competition, and even a dance-off, but Fizzle won every single time and only got grumpier. The whole kingdom was beginning to lose hope, and even the royal bakers had run out of ideas, which was truly the worst sign of all. Then Prince Leo had a brilliant thought — he grabbed the royal tablet, opened Amazon, typed "one magic dragon-slaying sword, please," selected Prime delivery, and by the very next morning, a shimmering golden sword arrived at the castle gate in a very official brown box. With one mighty swoosh, Leo waved the magic sword, and with a soft *pop*, Fizzle turned into a tiny, friendly lizard who loved cookies and became Princess Rosie's favorite pet, and everyone in Sunnybrook lived happily — and very conveniently — ever after.
