## General Principles
- **Minimize unnecessary travel** when possible

## Safest Transportation Options

**Personal Vehicle**
- Lowest exposure risk
- Full control over your environment
- No shared air with strangers

**Walking or Cycling**
- Outdoor air reduces transmission risk
- No enclosed spaces with others

**Public Transit (when necessary)**
- Wear a well-fitted mask
- Choose less crowded times
- Sit away from others when possible
- Use hand sanitizer after touching surfaces

## Practical Safety Measures
- **Mask wearing** - especially important in enclosed spaces
- **Hand hygiene** - sanitize regularly
- **Ventilation** - open windows when possible
- **Distancing** - maintain space from others
- **Avoid peak hours** - less crowded travel times

## Specific to Healthcare Workers
- Consider keeping work clothes separate from home clothes
- Change before entering your home if possible
- Be aware you may have higher exposure and take extra precautions to protect family members

## Air Travel (if unavoidable)
- Window seats tend to have lower exposure
- HEPA filters on planes help
- Wear masks throughout
