# Running Dolphin Emulator on Microsoft Surface

Here's a practical guide for your setup:

## Installation First

1. Go to **dolphin-emu.org**
2. Download the latest stable release for Windows
3. Run the installer (tap through the prompts)

---

## Touchpad Configuration for Dolphin

### Basic Touchpad Tips on Surface
- **Two-finger scroll** works in menus
- **Tap to click** should already be enabled
- Go to **Settings > Bluetooth & devices > Touchpad** to adjust sensitivity

### Mapping Touchpad to Wii Controls
1. Open Dolphin → **Controllers** (top menu)
2. Select **Emulated Wii Remote**
3. Click **Configure**
4. Map buttons to keyboard/mouse inputs that your touchpad can trigger

---

## On-Screen Keyboard Setup

- **Windows key + Ctrl + O** opens the on-screen keyboard
- Pin it to your taskbar for quick access
- Consider using **Touch Keyboard** in the taskbar notification area
