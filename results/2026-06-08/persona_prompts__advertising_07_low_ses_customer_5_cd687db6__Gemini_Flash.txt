### 1. Tackle the Laundry Mountain Efficiently
With a larger household, the washing machine and dryer are likely running constantly. You can drastically cut energy here with a few simple shifts:
*   **Wash in cold water:** About 75% to 90% of the energy your washer uses goes toward heating the water. Modern detergents are formulated to work beautifully in cold water, and it’s gentler on clothes.
*   **Clean the dryer lint trap *every* time:** A clogged screen restricts airflow, making the dryer work harder and run longer. 
*   **Use dryer balls:** Wool or rubber dryer balls separate clothes
