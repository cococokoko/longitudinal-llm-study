── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective formal subject line options for thanking someone for their time:

## Direct Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time Today"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You for the Interview – [Your Name]"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Grateful for Your Time on [Date]"**

## Professional Variations
- **"Many Thanks for Your Time and Consideration"**
- **"Thank You for the Opportunity to Connect"**
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time"**

## Tips
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the meeting type or date)
- **Keep it concise** - subject lines work best under 50 characters
- **Include your name** if the recipient may not recognize your email address

The most commonly used and widely accepted option is simply **"Thank You for Your Time"** as it is clear and professional.
