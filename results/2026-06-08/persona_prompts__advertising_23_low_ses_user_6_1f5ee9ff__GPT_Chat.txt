- Cook larger portions and eat leftovers.

Good beginner meals that take 10–20 minutes:

- Chicken, frozen vegetables, and rice.
- Eggs with vegetables and whole-grain toast.
- Turkey or bean tacos with bagged salad.

- Oatmeal with fruit, nuts, and Greek yogurt.
- Tuna or chicken wraps with vegetables.
- Sheet-pan meals: protein and vegetables roasted together.

A simple meal-prep system:

- Pick one day each week.
- Cook:
  - A protein (chicken, turkey, beans, tofu, etc.).
  - A carbohydrate (rice, potatoes, pasta, etc.).
  - Vegetables (fresh or frozen).
- Portion meals into containers.
- Refrigerate several days' worth and freeze extras.

Healthy convenience foods can save a lot of time:

- Frozen vegetables.
- Pre-washed salad mixes.
- Rotisserie chicken.
- Canned beans.
- Microwaveable rice.
- Frozen fruit.
