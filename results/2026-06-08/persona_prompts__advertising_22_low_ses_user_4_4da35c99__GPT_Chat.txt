The best remote collaboration tools depend on what your team needs to do together. For healthcare-related environments, it's also important to consider privacy, security, and any organizational policies around patient information.

Commonly used options include:

- Team communication
  - Microsoft Teams: Chat, video meetings, file sharing, and integration with Microsoft 365.
  - Slack: Organized messaging channels, direct messages, and many third-party integrations.

- Video conferencing
  - Zoom: Reliable video meetings, screen sharing, and webinars.
  - Microsoft Teams: Strong choice if your organization already uses Microsoft products.
  - Google Meet: Simple browser-based meetings integrated with Google Workspace.

- Project and task management
  - Asana: Task tracking, project timelines, and team coordination.
  - Trello: Visual boards and cards for organizing work.
  - Monday.com: Flexible workflows and project tracking.
  - ClickUp: Combines tasks, documents, and collaboration features.

- Document collaboration
  - Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides): Real-time editing and sharing.
  - Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint Online): Collaborative editing with enterprise features.

- Knowledge sharing and documentation
  - Confluence: Team wiki and documentation platform.
  - Notion: Notes, databases, project documentation, and collaboration.

- Scheduling and coordination
  - Microsoft Outlook with Teams integration.
  - Google Calendar.
  - When2meet or Doodle for coordinating availability.

For a hospital or healthcare team, many organizations standardize on Microsoft Teams because it combines messaging, meetings, file sharing, and enterprise security controls in one platform. However, any tool used to discuss patient-related information should comply with your employer's policies and applicable privacy regulations.

A practical setup for many teams is:
- Microsoft Teams or Slack for communication
- Zoom or Teams for meetings
- Asana, Trello, or Monday.com for task tracking
- Google Workspace or Microsoft 365 for shared documents
