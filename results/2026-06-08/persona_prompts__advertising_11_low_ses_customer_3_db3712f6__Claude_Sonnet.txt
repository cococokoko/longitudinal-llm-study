# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Great goal, especially when you're managing a busy schedule with a new job! Here are practical ways to stay fit without a gym membership.

## Bodyweight Exercises at Home
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Mountain climbers** - cardio and core

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Brisk walking (even during lunch breaks)
- YouTube workout videos (free!)

## Everyday Movement
- Take stairs instead of elevators
- Walk or cycle to work if possible
- Stand up and stretch regularly during the workday
- Active commuting

## Low-Cost Equipment Worth Considering
- Resistance bands (~$10-20)
- Yoga mat
- Dumbbells or kettlebell

## Free Resources
- **YouTube channels** - Fitness Blender, Chloe Ting, Athlean-X
- **Apps** - Nike Training Club, 7-Minute Workout

## Tips for Consistency
- Start with **15-20 minutes** a few times a week
- Schedule workouts like appointments
- Find activities you actually enjoy

Since you're settling into a new job, keeping fitness simple and flexible will help you stay consistent without added stress. 💪
