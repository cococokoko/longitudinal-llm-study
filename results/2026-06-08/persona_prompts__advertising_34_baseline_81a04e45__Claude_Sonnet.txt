# Effective Online Teaching Techniques

## Engagement Strategies
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minute chunks work better than long lectures)
- **Use interactive polls and quizzes** (Mentimeter, Kahoot, Poll Everywhere)
- **Ask frequent questions** rather than lecturing continuously
- **Incorporate think-pair-share** activities adapted for breakout rooms

## Communication & Presence
- Keep camera on when possible to build connection
- Use clear, concise language and avoid jargon
- Respond to messages/emails within defined timeframes
- Record sessions for students who can't attend live

## Technical Best Practices
- Test technology before each session
- Have a backup plan for technical failures
- Use good lighting and audio quality
- Share screens effectively with clear visuals

## Assessment & Feedback
- Provide **frequent, specific feedback** rather than waiting for major assignments
- Use varied assessment formats (video submissions, discussions, projects)
- Build in self-assessment opportunities
- Check for understanding regularly

## Building Community
- Create discussion forums for peer interaction
- Use icebreakers and informal check-ins
- Establish clear expectations and routines
- Offer virtual office hours

## What Research Suggests Works Best
- **Active learning** consistently outperforms passive watching
- **Spaced repetition** improves retention
- **Clear structure** reduces cognitive load
