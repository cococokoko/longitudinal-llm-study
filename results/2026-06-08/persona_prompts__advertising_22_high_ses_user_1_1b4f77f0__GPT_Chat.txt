  - Asana
    - Good balance of usability and structure
    - Useful for research projects, quality-improvement initiatives, and administrative work

    - Highly customizable workflows and dashboards
    - Useful for cross-department coordination
  - Jira
    - Strong for complex projects and technical teams

• Document collaboration
  - Microsoft 365 (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, SharePoint, OneDrive)
    - Real-time co-authoring
    - Enterprise-grade governance
    - Often preferred in healthcare systems
  - Google Workspace
    - Excellent real-time collaboration
    - Simple sharing and editing workflows

• Whiteboarding and brainstorming
  - Miro
    - Virtual whiteboards for planning, workflow mapping, and educational sessions
  - Microsoft Whiteboard
    - Integrates well with Teams

• Knowledge management
  - Confluence
    - Central repository for protocols, project documentation, and team knowledge
  - Notion
    - Flexible workspace combining notes, databases, and documentation

• Single sign-on (SSO) integration
• Audit logging and access controls
