Flights

Accommodation
- Compare hotels, vacation rentals, hostels, and guesthouses.
- Stay slightly outside major tourist areas and use public transit.

Transportation
- Use public transportation instead of taxis or rideshares.
- Walk or rent bikes when practical.
- Compare train, bus, and flight options for intercity travel.
- Book transportation tickets in advance when discounts are available.

Food

- Take advantage of free hotel breakfasts when offered.
- Buy groceries for some meals and snacks.
- Carry a reusable water bottle where tap water is safe.

Activities
- Look for free walking tours, museums, parks, and cultural events.
- Check city tourism websites for discount passes.
- Book attraction tickets online in advance if discounts are available.
- Prioritize the experiences you value most and skip lower-priority expenses.

Planning
