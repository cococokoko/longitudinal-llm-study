## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (shows up in local searches)
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood reach
- Facebook/Instagram local community groups
- Ask satisfied customers for reviews

**Networking**
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Attend community events and farmers markets
- Partner with complementary local businesses
- Word of mouth referrals with incentives

**Visible Presence**
- Yard signs or vehicle wraps
- Flyers in relevant locations
- Sponsor local events or sports teams
- Local bulletin boards

**Digital Local Targeting**
- Run geographically targeted Facebook/Google ads
- Local SEO (include your city in website content)
- Email list of local customers

**What tends to work best depends on:**
- Your specific type of business
- Your target customer age group
- Your budget
- Whether customers find you online vs. in person

**The most consistent advice** is that Google Business Profile and genuine customer reviews are probably the highest return starting points for most local businesses.
