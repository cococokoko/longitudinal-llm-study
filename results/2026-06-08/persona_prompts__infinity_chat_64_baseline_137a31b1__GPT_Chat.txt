TOEFL and IELTS are the two most widely accepted English-language proficiency tests for international students applying to universities in English-speaking countries. The TOEFL iBT is administered entirely on a computer and emphasizes academic English commonly used in university settings, with integrated tasks that combine reading, listening, speaking, and writing skills.
