# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

Here are practical strategies that can make a real difference, especially for a busy household managing multiple people's needs:

## Quick Wins (Low/No Cost)
- **Adjust thermostat settings** - even 2-3 degrees makes a meaningful difference
- **Unplug devices** when not in use (TVs, chargers, gaming consoles draw standby power)
- **Run full loads** in dishwashers and washing machines
- **Use cold water** for laundry when possible
- **Turn off lights** when leaving rooms

## Appliance & Equipment Choices
- Switch to **LED bulbs** throughout the home
- Use **smart power strips** to eliminate phantom loads
- Set **water heater to 120°F**
- Clean refrigerator coils and dryer lint traps regularly

## Bigger Impact Changes
- **Programmable or smart thermostat** - great for households with varied schedules
- **Weatherstripping and sealing drafts** around doors and windows
- **Insulation improvements** where possible

## Managing a Larger Household
- **Establish shared habits** - everyone turning off lights matters more with more people
- **Batch cooking** reduces oven use
- **Coordinate laundry** to run during off-peak hours if your utility offers lower rates

## Tracking Progress
- Review monthly utility bills to spot trends
- Many utilities offer **free energy audits**
