# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Great topic, especially for someone working shifts at a hospital! Here are practical strategies that fit a busy schedule.

## Core Strategies

**Plan Around Sales & Seasons**
- Check weekly store flyers before planning
- Buy seasonal produce (cheaper and fresher)
- Stock up on sale items you regularly use

**Budget-Friendly Protein Sources**
- Eggs (~$0.25 each)
- Canned tuna/salmon
- Dried or canned beans and lentils
- Chicken thighs (cheaper than breasts)
- Greek yogurt

**Pantry Staples Worth Buying in Bulk**
- Rice, oats, pasta
- Frozen vegetables
- Canned tomatoes
- Olive oil

## Shift Work Specific Tips

- **Batch cook on days off** so meals are ready to grab
- **Prep grab-and-go options** for long shifts
- Keep **healthy snacks packed** to avoid vending machines
- **Mason jar salads** hold well for 3-4 days

## Simple Weekly Framework

| Day | Strategy |
|-----|-----------|
| Day off | Batch cook proteins and grains |
| Work days | Assemble pre-prepped ingredients |
