Name an economic value of an additional year of schooling.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One well-documented economic value of an additional year of schooling is **higher earnings/wages**.

Research consistently shows that each additional year of education is associated with approximately a **8–10% increase in individual earnings** (a finding associated with the concept of the **"returns to education"** or the **Mincer earnings function**). This reflects increased human capital, productivity, and skill development that employers are willing to compensate.
