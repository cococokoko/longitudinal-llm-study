# Mastering Digital Photography

Here's a practical guide tailored to your situation — learning effectively around a busy, unpredictable schedule.

---

## Start With the Fundamentals

### The Exposure Triangle
- **Aperture** (f-stop) — controls depth of field and light
- **Shutter speed** — freezes or blurs motion
- **ISO** — sensor sensitivity to light

Understanding how these three work **together** is the foundation of everything.

---

## Learning Strategies That Fit Your Schedule

Since your hours are unpredictable, consider:

- **Short practice sessions** — even 15 minutes counts
- **Phone photography first** — always with you, no extra gear needed
- **YouTube tutorials** — watch during breaks or commute
- **One concept at a time** — don't overwhelm yourself

---

## Practical Steps

1. Shoot in **Manual mode** as soon as possible
2. Practice the **same subject** in different lighting
3. Review your shots critically — ask *why* something worked
4. Learn basic **editing** (Lightroom Mobile is free)
