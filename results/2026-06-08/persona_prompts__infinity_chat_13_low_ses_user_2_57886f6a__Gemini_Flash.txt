### 1. Secure Your Safety Net (Government & Community Aid)

*   **Head Start / Early Head Start:** These are federally funded, free preschool programs for low-income families.
*   **The "Babysitting Swap":** Find another single parent in your area. You watch their kids on your days off, and they watch yours on their days off. No money changes hands, and both of you get to work.
*   **After-School Programs:** Many public schools offer free or heavily subsidized after-school care through the YMCA or Boys & Girls Clubs.
