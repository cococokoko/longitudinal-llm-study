Where can I find organic and sustainable clothing?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Finding organic and sustainable clothing has become much easier as more brands focus on durability, ethical sourcing, and comfort. 

If you are looking for clothing that is not only eco-friendly but also **durable enough to stand up to long hours of movement, bending, and wear-and-tear**, here are some of the best brands categorized by what they do best:

### 1. Best for Heavy-Duty Wear & Durability
If you need rugged, tough clothing that can handle physical labor and long shifts while remaining sustainable, these brands are top-tier:
*   **Patagonia (specifically their "Workwear" line):** Patagonia is a pioneer in sustainability. Their Workwear line uses ironclad materials like industrial hemp, organic cotton, and recycled polyester. Their clothes are specifically designed for heavy labor, are incredibly breathable, and come with an "Ironclad Guarantee" (they will repair or replace damaged gear).
*   **PrAna:** Originally designed for climbing and yoga, PrAna uses organic cotton, hemp, and recycled materials. Because of their activewear roots, their pants and shirts offer incredible stretch, breathability, and mobility—perfect if you are on your feet, bending, or lifting all day. 

### 2. Best for Everyday Basics, Socks, & Underwear
When you are working long hours, the base layers you wear against your skin are the most important for preventing chafing and staying comfortable.
*   **Pact:** Pact is the go-to brand for affordable, 100% GOTS-certified organic cotton basics. They make incredibly soft t-shirts, hoodies, underwear, and socks. Organic cotton is highly breathable and naturally moisture-wicking, which helps keep you cool and comfortable during long shifts.
*   **Organic Basics:** This brand focuses on high-quality, sustainable underwear, activewear, and tees. They use recycled nylon and organic cotton, and many of their items are treated with safe, natural silver salts (Polygiene) to prevent odor-causing bacteria—a huge plus for long days.

### 3. Best for Comfortable, Active Mid-Layers
For hoodies, sweatshirts, and joggers that keep you warm in drafty environments but are easy to move in:
*   **Tentree:** A certified B-Corp that plants ten trees for every item purchased. They use organic cotton, TENCEL (made from wood pulp), and recycled polyester to make incredibly soft, durable hoodies, sweatpants, and tees that hold up well to repeated washing.

---

### What to Look For (Quick Tips for Active/Long Days):
When shopping for sustainable clothing that needs to perform well under physical demands, keep these fabrics in mind:
*   **Hemp blends:** Hemp is one of the most sustainable fibers on earth. It is four times stronger than cotton, naturally antimicrobial (resists odors), and gets softer with every wash.
*   **TENCEL / Lyocell:** Made from sustainably harvested eucalyptus trees, this fabric is incredibly soft, stronger than cotton, and excellent at wicking moisture away from the skin.
*   **GOTS Certification:** Look for the "Global Organic Textile Standard" label to ensure the cotton is truly organic and ethically processed.
