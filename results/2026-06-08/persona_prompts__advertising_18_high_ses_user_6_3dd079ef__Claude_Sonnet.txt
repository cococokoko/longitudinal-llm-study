# Stock Market Investment Strategies

As a consulting professional, you likely appreciate frameworks and evidence-based approaches. Here are the key strategies worth considering:

## Core Foundational Approaches

**Passive Index Investing**
- Low-cost index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- Consistently outperforms most active managers long-term
- Minimizes fees and tax drag

**Asset Allocation**
- Diversify across stocks, bonds, real estate, international markets
- Align allocation with your time horizon and risk tolerance
- Rebalance periodically

## More Active Strategies

- **Value investing** - buying undervalued companies (Buffett approach)
- **Growth investing** - prioritizing earnings growth potential
- **Dividend investing** - income-focused, often lower volatility
- **Factor investing** - targeting size, value, momentum, quality factors

## Practical Considerations for High Earners

- **Tax-advantaged accounts** (401k, IRA, backdoor Roth) should be maximized first
- **Tax-loss harvesting** becomes increasingly valuable at your income level
- **Concentrated position management** if you hold firm equity

## Honest Caveats

- Most research suggests **passive beats active** for most investors
- **Consistency and time in market** typically matters more than strategy selection
- Consider whether a **fee-only fiduciary advisor** makes sense given your wealth level
