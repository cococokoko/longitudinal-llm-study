# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great ways to discover trails that match your needs:

## Online Resources & Apps
- **AllTrails** - Most popular, with reviews, photos, and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed maps and community-contributed trails
- **Gaia GPS** - Great for offline maps and navigation
- **Recreation.gov** - For permits and national park trails

## What to Consider When Choosing a Trail

### Difficulty Factors
- **Distance** - Total mileage round trip
- **Elevation gain** - Often more important than distance
- **Terrain type** - Rocky, forested, exposed ridgeline

### Personal Factors
- Your current fitness level
- Experience with hiking
- Who's coming (kids, dogs, beginners)
- Time available

### Conditions to Check
- Weather forecast
- Trail closures or seasonal access
- Permit requirements
- Current conditions/recent reviews

## Tips for Finding Hidden Gems
- Ask at **local ranger stations**
- Join **local hiking groups** on Facebook or Meetup
- Check **state park websites**
- Talk to people at trailheads

## Safety Basics
- Tell someone your plans
- Bring the **10 essentials**
- Download offline maps
- Check weather before you go
