# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options that are simple and effective:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Mix coconut oil or shea butter with a few drops of essential oil (lavender, rosehip)
- Apply a small amount after cleansing

**Honey Face Mask**
- Raw honey applied directly for 15-20 minutes
- Great for hydration and mild antibacterial properties

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gently exfoliates skin

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado + coconut oil + honey
- Leave on 20-30 minutes before washing

**Scalp Treatment**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar rinse
- Helps balance pH and reduce buildup

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together and pour into small containers

## General Tips

- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean containers** to prevent contamination
- **Refrigerate** products without preservatives
- Start with **small batches**
- Research any essential oils before use (some can irritate skin)
