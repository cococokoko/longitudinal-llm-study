# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

Given your role, you're likely dealing with **high-stakes decisions, constant demands, and significant pressure**. Here are practical strategies:

---

## Immediate Stress Relief
- **Box breathing** (4 counts in, hold, out, hold) - usable in meetings
- Brief walks between back-to-back meetings
- Single-tasking rather than multitasking when possible

---

## Structural Approaches
- **Protect calendar boundaries** - block recovery time, not just work time
- Delegate decisions that don't require your level
- Create clear **"off" signals** at end of day (ritual shutdown routine)

---

## Longer-Term Habits
- Consistent sleep schedule (often the highest ROI intervention)
- Regular exercise - even 20-30 minutes matters
- Limit alcohol, which often increases anxiety
- Therapy or executive coaching - normalize this as a performance tool

---

## Executive-Specific Considerations
- **Loneliness at the top** is real - build peer relationships outside your company
- Separate your **identity from company performance**
- Know your personal stress signals *before* they become crises

---

## Worth Asking Yourself
- Is this stress **acute** (situational) or **chronic** (systemic)?
- Are you managing it or just **tolerating** it?
