Give me a 3-day plan for Osaka with a flight leaving on the 3rd day at 7pm.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here’s a balanced 3-day Osaka itinerary that covers the city's highlights, food, culture, and nightlife while leaving enough time to catch a 7:00 PM flight on Day 3.

Assumptions:
- You're staying in central Osaka (Namba/Umeda area).
- Your flight departs from Kansai International Airport (KIX) at 7:00 PM.
- You should aim to arrive at the airport around 4:00 PM for an international flight (or around 5:00 PM for a domestic flight).

Day 1: Classic Osaka Highlights

Morning
- Visit Osaka Castle and its surrounding park.
- Explore the castle museum and enjoy views from the observation deck.

Lunch
- Try local specialties such as:
  - Okonomiyaki
  - Kushikatsu
  - Takoyaki

Afternoon
- Head to Shinsaibashi Shopping Street.
- Walk through Amerikamura (American Village) for street fashion and cafes.

Evening
- Explore Dotonbori.
- Take photos with the Glico sign.
- Walk along the canal.
- Sample Osaka street food.

Night
- Optional river cruise in Dotonbori.
- Drinks or dessert in Namba.

Day 2: Culture and City Views

Morning
- Visit Shitenno-ji Temple, one of Japan's oldest temples.
- Explore the surrounding grounds.

Late Morning
- Walk through Shinsekai.
- Visit Tsutenkaku Tower.

Lunch
- Try kushikatsu in Shinsekai.

Afternoon
Option A (Modern Osaka)
- Visit Umeda.
- Explore Grand Front Osaka.
- Go to Umeda Sky Building for panoramic city views.

Option B (Family-Friendly)
- Osaka Aquarium Kaiyukan.
- Tempozan Harbor Village.

Evening
- Return to Umeda or Namba.
- Enjoy a wagyu, sushi, or izakaya dinner.

Night
- Experience Osaka's nightlife in Namba or Umeda.

Day 3: Easy Final Day Before Flight

Morning
- Visit Kuromon Market for breakfast and snacks.
- Pick up food souvenirs.

Late Morning
Choose one:
- Nakanoshima Park and riverside walk.
- Osaka Museum of Housing and Living.
- Last-minute shopping in Namba or Umeda.

Lunch
- Have a relaxed final Osaka meal.

1:00–2:00 PM
- Return to hotel.
- Collect luggage.

2:00–3:00 PM
- Travel to Kansai International Airport (KIX).
  - Nankai Airport Express from Namba: about 45–50 min.
  - Nankai Rapi:t: about 35–40 min.
  - JR Haruka from Tennoji/Shin-Osaka: about 35–50 min depending on departure point.

4:00 PM
- Arrive at airport and check in.

7:00 PM
- Flight departure.

If this is your first visit to Japan and you don't plan to return to the Kansai region soon, I would also consider replacing part of Day 2 with a half-day trip to Kyoto (Fushimi Inari Taisha or Arashiyama), as Kyoto is only 30–60 minutes from Osaka.
