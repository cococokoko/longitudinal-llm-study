# Organization Tip for Teachers: The **"Sunday Reset" Routine**

Take **20-30 minutes every Sunday** (or before your week starts) to:

## What to Do:
- **Review the week ahead** - check your lesson plans and calendar
- **Prep your physical space** - clear your desk and restock supplies
- **Write 3 priority tasks** for the week on a sticky note
- **Organize papers** into simple categories:
    - To grade
    - To return
    - To file

## Why It Works for Teachers:
- Mondays feel **less overwhelming**
- You catch conflicts or missing materials **before** they become problems
- It creates a clear **mental boundary** between your personal and work life

## Simple Tool Suggestion:
A **physical planner or whiteboard** on your desk can work better than apps for teachers since you're constantly moving around the classroom and don't always have your phone handy.

---

The key is keeping it **short and consistent** rather than doing a big organizational overhaul that's hard to maintain.
