── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Where can I find unique handmade gifts?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some great places to find unique handmade gifts:

## Online Marketplaces
- **Etsy** - probably the most popular platform for handmade items
- **Amazon Handmade** - similar to Etsy but on Amazon
- **ArtFire** - focused on handmade and vintage items
- **Uncommon Goods** - curated handmade and unique products

## Local Options
- **Craft fairs and markets** - seasonal events in most communities
- **Farmers markets** - often include local artisans
- **Local boutiques** - many carry locally made items
- **Art galleries** - sometimes sell functional handmade pieces

## Specialty Sources
- **Museum gift shops** - often carry unique artisan items
- **Cultural festivals** - great for internationally inspired crafts
- **Church or school bazaars** - community-made goods

## Social Media
- **Instagram** - many independent artists sell directly
- **Facebook Marketplace** - local artisans sometimes list here
- **Pinterest** - can lead you to independent makers

## Tips for Finding Quality Items
- Read seller reviews carefully
- Ask about materials and process
- Look for makers who specialize in one craft
- Consider **custom or personalized** orders for extra uniqueness
