# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great ways to travel more sustainably:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Walking or cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Public transit** - buses, trains, and subways significantly reduce per-person emissions
- **Rail travel** - trains are much cleaner than flying for medium distances
- **Carpooling** - sharing rides cuts emissions per passenger

## If You Need to Drive
- **Electric or hybrid vehicles**
- **Fuel-efficient car rentals**
- **EV charging networks** are expanding rapidly

## Air Travel Considerations
- **Choose direct flights** - takeoff and landing use the most fuel
- **Fly economy class** - smaller carbon footprint per passenger
- **Carbon offset programs** - though imperfect, they help
- **Consider if the trip is necessary**

## Accommodation Choices
- **Eco-certified hotels**
- **Locally owned guesthouses**
- **Places with green energy and water conservation**

## General Tips
- **Pack light** - less weight means less fuel
- **Support local businesses** at your destination
- **Avoid single-use plastics** while traveling
- **Research destinations** with strong environmental policies

## Planning Resources
- Rome2Rio for comparing transport emissions
- Google Maps now shows CO₂ estimates for routes

Would you like more detail on any specific aspect of eco-friendly travel?
