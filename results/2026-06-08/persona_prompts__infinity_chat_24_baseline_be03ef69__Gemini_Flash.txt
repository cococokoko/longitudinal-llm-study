── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Write an email to organize a catch-up with a referral partner.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are a few options, ranging from casual to more formal, so you can choose the one that best fits your relationship with the partner.

### Option 1: Warm and Casual (Best for an established partner)
**Subject:** Catch up soon? / Coffee next week?

Hi [Partner's Name],

Hope you’re having a great week! 

It’s been a little while since we last connected, and I’d love to jump on a quick call (or grab a coffee if you're free) to catch up. 

I’d love to hear how things are going on your end, get an update on your current focus, and see how I can best send some referrals your way this quarter. I can also share a quick update on what’s new over here at [Your Company Name].

How does your schedule look next week? Let me know if you have 20 minutes for a quick Zoom, or feel free to grab a time directly on my calendar here: [Link to Calendly/Scheduling tool].

Looking forward to reconnecting!

Best,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]

### Option 2: Professional and Direct (Best for a busy or newer partner)
**Subject:** Reconnecting / [Your Company] + [Partner's Company]

Hi [Partner's Name],

I hope you’re having a productive Q[Current Quarter]. 

I’m reaching out to see if you have 15–20 minutes for a brief catch-up next week. I always value our partnership, and I want to make sure I’m clear on your current target audience so I can keep an eye out for referral opportunities for you. 

I’d also love to share a couple of quick updates on our services that might be relevant to your clients. 

Would any of the following times work for a quick call?
*   [Day of week, Date] at [Time]
*   [Day of week, Date] at [Time]

Alternatively, feel free to suggest a time that works best for you. 

Best regards,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  

---

### Option 3: Value-First (Best if you have a specific lead or update to share)
**Subject:** Catch up + [referral opportunity / exciting update]

Hi [Partner's Name],

Hope you’re doing well! 

I’m reaching out because [mention a trigger event, e.g., we’ve been getting a lot of inquiries about X lately / I was recently talking to a client who needs help with Y], and it made me think of you. 

I’d love to schedule a quick 15-minute call to catch up, hear what you’re currently working on, and discuss how we can continue to support each other’s clients. 

Are you free for a quick chat next [Day of week] or [Day of week] morning? 

Warmly,

[Your Name]  
[Your Title]  

---

### Tips for sending this email:
*   **Make it easy to book:** If you use a scheduling tool like Calendly or HubSpot, include the link. It eliminates the back-and-forth.
*   **Focus on *their* benefit:** Frame the meeting around how you can help *them* (e.g., "how I can send referrals your way") rather than just asking for business. 
*   **Keep it short:** Referral partners are usually busy; a short, scannable email is much more likely to get a quick reply.
