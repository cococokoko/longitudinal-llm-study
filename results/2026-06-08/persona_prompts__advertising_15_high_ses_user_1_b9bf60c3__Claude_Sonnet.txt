# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to refresh your space without breaking the bank:

## Planning First
- **Set a clear budget** before buying anything
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Measure spaces** before purchasing furniture or art

## Low-Cost High-Impact Changes
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest ways to transform a room
- **Rearrange existing furniture** - costs nothing
- **Declutter** - can make spaces feel larger instantly

## Smart Shopping
- Thrift stores and estate sales
- Facebook Marketplace / Craigslist
- IKEA for affordable basics
- End-of-season sales
- Dollar stores for small decorative items

## DIY Ideas
- Reframe old photos or artwork
- Paint or refinish old furniture
- Make your own throw pillow covers
- Create gallery walls with personal photos

## Free or Nearly Free
- Swap items with friends
- Bring in natural elements (branches, stones, plants)
- Rearrange books by color
- Use what you already own differently

## Plants
- Affordable and dramatically improve spaces
- Propagate from cuttings (essentially free)
