── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to learn quick healthy cooking:

## Build Core Skills First
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Practice knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand simple flavor combinations

## Start Simple
- Begin with 3-5 ingredient recipes
- Master a few reliable meals before expanding
- Focus on meals you actually enjoy eating

## Use Good Resources
- YouTube channels (Joshua Weissman, Ethan Chlebowski cover healthy/efficient cooking)
- Apps like Mealime or Yummly for guided recipes
- Books like *Salt Fat Acid Heat* for understanding principles

## Save Time Strategically
- **Meal prep** on weekends - batch cook grains, proteins, vegetables
- Keep a stocked pantry (canned beans, frozen vegetables, whole grains)
- Learn one-pan and sheet pan meals
- Use a slow cooker or Instant Pot

## Practical Habits
- Plan meals for the week ahead
- Shop with a list
- Keep healthy staples on hand for quick assembly

## What Makes Meals Healthier
- More vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains
- Cooking at home generally beats most takeout
- Simple seasoning beats heavy sauces

**The biggest factor is just practicing regularly** - even cooking 2-3 times a week builds skills quickly.
