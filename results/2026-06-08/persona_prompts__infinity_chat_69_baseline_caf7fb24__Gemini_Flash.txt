── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
What is the difference between analysis and design? Can one begin to design without analysis? Why? Be concise.

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
**The Difference**
*   **Analysis** focuses on understanding the problem and defining **what** the system must do (requirements and constraints).
*   **Design** focuses on planning the solution and defining **how** the system will do it (architecture and implementation).

**Can you design without analysis?**
No, not effectively. 

**Why?**
You cannot build a correct solution to an undefined problem. Designing without analysis leads to "solving the wrong problem," resulting in wasted resources, missed requirements, and system failure.
