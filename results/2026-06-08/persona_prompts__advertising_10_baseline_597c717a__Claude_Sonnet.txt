# Learning a New Language Effectively

## Core Principles

**Consistency beats intensity** — 20 minutes daily outperforms 3-hour weekend sessions
**Comprehensible input** — exposure slightly above your current level drives acquisition
**Active use accelerates learning** — producing language, not just consuming it

---

## Proven Strategies

### Foundation Stage
- Learn the **most frequent 1,000-2,000 words** first (covers ~80% of everyday speech)
- Master basic sentence structures before complex grammar
- Use **spaced repetition systems** (Anki, Duolingo) for vocabulary retention

### Building Fluency
- **Immersion** — change phone/device language settings
- Listen to podcasts, music, and shows in the target language
- Read graded readers, then native content progressively
- **Speak early** — don't wait until you feel "ready"

### Practice Methods
- Find conversation partners (iTalki, Tandem, HelloTalk)
- Think in the language, not translate from your native tongue
- Keep a journal in the target language
- Shadow native speakers for pronunciation

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Perfectionism that prevents speaking
- Relying only on one app or method
- Ignoring listening and speaking for grammar study
- Inconsistent practice
