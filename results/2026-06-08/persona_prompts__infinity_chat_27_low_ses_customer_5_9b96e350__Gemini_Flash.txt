### **SCENE 1**

**INT. DILBERT’S BUNKER - DAY**

The bunker is made of rusted sheet metal. A single flickering bulb hangs from the ceiling. DILBERT sits at a desk made of a hollowed-out vending machine, typing on a keyboard that isn’t connected to anything.

**MOTHER-IN-LAW (V.O.)**

*(Sighs, rubbing his temples)*
