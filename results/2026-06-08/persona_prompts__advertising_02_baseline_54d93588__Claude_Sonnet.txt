# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** *(optional)* - Hydrating toner with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid serum to boost moisture
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides, shea butter, or glycerin
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (look for moisturizing formulas)

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser as morning
2. **Toner** *(optional)*
3. **Treatment Serum** - Retinol (start slow) or niacinamide
4. **Face Oil** *(optional)* - Rosehip or jojoba oil
5. **Night Cream** - Heavier moisturizer or sleeping mask

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- ✅ Hyaluronic acid
- ✅ Ceramides
- ✅ Glycerin
- ✅ Shea butter
- ✅ Squalane

## Things to Avoid
- ❌ Alcohol-based products
- ❌ Harsh exfoliants
- ❌ Hot water when washing
- ❌ Fragrance (can irritate)

## Extra Tips
- **Drink plenty of water**
- Use a **humidifier** indoors
- Exfoliate **once a week** with a gentle chemical exfoliant (lactic acid)
- Apply moisturizer while skin is **slightly damp**
