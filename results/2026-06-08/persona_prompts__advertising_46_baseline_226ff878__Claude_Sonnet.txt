# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition focus
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical guide with recipes
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - affordable approach

## Websites & Organizations
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based information (Dr. Greger)
- **PCRM.org** (Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine)
- **Oldwayspt.org** - cultural plant-based eating patterns

## Apps
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-based restaurants
- **Forks Over Knives** - meal planning

## Practical Starting Tips
- Start gradually rather than all at once
- Learn about **key nutrients** to monitor (B12, iron, omega-3s, vitamin D)
- Find a few recipes you genuinely enjoy
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian**

## YouTube Channels
- Mic the Vegan
- Physicians Committee (PCRM)

## Worth Knowing
- Quality varies among sources, so **cross-reference claims**
- A registered dietitian can give personalized guidance
