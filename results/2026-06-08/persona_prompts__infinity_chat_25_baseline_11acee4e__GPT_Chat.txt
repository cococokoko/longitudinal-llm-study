── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for the Opportunity
- Thank You for Your Assistance
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Sincere Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You Following Our Meeting
- Thank You for Your Guidance
- Thank You for Your Consideration

If you're writing after an interview or meeting, a more specific subject line is often best, for example:

- Thank You for Meeting with Me Today
- Thank You – [Your Name]
- Thank You for the Interview
- Thank You for Discussing the [Position Name] Role

"Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is one of the most professional and widely accepted options.
