How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective formal subject line options for thanking someone for their time:

## Direct & Professional
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Grateful for Your Time Today"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You for Our [Date] Meeting"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time and Insights"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**

## Slightly More Formal
- **"With Gratitude – [Meeting Topic]"**
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time and Consideration"**

## Tips to Keep in Mind
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the topic or date)
- **Keep it concise** – subject lines work best under 50 characters
- **Avoid vague phrases** like "Just wanted to say..."
- Match the **tone of your relationship** with the recipient

The most straightforward and widely used option is simply:
> **"Thank You for Your Time"**
