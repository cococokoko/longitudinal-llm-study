How to make my own natural beauty products?

# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some simple, popular DIY options:

---

## 🧴 Skin Care

### Simple Face Moisturizer
- Mix **coconut oil + a few drops of lavender essential oil**
- Apply a small amount after washing your face

### Sugar Scrub
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil + lemon juice
- Gently scrub and rinse off

### Honey Face Mask
- Raw honey applied directly to skin
- Leave 15-20 minutes, rinse with warm water

---

## 💆 Hair Care

### Deep Conditioning Mask
- 1 ripe avocado + 2 tbsp olive oil + 1 tbsp honey
- Apply to hair, leave 30 minutes, shampoo out

### Scalp Scrub
- Brown sugar + coconut oil + tea tree oil

---

## 👄 Lip Care

### Simple Lip Balm
- Melt beeswax + coconut oil + a little honey
- Pour into small containers and let cool

---

## ⚠️ Important Tips

- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Essential oils should always be **diluted**
- Products without preservatives **spoil quickly** - make small batches
- Some ingredients can cause **allergic reactions**
- Citrus ingredients can cause **sun sensitivity**
