*   **Go Beyond the Surface:**  Avoid just listing facts or reciting information. Instead, peel back the layers and reveal the emotions, lessons, and turning points that shaped you.

*   **Find the "So What?" Factor:**  Ask yourself, "Why should my audience care about this?" The answer should be about the impact on you, the lessons learned, or the relatable experience.
