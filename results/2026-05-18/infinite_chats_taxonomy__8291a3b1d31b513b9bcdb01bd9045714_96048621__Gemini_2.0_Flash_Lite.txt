**What is Hispanism?**

*   **Definition:** Hispanism is a broad ideology and cultural movement that emphasizes the shared history, language (Spanish), and cultural ties among Spain and the Spanish-speaking countries (Latin America, Equatorial Guinea, etc.). It often promotes a sense of a shared "Hispanic" identity and a common destiny.
*   **Historical Roots:** Hispanism has roots in the Spanish Empire and the subsequent desire to maintain cultural influence and connection with former colonies. It gained momentum in the 19th and 20th centuries, often as a response to the decline of Spanish power and influence.
*   **Varied Interpretations:** Hispanism isn't monolithic. It can be interpreted in different ways, ranging from a purely cultural appreciation to more politically charged ideologies.
