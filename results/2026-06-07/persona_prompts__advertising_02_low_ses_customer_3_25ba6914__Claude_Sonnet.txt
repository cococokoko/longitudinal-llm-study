# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a simple, effective routine to keep dry skin hydrated and healthy:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Use a cream or milk-based cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Hydrating toner** - Look for ingredients like hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - Choose a rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
4. **SPF 30+** - Sun protection is essential daily

## Evening Routine
1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser as morning
2. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or niacinamide serums work well
3. **Rich moisturizer** - Can use a heavier cream at night
4. **Facial oil** (optional) - Rosehip or jojoba oil to seal in moisture

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Shea butter
- Squalane

## Helpful Tips
- **Drink plenty of water** throughout the day
- Use a **humidifier** at home
- Avoid **hot showers** which strip natural oils
- Apply moisturizer while skin is **slightly damp**
- Exfoliate **gently** once a week
