# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great approaches to help you find ideal trails:

## Apps & Websites
- **AllTrails** - Most comprehensive database with reviews and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed trail maps and conditions
- **Gaia GPS** - Great for offline navigation

## Key Factors to Consider

### Difficulty Level
- **Easy** - Flat, well-maintained paths
- **Moderate** - Some elevation gain, longer distances
- **Strenuous** - Steep terrain, technical sections

### Your Priorities
- Scenic views vs. solitude
- Distance and time available
- Elevation gain
- Dog-friendly or family-friendly options

## Tips for Busy Executives
- **Weekday hiking** avoids crowds
- **Early morning starts** offer better weather and solitude
- Look for trails **near business travel destinations**
- Consider **guided experiences** for unfamiliar areas

## Popular Resources
- Local REI stores for regional recommendations
- National Park Service website
- State park websites
- Local hiking clubs and Facebook groups

## Questions to Narrow It Down
- What's your **fitness level**?
- What **region** are you in?
- How much **time** do you have?
- Any **specific scenery** preferences (mountains, coast, forest)?
