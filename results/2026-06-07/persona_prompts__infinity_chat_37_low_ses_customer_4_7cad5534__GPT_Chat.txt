- Versus carpet: Easier to clean and less likely to hold allergens, though typically less soft and quieter underfoot.
- Versus laminate: Can usually be refinished and often has a longer lifespan, but costs more.
- Versus luxury vinyl: Often considered more premium and can add greater resale appeal, though vinyl may offer better water resistance.
- Versus tile: Warmer and more comfortable underfoot, though generally less water-resistant.

Potential drawbacks to keep in mind:
- Higher upfront cost than many alternatives.
- Can be damaged by excessive moisture or standing water.
- May scratch or dent from pets, furniture, or heavy traffic.
