# Learning a New Language Effectively

Here's a practical, evidence-based approach:

## Core Principles

- **Consistency beats intensity** - 20 minutes daily outperforms 3 hours weekly
- **Comprehensible input** is the primary driver of acquisition
- **Active production** accelerates fluency alongside passive exposure

## Proven Methods

### Foundation
- Start with the **most frequent 1,000-2,000 words** (covers ~90% of conversation)
- Use **spaced repetition systems** (Anki, Duolingo) for vocabulary retention
- Learn pronunciation early - harder to correct later

### Intermediate Progress
- **Immersion** - podcasts, TV shows, books in target language
- **iTalki or similar** for conversation practice with native speakers
- **Shadowing technique** - mimicking native speech patterns

### Advanced Fluency
- Think *in* the language rather than translating
- Read native content (news, literature)
- Find communities of native speakers
