Here are some practical ways to find affordable eco-friendly travel destinations:

## Research Strategies
- **Use eco-tourism databases** like the Global Sustainable Tourism Council directory
- **Check certification sites** like Rainforest Alliance or Green Globe for verified destinations
- **Look at lesser-known alternatives** to popular spots (often cheaper and less crowded)

## Destination Types Worth Considering
- National parks and protected areas (often low-cost entry)
- Countries with strong sustainability infrastructure (Costa Rica, Portugal, Slovenia)
- Domestic nature destinations (reduces flight carbon footprint and cost)

## Practical Cost-Saving Approaches
- **Travel in shoulder seasons** - lower prices, smaller crowds, lighter environmental impact
- **Stay in eco-lodges or hostels** with genuine sustainability certifications
- **Combine slow travel** - fewer destinations, longer stays, lower transport costs
- **Use train travel** where available - typically cheaper and greener than flying

## Tools and Resources
- Responsible Travel (responsibletravel.com)
- Workaway or WWOOF for budget-conscious eco-experiences
- Google Flights carbon emissions estimates for comparison

## Worth Noting
"Eco-friendly" marketing varies significantly in credibility - looking for **third-party certifications** rather than self-reported claims helps avoid greenwashing.
