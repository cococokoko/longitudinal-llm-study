# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

Given your role, you're likely dealing with **high-stakes decisions, constant demands, and significant pressure**. Here are practical strategies:

---

## Immediate Stress Relief
- **Box breathing** - 4 counts in, hold, out, hold (quick reset before meetings)
- **Brief walks** - even 10 minutes significantly reduces cortisol
- **Single-tasking** - close tabs, silence notifications for focused blocks

---

## Structural Habits
- **Protect sleep** - non-negotiable, even with demanding schedules
- **Clear boundaries** on availability (evenings, weekends)
- **Delegate deliberately** - holding too much creates chronic stress
- **Regular exercise** - the most evidence-backed anxiety reducer

---

## Executive-Specific Considerations
- **Decision fatigue is real** - reduce low-stakes choices
- **Loneliness at the top** - find peers you can speak honestly with
- **Separate identity from performance** - your worth isn't your company's metrics
- **Therapy or executive coaching** - normalized and genuinely effective

---

## Warning Signs to Take Seriously
- Persistent sleep disruption
- Difficulty concentrating
- Irritability affecting relationships
- Physical symptoms (chest tightness, headaches)
