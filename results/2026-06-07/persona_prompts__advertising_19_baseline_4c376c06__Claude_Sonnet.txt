── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I improve my mental health?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some evidence-based approaches to improving mental health:

## Daily Habits
- **Sleep** - Aim for 7-9 hours consistently
- **Exercise** - Even 20-30 minutes of walking helps significantly
- **Nutrition** - Balanced diet affects mood and energy
- **Limit alcohol** - It's a depressant despite short-term relief

## Mental Practices
- **Mindfulness/meditation** - Reduces stress and anxiety
- **Journaling** - Helps process emotions and thoughts
- **Gratitude practice** - Shifts focus toward positives
- **Limit social media** - Can reduce comparison and anxiety

## Social Connection
- Maintain relationships with supportive people
- Consider community involvement or volunteering
- Be honest with trusted people about how you're feeling

## Professional Support
- **Therapy** (CBT, talk therapy) - Very effective for many conditions
- **Psychiatry** - If medication might help
- **Support groups** - Shared experience can be powerful

## Practical Steps
- Reduce chronic stressors where possible
- Set realistic goals and boundaries
- Spend time in nature
- Engage in meaningful activities

## Important Note
If you're experiencing **serious symptoms** like persistent depression, anxiety, or thoughts of self-harm, please reach out to a mental health professional or crisis line.
