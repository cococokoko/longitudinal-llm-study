# Learning to Play Guitar

Here's a helpful overview to get you started:

## Getting Started
- **Choose the right guitar** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), Classical has nylon strings (easier on fingers), Electric requires an amp but has lighter strings
- **Get essential accessories** - Picks, tuner, capo, extra strings
- **Learn proper posture** - Sitting and holding the guitar correctly prevents injury

## First Things to Learn
1. **Tune your guitar** - Use a clip-on tuner or tuning app (EADGBE)
2. **Basic chords** - Start with Em, Am, E, A, D, G, C
3. **Strumming patterns** - Begin with simple down strokes
4. **Finger placement** - Press close to frets, use fingertips

## Building Skills
- Practice **chord transitions** smoothly
- Learn simple **songs you enjoy**
- Develop **fingerpicking** techniques
- Understand basic **music theory**

## Practice Tips
- **Practice daily** - Even 15-20 minutes beats long occasional sessions
- **Go slow** - Speed comes with accuracy
- **Use a metronome** - Build rhythm early
- **Be patient** - Finger soreness is normal at first

## Resources
- YouTube tutorials (Justin Guitar is highly recommended)
- Apps like Yousician or Fender Play
- Local lessons for personalized feedback
