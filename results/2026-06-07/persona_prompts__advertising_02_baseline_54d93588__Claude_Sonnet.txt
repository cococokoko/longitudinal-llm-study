# Best Skincare Routine for Dry Skin

## Morning Routine

1. **Gentle Cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming/gel cleansers)
2. **Hydrating Toner** - Look for hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid serum works great
4. **Moisturizer** - Rich cream with ceramides or shea butter
5. **Sunscreen** - SPF 30+ (choose a moisturizing formula)

---

## Evening Routine

1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Toner** - Optional but helpful
3. **Treatment Serum** - Niacinamide or peptides
4. **Face Oil** - Rosehip, jojoba, or squalane oil
5. **Night Cream** - Heavier than your day moisturizer

---

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- ✅ Hyaluronic acid
- ✅ Ceramides
- ✅ Glycerin
- ✅ Shea butter
- ✅ Squalane

## Things to Avoid
- ❌ Alcohol-based products
- ❌ Harsh exfoliants
- ❌ Hot water when washing

## Extra Tips
- **Exfoliate** gently 1-2x per week
- **Drink plenty of water**
- Use a **humidifier** indoors
- Apply moisturizer on **slightly damp skin**
