4. Learn a few simple strumming patterns
Examples:
- Down, down, down, down

Keep a steady rhythm rather than trying to play fast.

5. Build finger strength and coordination
Practice:
- Simple finger exercises up and down the fretboard.
- Switching between two chords repeatedly for 1–2 minutes.

6. Learn easy songs

7. Practice consistently
A sample beginner routine:
- 5 minutes: tuning and warm-up
- 10 minutes: chord practice
- 10 minutes: chord changes and strumming
- 5 minutes: play a song

- Pressing too hard on the strings.
- Looking for speed before accuracy.
