  - Recycle according to local guidelines.
  - Compost food scraps if your area supports it.
  - Use reusable bags, bottles, cups, and containers.

- Water use
  - Take shorter showers.
  - Fix leaks promptly.
  - Run dishwashers and washing machines only with full loads.

1. Transportation choices,
2. Reducing food waste and eating more plant-based meals,
3. Lowering home energy use.
