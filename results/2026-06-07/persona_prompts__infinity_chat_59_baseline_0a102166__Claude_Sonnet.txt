# A Tip for Teacher Organization: **Use a Weekly "Brain Dump" Planning Session**

Set aside **15-20 minutes every Friday afternoon** (or Sunday evening) to:

- **Write down everything** on your mind - assignments to grade, emails to send, copies to make, parent contacts needed
- **Sort tasks by day** for the upcoming week
- **Identify your top 3 priorities** so you know what matters most

## Why This Works for Teachers Specifically

- Teaching is mentally demanding and it's easy to **forget small but important tasks**
- It gives you a **clear starting point Monday morning** instead of feeling scattered
- It helps you **spot conflicts early** (like two big assignments due the same day)

## Simple Tools to Try

- A **paper planner** kept on your desk
- A **sticky note system** on your monitor
- A free app like **Google Tasks or Todoist**

---

The biggest benefit is that it **gets tasks out of your head** so you're not mentally carrying them all weekend, which helps you actually rest and recharge.
