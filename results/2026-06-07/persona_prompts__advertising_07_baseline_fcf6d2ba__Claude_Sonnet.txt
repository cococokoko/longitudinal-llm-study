# Effective Ways to Reduce Household Energy Use

## Heating & Cooling (Typically 40-50% of energy bills)
- **Set thermostats strategically** – 68°F when home, lower when sleeping or away
- **Seal air leaks** around windows, doors, and outlets
- **Add insulation** to attics, walls, and basements
- **Use ceiling fans** to circulate air more efficiently
- **Change HVAC filters** regularly (every 1-3 months)

## Appliances & Electronics
- **Unplug devices** when not in use or use smart power strips
- **Run full loads** in dishwashers and washing machines
- **Wash clothes in cold water** – saves significant energy
- **Air dry dishes and clothes** when possible
- **Replace old appliances** with Energy Star certified models

## Lighting
- **Switch to LED bulbs** – use 75% less energy than incandescent
- **Use natural light** during the day
- **Install dimmer switches** and motion sensors

## Water Heating (~18% of energy use)
- **Lower water heater temperature** to 120°F
- **Take shorter showers**
- **Fix leaky faucets** promptly
- **Insulate hot water pipes**

## Smart Habits
- **Use a programmable/smart thermostat**
- **Monitor usage** with an energy monitor
- **Conduct a home energy audit**
