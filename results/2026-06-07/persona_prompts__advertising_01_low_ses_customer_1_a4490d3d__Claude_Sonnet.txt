# Learning to Play Guitar

That's a great hobby to pick up! Here are some tips to get started, especially with a busy schedule:

## Getting Started
- **Choose your guitar type** - Acoustic is great for beginners (no amp needed), electric can be easier on fingers
- **Learn basic chords first** - G, C, D, Em, Am cover hundreds of songs
- **Get a tuner app** - GuitarTuna is free and works well

## Fitting Practice Into a Busy Schedule
Since your time is unpredictable, consider:
- **Short sessions work great** - Even 10-15 minutes daily beats a 2-hour weekend session
- **Keep the guitar visible** - If it's out of the case, you're more likely to pick it up
- **Practice during breaks** - Even mental practice (visualizing chord shapes) helps
- **Use slow days** - When work is lighter, squeeze in longer sessions

## Free Learning Resources
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar is highly recommended for beginners
- **Apps** - Yousician, Fender Play (some free content)
- **Chord charts** - Ultimate Guitar app

## Realistic Expectations
- Fingertip soreness is normal at first
- Most people can play simple songs within **a few weeks**
- Consistency matters more than long sessions
