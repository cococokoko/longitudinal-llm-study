# Starting a Small Online Business

Here are some key areas to focus on:

## Planning
- Define your **niche and target audience**
- Research competitors
- Write a simple business plan
- Set realistic goals and budget

## Business Setup
- Choose a **business structure** (sole proprietor, LLC, etc.)
- Register your business name
- Get necessary licenses/permits
- Open a separate business bank account

## Online Presence
- Choose a platform (Shopify, Etsy, Amazon, your own website)
- Set up **social media accounts**
- Create a simple, professional brand/logo

## Products or Services
- Start with a **focused offering** rather than too many options
- Price competitively but profitably
- Plan your inventory or fulfillment method

## Marketing
- Social media marketing
- Email list building
- Word of mouth
- Consider paid ads once you have some revenue

## Practical Tips
- **Start lean** - don't overspend upfront
- Test your idea before going all-in
- Keep good financial records from day one
- Be patient - most businesses take time to grow
