Here are some well-regarded platforms for freelance writers:

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality blogging opportunities
- **MediaBistro** - Media and journalism focused
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for direct client connections

## Direct Outreach
- Many experienced writers find their best work through **direct pitching** to publications and businesses

- Rates vary significantly across platforms
- Portfolio quality matters considerably
- Some platforms take substantial commission cuts
- Building direct client relationships often yields better long-term results
