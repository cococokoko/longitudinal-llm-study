How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for Your Time Today
- Sincere Thanks for Your Time
- Appreciation for Your Time and Assistance
- Thank You for the Opportunity to Meet
- Thank You for Your Guidance and Time
- With Appreciation for Your Time
- Many Thanks for Your Time

If the email follows a specific meeting or interview, a more specific subject line is often preferable, for example:

- Thank You for Meeting on [Date]
- Thank You for Discussing [Topic]
- Thank You for the Interview
- Thank You for Your Insights on [Subject]

Among these, "Thank You for Your Time and Consideration" is often the most polished and broadly applicable formal option.
