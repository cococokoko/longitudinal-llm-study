## Core Effective Online Teaching Techniques

### Engagement Strategies
- **Break content into shorter segments** (10-15 minutes max for video lessons)
- **Use interactive polls and quizzes** to check understanding regularly
- **Encourage chat participation** so quieter students can contribute
- **Cold calling with warmth** - invite responses rather than demanding them

### Clear Communication
- **Over-communicate expectations** since students can't read body language as easily
- **Provide written summaries** of verbal instructions
- **Use consistent formatting** so students know where to find things

### Technical Foundations
- **Test your setup beforehand** - audio quality matters most
- **Have a backup plan** for technical failures
- **Record sessions** when possible for students who miss class

### Building Community
- **Create small group breakout activities**
- **Establish regular check-ins** with individual students
- **Use discussion boards** for ongoing conversation

### Assessment
- **Vary assessment types** beyond traditional tests
- **Provide timely, specific feedback**
- **Make rubrics transparent** upfront
