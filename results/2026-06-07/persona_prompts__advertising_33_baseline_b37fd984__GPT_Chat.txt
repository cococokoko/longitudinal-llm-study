How to make my own natural beauty products?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Making your own natural beauty products can be simple, affordable, and customizable. The key is to use skin-friendly ingredients, maintain good hygiene, and be aware that "natural" does not automatically mean safe for everyone.

General safety tips:
- Wash and sanitize all containers and tools before use.
- Patch-test new products on a small area of skin for 24–48 hours.
- Be cautious with essential oils, as they can irritate skin and may not be suitable for children, pregnancy, or sensitive skin.
- Products containing water can grow bacteria and mold without preservatives. Make small batches and refrigerate when appropriate.

Here are a few beginner-friendly recipes:

1. Simple Lip Balm
Ingredients:
- 1 tablespoon beeswax pellets
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil
- 1 tablespoon sweet almond oil

Instructions:
- Melt ingredients together using a double boiler.
- Stir well and pour into small containers.
- Allow to cool completely before use.

2. Gentle Sugar Scrub
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup sugar (white or brown)
- 1/4 cup coconut oil or olive oil

Instructions:
- Mix until combined.
- Use gently on damp skin, then rinse.
- Avoid using abrasive scrubs on irritated or acne-prone skin.

3. Oatmeal Face Mask
Ingredients:
- 2 tablespoons finely ground oats
- 1–2 tablespoons plain yogurt or water

Instructions:
- Mix into a paste.
- Apply for 10–15 minutes.
- Rinse with lukewarm water.

4. Basic Body Butter
Ingredients:
- 1/2 cup shea butter
- 2 tablespoons jojoba or almond oil

Instructions:
- Soften or gently melt the shea butter.
- Mix in the oil and whip until fluffy.
- Store in a clean jar.

5. Hair Conditioning Treatment
Ingredients:
- 1 ripe avocado
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:
- Mash thoroughly.
- Apply to damp hair for 15–20 minutes.
- Rinse and shampoo as needed.

6. Rosewater Facial Mist
Ingredients:
- Pure rosewater

Instructions:
- Pour into a clean spray bottle.
- Store according to the manufacturer's recommendations.
- Use as a refreshing facial mist.
