# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some popular DIY options that are straightforward to make:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, cool, and store in a clean jar

**Sugar Scrub**
- Coconut oil + granulated sugar + lemon juice
- Mix and use immediately or store short-term

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (simmer rose petals, strain, cool)

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioner**
- Avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Scalp Treatment**
- Coconut oil + tea tree oil (diluted ~2%)

## Lip Care

**Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + vitamin E oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Important Considerations

- **Preservation** - water-based products need preservatives or refrigeration to prevent microbial growth
- **Patch test** first, especially with essential oils
- **Shelf life** is short compared to commercial products
- **Dilution matters** - essential oils should typically be 1-2% concentration
- **Sanitation** - sterilize all equipment and containers
