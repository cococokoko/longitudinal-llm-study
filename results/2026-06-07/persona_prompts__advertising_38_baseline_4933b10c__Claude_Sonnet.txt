# Best Platforms for Freelance Writers

## General Freelance Marketplaces
- **Upwork** - Large client base, good for long-term contracts
- **Fiverr** - Good for packaged services, beginners can start here
- **Freelancer.com** - Competitive bidding system

## Writing-Specific Platforms
- **Contently** - Higher-end clients, portfolio-focused
- **ClearVoice** - Content marketing focused
- **Scripted** - Vetted writers, steady work
- **Textbroker** - Good for beginners, volume-based

## Content Mills (Lower Pay, Good for Starting Out)
- **iWriter**
- **WriterAccess**
- **Constant Content**

## Job Boards
- **ProBlogger Job Board** - Quality blogging/content jobs
- **MediaBistro** - Media and journalism focused
- **Journalism Jobs** - News and editorial work
- **LinkedIn** - Increasingly useful for direct outreach

## Direct Approaches Worth Considering
- Cold pitching publications directly
- Building your own website/portfolio
- Networking on Twitter/X in writing communities
- Substack (build your own audience)

## Tips for Success
- **Specialize** in a niche to command higher rates
- Build a strong portfolio early
- Upwork and ProBlogger tend to have the best quality-to-effort ratio for most writers
