# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

Here are some great ways to travel more sustainably:

## Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Train travel** - significantly lower emissions than flying
- **Cycling** - zero emissions, great for shorter distances
- **Walking** - the most sustainable option locally
- **Electric vehicles** - reduced emissions, especially with renewable charging
- **Carpooling/ridesharing** - reduces per-person emissions

## If You Must Fly
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Pack **light** to reduce fuel consumption

## Accommodation Choices
- Look for **eco-certified hotels**
- Consider **locally-owned guesthouses**
- Choose places with **solar power or green energy**
- Avoid excessive towel/linen changes

## At Your Destination
- Use **public transportation**
- Rent **bikes or e-scooters**
- Support **local businesses**
- Respect natural environments

## Planning Tips
- Travel **slower and longer** rather than multiple short trips
- Choose **closer destinations**
- Travel **off-season** to reduce overtourism pressure

## Useful Resources
- **Rome2Rio** - compares transport emissions
- **Atmosfair** - carbon footprint calculator
