That's a great hobby to pick up! Here are some practical tips to get started:

## Getting Started

- **Choose the right guitar** - acoustic is often recommended for beginners (no amp needed)
- **Learn basic chords first** - G, C, D, Em, Am cover hundreds of songs
- **Get a tuner** - a clip-on tuner or free app works well

## Building Skills

- **Practice consistently** - 15-20 minutes daily beats 2 hours once a week
- **Start slow** - accuracy before speed
- **Learn to read chord charts** - simpler than sheet music to start

## Resources Worth Considering

- **Apps** - Yousician, Fender Play, or Justin Guitar (free and highly rated)
- **YouTube** - Justin Guitar's free lessons are excellent
- **Local teacher** - fastest way to build correct technique and avoid bad habits

## Common Beginner Challenges

- **Fingertip soreness** - normal, builds calluses in a few weeks
- **Chord transitions** - the main hurdle; just takes repetition
- **Staying motivated** - learn songs you actually like early on

## Realistic Timeline

Most people can play simple songs within **4-6 weeks** with regular practice.

Is there a particular style you're drawn to - rock, acoustic, classical? That might shape the best approach for you.
