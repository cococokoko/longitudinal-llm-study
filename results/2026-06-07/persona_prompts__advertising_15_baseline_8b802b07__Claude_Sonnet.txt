# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Start with What You Have
- **Rearrange furniture** for a fresh layout
- **Declutter** to make spaces feel larger and cleaner
- **Repurpose items** from other rooms

---

## Low-Cost Updates with Big Impact

### Paint
- One of the **most affordable transformations**
- Try an accent wall instead of painting entire rooms
- Paint old furniture for a new look

### Lighting
- Swap outdated fixtures for modern ones
- Add floor or table lamps for warmth
- Try smart bulbs to change ambiance

### Textiles
- New throw pillows, blankets, or curtains
- Layer rugs for texture and color
- Slipcovers can refresh old sofas

---

## Smart Shopping Tips
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** for unique finds
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** for furniture deals
- **Dollar stores** for seasonal decor
- **DIY projects** using YouTube tutorials
- Wait for **seasonal sales**

---

## Free or Nearly Free Ideas
- Display books, plants, or collected items
- Frame artwork, photos, or fabric
- Forage natural elements like branches or pinecones
- Swap decor with friends

- **Gallery walls** - personal and inexpensive
- **Plants** - add life and color affordably
