# CUBICLE OF THE DAMNED
### A Play in Three Acts
*In the Style of Scott Adams' Dilbert, Set During the Apocalypse*

---

## CAST OF CHARACTERS

**DILBERT** - An engineer trying to optimize survival in a world that has ended. Still wearing his tie, which has curled upward.

**DOGBERT** - A small dog with delusions of godhood, now arguably correct about the godhood part.

**THE POINTY-HAIRED BOSS (PHB)** - Somehow still managing people despite civilization's collapse. Has not noticed the collapse.

**WALLY** - Has been preparing for the apocalypse his entire career by doing nothing. Thriving.

**ALICE** - Still the most competent person alive. Furious about it.

**RATBERT** - A rat. Now considered upper management relative to the remaining population.

**CAROL** - PHB's assistant. Gatekeeping the last bunker on Earth.

**TED** - Background character. Still gets laid off despite being one of twelve remaining humans.

---

## ACT ONE: THE MORNING STANDUP

*SETTING: A partially collapsed open-plan office. The ceiling has caved in on one side. A mushroom cloud is visible through the broken window. DILBERT sits at his desk, which is inexplicably still standing. He is reviewing a spreadsheet. WALLY sits across from him holding a cup of coffee that is somehow still hot and fresh.*

---
