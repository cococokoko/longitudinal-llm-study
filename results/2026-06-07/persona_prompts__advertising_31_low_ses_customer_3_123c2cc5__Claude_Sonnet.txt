## Destination Strategies
- **Choose less-visited places** - often cheaper and lower environmental impact
- **Travel during shoulder seasons** - lower prices, fewer crowds
- **Consider nearby destinations** - reduces carbon footprint and transportation costs
- **Look at developing countries** with strong conservation programs

## Budget-Friendly Eco Options
- Volunteer programs that include accommodation
- Eco-hostels and community-run guesthouses
- National parks and protected areas
- Camping in designated areas

## Practical Tips
- **Compare carbon costs** alongside financial costs
- **Use public transportation** at destinations
- **Stay longer in fewer places** rather than hopping around
- **Cook some meals** to support local markets

## Affordable Eco-Friendly Regions to Consider
- Southeast Asia (Vietnam, Thailand)
- Central America (Costa Rica, Guatemala)
- Eastern Europe
- Parts of South America

## Money-Saving Approaches
- Book accommodations directly with small eco-lodges
- Travel with a small group to share costs
- Look for work-exchange programs
