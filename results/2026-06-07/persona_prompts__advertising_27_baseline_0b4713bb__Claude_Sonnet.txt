── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint:

## Food Choices
- Eat less meat, especially beef (try even 1-2 meatless days/week)
- Buy local and seasonal produce when possible
- Reduce food waste by planning meals and using leftovers
- Compost food scraps

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Consider carpooling
- If buying a car, consider electric or hybrid

## Home Energy
- Adjust thermostat a few degrees (lower in winter, higher in summer)
- Switch to LED bulbs
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Wash clothes in cold water
- Air dry laundry when possible

## Shopping
- Buy less overall
- Choose secondhand/thrifted items
- Pick products with less packaging
- Keep electronics and appliances longer before replacing

## High-Impact Actions Worth Considering
- Fly less (flights are significant contributors)
- Switch to renewable energy if available
- Insulate your home better

## Realistic Perspective
- **Focus on your biggest sources first** - transportation and diet typically matter most
- Small consistent changes add up
- You don't need to be perfect
