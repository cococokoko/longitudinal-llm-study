How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective formal subject line options for thanking someone for their time:

## Direct & Professional
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time and Insights"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You – [Meeting Topic/Date]"**
- **"Grateful for Your Time Regarding [Subject]"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**

## Slightly More Formal
- **"With Gratitude for Your Time and Consideration"**
- **"Sincere Thanks for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Your Valuable Time"**

## Tips to Keep in Mind
- **Keep it concise** – subject lines are most effective under 60 characters
- **Be specific** when possible, referencing the meeting or topic
- **Avoid overly elaborate phrasing** in the subject line, saving detail for the body
- **Capitalize appropriately** for a polished, professional appearance
