# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, popular DIY recipes to get you started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, olive)
- Beeswax or candelilla wax
- Shea butter or cocoa butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, peppermint)
- Aloe vera gel
- Apple cider vinegar
- Honey
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds (exfoliants)

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Basic Moisturizer
- 2 tbsp shea butter
- 1 tbsp jojoba oil
- 5 drops lavender essential oil
- Melt, mix, cool, and store in a jar

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 1 tbsp beeswax
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tsp honey
- Melt together, pour into small containers

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- ½ cup sugar
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil

### 🧖 Face Mask
- 2 tbsp oats
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp plain yogurt
- Mix and apply for 15 minutes

---

## Important Tips
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- Use **clean, sterilized containers**
- Products **without preservatives** have short shelf life (1-4 weeks)
- Research essential oil **safety and dilution rates**
- Some ingredients can cause **allergic reactions**
