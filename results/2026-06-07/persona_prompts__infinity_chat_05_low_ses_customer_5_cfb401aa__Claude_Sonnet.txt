# Smartwatch Feature for Senior Citizens: **Family Circle Health Dashboard**

## Core Concept
A **shared health monitoring system** that connects seniors with their extended family network, recognizing that multiple family members often share caregiving responsibilities.

---

## Key Capabilities

### For the Senior
- **Simple, large-display interface** with minimal buttons
- Automatic health tracking (heart rate, falls, activity, sleep)
- **One-touch "I'm okay" check-in** button family members can request
- Medication reminder alerts with confirmation

### For the Family Network
- **Multiple caregiver access** - not just one contact, but the whole family
- Customizable permission levels (who sees what information)
- **Shared alert system** so one family member isn't solely responsible
- Group notification when something needs attention

---

## Standout Feature: **Responsibility Rotation**
Since family caregiving is often shared, the app allows families to:
- Schedule who is the "primary contact" each week
- Automatically route alerts to whoever is designated
- Prevent alert fatigue for any single person

---

## Why This Matters
> Caregiving rarely falls on one person - this feature **acknowledges the whole family** works together, reducing stress and preventing things from falling through the cracks.
