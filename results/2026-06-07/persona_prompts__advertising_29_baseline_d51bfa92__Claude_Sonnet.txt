How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Proper nutrition** - age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - daily walks, playtime, or activity suited to your pet
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm protection
- **Keep up with medications** as prescribed

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Spend quality time** with your pet daily
- **Provide enrichment** - toys, puzzles, and new experiences
- **Consistent routine** - pets generally feel secure with predictability
- **Socialization** - appropriate interaction with people and other animals

## Safety & Environment
- **Pet-proof your home** - remove toxic plants and hazardous items
- **Provide comfortable shelter** and resting spaces
- **Fresh water always available**
- **ID tags and microchipping** in case they get lost

## Specific Considerations
- Different species and breeds have different needs
- Senior pets need more frequent vet visits
- Watch for changes in behavior or appetite, which can signal health issues

## Good Habits
- Regular grooming
- Dental hygiene
- Healthy weight management
