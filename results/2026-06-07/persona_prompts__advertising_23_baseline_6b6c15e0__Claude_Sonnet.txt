── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Learning to cook healthy meals quickly is very achievable. Here are some practical approaches:

## Start with Simple Skills
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Master knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand basic seasoning and flavor combinations

## Build a Strategy
- **Meal prep on weekends** - cook grains, proteins, and vegetables in batches
- Keep a well-stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, olive oil, spices)
- Plan meals for the week before shopping

## Focus on Quick, Healthy Formulas
- Grain + protein + roasted vegetables
- Stir-fries with whatever vegetables you have
- Sheet pan meals (everything roasts together)
- Simple soups and salads

## Good Resources
- YouTube channels like Joshua Weissman or Ethan Chlebowski
- Apps like Yummly or Mealime for recipe ideas
- Books like *Salt, Fat, Acid, Heat* by Samin Nosrat

## Practical Tips
- Start with 3-4 recipes you actually enjoy
- Repeat them until they feel easy
- Gradually add new recipes
- Accept that speed comes with practice

## Key Mindset
- Simple meals can be very nutritious
- You don't need complicated recipes to eat well
