  - Historically, technological revolutions have eliminated some jobs while creating others.

  - Predicting specific future jobs is difficult, but technological change often generates demand in unexpected areas.

- Greater emphasis on adaptability
  - Workers may change careers more frequently.
  - Continuous learning and reskilling could become normal throughout adult life.
  - Skills such as critical thinking, creativity, communication, and learning agility may become increasingly important.

- Labor market disruption
