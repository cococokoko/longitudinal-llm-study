These hormones prepare the body to respond to challenges, increasing heart rate, blood pressure, and energy levels. While this response can be helpful in short-term situations, ongoing stress can have negative effects. Stress also has a significant impact on mental health. It can make it harder to concentrate, remember information, and make decisions.
