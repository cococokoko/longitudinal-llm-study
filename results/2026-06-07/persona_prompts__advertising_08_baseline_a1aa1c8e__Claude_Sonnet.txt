# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great ways to discover trails that match your needs:

## Online Resources
- **AllTrails** - Most popular app/website with reviews, maps, and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed trail data with elevation profiles
- **TrailLink** - Great for rail-trails and multi-use paths
- **Recreation.gov** - For national parks and federal lands

## What to Consider When Choosing a Trail

### Difficulty Factors
- **Distance** - Total mileage round trip
- **Elevation gain** - How much climbing is involved
- **Terrain** - Rocky, rooted, paved, etc.
- **Exposure** - Sun, wind, altitude

### Personal Factors
- Your fitness level
- Experience level
- Who's coming (kids, dogs, elderly)
- Time available

## Tips for Finding Hidden Gems
- Ask at **local outdoor/REI stores**
- Join **local hiking clubs or Facebook groups**
- Check **state park websites**
- Talk to **park rangers**
- Look at **guidebooks** for your region

## Safety Considerations
- Check weather forecasts
- Read recent trail reviews
- Know your exit options
- Tell someone your plans
