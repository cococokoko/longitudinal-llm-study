# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some simple, effective recipes to get started:

## Basic Ingredients to Stock Up On
- Carrier oils (coconut, jojoba, almond, argan)
- Beeswax or shea butter
- Essential oils (lavender, tea tree, rosemary)
- Raw honey
- Apple cider vinegar
- Aloe vera gel
- Oats, sugar, coffee grounds (exfoliants)

---

## Simple Recipes

### 🧴 Moisturizing Face Oil
- 2 tbsp jojoba oil
- 5 drops lavender essential oil
- Mix and apply to clean skin

### 💋 Lip Balm
- 1 tbsp beeswax
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- Melt together, pour into small containers

### 🧼 Sugar Scrub
- ½ cup sugar
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- Few drops of your favorite essential oil

### 🧖 Honey Face Mask
- 1 tbsp raw honey
- Apply for 15 minutes, rinse off

---

## Tips for Success
- **Start simple** with few ingredients
- **Patch test** everything before full use
- **Use clean containers** to prevent bacteria
- **Label everything** with date and ingredients
- Store in cool, dark places
