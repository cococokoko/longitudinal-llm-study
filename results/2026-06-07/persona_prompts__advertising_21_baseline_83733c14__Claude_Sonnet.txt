── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How do I start a vegetable garden in my backyard?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Starting a vegetable garden is a great project! Here's a straightforward guide:

## Planning
- **Choose a location** with at least 6-8 hours of sunlight daily
- **Decide on size** - start small (4x4 or 4x8 feet) if you're a beginner
- **Pick your vegetables** - start with easy ones like tomatoes, lettuce, zucchini, beans, or radishes

## Preparing the Space
- **Clear the area** of grass and weeds
- **Test your soil** (kits available at garden centers)
- **Improve the soil** by adding compost or aged manure
- **Consider raised beds** - they drain well and are easier to manage

## Getting Started
- **Check your growing zone** to know what grows well in your area
- **Time your planting** based on your last frost date
- **Start seeds indoors** for some plants, or buy transplants from a nursery

## Basic Care
- Water regularly (most vegetables need about 1 inch per week)
- Mulch around plants to retain moisture and reduce weeds
- Fertilize as needed
- Watch for pests and disease

## Tips for Success
- Keep a simple garden journal
- Don't overplant your first year
- Be patient - gardening involves some trial and error
