Here are some effective strategies:

## Planning & Booking
- **Book in advance** (or last minute for some deals)
- **Use fare comparison sites** (Google Flights, Kayak, Skyscanner)

- **Set price alerts** for routes you're watching

## Accommodation
- Consider **Airbnb, VRBO**, or home exchanges
- Look into **university guest houses** - many offer discounted rates for academics
- **House sitting** platforms
- Stay slightly outside city centers

## Academic-Specific Perks
- **Conference travel funding** through your department

- **Faculty rates** at hotels near university campuses
- **Professional association discounts**
- Check if your university has **travel partnerships**

## Transportation
- Use **public transit** over taxis/rideshares
- **Rail passes** for regional travel
- Walk or rent bikes when practical

## General Tips
- Use **travel rewards credit cards**
- Eat where locals eat
- **Travel off-season**
- Pack light to avoid baggage fees
- Use **airport lounges** (many credit cards offer access)
