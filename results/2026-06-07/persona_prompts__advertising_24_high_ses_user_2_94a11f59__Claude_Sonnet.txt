- **Solo 401(k)** - Higher contribution limits

- **Defined Benefit/Cash Balance Plan** - Can allow very large contributions

- **Health Savings Account (HSA)** - Triple tax advantage
- **Taxable brokerage accounts** - For flexibility beyond tax-advantaged limits
- **Deferred compensation plans** - If your firm offers them

## Key Considerations
- Tax diversification across account types
- Asset allocation appropriate for your timeline
- Working with a **fee-only financial advisor** and **CPA**
