── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some formal ways to write a **"thank you for your time"** subject line:

## Direct Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Thank You for Meeting With Me"**
- **"Thank You for the Interview"**
- **"Thank You for the Opportunity"**

## More Specific Options
- **"Thank You for Your Time – [Meeting Topic]"**
- **"Thank You for the Interview – [Your Name]"**
- **"Following Up – Thank You for Your Time"**
- **"Appreciation for Your Time on [Date]"**

## Tips
- **Be specific** when possible (mention the meeting or date)
- **Keep it concise** and clear
- **Include your name** if the recipient may not recognize your email address
- Avoid all caps or excessive punctuation

The most commonly used and widely accepted option is simply:

> **"Thank You for Your Time"**
