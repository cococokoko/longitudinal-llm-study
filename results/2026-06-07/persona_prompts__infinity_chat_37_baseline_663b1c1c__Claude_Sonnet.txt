# Benefits of Hardwood Flooring

Hardwood flooring offers several advantages compared to carpet, laminate, vinyl, and tile:

## Durability & Longevity
- Can last **decades or even a lifetime** with proper care
- Can be **sanded and refinished** multiple times to restore appearance
- Handles heavy foot traffic well

## Aesthetics
- Natural, warm appearance that many find attractive
- Adds a **classic, timeless look** to spaces
- Available in many species, colors, and grain patterns

## Home Value
- Generally **increases resale value**
- Considered a desirable feature by many homebuyers

## Practical Benefits
- **Easier to clean** than carpet
- Doesn't trap allergens, dust, or pet dander as much as carpet
- Works well with radiant heating systems

## Versatility
- Can be stained or refinished to change the look
- Complements many design styles

## Considerations/Drawbacks
- **Higher upfront cost** than many alternatives
- Can be scratched or dented
- Sensitive to **moisture and humidity**
- Can feel hard underfoot compared to carpet
- Not ideal for bathrooms or basements

## Compared to Alternatives
- **vs. Laminate/Vinyl** - More authentic look, can be refinished
- **vs. Carpet** - More durable, easier to clean
- **vs. Tile** - Warmer feel, less hard underfoot
