Here are some helpful resources for starting a plant-based diet:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition guidance
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research on plant-based eating
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical cookbook and guide

## Websites & Apps
- **NutritionFacts.org** - free, science-based information
- **Forks Over Knives website** - recipes and meal plans
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients to ensure you're meeting needs
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-based restaurants

## Practical Starting Tips
- Start with meals you already enjoy that happen to be plant-based
- Focus on adding vegetables rather than just removing meat
- Learn about protein sources (legumes, tofu, tempeh, seitan)
- Pay attention to B12, iron, and omega-3 intake

## Communities
- Reddit communities like r/PlantBasedDiet or r/vegan
- Local meetup groups for support and recipe sharing

## YouTube Channels
- **Mic the Vegan** - nutrition research breakdowns
- **Pick Up Limes** - practical recipes and lifestyle tips

## Key Considerations
- Consider consulting a registered dietitian
- Transition gradually if that works better for you
- Focus on whole foods rather than processed plant-based products
