# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Here's a practical, straightforward guide:

## Start Simple
- **Begin with just 5-10 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration
- Morning often works best before the day's demands take over
- Same time and place helps build the habit

## Basic Technique (Breath Focus)
1. Sit comfortably with your back relatively straight
2. Close your eyes
3. Focus attention on your breathing
4. When your mind wanders (it will), gently return focus to breath
5. That's essentially it

## Practical Tools
- **Apps worth trying:** Headspace, Calm, Insight Timer (free)
- **Good starting programs:** 10-day beginner courses on most apps
- **Books:** *Wherever You Go, There You Are* by Jon Kabat-Zinn

## Realistic Expectations
- Your mind **will** wander constantly at first - that's normal
- Benefits typically emerge after **2-4 weeks** of regular practice
- Research supports benefits for stress, focus, and decision-making

## Fitting It Into a Demanding Schedule
- Calendar block it like any important meeting
- Even **2 minutes** between meetings has value
- Walking meditation is a legitimate option

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Expecting to "clear your mind" completely
- Giving up after a few days
- Overcomplicating it
