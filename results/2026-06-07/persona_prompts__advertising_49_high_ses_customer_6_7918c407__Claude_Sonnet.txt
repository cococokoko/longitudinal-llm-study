Here are some effective ways to improve your home WiFi:

## Router Placement
- Position it **centrally** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** and in the open

## Hardware Upgrades
- **Replace an old router** (technology improves significantly every few years)
- Consider a **mesh network system** (Eero, Google Nest, Orbi) for larger homes

## Settings Optimization
- Use the **5GHz band** for faster speeds at shorter range
- Use **2.4GHz** for better range and older devices
- **Change your WiFi channel** to avoid neighbor interference
- Keep **firmware updated**

## Reduce Interference
- Move away from microwaves, cordless phones, baby monitors
- Minimize competing networks on the same channel

## Connection Tips
- Use **ethernet cables** for stationary devices (TVs, gaming consoles, computers)
- **Limit bandwidth-heavy devices** or use QoS settings to prioritize traffic
- **Restart your router** periodically

## Service-Level Check
