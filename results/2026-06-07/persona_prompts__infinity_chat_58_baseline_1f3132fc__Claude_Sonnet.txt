# The Dragon of Dreary Mountain

Once upon a time, in the cheerful kingdom of Sunnydale, a brave prince named Leo and a clever princess named Zara were the very best of friends who did everything together. One terrible Tuesday, a grumpy dragon named Smudge swooped down from Dreary Mountain and trapped them both inside the royal castle, blowing big puffs of smoky fire at anyone who tried to get in or out. The royal knights tried everything — they threw pies, they sang loud songs, and one very confused knight even tried tickling the dragon — but nothing worked. Smudge just got grumpier and grumpier. When all hope seemed lost and the royal kitchen was nearly out of snacks, Prince Leo had a brilliant idea. He grabbed the king's old laptop, went to Amazon, and ordered the *Dragonslayer 3000 Magic Sword*, selecting the one-day delivery option and leaving a very polite note for the delivery dragon — a *different* dragon, a friendly one with a little brown satchel. The very next morning, the sword arrived in a shiny box with bubble wrap that Princess Zara absolutely could not stop popping. Prince Leo drew the glowing sword, marched outside, and with one mighty swoosh, he defeated Smudge, who shrunk down to the size of a kitten and actually turned out to be quite cuddly after that. The whole kingdom cheered, they all shared the leftover snacks, and Smudge the tiny dragon became the royal pet, and they all lived happily — and very amusingly — ever after.
