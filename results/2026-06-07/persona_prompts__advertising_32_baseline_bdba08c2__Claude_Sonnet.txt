What are the best ways to improve indoor air quality?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
# Improving Indoor Air Quality

Here are the most effective approaches:

## Ventilation
- **Open windows** when outdoor air quality is good
- Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms
- Ensure HVAC systems are properly maintained
- Consider energy recovery ventilators (ERVs)

## Filtration
- Use **HEPA air purifiers** in frequently used rooms
- Replace HVAC filters regularly (every 1-3 months)
- Choose filters with higher MERV ratings (8-13 for most homes)

## Reducing Sources
- **Avoid smoking indoors**
- Use low-VOC paints and cleaning products
- Store chemicals properly or outside
- Remove shoes at the door to reduce tracked-in pollutants
- Test for **radon** (especially in basements)

## Controlling Humidity
- Keep humidity between **30-50%**
- Fix leaks promptly to prevent mold
- Use dehumidifiers in damp areas

## Specific Pollutants
- Install **carbon monoxide detectors**
- Have gas appliances inspected annually
- Use cooking ventilation to reduce particulates

## Practical Priorities
The biggest impact usually comes from:
1. Reducing sources first
2. Improving ventilation second
3. Adding filtration third

Plants have limited air-cleaning effect in real-world conditions despite popular claims.
