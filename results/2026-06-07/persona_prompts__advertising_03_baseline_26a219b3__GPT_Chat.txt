  - Break large projects into smaller tasks.

  - Turn off nonessential notifications.
  - Set specific times to check email and messages.
  - Use website blockers if certain sites frequently interrupt your focus.

• Use focused work sessions
  - Try working in blocks of 25–50 minutes followed by short breaks.
  - Reserve your highest-energy hours for demanding tasks.
  - Avoid multitasking whenever possible.

• Communicate clearly
  - Share availability and expectations with coworkers.
  - Update team members proactively on progress and blockers.
  - Establish boundaries with people at home during work hours.

• Take care of your physical and mental well-being
  - Move regularly throughout the day.
  - Stay hydrated and eat balanced meals.
  - Get sufficient sleep, as it strongly affects productivity and concentration.

  - At the end of each day, review what was completed.
  - Identify obstacles that reduced productivity.
  - Refine your routine based on what works best for you.
