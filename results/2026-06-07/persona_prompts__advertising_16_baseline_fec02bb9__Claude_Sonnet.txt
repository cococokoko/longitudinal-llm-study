# Managing Stress and Anxiety

## Understanding What You're Dealing With

**Stress** is typically a response to external pressures
**Anxiety** often involves worry about future threats, sometimes without a clear cause

Both are normal, but can become overwhelming without good management strategies.

---

## Practical Strategies

### 🧠 Mind-Based Approaches
- **Deep breathing** – slow, controlled breaths calm your nervous system
- **Mindfulness/meditation** – staying present reduces worry about the future
- **Cognitive reframing** – challenging unhelpful thought patterns
- **Journaling** – writing out worries can reduce their intensity

### 🏃 Physical Approaches
- Regular **exercise** (even walking helps significantly)
- Prioritizing **sleep** – poor sleep worsens anxiety considerably
- Limiting **caffeine and alcohol**
- Spending time **outdoors/in nature**

### 🤝 Social & Lifestyle
- Talking to someone you trust
- Setting realistic **boundaries**
- Breaking tasks into **smaller steps**
- Reducing unnecessary **commitments**

---

## When to Seek Help

Consider talking to a professional if:
- Anxiety interferes with daily life
- You feel overwhelmed consistently
- Physical symptoms appear (chest tightness, etc.)
- Self-help strategies aren't working
