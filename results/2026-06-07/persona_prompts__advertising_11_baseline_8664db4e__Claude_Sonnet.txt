# Staying Fit Without a Gym

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** – chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats & lunges** – legs and glutes
- **Planks** – core strength
- **Burpees** – full body + cardio
- **Dips** – using a chair or sturdy surface

## Cardio Options
- Walking or jogging outdoors
- Jump rope
- Cycling
- Swimming
- Dancing

## Home Workout Tools *(low cost)*
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells or kettlebells
- Pull-up bar (doorframe)
- Yoga mat

## Everyday Activity
- Take stairs instead of elevators
- Walk or bike instead of driving
- Active chores (gardening, cleaning)
- Stand more, sit less

## Simple Routines to Try
| Goal | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Strength | Push-ups, squats, planks |
| Cardio | Jogging, jump rope, burpees |
| Flexibility | Yoga, stretching |
| Weight loss | Mix of all three |

## Key Tips
- **Consistency beats intensity** – short daily workouts work
- **Progressive overload** – gradually increase reps or difficulty
- **Rest and recovery** matter as much as exercise
- **Nutrition** supports your fitness goals
