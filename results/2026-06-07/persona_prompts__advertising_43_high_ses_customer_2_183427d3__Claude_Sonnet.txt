# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor settings that need minimal decoration
- Select a venue that uses renewable energy

## Invitations & Paper
- Use digital invitations or wedding websites
- Choose recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Choose a caterer with sustainable practices
- Offer plant-based menu options
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Decorations
- Use locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent decorations rather than buying
- Choose potted plants guests can take home
- Avoid floral foam (not biodegradable)

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose rings with ethically sourced stones
- Rent suits/tuxedos rather than buying

## Waste Management
- Use compostable or reusable tableware
- Set up clear recycling stations
- Minimize single-use plastics

## Transportation
- Arrange group transportation for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit

## Offsetting
- Purchase carbon offsets for unavoidable emissions
- Plant trees in honor of your wedding

Would you like more detail on any specific aspect?
