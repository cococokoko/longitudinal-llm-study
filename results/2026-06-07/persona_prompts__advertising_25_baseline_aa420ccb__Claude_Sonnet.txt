── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I decorate my apartment on a minimalist budget?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some practical ways to decorate minimally and affordably:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for a fresh feel
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items you already own

## Low-Cost Additions
- **Plants** - even small ones add life and warmth
- **Thrift stores** - frames, vases, unique pieces for very little
- **Secondhand apps** like Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist
- **Dollar stores** for basic organizational items

## Free or Nearly Free
- Print your own art (many free downloads online)
- Collect interesting rocks, branches, or pinecones
- Rearrange books by color on shelves
- Use candles for atmosphere

## Smart Investments
- A few quality throw pillows can transform a couch
- Good lighting makes a huge difference
- One statement rug can anchor a room

## Minimalist Principles That Save Money
- Choose neutral colors so pieces mix easily
- Buy less but better quality
- Leave some empty space intentionally
- Focus on one or two focal points per room

## Practical Tips
- Paint is relatively cheap and very impactful
- Mirrors make spaces feel larger
- Consistent color palette makes things look intentional
