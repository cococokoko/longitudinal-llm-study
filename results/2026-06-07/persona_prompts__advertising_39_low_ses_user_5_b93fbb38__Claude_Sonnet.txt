# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting others, this is a great investment in yourself.

## Start Simple - Really Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily**
- Morning works well before others need you
- Even bathroom breaks or car time counts
- Consistency matters more than duration

## Basic Techniques to Try First

**Breathing focus**
- Breathe in for 4 counts, hold 2, out for 6
- Just notice your breath without controlling it

**Body scan**
- Mentally check in from head to toe
- Notice tension without judging it

**Grounding (5-4-3-2-1)**
- Name 5 things you see, 4 you hear, 3 you can touch...
- Works great during stressful moments

## Practical for Your Situation

- **Mindful tasks** - washing dishes, folding laundry can become practice
- **Transition moments** - between responsibilities, take 3 conscious breaths
- **No perfect conditions needed** - imperfect practice beats waiting for quiet

## Free Resources Worth Trying

- Insight Timer app (free, huge library)
- YouTube guided meditations
- Headspace or Calm have free basics

## Honest Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander - that's normal, not failure
- Benefits often appear in **2-4 weeks** of regular practice
- Even 5 minutes reduces stress hormones measurably
