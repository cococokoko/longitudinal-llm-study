── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some effective ways to improve your home WiFi:

## Router Placement
- Place it in a **central location** in your home
- Keep it **elevated** (shelf or table, not the floor)
- Avoid walls, appliances, and metal objects nearby

## Router Settings
- Use the **5GHz band** for faster speeds (shorter range)
- Use **2.4GHz** for better range through walls
- Update your **router firmware** regularly
- Change to a **less congested WiFi channel**

## Hardware Upgrades
- Consider a **mesh WiFi system** for larger homes
- Add a **WiFi extender/repeater** for dead zones
- Upgrade to a newer **WiFi 6 router** if your equipment is old

## Reduce Interference
- Keep away from **microwaves and cordless phones**
- Distance from **baby monitors and Bluetooth devices**

## Security & Performance
- Use a **strong password** to prevent unauthorized users
- **Restart your router** periodically
- Check if your **ISP plan** matches your needs

## Quick Wins
- Move closer to the router when possible
- Use an **ethernet cable** for devices that need reliable speed (gaming, streaming)
- Limit bandwidth-heavy activities during peak times
