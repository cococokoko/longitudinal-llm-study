# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write tomorrow's top 3 priorities** on a sticky note
- **Clear your desk** to a workable surface
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing

### Why This Works Well for Your Situation

- You arrive **ready to go** instead of scrambling
- When childcare chaos happens in the morning, you still have a **clear plan**
- It reduces that mental load you carry **home with you**
- No prep needed at night when you're exhausted

### Bonus for Teachers Specifically

Keep a simple **weekly grading tracker** - even just a handwritten checklist of what needs to be returned - so nothing falls through the cracks during busy weeks.

The goal is making your **school time as efficient as possible** so you protect your time and energy at home.
