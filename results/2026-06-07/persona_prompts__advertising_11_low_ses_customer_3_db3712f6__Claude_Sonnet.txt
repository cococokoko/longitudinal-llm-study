# Staying Fit Without a Gym

Here are some effective ways to maintain fitness without a gym membership:

## Bodyweight Exercises
- **Push-ups** - chest, shoulders, triceps
- **Squats and lunges** - legs and glutes
- **Planks** - core strength
- **Burpees** - full body cardio
- **Pull-ups** - if you have a bar or sturdy surface

## Cardio Options
- Running or jogging outdoors
- Cycling
- Jump rope
- Swimming
- Brisk walking

## Home Workout Tools (Low Cost)
- Resistance bands
- Dumbbells or kettlebells
- Yoga mat
- Pull-up bar for doorframes

## Free Resources
- YouTube workout channels (Fitness Blender, Yoga with Adriene)
- NHS Fitness Studio (free online classes)
- Apps like Nike Training Club

## Practical Tips
- **Consistency matters more than intensity** - even 20-30 minutes daily helps
- Use stairs instead of lifts
- Walk or cycle for short trips
- Set a regular schedule

## Structured Approaches
- HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training)
- Yoga or Pilates
- Couch to 5K program for beginners
