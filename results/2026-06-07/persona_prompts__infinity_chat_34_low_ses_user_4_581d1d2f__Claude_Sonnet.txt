Python is not demanding, so you have **lots of affordable options**. Here are solid choices:

| Laptop | Why It's Good |

### Minimum Specs to Look For
- **RAM:** 8GB (most important)
- **Storage:** 256GB SSD (avoid old spinning hard drives)
- **Processor:** Intel i5 or better
- **OS:** Windows 10/11 or Linux ready

---

### Where to Buy
- **eBay** - large selection
- **Facebook Marketplace** - local, no shipping
- **Back Market** - graded/tested devices
- **Your hospital's IT surplus** - sometimes sells old equipment cheap

The **ThinkPad T480** is probably the most recommended beginner option - very common, parts are cheap, and it runs Python tools very well.
