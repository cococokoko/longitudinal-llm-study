# Organization Tip for Teachers: The **"Sunday Reset" Routine**

Take **20-30 minutes every Sunday** (or before your week starts) to:

## What to Do:
- **Review the week ahead** - check your lesson plans and calendar
- **Prep your physical space** - clear your desk and restock supplies
- **Write 3 priorities** for the week on a sticky note
- **Organize papers** into simple categories:
  - To grade
  - To return
  - To file

## Why It Works for Teachers:
- Mondays feel **less overwhelming**
- You catch conflicts **before** they happen
- Students notice when you're prepared - it sets the tone
- Reduces that "scrambling" feeling during passing periods

## Simple Tool to Try:
A **physical weekly planner** (not just digital) can help you see your whole week at a glance, including after-school duties, meetings, and deadlines.

---

**The key idea:** A short, consistent reset prevents the pile-up that makes the whole week feel chaotic.
