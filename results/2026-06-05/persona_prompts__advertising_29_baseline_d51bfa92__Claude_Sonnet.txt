── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I keep my pets healthy and happy?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are some key ways to keep pets healthy and happy:

## Physical Health
- **Regular vet visits** - annual checkups, vaccinations, and dental care
- **Proper nutrition** - age-appropriate, quality food in right portions
- **Exercise** - daily walks, playtime, or activity suited to your pet
- **Parasite prevention** - flea, tick, and heartworm treatments
- **Spay/neuter** if appropriate

## Mental & Emotional Wellbeing
- **Enrichment activities** - toys, puzzles, and new experiences
- **Social interaction** - time with you and other animals when appropriate
- **Consistent routine** - pets often feel secure with predictability
- **Training** - helps dogs especially feel confident and understood

## Environment
- **Safe space** - somewhere they can rest undisturbed
- **Clean living area** - regular cleaning of bedding, litter boxes, cages
- **Appropriate temperature** - protection from extreme heat or cold

## Practical Tips
- Learn your specific pet's normal behavior so you notice changes
- Keep fresh water available always
- Watch for signs of stress or illness early
- Make sure ID tags and microchips are current

## Worth Noting
- Different species and breeds have very different needs
- Senior pets need more frequent vet attention
