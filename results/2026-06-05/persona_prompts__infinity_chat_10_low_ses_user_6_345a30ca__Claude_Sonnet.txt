## Honest Assessment
- The correlation, if it exists, is **weak and inconsistent**
- Many highly intelligent people show **low compassion**
- Many people with average intelligence show **high compassion**
- Compassion seems more related to **upbringing, values, and emotional development**

Your warehouse work context is relevant - research doesn't support the idea that physically demanding or less credentialed work reflects lower compassion or intelligence. Those assumptions are **not well-supported**.
