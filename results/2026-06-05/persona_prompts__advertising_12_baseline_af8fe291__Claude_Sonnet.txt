# Eco-Friendly Travel Options

## 🚆 Low-Carbon Transportation
- **Train travel** - significantly lower emissions than flying
- **Bus/coach** - one of the most efficient per-passenger options
- **Cycling** - zero emissions, great for short distances
- **Walking** - the most sustainable option locally
- **Electric vehicles** - especially effective where grids use renewable energy

## ✈️ If You Must Fly
- Choose **direct flights** (takeoff/landing produce most emissions)
- Fly **economy class** (smaller carbon footprint per seat)
- Consider **carbon offset programs**
- Choose airlines with **newer, fuel-efficient fleets**

## 🏨 Accommodation
- **Eco-certified hotels** (look for Green Key, LEED certification)
- **Locally owned guesthouses**
- **Camping** with minimal impact
- **Hostels** (shared resources = lower footprint)

## 📍 Destination Choices
- Travel **closer to home**
- Visit **off-season** to reduce overtourism
- Support **conservation-focused destinations**
- Choose **slow travel** (stay longer, travel less frequently)

## 💡 General Tips
- Pack light (reduces fuel consumption)
- Use public transport at destinations
- Eat local food
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Support local economies
