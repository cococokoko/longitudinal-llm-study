# Managing Stress and Anxiety as a Tech Executive

## Why Your Role Creates Unique Pressures
- High-stakes decisions with incomplete information
- Constant context-switching between priorities
- Public accountability (board, investors, employees, media)
- Always-on culture expectations
- Imposter syndrome at scale

---

## Practical Strategies

### **Immediate Relief**
- Box breathing (4-4-4-4 counts) before high-pressure meetings
- Brief walks between back-to-back meetings
- Single-tasking during critical conversations

### **Daily Habits**
- Protect morning time before reactive mode begins
- Hard boundaries on meeting blocks
- Regular physical exercise (non-negotiable)
- Sleep as a performance tool, not a luxury

### **Cognitive Approaches**
- Separate what you control from what you don't
- Challenge catastrophic thinking with evidence
- Reframe pressure as information, not threat

### **Structural Solutions**
- Build a trusted inner circle you can be honest with
- Work with an executive coach
- Consider therapy - many top executives do
- Regular digital detox periods

---

## Red Flags to Watch
- Chronic sleep disruption
- Irritability affecting relationships
- Decision fatigue becoming constant
- Physical symptoms (headaches, tension)
