# Starting a Small Online Business

Here are the key areas to focus on:

## Foundation
- **Business idea/niche** - identify your product or service
- **Market research** - validate demand and understand competition
- **Business model** - how you'll make money (products, services, subscriptions, etc.)

## Legal & Financial Setup
- Register your business (LLC, sole proprietorship, etc.)
- Get an EIN (if in the US)
- Open a separate business bank account
- Understand your tax obligations

## Online Presence
- Choose a domain name
- Build a website (Shopify, Squarespace, WordPress, etc.)
- Set up payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, Square)

## Marketing
- Define your target audience
- Choose 1-2 social media platforms to start
- Consider SEO basics
- Email list building from day one

## Operations
- Decide on fulfillment/delivery method
- Set up customer service processes
- Track key metrics from the start

---

**A few practical tips:**
- Start lean and validate before investing heavily
- Focus on getting your first 10 customers before scaling
- Keep overhead low initially
