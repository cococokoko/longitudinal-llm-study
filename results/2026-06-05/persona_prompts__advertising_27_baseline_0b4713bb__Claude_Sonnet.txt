── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I reduce my carbon footprint with daily habits?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Here are practical ways to reduce your carbon footprint across different areas of daily life:

## Food & Diet
- Eat less meat, especially beef (plant-based meals have significantly lower emissions)
- Reduce food waste by planning meals and using leftovers
- Buy local and seasonal produce when practical
- Compost food scraps

## Transportation
- Walk, bike, or use public transit when possible
- Combine errands into single trips
- Consider carpooling
- If buying a vehicle, consider electric or hybrid options
- Fly less, or offset flights when necessary

## Home Energy
- Lower your thermostat a few degrees (heating/cooling is a major energy user)
- Switch to LED lighting
- Unplug devices when not in use
- Improve insulation and seal drafts
- Consider renewable energy options or green energy plans

## Shopping & Consumption
- Buy less overall, especially fast fashion
- Choose secondhand items
- Repair things rather than replacing them
- Avoid excessive packaging

## High-Impact Actions Worth Knowing
- Diet changes and driving less tend to have the **biggest individual impact**
- Home energy efficiency can make a meaningful difference
- Small habits add up but systemic choices matter more

## Realistic Perspective
- Focus on your **highest-impact areas** rather than trying to be perfect everywhere
- Collective and policy changes matter alongside individual action
