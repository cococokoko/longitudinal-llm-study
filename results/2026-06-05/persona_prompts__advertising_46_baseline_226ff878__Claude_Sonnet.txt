# Getting Started with a Plant-Based Diet

Here are some reliable resources across different formats:

## Books
- **"How Not to Die" by Dr. Michael Greger** - evidence-based nutrition
- **"The China Study" by T. Colin Campbell** - research-focused
- **"Forks Over Knives"** - practical and beginner-friendly
- **"Plant-Based on a Budget" by Toni Okamoto** - affordable approach

## Websites & Apps
- **NutritionFacts.org** - science-based information
- **Forks Over Knives website** - recipes and guides
- **Cronometer** - tracking nutrients (helpful early on)
- **HappyCow** - finding plant-based restaurants

## YouTube Channels
- Pick Up Limes
- Cheap Lazy Vegan
- Mic the Vegan (research-focused)

## Practical Starting Tips
- Focus on **what to add** rather than what to remove
- Learn a few **simple staple meals** first
- Pay attention to **key nutrients** like B12, iron, and protein
- Consider consulting a **registered dietitian**

## Things Worth Knowing
- Quality varies among sources, so cross-reference claims
- Individual needs differ based on health conditions
- Gradual transitions often work better than sudden changes
