# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

Here are some practical tips to make your space look great without spending a lot:

## Shop Smart
- **Thrift stores & estate sales** - great for unique furniture and decor
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - often find quality pieces at low prices
- **End-of-season sales** - buy holiday decor after the holiday for next year

## DIY Projects
- Repaint old furniture instead of replacing it
- Create gallery walls with printed photos
- Make your own throw pillow covers
- Refinish wood furniture with stain or paint

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Fresh paint** - one of the biggest transformations for the money
- **New hardware** on cabinets and doors
- **Lighting upgrades** - swap out dated fixtures
- **Plants** - add life and color inexpensively

## Rearrange What You Have
- Try different furniture arrangements
- Rotate decor between rooms
- Decluttering alone can dramatically improve a space

## Free Resources
- Pinterest for inspiration
- YouTube tutorials for DIY skills
- Library books on interior design

## Set a Priority Budget
- Focus spending on **high-visibility areas** first
- Living room and entryway make the biggest impression
