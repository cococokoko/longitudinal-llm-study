── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How to say a formal thank you for your time in the subject line of an email?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
For a formal email subject line expressing thanks for someone's time, you can use:

- Thank You for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for Meeting with Me
- Thank You for Your Time Today
- Thank You for the Opportunity to Meet
- Appreciation for Your Time
- Sincere Thanks for Your Time
- Thank You for Your Assistance and Time
- Grateful for Your Time and Insights
- Thank You Following Our Meeting

If this is for a job interview, a particularly professional option is:

- Thank You for Your Time and Consideration
- Thank You for the Interview
- Thank You for Meeting with Me Today

These are concise, professional, and appropriate for most formal contexts.
