# Best Tools for Remote Team Collaboration

Here's a breakdown by category to help you choose what fits your needs:

## Communication
- **Slack** - messaging, channels, integrations
- **Microsoft Teams** - chat + Office 365 integration
- **Discord** - good for informal/async teams
- **Zoom/Google Meet** - video calls

## Project Management
- **Asana** - task tracking and workflows
- **Trello** - visual kanban boards
- **Monday.com** - flexible project views
- **Jira** - best for software/dev teams
- **Notion** - combines docs + tasks

## Document Collaboration
- **Google Workspace** - real-time doc editing
- **Notion** - wikis and knowledge bases
- **Confluence** - team documentation

## File Sharing
- **Google Drive** - simple and widely used
- **Dropbox** - reliable file storage
- **OneDrive** - Microsoft ecosystem

## Whiteboarding/Brainstorming
- **Miro** - virtual whiteboard
- **FigJam** - design-focused collaboration
- **Lucidspark** - diagrams and planning

## Key Considerations When Choosing
- **Team size** and budget
- **Existing tools** you already use
- **Async vs. real-time** work style
- **Technical skill level** of your team

## Popular Combinations
- Slack + Notion + Google Drive
- Teams + SharePoint + Jira
