Learning a musical instrument online has become very accessible. Here are some effective approaches:

## Structured Learning Platforms
- **Apps like Yousician, Simply Piano, or Fender Play** offer guided lessons with real-time feedback

- **YouTube channels** from established teachers (many are excellent and free)

## Key Practices for Success

**Build consistency**
- Short daily sessions (15-30 minutes) often beat occasional long ones
- Schedule it like a meeting you won't cancel

**Get feedback**
- Consider occasional live lessons via Zoom with a teacher to correct technique early
- Online communities like Reddit's r/guitar or instrument-specific forums can help

**Use multiple resources**
- Combine video lessons with sheet music or tabs
- Apps that listen to your playing and give real-time feedback accelerate progress

## Practical Tips
- Record yourself periodically to track progress and notice issues
- Learn songs you actually enjoy - motivation matters enormously
- Focus on fundamentals first (posture, technique) to avoid bad habits

- Progress is nonlinear - plateaus are normal
- Most people underestimate how much deliberate practice matters versus passive watching
