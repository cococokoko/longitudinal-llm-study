── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
One example of an optimization technique used in machine learning is Gradient Descent.

Gradient Descent is an algorithm that iteratively adjusts a model's parameters to minimize a loss function by moving in the direction of the negative gradient (the steepest decrease).

Other common optimization techniques include:
- Stochastic Gradient Descent (SGD)
- Adam (Adaptive Moment Estimation)
- RMSprop
- Adagrad
- L-BFGS (Limited-memory Broyden–Fletcher–Goldfarb–Shanno)
