# Making Natural Beauty Products at Home

Here are some popular DIY options that are straightforward to make:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Shea butter + jojoba oil + a few drops of essential oil
- Melt, mix, and cool in a clean container

**Exfoliating Scrub**
- Sugar or coffee grounds + coconut oil + lemon juice
- Mix to desired consistency

**Toner**
- Diluted apple cider vinegar (1:3 ratio with water)
- Or rose water (steep rose petals in distilled water)

## Hair Care

**Hair Mask**
- Avocado + honey + olive oil
- Apply, leave 20-30 minutes, rinse

**Dry Shampoo**
- Arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- Add cocoa powder for darker hair

## Lip Care

**Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + shea butter
- Melt together, pour into small containers

## Key Tips

- **Sterilize** all containers and tools
- **Patch test** new products before full use
- **Label** with ingredients and date made
- Use **distilled water** to extend shelf life
- Add **vitamin E** as a natural preservative
