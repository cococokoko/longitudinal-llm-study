# Improving Productivity While Working From Home

Here are practical strategies tailored to your situation as a single parent:

## Setting Up Your Environment
- **Designate a workspace** - even a corner with a consistent setup signals "work mode"
- Keep essentials within reach to minimize interruptions
- Use noise-canceling headphones during focused work

## Managing Your Schedule Around Childcare
- **Work in blocks** aligned with nap times, school hours, or quiet activities
- Identify your **2-3 most critical tasks** and tackle them during your most reliable focused time
- Be honest with employers/clients about your realistic availability windows

## Minimizing Interruptions
- Create simple signals for kids (a colored card system works well)
- Prepare engaging activities before starting focused work sessions
- Use the **"one more minute" technique** to teach kids to wait briefly

## Staying Focused
- Try the **Pomodoro method** (25 minutes work, 5-minute break)
- Batch similar tasks together
- Keep a running list so you can quickly resume after interruptions

## Being Kind to Yourself
- **Imperfect progress beats perfect paralysis**
- Some days childcare wins - that's okay
- Celebrate what you *did* accomplish

## Quick Wins
- Prep tomorrow's priorities the night before
- Use voice memos when you can't type
- Automate repetitive tasks where possible
