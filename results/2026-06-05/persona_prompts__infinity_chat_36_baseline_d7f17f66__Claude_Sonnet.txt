# Day Trading Strategies: An Honest Assessment

I'll give you a straightforward answer rather than just hype.

## The Reality First
**No single "best" strategy exists** - profitability depends on:
- Market conditions
- Your capital and risk tolerance
- Execution discipline
- Transaction costs

## Well-Regarded Strategies

### Momentum Trading
- Trade stocks/assets moving strongly in one direction
- Follow volume and price action
- **Risk:** Reversals can be sharp

### Mean Reversion
- Trade assets back toward their average price
- Works in range-bound markets
- **Risk:** Trends can persist longer than expected

### Breakout Trading
- Enter when price breaks key support/resistance
- Often combined with volume confirmation

### Scalping
- Many small, quick trades
- Requires very low spreads and fast execution

## What Research Actually Shows

- **~70-80% of day traders lose money** consistently
- Most profitable traders use **strict risk management** more than any specific strategy
- Edge often comes from **execution and discipline**, not the strategy itself

## What Actually Matters More
- Position sizing
- Stop losses
- Avoiding overtrading
- Managing emotions

## Honest Recommendation
Study **price action + risk management** before any specific strategy.
