# Starting a Small Online Business

Here's a practical overview to help you get started:

## 1. **Define Your Business**
- What product or service will you offer?
- Who is your target audience?
- What problem are you solving?

## 2. **Validate Your Idea**
- Research competitors
- Talk to potential customers
- Test demand before investing heavily

## 3. **Choose a Business Model**
- E-commerce (physical products)
- Digital products (courses, templates, ebooks)
- Services (freelancing, consulting)
- Subscription or membership

## 4. **Set Up the Basics**
- Choose a business name
- Register your business (LLC, sole proprietor, etc.)
- Get an EIN (if in the US)
- Open a separate business bank account

## 5. **Build Your Online Presence**
- Website (Shopify, Wix, WordPress, Squarespace)
- Social media profiles
- Google Business Profile

## 6. **Handle the Legal & Financial Side**
- Understand your tax obligations
- Track income and expenses
- Consider basic contracts if offering services

## 7. **Market Your Business**
- Social media marketing
- Email list building
- SEO content
- Paid ads (when ready)
