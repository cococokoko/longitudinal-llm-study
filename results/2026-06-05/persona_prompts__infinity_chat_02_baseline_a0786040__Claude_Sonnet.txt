## The Predatory Nature of 7

To understand 6's fear, we must first understand 7's character. Seven has always occupied a peculiar position in human culture. It is considered lucky, mystical, and powerful. There are seven deadly sins, seven wonders of the ancient world, seven days in a week, and seven notes in a musical scale. Seven appears in religious texts with striking frequency — seven seals in Revelation, seven days of creation, seven heavens in Islamic tradition. This is not a number that operates quietly.
