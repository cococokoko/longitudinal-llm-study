Video Meetings

Project Management

- Jira: Popular for software development and agile teams.

Document Collaboration

- Microsoft 365: Strong document editing and enterprise features.

Whiteboarding and Brainstorming

Knowledge Management

- Confluence: Structured knowledge sharing for larger teams.

File Storage and Sharing
- Google Drive: Easy sharing and collaboration.
- OneDrive: Best for Microsoft-centric organizations.
- Dropbox: Reliable file synchronization and sharing.

Best Tool Combinations
- Small startup: Slack + Notion + Google Workspace + Trello
