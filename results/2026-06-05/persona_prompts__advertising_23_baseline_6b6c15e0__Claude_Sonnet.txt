── Prompt ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
How can I learn to cook healthy meals quickly?

── Response ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Learning to cook healthy meals quickly is very achievable. Here are some practical approaches:

## Start with Simple Skills
- Learn a few basic techniques (sautéing, roasting, steaming)
- Master knife skills to speed up prep time
- Understand how to season food well

## Build a Strategy
- **Meal prep** on weekends - chop vegetables, cook grains in bulk
- Keep a stocked pantry (canned beans, whole grains, olive oil, spices)
- Plan meals for the week before shopping

## Focus on Simple Recipes First
- Stir-fries (15-20 minutes)
- Sheet pan meals (minimal prep, oven does the work)
- Grain bowls with whatever vegetables you have
- Egg-based dishes

## Helpful Resources
- YouTube channels like Joshua Weissman or Ethan Chlebowski
- Apps like Yummly or Mealime for recipe ideas
- Cookbooks like *Salt Fat Acid Heat* for understanding fundamentals

## Practical Tips
- Cook once, eat twice - make extra portions
- Use frozen vegetables (just as nutritious, no prep)
- A few quality tools help (sharp knife, sheet pan, good pan)
- Start with 2-3 recipes you rotate until comfortable

## Realistic Expectations
- Expect a learning curve at first
- Speed comes naturally with practice
- Even 30-minute meals are a win
