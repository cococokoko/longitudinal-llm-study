For immediate relief:

- Grounding techniques, such as identifying:

- Eat balanced meals and stay hydrated.
- Limit excessive caffeine or alcohol if they worsen anxiety.
- Schedule time for activities you enjoy and social connection.
- Use organizational tools such as calendars, to-do lists, or breaking large tasks into smaller steps.

For managing anxious thoughts:
- Notice and label the thought rather than treating it as a fact.
- Ask yourself what evidence supports or contradicts the worry.
- Focus on what is within your control right now.
- Consider writing worries down and reviewing them later instead of repeatedly thinking about them.
