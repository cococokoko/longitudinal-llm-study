# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

Given your busy role supporting family and managing household responsibilities, mindfulness can be genuinely valuable - and it doesn't require much time.

## Starting Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily**
- Consistency matters more than duration
- Same time each day helps build the habit
- Morning often works well before demands start

## Basic Techniques to Try First

**Breath awareness**
- Sit comfortably, focus on breathing
- Notice when your mind wanders, gently return attention
- That's literally the whole practice

**Body scan**
- Mentally check in from head to toe
- Notice tension without trying to fix it immediately

## Practical for Your Situation

- **Micro-moments count** - mindful dishwashing, mindful walking
- **Transition moments** - before answering the phone, between tasks
- **No special equipment needed**

## Free Resources Worth Trying

- Insight Timer app (large free library)
- YouTube guided meditations
- UCLA Mindful app

## Honest Expectations

- Your mind **will** wander - that's normal, not failure
- Benefits typically build over **weeks**, not days
- Some days will feel pointless - keep going anyway

## A Realistic Starting Point

Try **5 minutes of breath focus** tomorrow morning before household activity begins.
