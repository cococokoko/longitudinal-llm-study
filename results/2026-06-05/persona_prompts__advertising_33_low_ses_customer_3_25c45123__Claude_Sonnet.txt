# Making Your Own Natural Beauty Products

Here are some popular DIY options that are simple and effective:

## Skincare

**Simple Face Moisturizer**
- Coconut oil or shea butter as a base
- Add a few drops of essential oils (lavender, tea tree)
- Mix and store in a clean jar

**Honey Face Mask**
- Raw honey applied directly to skin
- Leave 10-15 minutes, rinse with warm water
- Great for hydration and mild antibacterial benefits

**Sugar Scrub**
- 1 cup sugar + ½ cup coconut oil
- Add lemon juice or vanilla extract
- Gently exfoliates skin

## Hair Care

**Deep Conditioning Mask**
- Mashed avocado + olive oil + honey
- Apply to hair, leave 20-30 minutes
- Rinse thoroughly

**Dry Shampoo**
- Arrowroot powder or cornstarch
- Add cocoa powder for darker hair
- Apply to roots, brush through

## Lip Care

**Simple Lip Balm**
- Beeswax + coconut oil + honey
- Melt together, pour into small containers
- Add peppermint oil for flavor

## Tips for Success
- **Use clean containers** to prevent bacteria
- **Patch test** new products first
- **Store properly** - refrigerate products without preservatives
- **Start simple** before trying complex recipes
