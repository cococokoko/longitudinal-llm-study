# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a simple, effective routine to keep dry skin hydrated and healthy:

## Morning Routine
1. **Gentle cleanser** - Use a cream or hydrating cleanser (avoid foaming cleansers)
2. **Toner** - Choose an alcohol-free, hydrating toner
3. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid serum works great for moisture
4. **Moisturizer** - Look for ingredients like:
   - Ceramides
   - Glycerin
   - Shea butter
5. **SPF 30+** - Always protect your skin from sun damage

## Evening Routine
1. **Cleanser** - Same gentle cleanser
2. **Serum** - Hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
3. **Moisturizer** - Can use a richer/thicker cream at night
4. **Facial oil** *(optional)* - Rosehip or jojoba oil to seal in moisture

## Key Tips
- **Drink plenty of water** throughout the day
- Apply moisturizer while skin is **slightly damp**
- Avoid **hot showers** which strip natural oils
- Use a **humidifier** indoors if possible
- Exfoliate **gently** 1-2 times per week
