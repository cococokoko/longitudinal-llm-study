# Building a Personal Brand on Social Media

## Start With Strategy

**Define your foundation first:**
- What's your core expertise or perspective?
- Who is your target audience?
- What problem do you solve or value do you provide?
- What makes your point of view distinct?

---

## Choose Your Platforms Wisely

| Platform | Best For |
|----------|----------|
| LinkedIn | Professional/B2B content |
| Instagram | Visual storytelling, lifestyle |
| X/Twitter | Thought leadership, commentary |
| TikTok/YouTube | Video, tutorials, personality |
| Substack | Long-form writing, newsletters |

**Recommendation:** Master 1-2 platforms before expanding

---

## Content Principles

**The basics that actually work:**
- Be **consistently specific** rather than broadly general
- Share genuine opinions, not just safe takes
- Document your journey, not just your achievements
- Mix formats: stories, lessons, questions, insights
- Show your thinking process, not just conclusions

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Trying to appeal to everyone
- Posting without engagement
- Copying others' style exactly
- Inconsistent posting then burnout
- Prioritizing followers over relationships

---

## Practical Starting Points

1. Post consistently for **90 days** before judging results
2. Engage with others **more than you broadcast**
3. Repurpose one idea across multiple formats
4. Build an email list early
