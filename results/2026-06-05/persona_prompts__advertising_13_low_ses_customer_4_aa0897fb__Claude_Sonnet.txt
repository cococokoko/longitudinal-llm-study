## The Core Method (Pan-Sear)

**Before cooking:**
- Bring steak to **room temperature** (30 min out of fridge)
- Pat **completely dry** with paper towels
- Season generously with **salt and pepper**

**Cooking:**
1. Cast iron skillet on **high heat** until smoking
2. Add high smoke-point oil
3. Sear **2-3 minutes per side** without moving
4. Add **butter, garlic, and thyme** - baste continuously
5. **Rest 5-10 minutes** before cutting

---

## Temperature Guide
- Rare: 125°F
- Medium-rare: 130-135°F (**most recommended**)
- Medium: 140-145°F
- Well done: 160°F+

---

## Quick Tips
- **A thermometer is your best friend**
- Thicker cuts benefit from **reverse sear**
- Don't skip the **resting step**
