## Effective Local Small Business Marketing

**Low-Cost/Free Options**
- Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) - critical for local search
- Nextdoor app for neighborhood-level reach
- Facebook local groups and a business page
- Word of mouth and referral programs

**Community Presence**
- Sponsor local events or sports teams
- Join your local Chamber of Commerce
- Partner with complementary businesses
- Attend farmers markets or community events

**Practical Digital Steps**
- Collect and respond to Google reviews consistently
- Post regularly on relevant social media platforms
- Build a simple, mobile-friendly website

**Traditional Methods That Still Work**
- Door hangers or flyers in targeted neighborhoods
- Local newspaper or community newsletter ads
- Vehicle signage if you make deliveries or service calls

**Key Principles**
- Focus on **one or two channels** rather than spreading thin
- Ask satisfied customers for reviews and referrals
- Be consistent rather than sporadic

**What tends to work best depends on your specific business type** - a restaurant has different needs than a plumber, for example.
