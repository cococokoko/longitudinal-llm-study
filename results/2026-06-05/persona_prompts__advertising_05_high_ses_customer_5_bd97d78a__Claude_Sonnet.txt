# Healthy Meal Planning on a Budget

Here are practical strategies to eat well without overspending:

## Core Principles

- **Plan before you shop** - decide meals for the week first
- **Build around whole foods** - grains, legumes, eggs, seasonal vegetables
- **Cook in batches** - saves time and reduces waste

## Budget-Friendly Protein Sources

| Option | Approx. Cost | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Eggs | Low | Versatile, nutrient-dense |
| Canned beans/lentils | Very low | High fiber and protein |
| Canned tuna/salmon | Low-moderate | Omega-3 rich |
| Chicken thighs | Moderate | Cheaper than breasts |

## Smart Shopping Habits

- **Buy frozen vegetables** - equally nutritious, longer shelf life
- **Shop seasonal produce** - significantly cheaper
- **Use store brands** - often identical quality
- **Buy staples in bulk** - rice, oats, dried beans

## Simple Weekly Framework

1. **Pick 2-3 proteins** to rotate
2. **Choose 1-2 grains** as bases
3. **Add 4-5 vegetables** in different forms
4. **Prep Sunday** for the week ahead

## Quick Meal Ideas

- Grain bowls with roasted vegetables
- Bean soups and stews
- Stir-fries with eggs or tofu
- Overnight oats for breakfast
