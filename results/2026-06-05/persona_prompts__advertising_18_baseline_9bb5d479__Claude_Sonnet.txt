# Stock Market Investment Strategies

Here are well-established approaches, ranging from beginner to more advanced:

## Core Principles First
- **Only invest money you can afford to leave invested** for years
- **Diversification** reduces risk significantly
- **Costs matter** - fees compound just like returns do

## Proven Strategies

### Passive/Index Investing
- Buy broad market index funds (S&P 500, total market)
- Low fees, historically beats most active managers
- Simple and time-tested

### Dollar-Cost Averaging
- Invest fixed amounts regularly regardless of market conditions
- Reduces impact of market timing mistakes
- Works well for long-term wealth building

### Value Investing
- Buy stocks trading below estimated intrinsic value
- Requires research and patience
- Associated with Warren Buffett/Benjamin Graham

### Growth Investing
- Focus on companies with strong growth potential
- Higher risk/reward profile
- Requires understanding business fundamentals

## Practical Recommendations
- **Start with tax-advantaged accounts** (401k, IRA)
- **Keep costs low** - expense ratios under 0.2% if possible
- **Rebalance periodically**
- **Avoid emotional decisions** during volatility

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Trying to time the market
- Chasing recent performance
- Overtrading
- Ignoring taxes
