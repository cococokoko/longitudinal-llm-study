# Best Skin Routine for Dry Skin

Here's a straightforward, effective routine:

## Morning
1. **Gentle cleanser** - cream or hydrating formula (avoid foaming)
2. **Hydrating toner** - with hyaluronic acid or glycerin
3. **Moisturizer** - look for ceramides, shea butter, or squalane
4. **SPF 30+** - mineral or chemical sunscreen

## Evening
1. **Gentle cleanser**
2. **Serum** - hyaluronic acid or niacinamide
3. **Rich moisturizer/face oil** - heavier than your daytime formula
4. **Occasional retinol** - 1-2x per week (builds skin resilience over time)

## Key Ingredients to Look For
- Hyaluronic acid
- Ceramides
- Glycerin
- Squalane
- Shea butter

## Quick Tips
- **Lukewarm water only** - hot water strips moisture
- Apply moisturizer **while skin is slightly damp**
- Consider a **humidifier** in your bedroom
- Drink adequate water
- Weekly **gentle exfoliation** (avoid harsh scrubs)

- CeraVe, La Roche-Posay (accessible)
- Tatcha, Drunk Elephant (premium)
