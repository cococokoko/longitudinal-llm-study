  - Tacos with lean protein and vegetables
  - Pasta with vegetables and a protein source

  - Proteins: eggs, canned beans, tofu, chicken, fish, Greek yogurt

  - Healthy fats: olive oil, nuts, seeds, avocado
  - Flavor boosters: garlic, onions, herbs, spices, soy sauce, lemon

  - Healthy recipe websites with short ingredient lists
  - Cookbooks aimed at beginners or meal prep
