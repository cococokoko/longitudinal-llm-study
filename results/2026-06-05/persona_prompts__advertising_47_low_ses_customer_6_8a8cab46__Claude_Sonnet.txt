# Tips for Running a Marathon

Here are some helpful tips to prepare for and complete a marathon:

## Training
- **Follow a structured plan** - Most plans are 16-20 weeks long
- **Build mileage gradually** - Increase weekly mileage by no more than 10% per week
- **Include long runs** - Build up to at least one 20-mile training run
- **Rest days matter** - Allow your body to recover

## Nutrition & Hydration
- **Practice fueling during training** - Use gels, chews, or real food
- **Stay hydrated** - Drink water consistently, not just during runs
- **Carb load** - Increase carbohydrate intake 2-3 days before race day
- **Nothing new on race day** - Stick to what worked in training

## Gear
- **Invest in good shoes** - Get properly fitted at a running store
- **Moisture-wicking clothing** - Avoid cotton
- **Body Glide or anti-chafe products** - Prevent blisters and chafing

## Race Day Strategy
- **Start slow** - Many runners go out too fast
- **Run your own race** - Ignore the crowd pace
- **Break it into segments** - Focus on smaller goals

## Recovery
- **Plan for rest after the race**
- **Stretch and foam roll regularly**
- **Listen to your body**
