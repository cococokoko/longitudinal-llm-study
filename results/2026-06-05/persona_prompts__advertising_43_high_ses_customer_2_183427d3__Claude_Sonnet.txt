# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical strategies across key areas:

## Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Select a venue that uses renewable energy

## Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper with soy-based inks if printing
- Minimize paper programs and menus

## Food & Catering
- Source locally grown, seasonal food
- Offer plant-forward or vegetarian menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters

## Flowers & Décor
- Choose locally grown, seasonal flowers
- Rent décor rather than buying
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral alternatives

## Attire
- Consider vintage or secondhand wedding attire
- Choose designers with sustainable practices
- Plan to rewear or donate the dress afterward

## Waste Management
- Provide clearly labeled recycling and compost bins
- Avoid single-use plastics
- Choose biodegradable confetti

## Carbon Offset
- Calculate your wedding's carbon footprint
- Invest in verified carbon offset programs

## Favors
- Give seed packets, local honey, or charitable donations
