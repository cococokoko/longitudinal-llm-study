# Getting Started with Meditation & Mindfulness

## Start Simple

**Begin with just 5 minutes daily** - consistency matters more than duration. You can gradually increase over time.

## Basic Meditation Technique

1. **Find a comfortable position** - sitting, lying down, or even walking
2. **Focus on your breath** - notice the inhale and exhale
3. **When your mind wanders** - gently bring attention back (this is normal and expected)
4. **Be patient with yourself** - there's no "perfect" meditation

## Practical Tips for Beginners

- **Same time daily** builds habit (morning works well for many people)
- **Quiet space** helps initially, though not required
- **Use guided apps** like Headspace, Calm, or Insight Timer
- **Start with body scans** if breath focus feels difficult

## Types to Explore

| Type | Good For |
|------|----------|
| Breath awareness | Beginners |
| Body scan | Stress/tension |
| Loving-kindness | Emotional wellbeing |
| Walking meditation | People who struggle sitting still |

## Everyday Mindfulness

- Eat one meal without distractions
- Notice sensations during routine tasks
- Take a few conscious breaths before meetings

## Realistic Expectations

- Benefits often take **weeks of practice** to notice
- A wandering mind **doesn't mean failure**
- Even 2-3 minutes has value
