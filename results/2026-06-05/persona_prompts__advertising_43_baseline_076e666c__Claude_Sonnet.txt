# Planning an Environmentally Friendly Wedding

Here are practical ways to reduce your wedding's environmental impact:

## 📍 Venue & Location
- Choose a local venue to minimize guest travel
- Look for venues with green certifications
- Consider outdoor spaces that need minimal decoration
- Pick a single location for ceremony and reception

## 💌 Invitations & Paper
- Send digital invitations or save-the-dates
- Use recycled paper if printing is necessary
- Avoid excess inserts and packaging

## 🌸 Flowers & Decorations
- Choose **seasonal and locally grown** flowers
- Rent decorations instead of buying new
- Use potted plants guests can take home
- Consider dried flowers or non-floral centerpieces

## 🍽️ Food & Catering
- Serve **plant-based or locally sourced** menu options
- Work with caterers who minimize food waste
- Donate leftover food to local shelters
- Avoid single-use plastics

## 👗 Attire
- Buy secondhand or vintage wedding attire
- Rent suits/tuxedos
- Choose a dress you can rewear or repurpose

## 🚗 Transportation
- Arrange group shuttles for guests
- Choose a venue accessible by public transit
- Consider carbon offsets for travel

## 💍 Rings & Gifts
- Choose ethically sourced or recycled metal rings
- Request experiences or charity donations as gifts
- Create a registry with sustainable brands

## ♻️ Waste Reduction
- Use compostable or reusable tableware
- Set up recycling and compost stations
- Choose a caterer who handles waste responsibly

## 💡 Quick Tips
- **Offset your carbon footprint** through verified programs
- Keep the guest list intimate
- Choose a **digital wedding website** over printed programs
