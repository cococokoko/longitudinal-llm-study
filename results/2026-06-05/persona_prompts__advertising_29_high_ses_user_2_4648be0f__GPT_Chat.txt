  - Use puzzle feeders, training exercises, toys, and enrichment activities.

  - Teach new skills and behaviors using positive reinforcement.

  - Provide a clean living space and a comfortable place to rest.
  - Keep toxic substances, dangerous foods, medications, and hazards out of reach.
  - Ensure adequate temperature control and shelter.

• Support emotional well-being
  - Spend quality time interacting with your pets.
  - Learn their individual preferences and stress signals.
  - Maintain predictable routines when possible.

• Practice good grooming and hygiene
  - Brush as appropriate for the coat type.
  - Keep nails trimmed.
  - Follow veterinary guidance on dental care, which is often overlooked but important.

  - Puppies, kittens, and young animals need socialization and training.
