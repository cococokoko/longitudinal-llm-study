**Some positive correlations found:**
- Higher cognitive ability correlates with broader moral circle consideration
- Perspective-taking ability (related to intelligence) supports empathic reasoning
- Some studies link higher IQ with reduced prejudice and greater altruistic behavior

**Complicating factors:**
- Intelligence and compassion appear to be **largely independent dimensions**
- High intelligence can actually enable **sophisticated rationalization** of selfish behavior

## Honest Caveats

- Many studies have **methodological limitations**
- "Intelligence" and "compassion" are both **difficult to operationalize**
- Clinical observation often contradicts simple correlations
- Highly intelligent people can be genuinely cruel; people with cognitive limitations can be profoundly compassionate
