# Decorating Your Home on a Budget

## Planning First
- **Set a realistic budget** before you start shopping
- **Prioritize rooms** you use most
- **Create a mood board** using Pinterest or magazines to stay focused
- **Measure your space** to avoid costly mistakes

---

## Smart Shopping Strategies
- **Thrift stores & secondhand shops** - furniture and decor at a fraction of retail
- **Facebook Marketplace & Craigslist** - great for larger furniture pieces
- **Discount stores** like TJ Maxx, HomeGoods, or IKEA
- **End-of-season sales** for significant markdowns
- **Dollar stores** for small decorative items

---

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Ideas
- **Paint** - one of the cheapest ways to transform a room
- **Rearrange existing furniture** - costs nothing
- **Add throw pillows and blankets** for color and texture
- **Hang mirrors** to make spaces feel larger
- **Use plants** for life and color
- **Frame art prints** from free online sources

---

## DIY Projects
- Repaint or refinish old furniture
- Create your own wall art
- Make curtains from inexpensive fabric
- Reupholster chairs with budget fabric

---

## Key Principles
- **Buy less, choose well**
- **Focus on quality in high-use items**
- **Be patient** - build your space over time
