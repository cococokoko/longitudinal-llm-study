# Quick Organization Tip for You

## Try the "5-Minute End-of-Day Reset"

Before you leave school each day, spend just 5 minutes doing this:

- **Write down your top 3 priorities** for tomorrow on a sticky note
- **Clear your desk** of anything you don't need
- **Set out materials** you'll need first thing in the morning

### Why This Works Well for Your Situation

As a single parent, your mornings are probably **already hectic** before you even get to school. Having tomorrow already planned means:

- You can **hit the ground running** without decision fatigue
- You won't waste mental energy remembering what needs to happen
- You feel **more in control** even on chaotic days

### Bonus Thought
Keep a **separate simple list** for parent communications that need follow-up - this is easy to forget when you're rushing home for childcare pickup.

---

The goal is making your **school time as efficient as possible** so you're not carrying work stress home when you already have a full evening ahead of you.
