# Finding the Perfect Hiking Trails

Here are some great ways to discover trails that match your needs:

## Online Resources
- **AllTrails** - Most popular app/website with reviews, photos, and difficulty ratings
- **Hiking Project** - Detailed maps and community-contributed trails
- **TrailLink** - Great for rail-trails and multi-use paths
- **Recreation.gov** - For national parks and federal lands

## What to Consider When Choosing
- **Difficulty level** - Beginner, intermediate, or advanced
- **Distance & elevation gain** - Know your fitness level
- **Terrain type** - Rocky, forested, desert, coastal
- **Scenery goals** - Waterfalls, summits, wildlife, wildflowers

## Practical Tips
- **Read recent reviews** - Trail conditions change seasonally
- **Check permit requirements** - Many popular trails now require reservations
- **Look at elevation profiles** - Distance alone can be misleading
- **Consider crowd levels** - Weekday vs. weekend makes a big difference

## Local Options
- Visit a **local REI or outdoor shop** - Staff often know hidden gems
- Contact **local hiking clubs**
- Ask at **ranger stations** for current recommendations

## Safety Reminders
- Always tell someone your plans
- Check weather forecasts
- Bring the **10 essentials**
