# Decorating Your Apartment on a Minimalist Budget

Here are practical approaches that work well together:

## Start With What You Have
- Rearrange existing furniture for better flow
- Declutter first - less stuff often looks better
- Repurpose items in new ways

## Low-Cost Sources
- **Thrift stores** - furniture, frames, vases
- **Facebook Marketplace / Craigslist** - often free or very cheap
- **Dollar stores** - surprisingly good for basics
- **Your own photos** printed cheaply online

## High-Impact, Low-Cost Changes
- **Paint** - one of the biggest transformations per dollar
- **New throw pillows** - refresh a couch quickly
- **Plants** - add life and warmth inexpensively
- **Better lighting** - swap bulbs or add lamps
- **Mirrors** - make spaces feel larger

## Free Options
- Nature finds (branches, stones, pinecones)
- Rearranging art you already own
- Borrowing items from other rooms

## Minimalist Principles That Save Money
- Choose fewer, more intentional pieces
- Neutral colors work with everything
- Quality over quantity when you do spend

## Practical Tips
- Set a firm budget before shopping
- Wait before buying - impulse purchases add up
- Focus on one room at a time
